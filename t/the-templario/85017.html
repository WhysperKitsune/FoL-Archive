The Templar

Protect (Night) - Protect a player from visits from any specific non-BD class. For example, if you protect someone from The Apostle, and The Apostle actually visits them, you will occupy and attack The Apostle. Cannot protect from the same class more than once. (Infinite uses)

Duel (Night) - Identify a player’s non-BD class. Kill them if you are correct, bypassing everything. Otherwise, suicide. For example, if you duel someone you identify as The Mastermind you will kill them. But if they were The Sheriff, you’d die instead. (Infinite uses)
