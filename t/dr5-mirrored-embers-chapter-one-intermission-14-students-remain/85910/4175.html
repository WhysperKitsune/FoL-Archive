The Execution for the Ultimate Historian, Josephine Farrierson

Hail Caesar

Suddenly looking around with panic at the rest of the room, Josephine looked around to her classmates, staring her down with unprecedented anger in their eyes. Then, Monoluna flicked her wrist, and out from her pelt-sleeve came a long, glowing green thread, which quickly wrapped itself around the Ultimate Historian’s neck and dragged her away… through a wall, and into what looked like an ancient Roman building.

A screen flicked on showing the historian looking around curiously at the empty pillars surrounding them… then from one of the pillars shot a knife attatched to one of Monoluna’s threads, piercing Josephine’s leg and causing them to buckle to the floor. They cried out in pain. Then another slipped out. And another. And another.

Knife after knife after knife implanted itself in the Ultimate Historian’s body, none of them poised to kill but all poised to cause absolute agony. Then, right as one was about to plunge into the Historian’s heart and put them out of their misery… it stopped. More and more of the phantom threads came up from the floor, forming a makeshift pyre of magical thread around the Ultimate Historian’s legs. They were fixed in place, bleeding, screaming, and struggling against their inevitable demise.

Aretena and Monkeuma walked up to the pyre, somberly watching as the murderer tried desperately, knowing it to be impossible, to esacpe their brutal fate. And then both touched the fire, and it lit up in flames, for seemingly no reason.

The screams could be heard from miles away.
