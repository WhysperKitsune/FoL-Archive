[Class Suggestion] The Seer

Class/Lore/Fanfic
Shrimpy June 30, 2018, 7:43pm #1

The Seer

Blue Dragon Support
Destine (Day) - Select a player to consume all charges of Destine. If ever they were to die, their death will be postponed by the number of Destine charges and they will not be notified of the attack. A destined player who is attacked has their remaining days to live decreased by one. - 1 charge
Prophecy (Night) - Predict who dies tonight (not predicting counts as predicting no deaths). If correct, gain 2 charges of Destine. - Infinite uses
Defeat the Unseen or Cult and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.

Converts to:

The Oracle

Unseen Support
Twist Fate (Day) - Select two players. If one were to die tonight, the other will die instead (works on Prince execution and King order execution). - 1 charge
Doom (Night) - Predict who is executed tomorrow (not predicting counts as predicting no daytime execution). If correct, gain 1 charge of Twist Fate. - Infinite uses
Defeat the Blue Dragon and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.
