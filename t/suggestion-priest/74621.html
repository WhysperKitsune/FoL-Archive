The Priest

Blue Dragon Social

Shepherd (Day) - Turn as many players as you like into sheep. All sheep are forced to vote the same as the majority of the sheep. You count towards the majority. (∞ uses)

Shibboleth (Day) - Pick a password. Anyone who uses your password in day chat becomes a follower. When you or a follower is on trial, the votes of yourself and other followers will count twice. (∞ uses)

Last Rites (Day) - Receive the feedback of target player whenever they die. Remains on target player until changed, like Scout. Needs a more a generic name, I know. (∞ uses)

Count Sheep (Night) - Learn the number of living Blue Dragon players. (2 uses)

Grapevine (Night) - Read all whispers from the entire match, but anonymized by hiding speaker labels and shuffling them in a random order. Maybe it should give all feedback also? (2 uses)

Confessional (Night) - Open up a tab for anonymous communication with scum. (∞ uses)

To illustrate the Shepherd ability, say 5 people vote to execute Prince while only 3 players pardon (counting your own pardon). By herding the 2 other pardons with any single one of the executes, you force that particular execute to flip into a pardon. So the final tally is 4 pardon and 4 executes, and Prince is saved.
