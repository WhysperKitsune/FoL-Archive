:// Welcome to .xyz. Good luck Agents, and godspeed.

Rules

~ Three teams of five. Team X, Team Y, and Team Z. Please include the team you’d like to be on in your signup, and feel free to swap teams until the game begins. Remember to be inclusive though guys, I’m trusting y’all! <3

~ Once the game begins, a series of clues / riddles will be posted for your first puzzle. The solution to every puzzle will be one word, though the way the clues should be interpreted per puzzle may be wildly different. Work to solve the puzzle with your teammates in your team DM. None of them will be easy, and you will likely need the help of the majority of your team to solve them.

~ Once your team thinks they have the solution, vote for it in your team DM.
~ Example: /vote ‘solution’

~ Once your team’s vote hits majority for a solution, it’s locked in. If you get it wrong, you’ll need to wait 6 hours to vote for a new one.

~ The last team to determine the solution will be required to vote out a member. This voting is done privately in a DM with me, which you will be tasked with creating.

~ Once that vote ends, the next puzzle gets posted. The next puzzle goes live ~12-24 hours after an annoucement, so that every player can at least know when the next puzzle will go live.
