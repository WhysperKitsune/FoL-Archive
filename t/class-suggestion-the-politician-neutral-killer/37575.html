The Politician - Neutral Killer
Passive 1: Royal Sway - You can step up with royals. If you become the Psycho king you will also be night immune permanently.
Passive 2: Calm Demeanor. You are immune to Poison, Bleed and target changing.

Day ability 1: Nightshade meal - Poison your target with nightshade. They will die in 2 days. (Unlimited)
Day ability 2: Political Pressure - Force 1 person to accuse someone else and stop further voting. (2 uses)

Night ability 1: Asset block - Occupy target player and prevent visitors. If they are poisoned or bleeding they will die immediately. Can target self. (Unlimited)
Night ability 2: Cunning strike - Attack the target. You will be night immune this night. Cannot be used if you poisoned someone the previous day. (Unlimited)

Goal: Eliminate the main factions.

KyoDaz:

I feel like Cunning Strike is only there to provide Night Immunity and killing vs Healers.

This is entirely correct. The idea is that if you are worried about N1 deaths or whatever they you use Cunning Strike. If think you are safe (you have a knight on you for example) then you can safely use poison and asset block to quickly kill others. If you think there are no healers in play you can also use Asset block on yourself while you let poison kill everyone until it gets to late game.

I didn’t want to let Cunning Strike and Poison be used in the same day/night cycle because that would make the KPN be greater than 1. I also didn’t want to make either of them limited because Poison is supposed to be an important part of using the class (if you think you are safe then poison everyone to death) but I don’t want to limit Cunning Strike either because it could cause problems if left with just a healer.

I also feel like Asset block is inherently a good enough ability when paired with poison to be worth the loss of passive night immunity.
