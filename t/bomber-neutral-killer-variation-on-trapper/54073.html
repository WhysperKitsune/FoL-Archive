The Bomber (Neutral Killer)

Nighttime abilities:
Tripwire (infinite uses): Set a tripwire on a player. If they use any abilities tonight, they will die. Unless you’ve targeted yourself, in which case anybody who uses an ability on you will die (You can only target yourself a limited number of times).

Daytime abilities:
Timebomb (limited uses): Set a timebomb on a player, notifying them. They will die after 2 nights, and kill anybody they are visiting along with anybody visiting them. If anybody visits them the first night though, the bomb will be defused.
