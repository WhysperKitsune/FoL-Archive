Gameplay

The day phase lasts 72 hours or until both the present and lynch is complete.
The night lasts 24 hours.
Ties are left to RNGsus

Cards

Friend

FACTION TYPE
PASSIVE (Passive) - If you are lucky to get a passive
DAY ABILITY (Day) - Random day ability if you are lucky
NIGHT ABILITY (Night) - Random night ability No luck required to get one of these. Everyone will have one.
GOAL. Lynch all evildoers.

The Ruiner

FACTION TYPE
PASSIVE (Passive) - If you are a lucky mafia you may have one
DAY ABILITY (Day) - Lucky people only
Night Ability (Night) - Night ability… DON’T WORRY EVERYONE GETS ONE
GOAL. Kill all that are not neutral or are not in the ruiner.

Friend’s Friend

Neutral
PASSIVE (Passive) - You have one of these
DAY ABILITY (Day) - You have one of these
NIGHT ABILITY (Night) - Wow…You also have one of these.
GOAL. Survive to see friends lose!

The Enemy

Neutral Killer
PASSIVE (Passive) - Night Immune. Maybe more!
DAY ABILITY (Day) - You have a good one…
Cake Overload(Night) - Give someone so much cake they explode. (Killing them and only them)
GOAL. Kill everyone that will stand in your way. Neutrals are fine.

To Join

To join, simply express interest in joining by saying something like “I want to join” or “/join” or “/in” or “sign me up, coach” or whatever you want

Presents!

Every day before the lynch, everybody must decide who gets the present. It can be a good present, and give you a boon, or a bad present, and curse you. The Enemy and Friend’s Friend may hold some sway here…
