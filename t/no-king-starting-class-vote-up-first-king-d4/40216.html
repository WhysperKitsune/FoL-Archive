I think tweaking starting King is better than this, but the root cause is the same

copy-paste from ptr notes

Hey Boslof, I’ve been giving it some thought, and I think the King needs a bit more tweaking before launch.

Order is potentially strong on the King, but they need to live for a decent amount of time for it to reach its full potential. However, without Allies, I don’t see this happening very often.

Don’t get me wrong - removing Allies is AMAZING. However, which is more valuable - Order, or a confirmed BD King? I’d be willing to bet that our Day 4 King executions will jump up by a lot once this patch hits, simply because Order isn’t really worth it.

I don’t know how much you’ve read of my “Some Ideas” mini-megathread, but I think that the solution to this is to give the starting BD King a few special mechanics. If you give the starting BD King an ability the BD will be unwilling to lose, and you punish the BD for incorrectly executing a BD King, the regicide rate is unlikely to spike as much. My personal suggestions are below, but pretty much anything works as long as it achieves this end goal.

orangeandblack5:

BD Boon: Starting Good King’s guards protect against conversion as well as kills.

BD Punishment: If a Good King dies to public execution or Butler, the royal bloodline is useless and royals may not step up early.

[Rework Suggestion] The King! A more vulnerable, more social class
