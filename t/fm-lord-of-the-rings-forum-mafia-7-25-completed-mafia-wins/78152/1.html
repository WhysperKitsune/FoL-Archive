Gameplay:

“That there’s some good in this world, Mr. Frodo… and it’s worth fighting for.”

This is a closed setup, i.e. there is no rolelist or classcard list. You will each receive a class card in dms with unique abilities and flavour.
The mafia will communicate in a private message on the forums.
Some classes may have abilities that perform the same effect at night as this is a high-power setup.
Days night cycles are 72/24, with the first 24 hours for the day dedicated to voting for the location of the day/night cycle and the remaining 48 hours dedicated to lynching.
There is a day one lynch.
The lynch is majority and EoD plurality unless specified by the location.
Day abilities may be performed regardless of what phase the day is in.
Every class has a hammering passive, this activates if the player hammers a player for a majority lynch, or if they are the last player to vote on a player for a plurality lynch.
There may or may not be conversion in the game.
There may or may not be claim vigilantes in the game.
There may or may not be tailoring and/or framing effects in this game.
There may or may not be neutrals in this game.
There may or may not be dead interaction in this game.
There are hidden mechanics in this game, however, your class card, the OP, host announcements and player flips will not lie to you.

Lynches:

“Your time will come. You will face the same Evil, and you will defeat it.”

To accuse a player, bold your message and type /vote [player] or /accuse [player].
Should majority be reached on a player, they will be executed and the day finishes early.
Should no majority be reached, plurality will take effect and the player with the most votes at the end of the day will be lynched, ending the day.

Definitions:

“May it be your light in the darkness; when all other lights go out.”

Empower - Someone who is ‘empowered’ cannot be roleblocked, or have their target changed. Can only be overruled by someone ‘preventing visits.’

Prevent Visits - Prevents all visits regardless of empowerment or immunity to role blocking etc.

Night Immune - Will survive any attacks at night that do not bypass night immunity.

Occupy - Prevent someone’s night actions, otherwise known as roleblocking.

Positive Effects - Includes healing, occupation/redirection immunity gained from another player, night immunity gained from another player, empowerment.

Negative Effects - Includes occupation, redirection, Framing, and Mind Warp.
