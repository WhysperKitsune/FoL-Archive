Rules and Punishments

All players are required to follow the Global Rules as well as the Forum Game Rules.

All violations are handled on a case-by-case basis by the Moderation Team. Punishments will be issued depending on the severity of the rulebreak, as well as the player’s history. Types of disciplinary actions are as follows:

  • Warning - not listed in Public Moderation Logs
  • Mute (for certain offenses only, such as spam) - not listed in Public Moderation Logs
  • Signup blacklists
  • Alt blacklists
  • Hosting blacklists
  • Suspensions
  • Permanent Bans
