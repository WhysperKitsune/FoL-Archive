More Mechanics

  1. Each Player can Act once per turn.
  2. An Act consists of Movement, Action and Resolution phases.
  3. Movement phase is straightforward, Action phase allows any interaction with any item on the map, entering Combat or any skills used. Resolution refers to any effects that the Action may have caused and resultants thereof.
  4. Status Ailments are present.
  5. Sight: If no allies are in direct Line of Sight with the enemy, the enemy will not appear in view. This applies to the enemy. Move with caution.
  6. Map Events: May Trigger and Change the map accordingly. Players can change the landscape as well, but that will depend on the map and what action the player can reasonably make.
  7. Bosses: Will be marked on the map accordingly.
  8. Enemy Turn: If the enemy moves, you will hear shuffling in a set direction. The host will tell you where the enemy is coming from, but if you can‚Äôt see them, you will not see them on the map.
