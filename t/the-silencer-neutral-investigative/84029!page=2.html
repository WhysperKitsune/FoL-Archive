Passive: Disagree: You cannot spawn with an alchemist and an inquisitor.

while i think this passive makes sense to prevent games from having an absurd amount of killpower, i also think it becomes slightly problematic because there’s so few neutrals left to spawn (the 2nd/3rd neutral slots are always Merc/Sellsword/Fool/Scorned, and the former twos’ existence is usually pretty obvious somewhat early)

thepigeonnyc:

Day ability: Leaked Info: Learn target player’s class type instantly. Unusable day 1. Unlimited uses.

i’m gonna assume for sanitys sake that Specials will appear as Investigative here, because hardconfirming someone as MM/CL with a daycop on a neutral is definitely not something anyone wants to see

i personally think both neutral cops and daycops in general don’t really belong in ToL and this ability doing both is mildly disgusting

this class existing would probably bring back the enhanced paranoia pretender used to bring, except on an even higher level because daycops don’t have the potential downsides of being occupied/redirected, targeting a dead person, or being effected by tailors (since neutrals aren’t effected by frames)

which in simple terms means this has even less counterplay than pretender does, and is arguably even stronger (checking for exact class typing is probably stronger than type pairing + royal blood, because socials aren’t going to fakeclaim Noble very often other than Apostle (who commonly claims Support classes anyway) and Drunk is an uncommon fakeclaim, which leaves S/Is claiming Princess as the real exception here)

in simple terms i think a neutral daycop limits scum fakeclaims even more than pretender did and one of the only things that this does better than pretender is that it announces itself as not being in the game if a Alch/Inq exists and also isn’t permanently death immune
