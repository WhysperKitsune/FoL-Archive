The Unseen had won, taking over Castle Adiart. But now, the small remainder of the Blue Dragon is fighting to take it over again!

Welcome one and all to a game where this time, the informed minority is the Blue Dragon, trying to get their Kingdom back from the clutches of the Unseen.

Rules:

  • Days will last 96 hours (4 IRL days)

  • Nights will last 48 hours (2 IRL days)

  • Players must post content within 24 hours. If no content has been posted for that long, then that player will recieve a prod. Failing to respond within 24 hours after the prod (as some players may be in different timezones) will result the player in getting replaced

  • Journals will be presented on death, but only updated ones

  • Blue Dragon will have a night chat only

  • A specific player can only be sent on trial once per day. If pardoned, they will gain immunity to being a valid vote until the next day

The Changes:

  • The Unseen has become the uninformed majority

  • Neutrals still spawn as usual besides mercenary which will spawn as old mercenary because soldier is in this game

  • Mastermind, Assassin and Sellsword will NOT spawn in the game

  • The cap size for the Blue Dragon is 4

  • If the Negotiator dies, the newest member will become the Negotiator. If the Negotiator dies and there is only the oldest Blue Dragon Killer, then they will become the new Negotiator

The Insurgent cannot be replaced

All conversions have unique abilities, except the Knight
