(Class Suggestion) Master Vampire (Neutral Killer)

Class/Lore/Fanfic
Htm April 8, 2018, 6:17pm #1

Master Vampire, Neutral Killer

Passive 1: Blood Suck - Immune to death. You are also occupation/target change immune but will not be seen.

Passive 2: Blood Thirsty - Will die quicker to poison/bleeding.

Day Ability 1: Bite - Bite someone in the day. It will cause them to bleed/poison (bleeding in cult games, poison in unseen games) and it will heal you out of bleeding/poison it. 2 use.

Day Ability 2: My Little Minion - Transform someone (BD(not prince)/King) into a vampire. They will stay exactly the same except they will gain you’re goal and get notified of who you are. They will not appear as vampire when they die, they will appear as their previous class. unlimited use, 1 day cool down. (Note: can’t have 2 vampires at a time, also your vampire will become immune to conversion) (Note: if you die they die too)

Night Ability 1: Blood Drink - Drink all of a person’s blood. They’ll die. Unlimited uses

Night Ability 2: Bat Combination - Kill you’re target going through everything. Can only be used if you have a vampire and that vampire will die. 0 use

Goal: Same as Reaper/Possessor’s goal

I tried making a fun and unique neutral killer. Feedback/suggestions are appreciated, also please tell me if this is too OP

Interesting idea, a NK that is able to convert. I just see a few problems with this.

1)What happens if the Master dies and you have another Vampire? He can’t kill, as he retains his class. How can he be expected to complete the win condition? He is doomed to lose.
2) The fate of the faction is in completely in the hands of the Master. The converted vampires have 0 control over their kills and such, and I see this as a problem. If you’re converted to a faction, you should have some choice in the actions of said faction. You can also be suicided by the Master and have 0 control over your own life.
3) I don’t think this class should be occupy immune. It’s got 0 claimspace, can’t jump like possessor, and can’t side with unseen/cult like sorcerer. Maybe the Occupy Immune should be hidden like MM?

I like this class overall though, the idea of an NK converting people is odd but interesting. It just needs clarification
