  • There are two Attractions: The Petting Zoo and The Arcade. A player can only visit one of the Attractions during the Night. They will not know what each Attraction does.
  • The Petting Zoo: Players may visit one of the animals.
  • Elephant: Players who visit the Elephant will bypass effects that prevent players from otherwise being visited during this Night.
  • Lion: If less than 4 players visit the Lion, all players visiting it will gain a 5% increase to ITA Accuracy during the following Day. The opposite will happen if 4 or more players visit the Lion.
  • Pony: A random player who visits the Pony will be unable to target or be targeted by actions during that Night. Players with fruit have double the chance of being picked.
  • Parrot: Players will know who else picked this option.
  • The Arcade: Three risky gambling games that promise great rewards. Players may play two different games each Night.
  • Slots: Low Intensity - There are 10 numbers (0-9), on 3 wheels. For each wheel, you choose a number. You have a 33% chance to get that number and a 33% chance to be 1 off in either direction. Getting numbers in a row, column, or diagonal of the 3-by-3 grid leads to punishments or rewards. All actions of players who get three different odd numbers or three different even numbers are prevented during that Night. All actions of players who get two of the same number and one different number of the same parity are unable to be prevented or redirected during that Night. All actions targeting players who get three of the same number during that Night will be prevented. Players who get three sevens die. Players with fruit have slightly higher chances of success.
  • Blackjack: Medium Intensity - You play with the dealer and anybody else who joins in. Dealer hits on anything below a hard 17. Players who bust are killed. Players who achieve the lowest score are vanillarizaed during that Night. Players who win get 1-shot Gutless Watcher. Players who hit 21 get 1-shot Super Commandant that provides its target’s full role.
  • Craps: High Intensity - Predict the result of the dice. On doubles, you die unless you correctly predicted doubles of any sort. On a 7, if you correctly predicted it, you reroll, ignoring results of 5 and 9, then 5,6,8,9 other than doubles, then 5-9 other than doubles. If you correctly predict a number greater than 9 or lower than 5, you gain a 1-shot Deathproof. On any other result, nothing happens.
