Dragon Trainer

Unique Blue Dragon Killer
Caring Broodmother (Passive) - While you are alive, all of your dragons will not harm you nor any blue dragon members. If you die, all Dragons currently hatched will rampage, killing their owner that night.
Nurture (Day) - Target a player. If that player has an egg, it will hatch into a dragon. - Unlimited Uses, 1 Night cooldown.
Foster Family (Day) - Target a player with a dragon or an egg. Give it to another player. - Unlimited Uses.
Dragonmother’s Gift (Night) - Give a player one of your eggs. - 3 Uses.
Dragon’s Fury (Night) - Cause all hatched dragons to kill their owner and any visitors to their owner. - Unlimited Uses.

Dragon Corrupter

Unique Unseen Killer
Spawn Corrupter (Passive) - While you are alive, all of your dragons will not harm any members of the unseen. If you die all dragons will rampage, killing their owner that night.
Shadow Steroids (Day) - Target 2 eggs. They will hatch into dragons. - 1 use.
Adorable Time Bomb (Night) - Give a player one of your eggs. - (however many uses are leftover from before you were converted.)
Draconic Rage (Night) - Cause all hatched dragons to kill their owner and any visitors to their owner. - Unlimited uses.

(Players ARE Informed that they have received a dragon egg.)
