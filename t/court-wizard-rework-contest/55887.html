Court Wizard Rework [Contest]

Class/Lore/Fanfic
PokemonKidRyan April 2, 2018, 2:04pm #1

This is a joint project by myself, @PokemonKidRyan and @Whammerist. We first of all had a look at the rework competition definition of support in which it is as follows:

Court Wizard Contest

Court Wizard is a support, so should have support abilities. This means their abilities either protect or empower other players.

There are 2 main support for Blue Dragon at this time being the Physician and the Court Wizard. If the Physician protects other players, then we can have the Court Wizard providing a passive empowerment to other members of the blue dragon.

The ideas had been thrown back and forth for many days in order to make sure that we could make the best product we can. Enjoy!

The Court Wizard

Blue Dragon Support
Magical Runes (Day) - Your night ability will target the class you choose today - 3 uses
Tornado (Night) - Swap the room of 2 players switching all targeting abilities which would happen against each of them - 3 uses
Magical Boost (Night) - Improve the reliability of the target’s ability tonight. They will not be informed that this has happened - Infinite use

Improvements

Based off of Magical Boost
Investigatives - Investigation will not be able to be tampered with
Support - Occupy and target switch immune
Order Execution - Execution will not be healable tonight
Offensive - Goes through immunities
Special - Conversions go through everything tonight except night immunity (Ignoring Imprisonment, Smite and Merc Guard)
Killer - Bypasses immunity
Social - Switches Occupy Immunity (Fool/Scorned lose it to make them seem good. Mystic and Noble given it to prevent their abilities being messed with via occupation)

To explain magical runes a bit better, it is a limited use ability because it can be extremely powerful if used at the right time. You can attempt to give a boost to something which is potentially in the game via the use of the day ability with the magical boost night ability too (As it will auto target the chosen class).
It also encourages deduction skills since it gets the player using the ability to think of what is alive and what they want to boost to give BD the best chance at different points of the game.

Edits:
CW no longer with barrier
CW becomes a boosting ability class
Tornado becomes 3 use as suggested together by @NuclearBurrito and @Sam17z (Thanks!)
