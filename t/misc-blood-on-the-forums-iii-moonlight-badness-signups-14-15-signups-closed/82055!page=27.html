the endgame trigger for this game can be confusion so let me explain

Good’s wincon is to kill the demon. This means lynching the Pukka, Po, or Shabbolath once, or lynching the Zombuul twice (since the zombuul secretly lives after its first death).

Evil’s wincon is to have the game not end before there are two are fewer players alive. The game may also be called for evil if everyone alive is evil before F2, since no one would nominate the demon if everyone alive was evil.

If both wincons are satisfied simultaneously, good wins. Otherwise, whichever side achieves their win condition first, wins.
