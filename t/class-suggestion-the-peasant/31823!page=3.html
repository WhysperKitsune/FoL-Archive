Livicus June 6, 2018, 5:46am #49

The Peasant

Unique Neutral Special

Goal: Choose a Class to Become and fulfill the Goal of that Class.

Passives

Pitchfork Fury:
You will kill your accuser if executed.

Abilities

Hide: (Night) (3)
(Identical to the Fool’s ability)

New Identity: (Night) (oo)
Choose a dead class and become it starting from the following night. It works similar to the Mastermind’s Dirty Work ability. Doing this changes all passives and abilities to the chosen class.

Parameters:

  • If the target was defiled then you cannot become them.
  • You cannot become a unique class.
  • You cannot become a class that has already won unless it is fool.
  • You cannot become a Scorned who suicided.
  • If you become Scorned you will have the same targets as the dead Scorned.
  • You can become a Sellsword.
  • You may become the Mastermind if he dies after Day 4.
  • If you select a dead Cult Leader, you can choose to become a Cult Underling the next day.
  • You cannot become the King. EVER.
