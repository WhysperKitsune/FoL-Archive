Simple Role Flag

Feedback/Suggestions
Alice June 30, 2018, 7:36pm #1

To put it short and simple, the influx of new players due to the Steam summer sale is basically causing more toxicity to pile up as they play badly, which shouldn’t come to be a surprise, but this problem is stupidly magnified whenever they end up getting a class that happens to be a cornerstone of their faction. So I propose a Simple Role system in order to mitigate this problem and prevent new players from getting too demanding roles before they even know how to even play the game, which in turn would stop them from being flamed over their poor plays strongly impacting the game thanks to the importance of their classes.

The Simple Role flag should be a toggle-able option in the account settings tab and when checked it will prevent the player from getting the King, the Prince, and the Mastermind as roles provided that at least one other player is not under this flag and they do not already have one of the SR classes as their own. In addition, players will be automatically flagged under this system if they have yet to play an N number of games, have left many games recently, or have several reports against them.

Essentially, this flag would prevent new or toxic players, or allow those who want to play a game casually, to roll key classes in the game and severely hinder their faction by either ignorance, malice, or just because they wanted a relaxing game rather than being expected to carry as Prince. This in turn should both lower the toxicity and increase the quality of ToL matches.
