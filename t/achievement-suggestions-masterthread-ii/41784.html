Let’s bring back the achievement master thread with current stuff!

  1. It can range from easy to very difficult, but has to be realistically achievable.
  2. It CANNOT encourage people to gamethrow to achieve it.
  3. SFW for all ages.
  4. Relative to the game’s theme (medieval/fantasy) and/or lore.
  5. Think up achievements that would encourage you to play your class role correctly.
