General (and probably redundant and mostly for invest classes anyway) BD strategy guide

NuclearBurrito November 1, 2017, 6:33pm #1

As a BD you have 2 thing’s you need to do.

  1. Protect the prince

  2. Find the MM/CL

The details depend on if it is unseen or cult.

Protecting the Prince

To protect the prince you need to find him before the evils do and create a chain of protectives to keep him alive. That much is obvious but there are something’s to keep in mind.

  1. Unseen and Cult are doing the same thing and they will convert to get invest classes to speed things up. If you are an invest then don’t out yourself in public early on even if you found an evil. Executing an assassin won’t slow them down and you will be converted.

  2. Do not let possable Prince’s to get on the stand. If someone is killer/offensive then you wisper. Occupy Immune? Wisper! Not talking? Wisper! If they don’t respond then you can out them and they will already be prepared to talk.

  3. At the start finding Ally’s is more important than finding enemies (other than NK). You need to coordinate your Ability’s with others as soon as you can.

Finding enemy leaders

Unseen: Finding the Mastermind at the start of the game is nearly impossible. The only ways to do it is either an Alch outing his Immunity or a Butler doing it. IF THE MASTERMIND IS FOUND BEFORE D4 DO NOT KILL HIM. Instead have the Prince jail him until D4 and THEN you execute. Some things to keep in mind when finding MM

  1. The Mastermind will be the one initiating busses. If someone seems to be getting very lucky as sheriff or finds an assassin early on as princess then try to check them after N4.

  2. Unlike the other members the unseen will fight tooth and nail to keep thier MM alive. If anyone is only clear because of something an unseen member said no matter how much sense it makes you should check them.

Cult: The cult can be tricky but it can also be a bit simpler. I unlike with the unseen the cult leader can be found immediately and occupy immunity is not suspicious. If you find someone as cult you should get there claim. If it’s a seeker/invoker claim then execute them. A Ritualist’s claim will be similar to that of the CL and will thus be lumped together. Once found you should throw the CL in jail for the rest of the game until the rest of the cult is found. Things to keep in mind:

  1. If you try to repeatedly jail a cult member other than the cult leader then they will be sacrificed. This is a good thing because it means you spend less time jailing the wrong member.

  2. If someone was Occupy immune within 4 nights of a Ritualist existing then they might be cult.

  3. The observer is the best invest class for dealing with cult. Save your peaks for when you think you have found a cult leader. Once you found him you should out him for the Prince.
