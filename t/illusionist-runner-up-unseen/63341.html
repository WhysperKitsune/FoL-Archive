The Illusionist

Unseen Social

Passives:

Psychic - Immune to Occupation and Target Changing.

Day Abilities:

Haunted Gossip - All whispers are hidden.(infinite)

Telepathy - Telepathically send a message to everyone; they will not know who sent it.

Night Abilities:

Call of the Dead - Use a dead player’s ability if they were bd (you can still use it if they were a possessor jump), if their were multiple night abilities you can choose, you cannot use prince king or killer abilities .(3 uses)

Mindwarp - Give a player incorrect feedback on their abilities.(infinite)

Changes

  • Added Call of the Dead
  • Changed Mental Blur to a day ability and changed effects
  • Added Two Passives(Psychic, Haunted Gossip)
