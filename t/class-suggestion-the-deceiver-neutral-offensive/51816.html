Shurian May 7, 2018, 6:53am #1

Deceiver

Neutral Offensive
Win Condition: Survive to see 3 Blue Dragon to be executed during the day.
Accurate: (Passive) You are Immune to target changing.
Determination (Day): Become Occupy and Death Immune tonight. (2 uses)
Mislead (Night): Force a player to target someone. Cannot be used to target yourself or themselves.(Unlimited uses)
Frame (Night) Frame a player. He will appear suspicious to Investigative abilities tonight. (3 uses)

Conversion Immune.

LORE:
A Sheriff who once solved cases for the Blue Dragon. His wife was hanged for treason, even when there were no procuring evidence against her. In the light of her being wrongly accused, he decided that the Blue Dragon were no longer worthy of his services, seeking revenge against the Blue Dragon. They must suffer in the same way his wife died, he must have his revenge.

CLARIFICATION: Once 3 Blue Dragons have been executed during the day, this class wins even if he dies the day after. I may tone down the Win-Con to 4 if 3 is too easy.
Any thoughts on this? Was thinking of a neutral offensive. Hopefully this tilts in favour
