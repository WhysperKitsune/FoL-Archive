The Chamberlain (Blue Dragon Killer)

Passive: Royal Support - As a Senior Officer of the Royal House, you can step up as King.

Night Ability 1: Undisturbed (3 uses)
You will stay in your room tonight and attack anybody that might disrupt you.
Notes: Anybody using an ability that causes an effect, this includes: Occupying, Attacking, Converting. Also does bypass Immunity.

Day Ability 1: Doctrine (1 use)
You will send a Public Announcement to everyone as The Chamberlain.

Day Ability 2: Smithy (1 use)
Your next usage of Undisturbed will be stronger and will kill every visitor.

The Exiled (Unseen Offensive)

Passive: Former Royalty - With a background as a Senior Officer, you can step up as King.

Night Ability 1: Disturbed (2 uses)
You will disturb your target and falsify your targets actions and results tonight.

Night Ability 2: Banished (2 uses)
You will hide the Class of the Assassin’s next kill.
