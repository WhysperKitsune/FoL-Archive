Some Changes to Classes

Class/Lore/Fanfic
CheesyKnives May 31, 2018, 5:55pm #1

Mastermind:

Change Foresight to:

Foresight (Night) - Target a player to see who visits them and who they visited at night. 4 uses.

Even though Observer is a hard class to prove , it can’t be easily faked. Giving an evil the ability to watch visits will make people doubt Observer claims and give MM’s a better option to fake. You can still fake princess , but you’ll have to rely on people to actually out their class/claim.

EDIT - I no longer stand by this change 100%. ^

Hunter:

Change Wolf Companion to:

Wolf Companion (Day) - Summon a wolf to attack someone. They will bleed and die tonight unless healed. Unusable until Day 3. 2 uses.

Hunter is a unique class that can easily prove himself with his night ability. Making the circumstances more akin to a Cult Leader’s bleed would help in case there was no Hunter.

Assassin:

Change Distract to:

Distract (Day) - Target player will be occupied tonight. Infinite Uses.

Right now, if you are occupied in an Unseen game. It’s probably by a Blue Dragon member. Most converts become an Assassin/Mastermind after a while, so you can mostly trust someone who claims Butler/Drunk. While this wont solve Drunk claims, it can throw shade on Bulter claims and hinder the Blue Dragon.

EDIT - I no longer stand by this change. ^

Drunk:

Change Debauchery to:

Debauchery (Night) - Get in a player’s way, causing them to target you. You will heal yourself off any attacks redirected towards you, and you will be conversion immune. Infinite Uses.

I really liked the change that happened with Sheriff’s scout. It still has its initial function , but now has some ambiguity. I would like this to apply with the Drunk’s debauch too. A bonus , reapers won’t automatically be destroyed by Drunk’s if this change is added!

Herbalist:

Change type from Offensive to Social.

The Herbalist , quite strangely, has no offensive abilities, but is still considered Offensive. Changing the Herbalist’s class type to Social would fit with their abilities more, and also prevent the Mastermind slipping up on a Princess claim if they find one , and a Princess not being able to know if they were converted.
