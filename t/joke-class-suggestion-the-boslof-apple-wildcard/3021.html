The Boslof [Apple Wildcard]

Passive: Disgruntled
If you whisper The Boslof, he will silence you, unknowingly making your messages be hidden from everyone except him.

Passive: An Apple A Day
A physician who attempts to heal you will unknowingly fail to do so.

Day Ability 1: Announcement (3 uses)
Announce a message to the court anonymously. (appears as “The Apples: example message”)

Night Ability 1: Fruitify (Unlimited Uses, 1 day cooldown)
Convert someone to the apple faction.

Night Ability 2: Frame (3 uses)
Blame someone’s specs for a glitch, making them appear as an Apple to Investigators.
