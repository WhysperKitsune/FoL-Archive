EDIT:: I don’t like this idea anymore, since having to choose between Royal Finger or Decide Fate adds a high level of strategy to current King. So nevermind lol.

I love the new King without Order Execute. The differences between Good and Evil King seem to have been cleverly implemented as well. However, I am of the opinion that further changes could be made to emphasize the concept of King as a political leader. I very much like the Gladiator ability from Mafia, so I replaced Royal Finger with it and made King’s double-accuse a passive.

The King

Special

Majesty (Passive) - Your accusations of treason count as two. You are immune to death at night once.

Loyalty (Passive) - (Starting Good King only) If you are executed, nobody can be accused of treason for one day (two days, if you are executed before Day 4).

Royal Finger (Day) - Only you or the person you accuse may be accused of treason today. - 1 use

Decide Fate (Day) - Decide whether to execute or pardon the player on the stand. - 1 use

Designate Heir (Night) - Permanently grant or remove royal blood. - 1 use
