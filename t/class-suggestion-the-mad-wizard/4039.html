The Mad Wizard

Neutral Killer

Studious-Passive
You are never in your room when you need to be killed, you are always studying (Night Immunity)
NOTES:
I personally think he never grew out of his learning

Day Ability 1-Dark Chant
You will chant some dark magic, making your next target show up as their Unseen/Cult class-2 uses

Night Ability 1-Chain Lightning
You will zap somebody tonight, killing them and the person they visit.-Infinite uses

Night Ability 2-Fire Ward
You will burn somebodies room, killing them and anybody who visits them along with getting rid of their logs- 3 uses

You win when you kill everybody, except those who need to survive

Lore
After the new king’s crowning, the Court Wizard began to argue with the new king, one agruement result in the old man being thrown into the dungeon. The wizard was locked in the dungeon for 5 weeks. They got food and water every other day. The wizard was let go after the princess’s begging. After this, the wizard began to distance himself from the royals and the people and started to use his good powers for evil. A few years later, the king asked them to help him to crush some rebels in his court. Seeing this as his chance to get revenge, he gladly agreed to go to the court, and of course, execute his revenge
