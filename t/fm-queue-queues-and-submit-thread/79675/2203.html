This is a FPS game with RPG elements released in 2000 based in 2050’s near future as global pandemic is taking toll on the population and homemade terrorists groups are formed in the countries wanting to steal the cure to the disease to the lower class of citizenship while the important players of the society are supplied with the cure, but what is really going on? Who is pulling the events behind the closed doors? There’s no such thing as Illuminati…you’re complete bonkers

What are the factions in this game?

The uninformed majority AKA the town are known as United Nations Anti-Terrorist Coalition in short as UNATCO who represent as protectors of peace, the informed minority is called National Secessionist Force also known as NSF that represent the miserable, oppressed, the scum of the people that fight in the interest of the citizens that needs second chance of surviving the global pandemic in opposition against UNATCO that safeguard the cure as well, there is also another informed minority called the Illuminati and they are hiding among the faithful and loyal companions concealing their true agenda, there’s also another third party threat who is kept secret, as always in previous installments of Enemy Within, the goal is to eliminate all opposition

This plays like standard Forum Mafia however there are some mechanics that will be sorted out as Special Forum Mafia such as: All roles have rather limited action uses instead of unlimited because once you used your action which you won’t have it returned for the rest of the game however to compensate this then there is one special class from UNATCO have the ability to replenish the player for their limited uses which works on all factions so there’s good chance that they are supplying the enemy unaware, NSF as the Mafia is using Factional Kill system and they also have other tools, it’s up to the selected freedom fighters in how they combine their team effort to achieve their goals, Illuminati in other hand are in capable of converting players to their cause, what else they have in their sleeves is classified as well to the unknown threat is classified however there are hints somewhere.
