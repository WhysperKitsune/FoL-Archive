Welcome to Viva la Revolution (The reroll)!

A game mode where four factions fight against each other, though some do have the potential to win with others. While the town is large, the other three factions are only one member each.

Mechanics

• Days last 72 hours, and nights last 24 hours.
• Lynch is majority. There is no day one lynch, and day one ends when all players have confirmed.
• There are no wills/journals.
• Multiple antagonist factions are capable of winning the game, but town can not win alongside them.

Role Cards

One-Shot-Invincible Mafia Vigilante

One Shot Invincible - You survive one kill from any source (except a lynch). You will be notified when this occurs.
Shoot - Each night phase, you can shoot a player, killing them. (Bullet-Kill)
You win if you are amongst the last two living players.

One-Shot-Invincible Arsonist

One Shot Invincible - You survive one kill from any source (except a lynch). You will be notified when this occurs.
Douse - Each night phase, you can douse a player in gasoline. They are not notified.
Ignite - Instead of dousing someone, you can instead ignite all doused players, killing them. (Fire-Kill)
You win if you are amongst the last two living players.

One-Shot-Invincible Anarchist

One Shot Invincible - You survive one kill from any source (except a lynch). You will be notified when this occurs.
Plant Bomb - Each night phase, you can plant a bomb on a player. They are not notified. They will explode in two nights, killing them. (Bomb-Kill)
You win if you are amongst the last two living players.

Macho Bombproof Bomb Defuser

Bombproof - You are immune to Bomb-Kills.
Macho - You can not be targeted by the Firefighter or the Doctor.
Defuse - Each night phase, you can visit a player. That player can not have a bomb planted on them that night, and if they already had a bomb planted on them, it is defused. They are not notified.
You win when all threats to the town have been eliminated and there is at least one town player alive.

Macho Fireproof Firefighter

Fireproof - You are immune to Fire-Kills.
Macho - You can not be targeted by the Bomb Defuser or the Doctor.
Cleanup - Each night phase, you can visit a player. That player can not be doused in gasoline that night, and if they were already doused, it is removed. They are not notified.
You win when all threats to the town have been eliminated and there is at least one town player alive.

Macho Bulletproof Doctor

Bulletproof - You are immune to Bullet-Kills.
Macho - You can not be targeted by the Bomb Defuser or the Firefighter.
Heal - Each night phase, you can heal a player if they are shot. They are not notified. You can not protect against Bomb-Kills or Fire-Kills.
You win when all threats to the town have been eliminated and there is at least one town player alive.

Tracker

Track - Each night phase, you can follow a player, tracking who they visit that night, if anybody.
You win when all threats to the town have been eliminated and there is at least one town player alive.

Citizen

Your weapon is your vote. You have no night actions.
You win when all threats to the town have been eliminated and there is at least one town player alive.
