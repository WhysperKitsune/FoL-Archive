[Class suggestion] Reapers Apprentice (Neutral Invest)

Class/Lore/Fanfic
NOBLE1 March 1, 2018, 3:39pm #1

Lore: sent down by the reaper to help the Neutral killer to succeed his or her goal.

Faction-Neutral
Class Type- Investigative

Passive 1 - slowly dying - If the NK dies then he will take your place. He is not allowed to use his abilities for a day after taking your body. Also if the NK attacks you then they will be given a message that youre the reapers apprentice. The apprentice will then know who the NK is.

Day Ability 1 - soul snatcher - take a players night ability and use it tonight.

  • This will always take the unlimited ability unless they don’t have a unlimited ability.
  • If you take someone’s night ability that has unlimited then they won’t know you took one, but if there is a limit then you will be alerted that some of your soul has been taken.
  • If you take someone’s soul for the third time then you gain their passive if they have one. This will count as your second passive and you can only do this once
  • unlimited uses

Night Ability 1 - full body swap - become a class of your choice(except any Unique classes) you will become that class for the next day and night.

Notes:

  • if you become a BD member and are checked that night by an invest Ability then you will show up as BD for that night.
  • if you become a fool and get killed during the day that morning then you will win still.
  • 2 uses

Win condition- help the NK win at all cost

Tell me what you think and if this would fit in with the game
