Neutral Special

Scarecrow

Neutral Investigative

Stumble at Straws (Passive) - If a player with straw is executed, all players with straw will receive different consequences based on their class type. This passive is removed on death/objective completion. This passive cannot trigger 2 days in a row.

Investigative Results (1 Night)
  • Sheriff/Pala/Inquisitor: Your Target is a Member of the Unseen/Cult/A Heretic (Framed Results)
  • Observer

    Little Bird: Works as Normal
    Follow: Your Target was visited by no one
    Stalk: Your Target visited (whoever was killed)

  • Maid

    Change Sheets: Works as Normal
    Matchmake: Targets are incompatible

  • Princess

    Flirt: Your target is either Killer or Offensive
    Will O Wisp: Your target is a member of the Unseen/Cult

  • Offensive Classes: Day Occed (1 Day)
  • Killer Classes: They are Healed I.E Anyone who is a killer is healed (1 Night)
  • Support Classes: Night Occed, bypass immunity (1 Night)
Social Classes (1 Night/Day)

If Priest Their Night actions will Succeed despite Occ, If Mystic their actions will succeed despite day occ. Noble and Fool will be occ immune. No effect on scorned.

Special Classes (1 Night)

Alchemist will receive different punishments depending on their potion. Truth = Investigator Punishment, Tar = Offensive Punishment, Health = Support Punishment, Poison = Killer “Punishment”
All others: No effect

Man of Straw(Passive) - Immune to bleeding.

Pile of Straw (Day) - You will plant straw on a person, you will be notified if anyone visited target player. Anyone who visited will now have straw. You will not be notified who visited your target. - 2 uses.

Needle in the Hay Stack (Day) - Discover who has straw. - 5 Uses

Flock of Crows (Night) - Dispatch a flock of crows to follow a player, planting straw on them, and everyone that they visit. - Infinite

Brainless (Night) - Fill your room with straw, giving straw to anyone that visits you. You will receive feedback on abilities that were used on you. You will be death immune this night. You will not be notified of who visited you. - 2 Uses

Survive to see everyone alive have straw
