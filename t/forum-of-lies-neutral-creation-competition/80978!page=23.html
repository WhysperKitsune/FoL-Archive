Crone Notes

The Crone and the Maiden were inspired by psychoneirik‚Äôs Attorney, Accused, and Prosecutor classes, except with a few modifications that I made. To start, rather than making an unique neutral I made the accused player be a random player that can be anyone except either neuts or the Prince. The main issue with the Accused is that since they were a neut then there was pretty much no reason to bother with the entire trial shenanigas as they couldn‚Äôt convert, nor kill, nor be required to be executed for the BD wincon. So in order to give the two classes a more impactful existence, the accused will be a random player, which means they can be the MM, the NK, a random BD, or whatsoever.

The thematic inspiration behind these two characters are the lover/mark and the Maiden being star-crossed lovers, with the jealous Crone wanting to split these two apart.
