The Oracle

Neutral Offensive

Mist of the Future (Passive): You appear as a member of the Unseen or Cult (depending on which is present in the game) to investigative abilities. You are immune to occupation and redirection. You may use both your day abilities in the day.

Fortune Foretold (Passive): For each class you guess correctly, you get one use of Magic Cloak

Magic Cloak (Day): You will be death immune for the night - 1 use

Foresight (Day): Find a player’s class type. - 2 uses

Dark Prophecy (Night): Guess a player’s class at night. If you guess correctly, you will receive permanent access to their journal, a log of their actions (and their feedback), and their feedback for the night of which you used this ability. You may not target the same player twice upon a correct guess. You may wish to do one of the following towards your target upon submission of your guess - Infinite Uses

  • You may prevent them from using their night action for that night and any day actions for the following day
  • You may choose to manipulate their feedback into whatever you wish
  • You may choose to remove half of the charges of all limited abilities, rounded down

Death of Destiny (Night): Guess a player’s class. If you are correct, you will occupy and attack them. (1 use)

See the Blue Dragon defeated at any cost.

You can either go Spiteful King or the custom King I made. It doesn’t matter to me.

The Oracular King

Neutral Special

Pass the Torch (Passive) - If you die, a vote to determine the next king will be held at start of the next day.

Mystical Authority (Passive) - Immune to occupation and redirection. You are immune to death once. Your abilities will not be seen by investigative abilities. You can use both day abilities in the same day.

Grand Trial (Day) - Instantly put a player on trial for treason lasting up to 24 hours possibly extending day. They may provide a defense within 12 hours, and then the players alive may either vote to execute or pardon. If a majority of players vote to execute, the day will end and the player on trial will be lynched. Otherwise, the day will continue as normal. - 1 use

Allies (Day) - Choose up to two players. During this night, you will have a private chat with them. - Infinite Uses

Royal Scry (Night) - Determine a player’s exact class - 2 uses

Inevitable Fate (Night): Guess a player’s class at night. If you guess correctly, you will receive permanent access to their journal, a log of their actions (and their feedback), and their feedback for the night of which you used this ability. You may not target the same player twice upon a correct guess. You may wish to do one of the following towards your target. - Infinite Uses

  • You may prevent them from using their night action for that night and any day actions for the following day
  • You may choose to manipulate their feedback into whatever you wish
  • You may choose to remove half of the charges of all limited abilities, rounded down
  • You may choose to occupy and attack them (1 use only, shared with Oracle uses)

See the Blue Dragon defeated at any cost.

Feel free to make changes to this class if it’s a tad too strong or undesired. This is an anti-claim neutral.

The Phantom

Neutral Offensive

Intangible (Passive): You are immune to occupation and redirection.

Lurking Darkness (Passive): Once two thirds of the court is cursed, the shadows will engulf the court. All protective abilities will fail the following night of your victory and the day will be cut down by half. You will fade away into the darkness, leaving the game immediately.

Destiny Bond (Day): Choose a cursed player. If you are attacked, they will die in your place. - 2 uses

Channel the Darkness (Day): All cursed players will be prevented from using their abilities, but they will be healed. - 1 use

Curse (Night): Choose a player to curse. Cursed players will not be able to be healed for the rest of the game. - Infinite uses

Shadow Force (Night): Summon all cursed players. Any player they visit or they are visited by will be cursed. - 1 use

Survive to see two thirds of the court cursed, rounded up

The Phantomic King

Neutral Special

Shadow Succession (Passive): Once you die, a King election will be held. If you die due to Dark Tenacity, there will be no future King elections.

Dark Tenacity (Passive): You are immune to occupation and redirection. Once two thirds of the court is cursed, the shadows will engulf the court. All protective abilities will fail the following night of your victory and the day will be cut down by half. You will fade away into the darkness, leaving the game immediately.

Grand Trial (Day) - Instantly put a player on trial for treason lasting up to 24 hours possibly extending day. They may provide a defense within 12 hours, and then the players alive may either vote to execute or pardon. If a majority of players vote to execute, the day will end and the player on trial will be lynched. Otherwise, the day will continue as normal. - 1 use

Allies (Day) - Choose up to two players. During this night, you will have a private chat with them. - Infinite Uses

Ghostly Guards (Night) - Choose a player. They will be cursed and immune to death tonight.

Shadow Force (Night) - Summon all cursed players. Any player they visit or they are visited by will be cursed - 1 use

Survive to see two thirds of the court cursed, rounded up

Inspired by that Vampire class in HoB but I made it unique.
