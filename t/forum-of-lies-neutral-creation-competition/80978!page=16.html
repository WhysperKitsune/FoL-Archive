The Worshipper

Neutral Support

You Are my Everything (Passive) - Before the end of Night 1, you must choose a player to worship. They will become your target. If you do not, it will be randomized. If this player is ever unable to win or wins early, you will commit suicide.
Sweet Talk (Passive) - If the one you worship is not aligned with the Blue Dragon, you will survive the first attack on you.

Comparison (Day) - Choose a player and see if they can win with your target as their current class. Unusable Day 1, and unusable if your target is dead. 3 uses
Ambush (Day) - Slice a player with a dagger and bleed them. They will die in 2 nights unless healed. 1 use

Watchful Eye (Night) - Prevent all non-conversion visits to your target. If your target is dead, you may target anyone you have identified as able to win with your target. 3 uses
True Fanaticism (Night) - Kill 1 random visitor to your target that is not of their faction. 1 use

Ensure your target wins the game

This crowns into…

The Obedient King

Neutral Special

Pass the Torch (Passive) - If you die, a vote to determine the next king will be held at start of the next day.
Head of State (Passive) - Immune to occupation and redirection. Your abilities will not be seen by investigative abilities. You can use both day abilities in the same day. You will survive the first attack on you if your target is not aligned with the Blue Dragon.

Fanatical Uprising (Day) - Instantly put a player on trial for treason lasting up to 24 hours possibly extending day. They may provide a defense within 12 hours, and then the players alive may either vote to execute or pardon. If a majority of players vote to execute, the day will end and the player on trial will be lynched. Otherwise, the day will continue as normal. If this person is your target, you will revert to The Worshipper and your target will take the throne from you, and you will not put them on trial. - 1 use
Allies (Day) - Choose up to two players. During this night, you will have a private chat with them. - Infinite Uses

Guard! (Night) - Choose a player. They will be immune to death tonight. - 3 uses
Lurking Dagger (Night) - Prevent all non-conversion visits to your target tonight, and kill 1 random visitor that is not of the same faction as them. This may target someone you’ve identified as able to win with your target if your target dies. 2 uses

Ensure your target wins the game
