Class/Lore/Fanfic

The Shaman - (Neutral Killer)

Passive: Spirit Guard - You are Immune to death at night.

Passive: Voodoo - As long as you hold a Voodoo doll, any actions toward you will be directed at the earliest doll you made.
For example, if attacked, they will instead kill a player the doll was bound to. If Investigated, they will be given a result from the player the doll was bound to. Same with Occupation, etc.

Day Ability: Bind Doll - You will bind a Doll to a player today. You will be able to control what you do to them at night. - Unlimited uses, can only have 3 dolls at one time.

Night Ability: Stab Doll - Stab a doll and kill the player it was bound to. - Unlimited uses

Night Ability: Puppet Doll - You will puppet a doll and control the player to forcefully visit another player. - Unlimited uses

Goal: You must eliminate all of the Blue Dragon, Unseen, Cult and Any Neutrals that would seek to harm you.
