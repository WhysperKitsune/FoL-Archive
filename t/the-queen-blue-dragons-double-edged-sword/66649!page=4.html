There might be something interesting in a character that needs set-up time to perform a delayed action. Perhaps they can place an effect on a character that has a specific trigger, and then leave it there, the way the Mercenary’s Shield works now. A quick example:

Gem of Life (Night) - You leave a gemstone charged with healing magic on a player’s room. The gem triggers when the player is attacked, or begins the night poisoned or bleeding. If this ability is used while the gemstone is still active, it will move the existing one into another player’s room. - 3 uses

Gem of Poison (Night) - You leave a gemstone charged with poison on a player’s room. The gem triggers when the player leaves their room, poisoning them. If this ability is used while the gemstone is still active, it will move the existing one into another player’s room. - 1 use

Gem of Darkness (Night) - You leave a gemstone charged with shadows on a player’s room. The gem triggers when the player is the target of an investigation effect, preventing it. If this gem triggers, the target’s visits will be undetectable. If this ability is used while the gemstone is still active, it will move the existing one into another player’s room. - 2 uses

Gem of Shielding (Day) - You will crush a protective gemstone to prevent all visits to you. - 2 uses.

Just an example of how the mechanic could work.
