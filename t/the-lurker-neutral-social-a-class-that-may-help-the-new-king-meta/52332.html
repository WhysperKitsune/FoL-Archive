The Lurker, Neutral, Social

This class is a unique class and will become evil king if elected

Passive 1: Royal Blood: same as normal royal blood

Passive 2: Magic Barrier: immune to death once and immune to poison/bleeding

Day Ability 1: Spy: see all whispers to and from king, 2 use

Day Ability 2: Lurk: same as maid spy, unlimited uses

Night Ability: Uncover: find out if a player is an unseen/cult/BD/neut/NK if you find an unseen/cult/NK they will discover who you are.

Goal: live to see the BD and the watchful guard destroyed

Credit goes to Unknown for the Watchful Guard, I changed somethings around a bit, if you want to see his original one it is on comment 8

The Watchful Guard (name may change), Neutral, Offensive

The Watchful Guard is a unique class

Passive 1: Rusty Armour: immune to death once

Passive 2: Untouchable: immune to poison/bleeding, occupation, and target change

Day Ability 1: Looking at You: you’re target can’t look at whispers, unlimited uses

Night Ability 1: Spy Removal Service: kill somebody if they are a lurker, a noble, an aristocrat, or an apostle, goes through night immunity, 1 use

Night Ability 2: Don’t You Dare: if your target is being targeted by a lurker, sheriff/paladin, marshal, Seeker, noble, aristocrat or apostle, prevent the visit and identify them.

Goal: Live to see the lurker destroyed and prevent an unseen/cult win

feedback/suggestions would be appreciated
