[Class suggestion] The Abjurer + The Necromancer

Class/Lore/Fanfic
RopeStringFace July 24, 2017, 2:17pm #1

The abjurer
Blue dragon offensive

Night ability one: Counter spell
Infinite uses
your target will be occupied if they attempt to occupy or kill tonight. You will not be informed whether you occupied them or not. you can target the same target no more than twice.

Night ability two: Force Field
infinite uses
occupy anyone who attempts to visit your target tonight (does not bypass occupy immunity). Can be used on the same target no more than twice.

The Necromancer
Unseen (insert class type, not really sure what to put xD)
Starts with two souls

Night ability one: Bid soul
costs one soul
Spend a soul to bet on two player’s deaths; you will gain two souls when they die.

Night ability two: Resuscitate
costs one soul; can only be done to a single dead body twice
Order a dead player to do something to a live player of your choice tonight:

-Killer: If your target is immune to death, they won’t be tonight
-Offensive: Occupy the target
-Investigative: Reveal your targets class type
-social: your target cannot whisper tomorrow
-support: your target will be immune to death tonight
-wildcard/special: nothing

E.x.: you order a dead physician to target the assassin; the assassin will be immune to death tonight.

Day ability 1: Medium
costs one soul
you will be able to hear the dead tonight, and speak to them (they will think you are the priest)

Necromancer partially based on cupcakeaj2’s necromancer suggestion.

Abjurer Lore: A court wizard who focuses on abjuration spells
Necromancer lore: A sage who focuses on necromancy spells
