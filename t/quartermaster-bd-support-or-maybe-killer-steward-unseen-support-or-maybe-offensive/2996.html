The Quartermaster

Blue Dragon Something
Recognisable Craft(Passive) - You will know when someone uses an ability you gave them and who they use it on
Falsify(Day) - The equipment you give tonight will fail to perform its purpose - Infinite Uses
Give Shield(Night) - Give a shield to a player, during the day they may choose to exchange their night action for “Use Shield(Night) - Use your shield to protect a player or yourself”, if the Shield was falsified then it will not protect anyone - Infinite Uses
Give Sword(Night) - Give a sword to a player, during the day they may choose to exchange their night action for “Use Sword(Night) - Use your sword to kill a player”, if the Sword was falsified it will not protect anyone - Infinite Uses
