RopeStringFace December 30, 2017, 2:43am #1

Warlock
Neutral Killing
Passive one: Fiendish resilience
Immune to death a night.
Passive two: Seamless
You appear as the king’s faction when checked by him via allies (BD for good, unseen/cult for evil, neutral for neutral). When an observer window peaks you, they will see your faction as blue dragon.
Night ability one: Animate dead
(infinite uses, 1-day cooldown) Gain a use of Hush, and gain a charge of Hold parliament. Seamless and Fiendish Resilience will be suppressed for the night.
Night ability two: Mind Blank
(infinite uses) You will be informed of the names and classes of everyone who visited you tonight; if no one did, you will instead be informed of the names and class types of everyone who visited you the previous night. If you are poisoned or bleeding, you will heal yourself. In addition, you will be immune to occupation the following 2 nights (though for that time any occupiers will think their occupation was a success)
Day ability: Hush
(1 use) Your impenetrable gaze will silence your target for the rest of the day, and one of your skeletal minions will kidnap them at the end of the day, occupying them for the night and killing them. If they survive the attack, they will be silenced the next day as well.
Day ability: Hold parliament
(costs charges equal to the number of players including yourself still alive) The game instantly ends, with you as the sole victor (scorned may still have won, as could fool; alchemist loses; mercenary loses unless you are there target)

Goal: survive to use “Hold Parliament”
