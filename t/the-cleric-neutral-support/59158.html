Neutral, Support.

Blessing (Day) - Select a player. If they visit you tonight they will get a blessing that lasts for two days. The blessing makes the player immune to conversion, hides their visits and makes them appear not suspicious to investigators. 2 uses.

  • Your sanctuary passive only works on people that TRY to visit you. It will not work on people that are redirected into you by a court wizard or invoker. (Would be weird to make them redirect immune if they were already redirected into you‚Ä¶)
  • However, brotherhood passive does work on all visitors, whether they try to visit you or are redirected by someone else.

  • Neither blessing or curse give a notification to the player when you use the ability. This makes cleric somewhat fake claimable (unless someone gets occupied while trying to visit you).
