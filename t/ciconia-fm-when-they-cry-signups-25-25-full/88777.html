Ciconia FM: When They Cry - designed and hosted by @astand, cohosted by @KyoDaz.

Reviewer is @osieorb18, alt mod is @DatBird.

Rules:

  • Follow all Global Rules and Official Forum Game Rules.
  • Make at least 15 game-related posts each day (unless day ends early). If you fail to meet this requirement, you might get replaced. For 0 posting in full game day I will force replace you without any questions.
  • Don’t edit posts. This includes quickediting for typos, just don’t do it.
  • Alt accounts are allowed. Tell me your identity privately and send an application to @DatBird if you wish to play on an alt. You are allowed to speculate on identities of alt accounts.

General information:

  • Closed setup.
  • The game will contain bastard and/or experimental mechanics. However, OP will never lie, your rolecard will never lie, and no flips will ever be false.
  • Days are 48 hours, nights are 24 hours.
  • Eliminations are majority + plurality. In case of a tie, elimination will be randed.
  • At EoD, votes made up to :59 mark will be accepted (but not at :00 mark).
  • It will not be announced if the game reaches MYLO/LYLO.
  • Order of the Public Bath is uninformed majority and Ninth Prime Chivalric Order is informed minority.
  • Order of the Public Bath’s win condition is: Eliminate all members of the Ninth Prime Chivalric Order and all other possible threats to Order of the Public Bath.
  • Ninth Prime Chivalric Order’s win condition is: Achieve parity with the Order of the Public Bath and eliminate all other possible threats to Ninth Prime Chivalric Order.
  • Other win conditions may or may not exist.

Game mechanics:

  • There are no conversions.
  • There may or may not be lost wolves.
  • Flavor/role claims are allowed at your own risk.
  • Flavor (character name, in-universe faction and kette) isn’t alignment indicative, but might be role indicative for some roles while being misleading or not role indicative for others.
  • Ninth Prime Chivalric Order’s factional kill is assigned. Performing it doesn’t prevent using other night actions.
  • You can’t perform actions on N0 unless specified otherwise.
  • Deadline for submitting actions is one hour before the end of the phase (whether day or night).
  • If you have several different night (day) abilities, then you can use all of them during a single night (day), unless specified otherwise.
