Normal Bet (Day)- A target of your choice will be occupied tonight. Can be used multiple times per day. - Unlimited uses, costs 1 token to use.
Risky Bet (Day)- Kill a player in plain daylight. You won’t get any tokens for the kill. - Unlimited uses, costs 4 tokens to use.
Your goal is to defeat both main factions and any neutrals that may stand in your way.
