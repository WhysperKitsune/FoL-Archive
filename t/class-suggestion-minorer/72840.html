Minorer

Blue Dragon Special

Silver Minor (Passive) - Immune to occupation and redirection.

Blue Dragon Loyal (Passive) - If you was attemped to succeed convert, you will kill yourself and make the convert ability go in cooldown.

Warp Minor (Day) - Target a player, if they got targeted by a Day Ability, swap it to second player of your choice. Not effect self-target ability. 4 Use.

Teleportation Minor (Day) - Guarantee to visit your target tonight. 2 Use. Can use D1

Water Minor (Night) - Heal a player or yourself but cant cure any poison/bleed except targetting yourself. Unlimited Use. 1 Night Cooldown

Golden Minor (Night) - Target a player, make them target themselves tonight. Anyone who visited your target will also get targeted to self. Bypass redirect immunity. 2 Use. Cannot target king.

Win Condition:
You must defeat the Unseen and the Cult or any Neutral that tend to harm you.
