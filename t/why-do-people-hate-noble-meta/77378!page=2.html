Effectiveness and fun are not directly correlated. Although they do share common variables.

Meta’s form because they are effective. Many meta’s are unfun such as the whisper claim meta’s and now noble meta.

Others are fine, such as log formatting and the optimal reaction to an outed prince.

And others still are debated to this day such as VFR.

Generally the unfun meta’s are the ones that disallow players to make unique plays on either side. But since restricting what moves that scum can do tends to be a good idea Meta’s that go against that form. Not all meta’s are like that and so they aren’t all bad, but the good one’s don’t get talked about because no one complains about them.

This is all highly subjective, but just understand that while effectiveness CAN be unfun doesn’t mean it’s NECESSARILY unfun. But when it does people notice and it sucks.
