Rules

Follow the https://forum.imperium42.com/t/global-forum-discord-game-meta-rules-please-read-first/244, and the https://forum.imperium42.com/t/official-forum-mafia-and-forum-of-lies-rules/78926. Please make sure you have enough time for this game before you sign up.

Gameplay

  • Days are 48 hours long or until an execution ends it, Nights last 24 hours. You can execute a player during Day 1 and it will last 48 hours as usual.
  • You will be provided a DM with your classcard by the host, send your actions there.
  • Blue Dragon / Thieves Guild will talk through a special Discord server or forum message.
  • The Thieves Guild has a factional kill that can be used each night, it can be performed by one of the members of the Thieves Guild for an exchange for their usual action.
  • The Medium and Priest will speak with the dead through a separate Discord server or forum message.
  • There is no Neutrals Killers, they are only exclusive Neutral classes.

New Mechanic

  • The classes such as Smugler and Merchant can give other players certain items.
  • They are always 1-shot unless stated otherwise.
  • They can be used at any time in the game.
  • The effects on items don’t overlap with each other.
  • They don’t count as action thus you can use an item along with your day/night actions.

Executions

  • To accuse someone say “/vote [player]”. Please make the life of the hosts easier by bolding it (by putting ** ** around it) and pinging the host (@Twil1ight) everytime you vote/unvote.
  • Should the votes reach the majority, that player will be executed and the day will end.
  • When the day ends, the player with the most votes will be executed.
  • Voting “/no execution” is an option.
  • You may self vote now.

Picking a New Chief

  • If there are less than 4 players a new Chief will not be selected.
  • When the Chief a 12 hour period starts. In this, players may vote in their class PM for who they want to become the next Chief.
  • Whoever receives the most votes in this period will become next Chief . If votes are tied, a Chief election will be held the next day to determine it.

Action Results

You find all possible night feedback here: https://forum.imperium42.com/t/forum-of-lies-hosting-feedback-guide/75082

Action order

Night abilities will be processed in following order/priority:

  1. Visit Prevention
  2. Occupation
  3. Targetted redirection
  4. Non targetted redirection (like Tavernkeeper)
  5. Killing
  6. Protection
  7. Miscellaneous Effects (like Bounty)
  8. Investigation 1 (Faction checks, type checks, etc)
  9. Conversion
  10. Investigation 2 (Every kind of investigation which checks for abilities used this night)

In case there are conflicts on the same level (example: two Drunks try to redirect the same player), all these conflicting effects are canceled.

Journals

DM the host what you want on your journal. When you die, the last update to your journal will be revealed to everyone. It has a character limit of 1000 characters.
