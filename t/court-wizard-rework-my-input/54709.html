Mr-EMan March 26, 2018, 11:59pm #1

Here are some of my ideas on how to make the Court Wizard a more powerful ally for the Blue Dragon. I didn’t change anything for the Sage as I believe him to be in a better spot since Tornado is much stronger with the additional information from Mastermind and Assassin along with the added chaos.

The Court Wizard abilities below are similar to that of the Psychic’s and the Knight’s without killing or talking to anyone. This why it isn’t Social or Offensive, but instead purely Support for the Blue Dragon!

Court Wizard – Blue Dragon Support
Day:

Astute Observation - 2 charges
Outputs an anonymous message to everyone stating the number of evil faction members currently in the game. Outputted number does not include Neutral Killer.

//Ability is similar to the Mystic’s Telepathy ability. This allows the Court Wizard to prove himself if questioned as well as to provide the BD team useful information as to how many they are up against. Ideally used in end game situations with 7 or less players remaining. Can also be used in the early game to discover if the evil faction was eliminated early.

Night:

Ice Cloak – 1 charge
Applies a protective barrier around another player giving them an immunity to death once for up to 2 nights. Alerts the player given the barrier if they were attacked.

//Playing on the Court Wizard’s lore of having “Ancient knowledge of fire and ice” and “the last of my magic to summon forth a runed spell of protection”, the CW can protect high valued targets that may need additional protection. Cannot be used on themselves or the King. The alert message is, “Your Ice Cloak has broken, you are no longer protected!”.

Magical Barrier – Infinite Charges
Protect a player from all negative effects and conversion. Does not prevent kills.

//Nothing changes.

The Sage – Unseen Support
Night:

Wall of Fire - 2 charges
Summon a wall of fire at a player’s door, preventing everyone but the Assassin from visiting them.

//Nothing changes.

Tornado – Infinite charges
Swap two players’ positions, causing all players targeting one of them to target the other.

//Nothing changes. I believe only the Sage should have the Tornado ability as it sows chaos and only aids the Unseen. Heals can be prevented and investigations can be ruined. It also helps that the Sage knows who the Mastermind and Assassin will be targeting for maximum effect.
