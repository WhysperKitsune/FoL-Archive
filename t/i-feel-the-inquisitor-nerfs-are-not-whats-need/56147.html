I feel the Inquisitor nerfs are not what's need

Feedback/Suggestions
RawkHawk April 4, 2018, 1:20am #1

So when I originally saw the Inquisitor nerfs, I had a feeling they weren’t really right. I’m fairly certain he was nerfed since I believe he undoubtedly had the highest win rate. In the newest patch I am sure his win rate has gone down, but he is completely unfun to play as.

Right now, inquisitor has two abilities. His main ability to check if someone is magic, or his second ability to instantly kill someone. To counteract this, the sorc was given magic dust which allows her to make everyone look like a magic user, and it was buffed to make her seem not magic. The constant buffs to magic dust have made finding a magic user almost impossible. Your main ability is useless, and you have no way of knowing unless they die.

Before, you could use ruthless efficiency to have a confirmed check at the risk of killing bd, or if you checked someone as a magic user and they came up not magic, you were fine. But now both of these methods are gone as ruthless efficiency always kills and find magic gives false positives AND false negatives. You can’t rely on your own abilities anymore.

I would really like to see magic dust nerfed in some way, buff the sorc differently, but change inquisitor’s win condition. Right now it consists of sit back and hope bd find the sorc, since everything he can do is now useless.
