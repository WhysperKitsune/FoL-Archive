Reworked!

The Volunteer (Blue Dragon Killer)

Passive: Training - If you are recruited by a Sheriff, you will gain Convert Immunity as a Knight.

Passive: Shameful conduct - If you kill a member of the Blue Dragon, you will be stripped of your weapons and be unable to kill.

Night Ability 1: Fealty (infinite uses)
Attack somebody tonight, killing them. If they are Immune to Death, they will be told who you are but attacking again directly after will kill them.

Night Ability 2: Defensive Position (2 uses)
Stand guard in your room tonight, you will gain immunity to everything tonight but you are still visited. You won’t be told of any results though.
Notes: Death immune, Occupy immune, Conversion immune, etc

#The Untrained (Unseen Offensive)

Night Ability 1: Accident prone (Infinite)
Falsely attack somebody. The person you target will be told they were attacked but protected.

Night Ability 2: Deceive (Infinite)
You will occupy your target tonight but they will be told “Something prevented you from visiting your target tonight”

Day Ability 1: Daybloom (3 uses)
Expose your target to a harmless plant. The target will think they were Poisoned by Nightshade.
