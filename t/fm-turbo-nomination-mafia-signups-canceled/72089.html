###Rules

https://wiki.mafiascum.net/index.php?title=Nomination_Mafia

  • Nightless
  • On odd-numbered Days, normal lynching mechanics apply.
  • On even-numbered Days, a nomination mechanic applies to the lynch. At the start of the Day, the mafia secretly select three players of their choice (which can be of any alignment, Town or Mafia) to be available to be lynched. The mod announces who the nominated players are for this day, and then everyone (including the three nominated) can place votes only on those three players until one is lynched.
  • Town must lynch every day; as such, a plurality lynch rule applies at deadline if the town cannot decide.
  • Mafia have daytalk.

###Gameplay
Days will be 15 minutes long, or shorter if someone dies
