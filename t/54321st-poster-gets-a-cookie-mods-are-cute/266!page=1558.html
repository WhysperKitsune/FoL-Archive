Arete December 23, 2019, 10:07pm #31275

The ACT and the SAT are two tests used for college/university admissions in the United States. The SAT currently has a reading portion and a math portion, both scored out of 800, which are then added to get a score out of 1600. The ACT has a reading portion, an English portion (grammar), a math portion, and a science portion (reading but for science), each of which is scored out of 36, and all of which are averaged to get a composite score also out of 36. Both tests have an optional essay portion that some but not all schools require you submit.
