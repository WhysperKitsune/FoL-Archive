The Devourer (Neutral Killing)

This NK is special, because it has two forms. It starts the game in its “sleeping” form and may evolve into its “awakened” form. The devourer awakens after successfully killing 4 players at night. Transformation occurs the moment the Devourer achieves this goal (at the end of a night).

SLEEPING FORM:

PASSIVE (Eternal Presence): Immune for the first three nights. If The Devourer would die the moment he transforms, he will not die, even if it’s past night 3.

DAY (Premonition): Choose a player during the day. If that player dies during the following night (for whatever reason OTHER than you killing them), you will receive an additional kill for your awakening condition. Infinite use.

NIGHT (Demonic Impale): Attack a player. If you successfully killed the player, also occupy them. This is a normal attack. Heals and knight guards stop it. Infinite use.

AWAKENED FORM:

PASSIVE (Unyielding): Immune to occupation and target changing. Drunks debauching will kill the drunk.
PASSIVE (The Awakened One): Immune to death except execution during the day. Does not die to Prince’s night ability. Does not die to Order of Execution. Does not die to any other ability. Only killable during the day through voting. (Therefore instantly wins any 1v1 stalemate, should it occur)

DAY (Devour Hope): This ability, once used during trial, instantly executes the person on trial. This acts exactly like the king’s ability to choose fate, except it can only execute. Upon use, the court will see the message “The King or The Devourer has decided to execute [number].”. Single use.

DAY (Overwhelming Presence): This ability, once used BEFORE you are put on trial, negates all votes for the day, ultimately wasting the day. This acts exactly as The Fool’s passive. Upon use, the court will see the message “Tremendous dread fills the court. The immense fear prevents voting.”. Single use.

NIGHT (Isolate and Devour): Attack a player. This ability kills your target, no matter what. Pierces immunities, hits through prince’s jail and through healing. Infinite uses.

NIGHT (Adapt and Overcome): For the night, all players that visit your target will be killed. This ability kills affected players no matter what. Pierces immunities and hits through healing. May target yourself. Two uses.
