The Remnant (Neutral Wildcard)

Passive Ability: Immune to Occupy

Day Ability 1: Expose (1 use)
Your target’s Class will be revealed to the entire court

Night Ability 1: Tamper

[details=If your Target is an Investigative - ]Results will be wrong
An example would be Observer seeing the wrong people visit[/details]

[details=If your Target is a Social -]
They can’t speak during the day
They will be notified at the beginning of the day[/details]

[details=If your Target is a Killer - ]
Prince will be forced to execute

Paladin, Assassin, Knight and Reaper will go to somebody at random (Not including you or themselves)

Possessor - They will go on as normal but if you were their target, you will survive[/details]

[details=If your Target is Offensive - ]Butler/Servant - (If able to) Forced to Poison King’s Wine OR Will Serve to somebody at random

Enforcer - Will be forced to protect you

Herbalist - Will be forced to target somebody at random with Drug

Invoker - Will be forced to target somebody at random with Chains

Sage - Will be forced to target somebody at random with Shock[/details]

[details=If your Target is Support - ]Alchemist - Will be forced to heal you

Court Wizard - Will be forced to target somebody at random with Ice Ward

Mercenary, Sellsword - Will kill a player at random

Physician - Will be forced to heal you

Ritualist, Soulcatcher, Priest - Will be occupied[/details]

[details=If your Target is Special/Wildcard - ]Fool - Will be forced to target somebody at random with Fireworks

Cult Leader/Mastermind - Will be forced to target a player who is Convertable[/details]

(Maybe?)
The King - During the day, if the King votes Execute or Pardon, it will instantly be treated as if he used Decide Fate

Goal
Live to see Blue Dragon and The Unseen be defeated (Win with other Neutrals)

Lore

Long ago, there was a Young Prince who excelled at his job at bringing justice to The Unseen. Late one night, he was paid a visit by The Unseen Mastermind. After hours of back and forth battling, the Mastermind managed to get into his mind. The Young Prince succumbed to the Mastermind and gave him his loyalty.

This was the only record of a Prince ever being converted over to The Unseen. Huge precautions were taken after that. After years of service to The Unseen, he was soon promoted to Mastermind. However, his ties with The Blue Dragon made him very risky to keep around. Many of The Unseen were jealous that a young man such as the former Prince were given such a high ranking.

During one of the nights, the Young Mastermind paid a visit to the Blue Dragon Sheriff to attempt a conversion, but he saw through the Young Mastermind and saw his true self. He saw the man he was before, the Young Prince. The Sheriff took his sword and attempted to cut him down but the Young Mastermind escaped, barely. He retreated back to The Unseen hideout to report his failure. He was banished.

A former Prince. A former Mastermind. The Remnant was said to have been one of the only Princes and Masterminds who had lost their way. A failed Blue Dragon and a failed Unseen. All he could think of now was to see them all burn.
