  9. The Mastermind and the Masterminds Spy will talk through a special DM at night.
  10. Lynching is forced, if no one is voted then a player will be killed at random, lynching is majority based.

  • This setups uses a Daystart.
  • Each player are able to vote.
  • You can no action.
  • If the Mastermind and the Masterminds Spy are in a 2v2 situation, the Mastermind and the Masterminds Spy will win.
  • This game is flipless.
  • The game ends when the Mafia faction have majority or can not be stopped from getting majority.
