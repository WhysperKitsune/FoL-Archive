Cuatrolan February 19, 2018, 7:01pm #1

Total Madness is a gamemode idea made by me.

It works like this:

20 players needed to start the game.
There is no Fool or Scorned.

Both Cult And Unseen Spawns, If Cult Leader or Mastermind tries to convert the other faction members, it will fail, if both factions tries to convert the same Blue Dragon/Mercenary in the same night, it will also fail.

Layout:

-----------------------------The King

60% Chance to be Blue Dragon
20% Chance to be Evil
19% Chance to be Neutral
1% Chance to be Psycho
If the King is Psycho, there will be only 1 other Neutral Killer (See below)

-----------------------------Blue Dragon

2 Princes (They know who is getting targeted by the other Prince, they know each other at start and they cant jail the same person, they also cant target the other prince

8 Blue dragon members (1 confirmed Sheriff with a 2nd Sheriff chance and 1 confirmed Paladin with 2nd Paladin chance)

If there is a Psycho King, there is 9 Blue Dragon members instead of 8

Blue Dragon can have 3 of the same class if not unique

-----------------------------Evil And Neutrals

1 Mastermind
1 Assassin
1 Cult Leader
1 Random cult member
2 Neutral Killers
3 Neutrals, If there is a Sorc, Inquisitor is in the game, if not, there is Mercenaries and Alchemists.

Whats the point of Total Madness gamemode?

Total Madness will be a super fun mode to play. Nobody will know whats happening the first days. Its a challenge to all veteran players and a new way to play Throne of Lies!

FAQ

Unseen and cult has to defeat each other?
Yes.

Neutral Killers can side to erradicate all 3 factions?
Yes.

Why no Fool or Scorned?
Trollboxes in a 20 player game is too OP.

Can Paladin find a Unseen member and Sheriff Cult members?
Yes. In this gamemode, both investigators can get evil members.

Smite kills the Mastermind?
No, but the Paladin gets a notification saying Mastermind tried to convert. Mastermind will fail the conversion. Paladin does not know who is the Mastermind.

Hope you like the Idea! if you like it, I would love to see your feedback. Also if you have a suggestion please tell me so!

ChangeLog

Changed the 3 of the same a bit
Added second prince
Fixed the empty slot if there is a Psycho King
