Lore Subject: The Crown of the Cult

Lore: True to his subjects and loyal to a God. The King as cultist must Shepard his wayward flock into the loving embrace of Mithras. He is not evil nor malicious but disappointed in the path his children and court take. With the sage wisdom of his beloved Cult Leader he sets about amending the ways of his sceptical subjects. After all a King must look out for his people and the only fitting future for his kingdom is to join with Mithras lest a fate far worse befall them all. The disappointment the King endures does not mean he is without mercy however. His subjects are given every opportunity too join his righteous path. Only in their refusal is their life forfeit and they will serve either as blood or as body.
