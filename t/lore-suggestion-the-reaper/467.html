Comments/Explanations:
The idea behind these tales is to cement that the reapers are feared throughout whatever world this takes place in. The Reapers in my view should be an outside force feared by all the factions. I believe the Blue dragons would be oblivious mostly to them thanks to their loose ties with Corax. The Black rose would wish to avoid them and would have the most information on them and the Cultists would try to appease them.

This entry is to display the relationships that all 3 factions share with the reapers and gives them a nice history as to why perhaps a reaper may show up at the gathering e.g Story two shows they can take human form if needed without giving the lore characters too much information.
