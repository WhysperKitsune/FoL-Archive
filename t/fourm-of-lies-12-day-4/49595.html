Forum of lies 12 hosted by @FadeBlade

Rules

  1. Follow the Global Forum Rules
  2. If you haven’t posted content in 36 hours, you’ll be prodded for inactivity. If you don’t respond to that prod in 16 hours, you will be force replaced.
  3. No private messaging others in the game about the game.
  4. Do not screenshot private host messages
  5. Don’t throw the game. This includes any form of intent to make you or your faction lose
  6. Refrain from posting so many times in a row.
  7. Players are not allowed to use big letters. These are reserved for the host.
  8. Please add links in the main post to every notable event (FoL Events)
  9. You are allowed to leave the game but doing it will net you a temporary blacklist meaning you can’t join a forum of lies for a while. Further leaving may net with a permanent blacklist
  10. Lets all have fun and play together!

Gameplay

  1. Days last 96 hours, or until majority is reached for execution. Nights last 48 hours.
  2. All actions, must be sent to the mod.
  3. Unseen/Cult will talk through a special Discord server.
  4. Priest will speak with the dead through a separate Discord server.
  5. All classes will be used, and will be used as they are found in the Class Card Thread..

Executions

  1. How voting is recorded is decided upon the host and may change at the choice of the host in charge of the game
  2. To accuse someone say “/vote [player]” it must be made bold and in a post of its own as to make finding the votes a easy task for the game host.
  3. Should the votes reach the majority That player will go on trial
  4. The day timer does not stop when the target is on trial.
  5. If the day ends while the accused is on trial, the trial will not end, but the day will end as soon as the trial finished.
  6. When they are put on trial they have 12 hours to post a defence
  7. When they have posted a defense or the 12 hours have expired the players must vote Guilty or Not Guilty on the thread
  8. The trial ends when majority is reached or 24 hours has expired

Picking a new king

  1. If there are less than 4 players a new king will not be selected.
  2. After a king’s execution the day does not end, an election shall be held right after.
  3. When the king dies 12 hours is given for any player with royal blood to step up and take the throne. After that 12 hours anyone is free to step up.
  4. If only 1 person steps up when the day ends that person becomes the king
  5. If 2 people step forward everyone votes on who should be the next king. Whoever has the most votes at the end of the day becomes the king. If the votes are tied whoever stepped up 1st becomes the king

Journals

PM the host what you want on your journal and keep editing that journal as to keep the forum class clean. When you are killed your journal as it is shown when you are killed will be revived
