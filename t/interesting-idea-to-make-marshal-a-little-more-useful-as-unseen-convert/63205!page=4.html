To sum, the reasons I brought up this idea are:

1.Marshal’s currently can’t effectively accuse anyone without dying the next day, as long as BD are paying attention. This means they either suicide themselves, or remain quiet till late and are put up for “Finding Nothing”

2.IMO: Classes should be able to effectively lie about the previous class they were, from both an ease of lying perspective, and because the Unseen thematically are supposed to be good at lying. Good at lying here means backing up what they say with verifiable evidence, not suicide accusations, which is a 1 for 1 trade in the end.

  1. If you disagree with the previous point, it is silly to give an investigative class for the Unseen, a weak investigation ability, and then another investigation ability that makes the prior ability needless. Meaning Snoop is stronger than Probe and you don’t need both on the same class.
