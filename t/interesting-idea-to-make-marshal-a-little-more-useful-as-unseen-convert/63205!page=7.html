Alright I guess this is the opinion, but I find that most Unseen converts should be able to effectively claim their former class.

This is due to the fact that it is possible you will claim, to either King, Prince, or Mystic, or you will out someone using your abilities. Like a Knight CSing someone and them not dying, or a Drunk DBing someone and be attacked, or a Sheriff investigating and finding Unseen.

Specifically for the Sheriff, it is highly likely you will out yourself in the current meta by D2 as your Investigates are highly useful to the court, and can be funneled through King relatively safely even if you only find NS. Meaning you out yourself early.

Once outed you can’t change your class, and as previously stated Sheriffs are incredibly likely to claim and claim early and willfully because even NS info is useful to the court.
I’ll mention the only successful BD class claim change I’ve seen be successful is the Hunter/Prince gambit, which is silly but might work.

Now a Marshal has 2 paths before them, suicide accusation, or say nothing and be put up for uselessness by roughly D5. Neither of those are very appealing, and the only thing the Marshal offers Unseen is a 1-1 trade, and class discovery

This is relatively weak compared to say Frenzy, Snare Trap, Dark Wisp, The Aristocrats entire package which does what the Mashal does far better and faster and manipulates votes in your favor, Immunites removal/Occupation in Alchoholics, Falsified Healing/Dead Changing in Phys, Mind Warping combined with Private conversations with potential evil Neuts for Illusionist, and Occupation for the Servant.

Not to mention the fact that the above strat, suicide accusation, is not reserved solely for the Marshal, it can equally work for an Alchoholic or a Duchess. So the Marshal’s abilities are weak in general, and their strat isn’t even unique to their class. That leaves class investigation, which is only useful for finding the Prince and Hunter, any other BD will require little to no forethought for attacking, as people may be guarding Prince potentially, and everyone accept Hunter lack the ability to harm you if you attack them.

Why do you care if you discover a Phys vs a Butler or a Princess vs a Drunk. What does it matter, you have to eliminate them all anyways as Unseen what particular class you target will be superfluous to what your MM and Assassin need to do, save admittedly for Prince and Hunter.

In addition, why do you need logs if all you care about is what class the character is if they’re BD, or if they are Neut. You won’t care what kind of Neut they are, and they won’t write that in their log anyways, so Snoop is useless for that, until maybe that weird Sellsword strat comes out. And you don’t need to know what a Prince/Hunter/Phys/Butler/Princess/Sherif has done only who they are, which Probe already does.
