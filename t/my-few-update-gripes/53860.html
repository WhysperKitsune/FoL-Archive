Riley_Sinn March 23, 2018, 1:16am #1

Hello there!

I don’t mean to make my first post such a Debby… So first off I’d really like to thank the devs for continuing to support this game in the effort to make it better! That said… I really think the new update has taken a step backward, as far as my interest in the game is concerned. I truly feel that some very amazing roles have been neutered, which really degrades the experience.

I believe removing the dead interactions entirely has drastically hurt the appeal of the game to me. It has simply neutered the phys and psy, whilst making the possessor a demi-god.

I also believe the princess change has made her far too weak and less interesting. She was one of my favorite classes, and while I like trying to encourage her to flirt more - the will-o was her only protection in working with the king/prince daily.

While I like how the evil king percentage has sort of killed the whisp meta… the other changes I could do without.

Thoughts?

-Praise the Sun
