SUNMI November 21, 2017, 4:03am #1

Alright, I just made my forum account - however I made it specifically to wonder what happened with the shift of balance in the game. Personally, after the changes in the previous patch before the current one I believe the game was in a fine state. All roles, for the majority - felt impactful.

However, today the King has to be one of the most useless classes in the game for such a major role. I don’t get the reasoning for taking away the king’s investigative abilities if they’re one of the roles that players who are siding with the BD are meant to look up to and side with.

Right now, the king is at the mercy of every single role in the game and can’t figure out anything for himself unless someone has royal blood (which is a small chance). Therefore, the king will have no leads himself to guide his kingdom to victory. Instead, the king must bow down to the whispers game and use his gut to figure out who’s lying, which makes no real sense at all to me. How does the king know who he can trust and listen to if he can’t even identify who’s who? This change seems very messy and disrupts the games balance because the king relies too much on investigative roles than he can’t even tell are on his own side.

“Allies” is not even used to find “Allies” at this point, and I think that the changes to the King NEED to be reverted.It’s too hard to tell who you can trust with all the conversions going on in the game, the introduction of new neutral classes, and different people all whispering you different things that you can’t even tell yourself are true because you have no idea how to figure that out. This is terrible for such a major role, because the King is expected to give the court leads.

TLDR: Please revert the changes to the King. He’s lost his identity and his role now is completely useless, depending too much on the player’s gut rather than their game knowledge. You have no investigative abilities as a “leader” role, and are dependent on your own intuition to trust someone else’s claim through whispers. Finding someone with Royal Blood doesn’t even matter now considering they can be converted and keep this passive.
