How to vote:

In order to vote, type **/vote [player]** in the main thread, and send a link to that post in the voting thread. There is no need to ping the host account.

You can find a link to your post in the bottom right corner by pressing the chain icon.

Or, you can click the timestamp in the top right corner of a post and grab the link there.

We understand that EoDs can get quite hectic, so we will allow a 5 minute period after the day ends to copy links into this thread.
