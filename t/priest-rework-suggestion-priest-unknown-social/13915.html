[Priest Rework Suggestion] Priest - Unknown Social

Class/Lore/Fanfic
NuclearBurrito November 14, 2017, 9:32pm #1

Priest - Unknown Social (starting Priest is always BD. Priest in not guaranteed or known to all by default)
Passive: Head - All church members know your class and cannot kill or faction check you. (Does not stop assassin/CL poison/bleed) Church members and only church members can convert you.

Passive: Pass the Touch - If you die then the oldest non-unique member becomes the new Priest. They keep their faction.

Day ability: Recruit - Invite someone to the church. If they accept then for the rest of the game they can chat with the other church members. You cannot accept the invitation as a unique class if there already is one in the church other than the Priest. (Unlimited. There are a max of 3 other members)

Night ability 1: Veto - Remove a church members Occupy immunity and Occupy them tonight (Unlimited)

Night Ability 2: In the light - Reveal your class to everyone at the start of the day. You gain an extra vote until you die.

Goal: Same as factions. Survive if neutral

Notes:
To be clear. Being recruited into the church does not change your class or ability’s. All it does is tell you who the Priest is, Who the other church members are and lets you talk to them at night.
