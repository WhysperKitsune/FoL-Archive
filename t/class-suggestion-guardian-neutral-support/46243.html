(Class Suggestion) Guardian, Neutral Support

Class/Lore/Fanfic
cameron February 18, 2018, 6:30pm #1

Neutral Support, Unique, convertable. (Converted class I haven’t wrote out yet).

The Guardian is a Neutral Support class, with the objective of surviving until the end.
Night music: The Angels Weep (Inquisitor’s music).

Passives:

Willpower - Poison/Bleed will have an extra day to kill you.
Survivor - You will be immune to death at night once, and it cannot be bypassed.

Day abilities:

Important Matter - Choose a player, you will be able to communicate with them anonymously the following night. 2 Uses.

Night abilities:

Accompany - Watch over a player, making them immune to attacks. You will be notified if you protected the target player. Infinite uses.
Lockdown - Any attackers will be prevented from visiting you. 2 uses.

Lore:

Throughout the conflicts between Blue Dragon and their enemies, the Guardian does not care for who wins, he is only there to do what he likes doing, and that is protecting people from harm.

Info:

My idea for the Guardian came to mind since Alchs always bomb people, Mercs are stuck guarding one player, and blue dragon are like butter, the assassin/cult are knives. The way to balance out the Guardian imo was to make them NOT occupy immune, give them ONE death immune night, and make them Neutral.
