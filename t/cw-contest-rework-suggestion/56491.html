The target will be confuse the next night and will not be able to choose what he do that night. It will randomly pick for him an ability and a target.
If your target is blue dragon faction member. It will block the conversion. (infinite uses)

Abilities suggestion N°3 - [The Great] Court Wizard

  1. Reverse (night ability)

Reverse the ability from the visitor of the target. It means the player who visit the selected target will do his own ability on himself.
Example: 8(assassin) kill 12(drunk). You cast Reverse on 12. The assassin will kill himself.
Example²: 8(drunk) deb 9(butler). You cast Reverse on 9. The drunk deb himself.
(3 uses)

  1. Mana armor (night ability)

Protect yourself or a player from wolves and conversion. (infinite uses)

  1. Disappear (Day ability)

You can cast this spell on someone else (3 uses). It will make the target hid for the night. (same as the fool)
