Right now the game is in a fairly good state. That being said there are still some things that need changing before the game is basically perfect.

This is in order of importance
1: Ally’s needs to be replaced. I’m sure it will take a few attempts to get right but the king should not be able to Investigate. This Ability could be replaced with a social or Support Ability in order to furfill it’s old function.

2: The Inquisitor and sorcerer need a rework. A neutral killer does not deserve a hardcounter. Period. I suggest making the sorcerer a normal Neutral Killer and making the Inquisitor a Neutral with the current Sorcs wincon and similar night Ability’s.

3: The Illusionist and the Apostle need access to dead chat. By that I mean like how the Apostle has dark words until the latest patch. The illusionist and Apostle both need that passive in order to provide engagement to dead members of that faction.
