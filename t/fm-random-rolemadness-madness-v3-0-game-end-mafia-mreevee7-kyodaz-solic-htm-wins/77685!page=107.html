Zombie

Role Description:

The Zombie infects a player each night and wins if the number of infected players ever outnumber the number of healthy players.

Notes:

This is an independent/third party-aligned role. Infecting someone does not convert said player - an infected player keeps their role and alignment. This is a much less broken version of a Cult Leader. If your setup has a Zombie, maybe consider also including a Deprogrammer-type role.
