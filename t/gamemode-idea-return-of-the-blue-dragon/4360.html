[Gamemode Idea] Return of The Blue Dragon

Class/Lore/Fanfic
Ashe October 16, 2017, 3:52am #1

The discussion in the Taskmaster thread made me think about an reverse gamemode, so here’s that.

The Unseen had won, taking over Castle Adiart. But now, the small remainder of the Blue Dragon is fighting to take it over again! The changes are are as follows: The Unseen becomes the uninformed majority, not knowing who the others are, while the Blue Dragon are now the informed minority. The number of Unseen and Blue Dragon that appear are swapped. Neutrals still spawn as normal. There are new classes, and the majority of existing classes are changed to spice things up. The Mastermind, Assassin and Sellsword will never spawn. The Blue Dragon starts with The Negotiator and The Insurgent. The faction cap for the Blue Dragon is 4. If the Negotiator dies, the newest member of the Blue Dragon other than the oldest killer will become the Negotiator, unless the only other member is the oldest killer, in which case they will become the Negotiator anyway. The Insurgent can’t be replaced.

The new classes:

The Negotiator

Blue Dragon Special
Undercover Rebel (Passive) - You will appear to be a Servant to investigators for the first three nights.
Busy (Passive) - Immune to Occupation.
Call to Arms! (Day) - Call the Blue Dragon to arms, empowering them tonight. The Blue Dragon will receive a message when this is activated. The individual effects can be found on each class card. - Infinite Uses, 2 day cooldown.
Persuade (Night) - Persuade someone to join the Blue Dragon. - Infinite uses, 1 day cooldown.

The Insurgent

Blue Dragon Killer
Vengeance (Day) - Your attack will pierce night immunity tonight. - Gains 1 use for each dead member of the Blue Dragon.
Eliminate (Night) - Kill a target player if they are a member of the Unseen. If they are a neutral, they will instead be occupied. You will know which happened. - Infinite uses.
[Call to Arms Only] Without A Trace (Night) - Kill a target player, regardless of faction. You will take their journal, making it appear as though they didn’t leave one, and you will be able to read it. - 2 uses.
Defeat the Unseen and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.

The BD converted Mercenary:

The Soldier

Blue Dragon Support
Defend (Night) Protect a Blue Dragon player, giving them visitation immunity. - 3 uses.
Payback (Night) Attack a player who voted a Blue Dragon player for treason. Call to Arms Effect: Attack any player. - 1 use.
Defeat the Unseen and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.

The converted Prince, guaranteed to spawn:

The Kidnapper

Unseen Killer
Unstoppable (Passive) - Immune to being occupied at night.
Royal Blood (Passive) - As a Royal, you can put your name forward for nominations to become the next King.
Imprison (Day) - Choose a player to imprison at night, Occupying the target (Bypasses Occupy Immunity). - Infinite uses.
Execute (Night) - Kill the imprisoned player (Bypass Night Immunity). If you execute a member of the Unseen, you lose all your remaining executions. - 3 uses.
Defeat the Blue Dragon and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.

Now, for the changed classes:

The Butler

Blue Dragon Offensive
Dutiful (Passive) - Immune to occupation.
Serve Wine (Night) - Occupy a player by getting them drunk. Call to Arms Effect: Get a player so drunk that they will be occupied again tomorrow night. - Infinite uses.
Concentrated Wine (Night) - Throw a party, occupying all players who visit you tonight. - 2 uses.
Defeat the Unseen and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.

The Servant

Unseen Offensive
Dutiful (Passive) - Immune to occupation.
Nightshade Wine (Day) - After 3 days you can poison the The King, causing him to die in two nights if not healed by a Physician. If the King is a member of the Unseen, you will take your own life. - 1 use.
Serve Wine (Night) - Occupy a player by getting them drunk. - Infinite uses.
Concentrated Wine (Night) - Throw a party, occupying all players who visit you tonight. - 2 uses.
Defeat the Blue Dragon and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.

The Court Wizard

Blue Dragon Support
Illusion (Night) - Cast an illusion in the target player’s room. They will believe they were shocked, and receive matching feedback, but will still really visit their true target. - Infinite uses.
Ice Ward (Night) - Freeze a player’s room preventing anyone from visiting them. Call to Arms Effect: All Blue Dragon members will still be able to visit the target. - 2 uses.
Defeat the Unseen and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.

The Sage

Unseen Offensive
Shock (Night) - Shock the target player, causing them to target themselves. - Infinite uses.
Wall of Fire (Night) - Summon a wall of fire at a player’s door, preventing everyone from visiting them. - 2 uses.
Defeat the Blue Dragon and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.

The Drunk

Blue Dragon Offensive
Royal Blood (Passive) - As a Royal, you can put your name forward for nominations to become the next King.
Debauchery (Night) - Get in a player’s way, causing them to target you. - Infinite uses.
[Call to Arms Only] Hangover (Night) - Make a player unable to talk or hear what others say the next day. - 2 uses.
Defeat the Unseen and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.

The Alcoholic

Unseen Offensive
Royal Blood (Passive) - As a Royal, you can put your name forward for nominations to become the next King.
Debauchery (Night) - Get in a player’s way, causing them to target you. Does not work with conversion abilities. - Infinite uses.
Happy Hour (Night) - Occupy and get rid of your target’s night and occupy immunities for the night. - 2 uses.
Defeat the Blue Dragon and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.

The Hunter

Blue Dragon Killer
Isolation (Passive) - Immune to occupation.
Wolf Companion (Night) - Summon a wolf to attack someone. They will die instantly, but if they are death immune they will instead bleed and die in one day unless healed. - Can only be used if you are the oldest Blue Dragon Killer. Infinite uses.
Spike Traps (Night) - Set spiked traps in your home to hurt all intruders. If trapped, the
visitors will bleed and die in a day, if untreated. You will be immune to death. Call to Arms Effect: Visitors die instantly, piercing death immunity. - 2 uses.
Defeat the Unseen and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.

The Poacher

Unseen Offensive
Retribution (Passive) - If found guilty of treason, you will kill your accuser before your execution.
Isolation (Passive) - Immune to occupation.
Bear Companion (Night) - Summon a bear companion to maul a target player, making them bleed and die in one day unless healed. - 2 uses.
Spike Traps (Night) - Set spiked traps in your home to hurt all intruders. If trapped, the visitors will bleed and die in a day, if untreated. You will be immune to death and conversion. - 2 uses.
Defeat the Blue Dragon and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.

The Knight

Blue Dragon Killer
Protection (Night) - Protect the Blue Dragon faction at night, giving up your life to save theirs if needed. Call to Arms Effect: Kill their attackers when you die. - Infinite uses.
Cold Steel (Night) - Kill a target player. - Can only be used if you are the oldest Blue Dragon Killer. Infinite uses.
Defeat the Unseen and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.

The Enforcer

Unseen Support
Sacrifice (Night) - Protect a player, giving your life to kill their attackers. - Infinite Uses
Frenzy (Night) - Kill a target player. Bypasses night Immunity, Occupying, and healing. - 1 use.
Defeat the Blue Dragon and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.

The Noble

Blue Dragon Social
Royal Blood (Passive) - As a Royal, you can put your name forward for nominations to become the next King.
Maid Spy (Day) - Send a maid to spy on someone during the day. You will be able to hear whispers to and from them. - Infinite uses.
[Call to Arms Only] Manipulate (Day, Special) - Choose who the person you used Order on will vote. - Infinite uses.
Crier (Night) - Send for a crier to deliver a message to the court at the start of the next day. - Infinite uses.
Order (Night) - Negate the votes of a target player the next day. They can’t accuse anyone or vote tomorrow. Call to Arms Effect: The day after using this ability, you will gain Manipulate. - Infinite uses.
Defeat the Unseen and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.

The Observer

Blue Dragon Investigative
Follow (Night) - Observe a player to see who visits them and who they visited at night. - Infinite uses.
Window Peek (Night) - Reveal a player’s faction. Call to Arms Effect: Reveal their exact class. - 2 uses
Defeat the Unseen and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.

The Nightwatch

Unseen Investigative
Follow (Night) - Observe a player to see who visits them and who they visited at night. - Infinite uses.
Window Peek (Night) - Reveal a player’s faction. - 2 uses
Defeat the Blue Dragon and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.

The Physician

Blue Dragon Support
Remedy Studies (Passive) - Immune to nightshade poison, and all forms of bleed.
Heal (Night) - Heal a player, preventing them from dying tonight, and cures poison. - Infinite uses.
[Call to Arms Only] Concentrated Nightshade (Night) - Poison the target player with a concentrated dose of nightshade, causing them to die instantly. - 1 uses.
Defeat the Unseen and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.

The Herbalist

Unseen Support
Remedy Studies (Passive) - Immune to nightshade poison, and all forms of bleed.
Heal (Night) - Heal a player, preventing them from dying tonight, and cures poison. - Infinite uses.
Exhume (Night) - Choose a dead player, Privately identify the class that killed them. - Infinite uses.
Defeat the Blue Dragon and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.

The Priest

Blue Dragon Support
Devoted (Passive) - You are Immune to one attack on your life.
Call the Dead (Passive) - Communicate with any dead Blue Dragon members at night.
Bind Soul (Day) - Makes a player Immune to death tonight. - 1 use.
Holy Barrier (Night) - A target Blue Dragon player is protected from death tonight. - 2 uses.
[Call to Arms Only] Vessel (Night) A target dead Blue Dragon player will be able to use their night ability, and it won’t be visible to the Nightwatch/Observer. - 1 use
Defeat the Unseen and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.

The Soulcatcher

Unseen Support
Devoted (Passive) - You are Immune to one attack on your life.
Call the Dead (Passive) - Communicate with the dead at night.
Bind Soul (Day) - Makes a player Immune to death tonight. - 1 use.
Soul Trap (Night) - If the target player dies at any point after using this ability, you will collect their soul. - Infinite uses.
Reanimate (Night) - Bring a dead Unseen player back to life. Each night they remain alive, a soul will be consumed. If you have no souls, or are converted, the player dies. You can target all players, but will only successfully revive Unseen members. They will be convert immune. - 1 use.
Defeat the Blue Dragon and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.

The Princess

Blue Dragon Investigative
Royal Blood (Passive) - As a Royal, you can put your name forward for nominations to become the next King.
Flirt (Night) - Check to see what class type the selected player is. You will receive two results, one of which will be their real type, and one won’t be. Call to Arms Effect: Determine the target’s class type. - Infinite uses.
Blinding Wisp (Night) - Uncage a blinding wisp in a target player’s room (you can target yourself), making anyone who visits not receive feedback, and a notification that “They couldn’t see through the blinding light.” - 3 uses.
Defeat the Unseen and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.

The Duchess

Unseen Investigative
Royal Blood (Passive) - As a Royal, you can put your name forward for nominations to become the next King.
Flirt (Night) - Check to see what class type the selected player is. You will receive two results, one of which will be their real type, and one won’t be. - Infinite uses.
Dark Wisp (Night) - Uncage a dark wisp, sucking out the light from a target player’s room (you can target yourself). Killers will not be able to kill the target player due to the darkness, but no other classes will be effected. - Infinite uses.
Defeat the Blue Dragon and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.

The Psychic

Blue Dragon Social
Telepathy (Day) - Telepathically send a message to everyone; they will not know who sent it. - 2 uses.
Misdirect (Day) - Force a player to accuse another player of Treason. - 2 uses.
Link Fates (Night) - Two target players will suffer the same negative effects tonight, except killing and conversion. Call to Arms Effect: This includes killing. - Infinite uses.
Defeat the Unseen and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.

The Illusionist

Unseen Social
Telepathy (Day) - Telepathically send a message to everyone; they will not know who sent it. - 2 uses.
Misdirect (Day) - Force a player to accuse another player of Treason. - 2 uses.
Link Destinies (Night) - Two target players will benefit from the same positive and negative effects other than killing and conversion tonight, like healing or occupation. - Infinite uses.
Defeat the Blue Dragon and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.

The Sheriff

Blue Dragon Investigative
Show Badge (Passive) - The Kidnapper will think you are the Marshal if you are jailed.
Scout (Day) - Have a scout to follow the target player. The next morning you will discover their faction. - 2 uses.
Stakeout (Night) - You will stakeout a target player’s room, finding out any visitors’ factions. Call to Arms Effect: You will find out their exact classes. - Infinite uses.
Defeat the Unseen and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.

The Marshal

Unseen Investigative
Show Badge (Passive) - The Kidnapper will know you are the Marshal if you are jailed.
Fact Checking (Day) - Determine if a previously investigated player has been converted to the Blue Dragon since checking them. - 2 uses.
Expert Investigation (Night) - Determine if a player is a member of the Blue Dragon faction. - Infinite uses.
Defeat the Blue Dragon and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.

The Prince

Blue Dragon Killer
Unstoppable (Passive) - Immune to being occupied at night.
Royal Blood (Passive) - As a Royal, you can put your name forward for nominations to become the next King.
Imprison (Day) - Choose a player to imprison at night, Occupying the target (Bypasses Occupy Immunity). If you choose to imprison, you will not be able to talk with the rest of the Blue Dragon tonight. - Infinite uses.
Interrogate (Night) - Interrogate your imprisoned player, finding out their class type. - Infinite uses.
[Call to Arms Only] Execute (Night) - Execute your imprisoned player, bypassing night immunity. - 2 uses, not limited to the oldest killer rule.
Defeat the Unseen and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.

Classes that stay the same:

  • Aristocrat
  • All neutrals

That’s all of it. Now, it’s probably not very well balanced or anything, so I’m always looking for feedback. Let me know what you think.
