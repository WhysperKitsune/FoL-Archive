Now in-game there are a few options available to you:

Option 1:

The loud game

The strategy here is to out ASAP to get everyoneâ€™s claims. If someone refuses to claim to you than wolf them. Otherwise expect to use up your bears early on. It is a good idea to step up to become king if you get the chance while doing this.

This is the defacto hunter strat

Option 2:

The clutch game

Here you want people to not realize you exist. Try and avoid bringing attention to yourself for as long as possible to avoid having to use bears. Once it starts to transition to late game you wolf who you think are sus and start bearing. If someone ever claims hunter then wolf them and counter claim them the next night so they are not healed. If you do then continue as if it were a loud game.

Option 3:

The hidden leader game

Remember that your job as hunter is to be agressive. Lead the discussion and get people talking because they are more likely to attack you if you do. And you want people attacking you instead of someone else so you can counter attack. So if you just are super active and lead discussions then you will likely be attacked. Use bear and switch to a loud game.
