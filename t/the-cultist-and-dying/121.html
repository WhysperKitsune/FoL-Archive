I am liking the idea of the cult however something has been troubling me for some time now.
I don’t think people are gonna like becoming a cult member just to die the next night. it is way more likely that they will attempt to get the cult leader killed so they will survive. you need to find a way to encourage the cult members to die for these class to work or people will just hate cult

My idea is when cult members are killed by the cult leaders power they do die but instead of being totally useless they become 1 of 5 unique classes Depending on what they were before they joined the cult and died. for example, if a butler joined the cult and was killed by the cult leader he would become a Puppeteer.
also instead of talking with the with the rest of the dead at night you talk with all the living cult members at day so you can plan what you will do with your new classes.
of course, if you were to die in a way without being killed by the cult leader you will not get a new class and will talk with the normal dead instead with the living cult but you can still hear them at night

Class 1) The Snatcher(Cult_Social)
Night Ability 1:Night Cool
[1x] Steal The Body Of Your Host Controlling Everything they do tomorrow during the day(you are unable to use their day abilities or alter their Journal)

Class 2) (Cult_Investgative)
Night Ability 1: Journal Theif
[1x] Steal your targets Journal so the other cult members are able to read it(They don’t lose it I just can’t think of a better name then the journal thief)
Night Ability 2) Unravel Clue You Get A Clue To Your Targets Role (I believe you need 1 of the Investigative to get a clue because none of them at the moment get clue’s right now)

Class 3) The Faceless (Cult_Support)
Night Ability 1) Trick The Trickster
The Cult Member you target ignores being occupied
Night ability 2) False Aura
[2-uses] [1 Night Cool Down] The Cult Member You target Appears as a blue dragon to all Blue dragon investigative results

Class 4) Blood Guardian (Cult_Defence)
Night Ability 1: Blood Shield
The Cult Member you target becomes immune to death this night
(i honestly can’t think of any other defence ability because the only defence for the blue dragon is the court wizard so the only class that can become the blood guardian is the court wizard)

Class 5) The Puppeteer (Cult_Offensive)
Night Ability 1: Pull The Strings
[2-uses] [1 Night Cool Down]
Control who your targets Target is this night(Will Fail If Your Target A killer)

If all living cult members are killed all the dead cult members who are part of these classes lose their powers and join the normal dead
doing this would make the cult members happy to die by the hand of the cult leader while still having a part in the game and would add a bit more options for the cult to do instead of convert kill, convert kill all the time
