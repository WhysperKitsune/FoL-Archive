2. I got converted. When do I change my claim/log?

First of all, if you have claimed your role to the Psychic, the Prince or the King, do not change it after being converted. This raises too much Suspicion.

If you did no claim a role before you got converted, there are a few things to consider.

1. Are you occupy immune after your conversion?
2. Were you occupy immune before your conversion?

Those 2 Questions are pretty important.

Some classes might lose their Immunity after being converted.

Occupy immune classes are: The Possessor, The King, The Mastermind, The Scorned, The Prince, The Butler, The Servant, The Drunk, The Alchoholic, The Fool, The Hunter, The Poacher, The Invoker and the Sorcerer.

If your status on occupy immunity has changed, you might want to change your claim into a class, that fits to your immunity.

3. I have become the Cult Leader. Do I change my Log to a special / investigative class?

As said before, if you have claimed your role to the Psychic, the Prince or the King, it is rarely a clever idea to change your claim. Otherwise feel free to adjust your logs.

~ Important ~

Certain classes are considered Unique, meaning a maximum of one will appear in any given game.

The Unique classes are: The Assassin, The Cult Leader, The Hunter, The King, The Mastermind, The Poacher, The Possessor, The Prince, The Reaper, The Sage, The Scorned, The Inquisitor and The Sorcerer.

If you claimed one of these classes and the original Person with this class died, you should change your claim immediately.

Also most classes cannot appear more than 2 times at the same time in a game. So for example, if you claimed Sheriff and 2 Sheriffs are dead, you should change your claim as well.

Also, if you talked alot before you got converted, keep talking alot after you got converted. Alot of players suddenly get quite after being converted to not expose anything or anyone.
