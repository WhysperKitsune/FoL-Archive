I feel like the changes make the game a bit too heavily favored for BD.

With alchs spawning more frequently and a guaranteed priest in every game, it can be very difficult to win as cult. The bleed thing helps, but there’s so many convert immune people in game now.

I mean I’m still having fun, but I feel like with only 1 NK per game, and tons of unconvertable alchs/priest/prince/etc, and a buffed Hunter (which was a good change imo) in each game, the balance is way too shifted in favor of BD.

So many cult games I’ve been in are hit a convert immune -> jailed -> paladin outs 1 -> eradicate backfires, etc.

I think it’d be really cool if the devs posted winrates for each. From my anecdotal evidence I’d say BD >> Unseen > Cult. And for neutrals, ignoring fool/scorned, I’d say Alch is the easiest to win on, followed by merc, followed by Possessor, with reaper being the hardest class in the game to win on.
