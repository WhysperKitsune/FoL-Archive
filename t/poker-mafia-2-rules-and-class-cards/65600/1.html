Rules and gameplay

What is poker mafia?

Poker mafia works like a standard mafia game, with a town faction, an evil faction and a few neutrals. However, in Poker mafia most players start without any abilities and instead must get their abilities by placing a bid on the Ability Pool.

(If you played the first Poker mafia game, you can skip to the “What’s new?” section).

Tokens

Tokens are the main currency of the game, and they are used to place bids on the Ability pool or to buy tickets for the Roulette.
All players get 5 tokens at the beginning of each day, and any unspent tokens will carry over to the next day. You can also get more tokens by winning at the Roulette, from Events or by Selling back your abilities.
Besides normal tokens, there are also 2 types of special tokens that you can only get from certain events or abilities:

  • Roulette tokens: These can only be used to buy tickets for the Roulette.
  • Special tokens: These can only be used to place a bid on the ability pool. A special token will always get the highest bid on an ability against any amount of normal tokens.

Ability Pool

Every day there will be an Ability Pool with a certain number of abilities. The ability pool works similar to an auction: every player can place any number of tokens on one or more abilities in the pool, and at the end of the day the player that placed the highest bid on each ability will get said ability. Please note that unlike most auctions, players who lose a bid will NOT get their tokens back. Also, every bid you place will be private and won’t be revealed to anyone.

The abilities on the pool will change every day, and the number of abilities in the pool will be equal to the number of living players minus 2, with a minimum of 2 abilities. In addition to those abilities, there will always be one extra Golden ability. Golden abilities are really powerful abilities that only have 1 use.

To place a bid on the pool, send a message to the host saying which ability and the number of tokens you want to place, for example: “I want to place 5 tokens on ability #4” or “/bet 3 tokens on 4”.

Roulette

During the first 24 hours of each day, the Roulette will be available. During this time players will be able to buy roulette tickets for 1 token each (you can buy as many tickets as you want).

Available tickets:

Gambling ticket: This ticket allows you to place a bet on the roulette. You can bet for a color (red or black), a number (between 1 and 3) or a combination of both.

  • Guessing the color correctly gives you 2 tokens for each ticket.
  • Guessing the number correctly gives you 3 tokens for each ticket.
  • Guessing both the color and the number correctly gives you 6 tokens for each ticket.

You can buy as many gambling tickets as you want, but you may only place one bet.

Rare tickets: Out of everyone that buys these tickets, only 1 of them will get a price. Only 1 of these will be available each day.

  • Jackpot ticket: Every token used to buy tickets from the roulette will be added to the jackpot (roulette tokens count as normal tokens for this purpose). The winner ticket will get all the money in the jackpot. Jackpot starts with 5 tokens.
  • Golden ticket: The winner ticket will get a golden ability.
  • Special ticket: The winner ticket will get a special token.

Daily Events

Every day there will be a random event. Events give players new ways to get tokens or abilities. For extra info, see the “List of events” section.

Selling back

If you don’t like one of your abilities you can sell and it will go back into the ability pool. People will be able to bid for said ability like normal, but the tokens from the highest bid will go towards the seller. Sold abilities will have keep the same amount of remaining uses. You can only sell abilities during the day.

To sell an ability send a message to the host telling him which ability you want to sell.

Using multiple abilities

There is no cap to the number of abilities a player can have at the same time, but they can only use one day ability each day and one night ability each night, however every player can pay 3 tokens to use an extra ability. You can do this multiple times but it will cost you 3 tokens each time. You can’t use the same ability multiple times per day/night.

What is new?

If you played the first poker mafia, you already know the main mechanics. However, some stuff has changed:

  • All classes are able to get abilities from the pool.
  • Godfather and all neutrals have been reworked.
  • Two more neutrals have been added: The Entertainer and the Loan Shark
  • A second NK has been added: The Elite (only 1 NK will spawn, however).
  • Townies and mafiosos will be able to choose a gift at the start of the game (see class cards for the gifts they can choose from).
  • Gambling events have been removed and replaced with the “Roulette”.
  • There are still daily events, but they no longer involve gambling (see the events section for more information).
  • All classes can use more than one day or night abilities, but they must pay 3 tokens for every extra ability besides the first one. (You can’t use the same ability multiple times).
  • Godfather no longer needs to use tokens to kill.
  • Godfather no longer buys abilities for his mafiosos, instead he can give them tokens.
  • Selling an ability back no longer gives you a fixed amount of tokens, instead you get the tokens from the highest bid.

Special rules and interactions

  • Lynching a player will set the day timer to 12 hours. Players can keep discussing and placing bids on the ability pool, but won’t be able to use day abilities during this time. Players cannot sell back abilities during this time either.
  • Lynching a player ends the roulette immediately.
  • Bids are private and will not be revealed to anyone, except for some specific abilities or events.
  • You can place or change your bets at any point during the daytime.
  • Each night, a player will get ALL the abilities in which they had the most tokens.
  • If there is a tie for the highest bid, the person with the less number of non-starting abilities will get the ability. If that is also a tie, it will be solved by rng.
  • There are 8 different ability types: Support, Offensive, Social, Investigative, Killing, Gambling, Passive and Scum. There are also certain classes with starting abilities. These abilities will appear as “Special” to investigators.
  • The abilities are given to the player at the beginning of the night, which means they can use them that same night.
  • You can have more than 2 day and night abilities. There is no limit.
  • If you use all uses on an ability, you will lose said ability. This means investigators won’t be able to see that ability and you won’t be able to sell it. This also applies to “limited” passive abilities such as one-time death immunity.
  • You will know whether you got an ability or not at the beginning of the night. If you lose a bid, you will not know who got the ability.
    *To prevent angle-shooting, every player is able to delay their actions or bids by asking the host.
    *To help with fakeclaiming and prevent angle-shooting, players can ask the host for any flavor text such as “You were occupied last night” etc.
  • Every player is able to ask the host any questions regarding mechanics or interactions, but asking for info about the game that can be considered cheating or angleshooting might result in a modkill.
Class cards

Townie
Town
Choose one of the following gifts at the start of the game:

  • Abilty gift: Start the game with a random, non-golden ability. (You can’t get scum abilities from this gift).
  • Token gift: Start the game with 3 extra tokens.
  • Golden gift: Start the game with a Golden ticket. At the start of the game, one person with a golden ticket will be selected at random and get a free Golden ability. (Golden abilities are powerful one-shot abilities).

Goal: Defeat Town and the neutral killer

Mafioso
Mafia
Choose one of the following gifts at the start of the game:

  • Abilty gift: Start the game with a random, non-golden ability.
  • Token gift: Start the game with 3 extra tokens.
  • Golden gift: Start the game with a Golden ticket. At the start of the game, one person with a golden ticket will be selected at random and get a free Golden ability. (Golden abilities are powerful one-shot abilities).

Goal: Defeat Town and the neutral killer

The Godfather
Mafia
Filthy Rich (Passive) - Immune to death at night. You get 3 extra tokens every day. You can give any amount of tokens to your mafiosos. Only the starting Godfather has this passive.
Pass the torch (Passive) - If you die, a mafioso of your choice will become the Godfather.
Kill (Night) - Attack a player. Unlimited uses.
Goal: Defeat Town and the neutral killer

The Gambler
Neutral
Lucky (Passive) - You get 3 roulette tokens every day. (Roulette tokens can only be used to buy tickets for the roulette).
Safe Bet (Night) - Become immune to death tonight. Unlimited uses, but costs 5 tokens to use. Every time you use this ability its cost increases by 3 tokens permanently.
Goal: Survive

The Loan Shark
Neutral
Bribe (Passive) - You are immune to death at night. If you are attacked you will give your attacker half of your tokens instead.
Loan (Night) - Give a player any amount of your tokens. Said player will be “In debt” until they pay you twice that amount of tokens. You cannot use this ability on a player that is already “in-debt” - Unlimited uses.
Collect (Night) - Occupy all “In debt” players.
Goal: Get a total of 30 or more tokens. If you win, you will leave the casino and all players will lose 5 tokens.

The Leprechaun
Neutral
Broke (Passive) - You start the game without tokens.
Lucky 7 (Passive) - Start the game with a random non-golden ability of each type except killer. (Support, Social, Offensive, Investigative, Passive, Gambling and Scum). These abilities cannot be sold and will only have 1 use.
Goal: Survive to see town lose

The Entertainer
Neutral
Last present (Passive) - Whoever kills you will get a random Golden ability. If you are lynched, the first person to vote you will get it.
Birthday present (Day) - Give a present to a player. They can open it or give it to someone else. Whoever opens it will get a random ability. 3 uses.
Exploding Present (Day) - Give a present to a player. They can open it or give it to someone else. Whoever opens it will die. 1 use.
Magic Trick (Night) - Swap places with another player. Anyone visiting one of you will instead visit the other. Unlimited uses.
Goal: Survive

The Cheater
Neutral Killer
Ace under the sleeve (Passive) - Start the game with 2 Special tokens.
Cheating death (Passive) - Immune to death.
All-in (Night) - Kill a player. Unlimited uses.
Goal: Defeat the Town and Mafia

The Elite
Neutral Killer
VIP (Passive) - Start the game with a Golden Killing ability of your choice. You cannot sell this ability. All of your Golden abilities have unlimited uses but a 1-day cooldown.
Untouchable (Passive) - Immune to death.
Goal: Defeat the Town and Mafia

List of events
  • Midas touch: Today’s ability pool will have 3 golden abilities instead of 1.
  • Mega pool: Today’s ability pool will have 5 extra common abilities.
  • Wildcard: Everyone will be able to publicly vote for an event. The event with the most votes will start.
  • Silver medal: The second highest bid on each ability from today’s pool will also get a copy of said ability.
  • Consolation prize: At the end of the day, players that didn’t get any abilities from the pool will get a random ability.
  • 2-for-1: For every roulette ticket you buy today, you will get a second one of the same type for free.
  • Roulette madness: All 3 rare tickets will be available today. Everyone will get 1 free rare ticket of each type.
  • You are special: Everyone will be able to vote for a player other than themselves. When the majority is reached, said player will get a special token.
  • Gift Rain: At random times during the day, a “gift” will appear. The first person to write /claim in public will get the gift! That person can choose to open the gift or give it to someone else. Each player can only claim 1 gift (whether they keep it or give it to someone else). Any abilities you get from gifts will be given to you immediately.
  • Pawn shop: Double any tokens you get from selling back an ability.
  • Black Market: Any ability you sell back today will give you 5 tokens and won’t go into the pool.
  • Gift exchange: Every player is able to select one of their abilities. The players that do will exchange said ability with a random player.
  • Pay to win: You can pay 5 tokens to instantly gain a random ability. You can do this as many times as you want.
  • Lynch Piñata: Whoever is lynched today will have an amount equal to twice their tokens split between the players that voted to lynch them. The first person that voted to lynch them will get twice as many tokens. (Any tokens spent on tickets or on the ability pool will be split as well. This event only affects normal tokens).
  • Charity: Donate any amount of tokens. An amount equal to twice the tokens donated by all players will be given to the player with least tokens.

  • Whenever you bid at least 1 token on a gambling ability on the pool, place 1 free token. Every gambling ability you get from the pool will have 1 extra use.
  • Whenever you bid at least 1 token on a scum ability on the pool, place 1 free token. Every scum ability you get from the pool will have 1 extra use.
