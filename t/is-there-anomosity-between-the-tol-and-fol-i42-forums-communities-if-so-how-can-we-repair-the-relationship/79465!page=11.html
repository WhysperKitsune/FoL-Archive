Arete December 14, 2019, 6:49pm #202

The problem with moving or trying to move discussions to the forums is that there’s a higher barrier to entry to the forums – if you want to join the Discord, most people who play ToL already have a Discord account, so literally all you have to do is click a button, whereas joining the forums has a slightly higher barrier to entry that’s nonetheless a significant deterrent

This means that ideas shared on the Discord will inevitably reach a wider audience (please do not turn this into an argument about whether forum people or non-forum-people have better suggestions/feedback), which means that most people, even if they do have a forum account, will prefer to share them on the Discord to reach a greater number of people

Additionally, starting a discussion on the forum feels more significant – I would feel weird about starting a discussion about some minor thing in its own thread on the forum, whereas it’s really easy to go into #general on the Discord and say ‘so what do we think about Alch’
