[Class Suggestion] The Revolutionist - Neutral Social

Class/Lore/Fanfic
Kape November 20, 2018, 10:17pm #1

The Revolutionist - Unique Neutral Social
Goal: Survive to see at least 2 other neutrals win

Passive:

  • Perfect Infiltration: You will spawn instead of random Blue Dragon class

Day:

  • Night-time Conference (1 use):
    Invite all Neutrals you found using EI to your room tonight. You will be able to talk to each other.

Night:

  • Extended Invitation (unlimited uses):
    Discover if a player is Neutral. If they are you will learn their class and they will be notified about your number.
    You will also get results of all abilities targetting them.

  • Care Package (2 uses)
    Help a Neutral you found with EI accomplish their win condition.
    -Reaper, Sorc, Poss: guarantees their attacks will kill their targets tonight
    -Alch, Merc, Sellsword: they will be death immune tonight
    -Pretender, Fool, Scorned, Inquis: give them back one use of their abilities

The survive 'til [x] clause is incredibly difficult to meet considering there are so many mechanics that make this wincon swingy and unfun to play.

When SS spawns, you are a proxy SS.
When Inq spawns, you are an anti-BD neutral.
When Pretender spawns, good freaking luck. That role is difficult to win with already AS the Pretender.

This class spawning makes it incredibly difficult for BD to win, as not only do they lose a player, but the scum faction gains a player in many cases.

This design ends up being either too unfair for BD, or too unfair for the Revolutionist depending on what changes. In other words, I don’t think this class works conceptually.
