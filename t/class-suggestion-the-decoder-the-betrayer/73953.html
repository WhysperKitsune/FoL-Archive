The Decoder
Blue Dragon Social

Motion Sensor (Passive) - When someone dies, panel updates that cell black color.
When Poss jumps , the panel still remain same and not update white. Reaper hollow body does not trigger this.

Collect Feedback (Day) - Bug a player, panel updates that cell/cells green color to whoever the bugged player visits and yellow color from bugged player(Infinite Uses)

Screening Updates (Night) - Choose 4 cells from the panel, that cells reflects their class color.(Infinite Use)
Blue -BD , Red - Cult , Purple - Unseen , White - Neutrals

Camera Checking (Night) - Choose a number and see if he/she is in the panel, if so reveal which box it is. (3 Uses)

Note: The panel has 3x4 cells, which exclude King, Prince, Decoder, NK
