Serious thoughts on this particular EFoL

Day 1 Lynches

I honestly don’t particularly mind the D1 lynch? It’s obviously different from FoL but it’s not like it’s turning it into an entirely different game or anything. If it sticks around someone is going to need to put serious thought into balancing the set-up, 3 starting Cultseen + possible EK is just kind of broken, but 2 starting Cultseen + probable GK might be underpowered if one of them ends up getting caught D1.

Inquisitor

Probably fine but it wasn’t forcerolled so I can’t actually say whether it would work well as implemented or not. In my opinion, the Inq spawnrate should probably be higher than that of most neuts if you want it to actually affect the game – even before Kai wound up outed as the “second” neut, lots of people were soft or hard claiming. I think probably there are going to need to be a couple games where it actually shows up before people start treating it as a meaningful thing that exists.

Logs

Obviously FoL isn’t ToL, but if it’s going to be ToL-based at all it kind of needs logs to stay a thing. I know one of the concerns mentioned was the thing where some people aren’t particularly active and then just leave giant readlists in their logs; one potential compromise solution would be to have a word or a character limit in logs for them.

I don’t think the lack of logs affected the game as much as it could have (though it did mean that people trusted Alice’s flip), but I do think it could have affected it a lot more if the game had gone past D3. Of all the changes in this EFoL, this is the one that feels most wrong.

tl;dr: Logs good, overpowered cultseen bad, Inq probably fine, D1 lynch fine iff the second thing gets resolved
