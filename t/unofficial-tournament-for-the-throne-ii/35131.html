lunamink January 10, 2018, 12:55am #9

The player receives the points regardless of death so,

Ex. I am a hunter, I die N1. BD ends up winning the game, I still receive my 10 points for winning with my faction.

The original cult leader holds to that same idea but with the extra incentive to survive the entire game to get more points,

Ex. Cult Leaders dies N2, Cult wins, 11 points
Ex 2. Cult Leaders survives entire game, cult wins, 12 points.
