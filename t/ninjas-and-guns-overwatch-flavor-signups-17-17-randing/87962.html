To rand the setup, I will do the following:

First, I will roll a d10. 1-5 will obviously be 1-5, while A-E will be 6-10.

In cases like this, I will roll a d2 on the two mafia roles. Keep in mind there cannot be two duplicate Mafia Jailkeepers.

If I roll a 2 on the d2, then I will add a Mafia Ninja JOAT and roll a second. If I get another 2, I will add a Mafia Jailkeeper.

Gameplay

  • Days are 48 hours unless majority is reached early and nights are 24 hours.
  • Yeets are majority and plurality. In cases of a tie, the yeet will be determined randomly among the players receiving the most votes.
  • No-yeet is a valid vote, and may be hammered to end the day early. In cases where no-yeet and another vote are tied at the end of the day, no-yeet will have priority in the rand.
  • Self-votes are considered valid votes. Accompanied AtE is not required.
  • Votes are locked in MyLo and LyLo scenarios. That is to say, in situations where a misyeet or a no-yeet will guarantee a Mafia victory, the first vote cast by a given player is final and cannot be changed.
  • All votes must be in the form /vote [name of player] to be considered valid, and all unvotes must be in the form /unvote . This is to enable the smooth processing of votes. Pinging the host(s) is not required, but is welcomed.
  • The Mafia factional kill is mandatory. If no factional kill is submitted, the kill will be determined randomly from among living members of the town.
  • The Mafia will communicate through a private Discord server or forum topic.
  • Mafia can perform the factional kill alongside any abilities they have.
  • Alts will not be allowed.

Town Innocent Child

You win when you eliminate the mafia.

Town Bodyguard

Each night, you may guard a player. If they are attacked, you will sacrifice yourself and die in their place.

You win when you eliminate the mafia.

Town 1-shot Bulletproof

The first time you are attacked, you will survive.

You win when you eliminate the mafia.

Town Vigilante

Each night, you may target a player. You will attack them.

You win when you eliminate the mafia.

Town Jack of all Trades

Each night, you may use one of the following abilities:

  • Vigilante: Target a player. You will attack them.
  • Cop: Target a player. You will inspect their alignment, and receive a Town or Mafia result.
  • Jailkeep: Target a player. You will jail them, roleblocking them for the night and protecting them from attacks.

You may only use each ability twice, and you must use each ability once before you can use one again. Like a regular Jailkeeper, you may not consecutively target the same player with your Jailkeep ability.

You win when you eliminate the mafia.

Mafia Goon

You have no special abilities, but possess a 24/7 scum chat and a factional kill.

You win when you reach parity with the town.
