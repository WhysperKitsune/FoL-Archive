[Class Suggestion] The Bishop and the Heretic

Class/Lore/Fanfic
Livicus January 31, 2018, 11:14pm #1

I know that the Priest was removed a few months ago, but in hopes of having him back, I made this. BD doesn’t have a support class that recharges limited abilities (after that CW change). I planned for the Priest to be conversion immune but there’s currently some debate on that topic.

The Bishop

Unique Blue Dragon Support

Goal: Same as all of BD

Passives

Devout: Your voice cannot be tampered with. (Icy Touch, Distract, etc. will not work on you.)

Faithful: You cannot be converted starting from Day 6. If you are attempted to be converted after Day 6, you will gain a charge of all limited abilities.

Abilities

Blessing: (Night) (Unlimited)
Choose two targets. Any buffing night action done to you (Heals, Guards, Barriers, Sacrifices, Smites, etc.) is done to the targets as well. You will see any feedback that happens if using a buffing night action with successful results.

Ex: Does not see: 14 did not require any healing.
Sees: 14 was cured of their Nightshade poisoning.

Renewal: (Night) (2)
Choose one person and restore two charges on all limited abilities. Will not go over original limit. Will not work unless that person is completely out of charges for at least one limited ability.

Shows Message: “You were renewed!”

Parameters:

  • Renewal will not affect a Prince’s Execute if a BD was executed
  • Renewal will not work on Physicians’ Reanimate

Consecration: (Day) (3)
Choose one person; prevent any day abilities from targeting them that day, and if the person is affected by Icy Touch or Distract, cancel it.

Exceptions are abilities that affect the night phase, like Imprison and Scout.

The Heretic

Unique Unseen Support

Goal: Same as Unseen

Passives

Devout: Your voice cannot be tampered with. (Icy Touch, Distract, etc. will not work on you.)

Abilities

Heresy: (Night) (2)
Choose one member of the Unseen, give them one charge to one limited ability.

Deceive: (Night) (2)
Have all members of the Unseen appear as not suspicious tonight.

Consecration: (Day) (3)
Choose one person; prevent any day abilities from targeting them that day, and if the person is affected by Icy Touch or Distract, cancel it.

I don’t know what abilities would work very well for the Heretic, and I am not sure if they should be a Support or Social class, and if one is chosen, how abilities should be changed or not.

Livicus:

Devout: Your voice cannot be tampered with. (Icy Touch, Distract, Troll Box, etc. will not work on you.)

Voice of Spirits: You can hear the dead at night. You cannot speak to them.

So, about the passives… I like one of them, but I’d like it even more if one thing was changed.
On Devout, no Troll Boxing immunity. TB is more like a ventriloquist act than some form of voice tampering. Other than that, it’s so easy to confirm you didn’t say 5 is cult that having a passive blocking that isn’t needed and would be bad for the balance of the game, in my opinion.
As for Voice of Spirits, I’d honestly take it out and I’ll tell you why. Psychic conversions. At first, I thought ok, we make it so that the Priest can’t hear the Psychic and his target and that solves it, but then I remembered how often it happens that players who recently died always ask in the nights why is there no psychic. So this passive would be just unbalanced in that way. That and the fact that you just got the Possessor another counter. So yeah, I’m against this passive.

Livicus:

Blessing: (Night) (Unlimited)

Choose one person; give them one charge for all limited abilities. Charges for abilities cannot go above their original amount. You cannot choose the King.

This is another thing that I found to be a little unbalanced but with a sort of easy fix. Imagine how powerful is a Hunter who gets both his Wolf and his Bear back. I’d make it so that you recharge only one of the target’s limited abilities. By default, it would be the first one. If it was fully charged, you’d go for the second.

Livicus:

Renewal: (Night) (1)

Choose one person; fully restore all charges on limited abilities and make that person immune to conversion for that night. (Can choose the King)

Again, one ability, not all, is my opinion. In this case, not so much, though, since it’s a one time use. This is an extremely powerful ability.
What I’d do with this is actually the following:

  1. Switch the names between night ability 1 and 2.
  2. Make it so that the new Blessing has unlimited uses and makes a person immune to conversion that night. Leave the recharge for the other ability.
  3. Change the new Renewal to limited time uses. Something like 3 uses.
Livicus:

Consecration: (Day) (3)

Choose one person; prevent any day abilities from targeting them that day, and if the person is affected by Icy Touch or Distract, cancel it. You will be able to whisper privately to the person you consecrated for the rest of the day.

Blocking day abilities on the target is already powerful enough, the whisper thing is too much in my opinion. Taking it out would also help you decide on Support, instead of Social. Which I believe is the best choice to follow with the Priest.
