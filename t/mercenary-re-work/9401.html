Mercenary Re-Work

DeathSwitch November 3, 2017, 10:47pm #1

Merc games are very slow and RNG dependent. I almost never get to attack my contract’s accuser and I only have four guards. So you have to guess which night they are attacked which is based on a variety of different factors. For example, a drunk outs himself at court for whatever reason, it’s a good idea to guard him. Sometimes you are just unlucky and they die in the first three nights.

A good idea is to save your guards for the second contract with a high priority kill such as the Prince. But that’s no guarantee.

The neuts out meta means that merc is a hard claim to announce during the execution block. So they may have to either stay under the radar (which is suspicious) OR they have to claim another role which could easily get you killed. If the Unseen/Cult know you’re a merc, they will want to convert you or kill you at night.

Here is a simple solution. Return the Retribution ability but change it to something like False Accusation.

How this works:

If a Blue Dragon accuses another Blue Dragon of treason and he goes to the chopping block, the Merc will attack the accuser and save the person’s life. This will prove that you are not a neutral killer (reaper/possessor) and the Blue Dragon player lives another day. The King, Cult, and Unseen will not be affected by this ability.
