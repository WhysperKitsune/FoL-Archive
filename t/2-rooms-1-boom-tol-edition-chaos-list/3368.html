The Game:

The game is based around two main teams: the Red Team and the Blue Team.
On the Red Team, there is a Bomber, on the Blue Team there is a President.
All players will be split into two rooms,which will be represented by Discord Servers.
Every round a number of players will be chosen to be sent to the other room.

At the end of the game, the Blue Team wins if the President and Bomber are in separate rooms, while the Red Team win if they are in the same room.
However, in this particular game both teams will have an additional win condition: The President must card share with the Doctor by the end of the game to cure their illness, and the Bomber must card share with the Engineer to arm the bomb.

In additon, there are roles which are on neither team, they are on Grey Team. Details will be given below.

Electing a GLORIOUS LEADER

At the start of the game, a random player in either room will be chosen as OUR GLORIOUS LEADER for that room.

At any time, a player may attempt to Usurp a leader, by doing /nominate [new leader]. Should the majority of other players in the room do /approve, the leader will be replaced.
At any time, a Leader may give their leadership away to another player.
At the end of each round, the Leader must have chosen players to send to the other room, or else RNG will pick.

Color/Card sharing.

During each round, players may do /card share [Player] or /color share [player. If the other player /accepts, both will show each other either their full rolecard or just the team they are on.
You may also /card reveal [player] or /color reveal [player] to simply reveal your card to them.

Finally, you may /public card reveal or /public color reveal to reveal your card/color to absoloutely everyone else in the room.

Rounds:

Each round the Leader of each room will send Hostages to the other room

  • Round 1 - 4 Hostages and 4 days to chose
  • Round 2 - 3 Hostages and 3 days to chose
  • Round 3 - 2 Hostages and 2 days to chose
  • Round 4 - 2 Hostages and 1 day to chose
  • Round 5 - 1 Hostage and 1 day to chose
