5 problems in the game in version 1.1.2b

Feedback/Suggestions
beo May 7, 2018, 8:27pm #1

Hello

I guess I’m pretty experienced player who has 500+ hours of game time. I just would like to post my opinion about current state of the game. In my opinion there are 5 things that are not good (not healthy) for the game.

  1. Alch claims D1 work too good.

Killing classes (evil and NK) do not want to waste a night for an attempt to kill alch claim. Also BDs usually check him if he is not occ immune and support class and then just let him live for the rest of the game.

  1. Jailing/occupying Assasins and CL is OP.

As soon as BD team finds Assasin or CL which can happen even in N1 they can just jail/occupy him for 3 nights and after that execute making Cult or Unseen team completely useless for 3 nights while investigative BD classes search for NK and other evil members and rest of BDs confirm each other.

  1. Mercs are usually contracted to evil and neutral classes.

That means that Merc can’t claim Merc to BD team (if he is smart). Otherwise his contract usually should be just executed cause it’s just logically correct: BDs have high chance to get MM or CL together with thier Merc by doing that.
As Nobles have Spy skill Mercs cannot even claim Merc to their own target. This puts Mercs in a position when all teams (BD, evil and NK) are against them since all teams consider them as non-ally cause they do not know who is thier contract.
I think it’s not the way it’s gotta be.

  1. NK aka Possessor/Reaper/Sorcerer has too low chance to win the game.

This makes playing them not that fun and I see many players who almost give up when playing them by making very weak claims or just leaving the game at start.
It’s hard to say what can be changed in order to achieve balance. Maybe make Neutral King spawn sometimes who is allied to NK?

  1. It’s very hard to distinguish cult classes from BD classes.

Is it Butler/Drunk or invoker? Is it Cw or Ritualist? Is it Seeker or Princess? In most of scenarios the only way to understand who is who is just to random execute them and see the result. Of course, there are some differences between these classes that can make you unserstand who is who like Butler can Party unlike Invoker, Princess has Royal Blood unlike Seeker, but that’s not enough.

Thanks for reading this
