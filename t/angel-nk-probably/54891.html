Angel (NK Probably)

Class/Lore/Fanfic
Jazz March 27, 2018, 5:11pm #1

Lore Summary - A maddened angel obsessed who wishes to learn what makes humans wicked, and then to purify the corrupt

Passive - Divine Intervention - Immune to death and occupation at night

Passive - Holy Knowledge - You will know exactly which factions/class types you have yet to identify

Day Ability 1 - Act Casual - You will appear as a goodboi to investigators tonight - 3 uses

Night Ability 1 - A Glimpse of the Soul - You will learn your target’s exact class, if you have then investigated at least 1 member of each faction that is still in the game and 1 class of each type that is still in the game, you gain 1 use of Purge the Wicked, a player will be notified that you looked into their soul - Infinite Uses

Night Ability 2 - Purge the Wicked - Kill all other players, deaths from this method cannot be prevented in any way - 0 Uses

Goal - Be the only player alive at the end of the game

Notes: Yo help me balance this
