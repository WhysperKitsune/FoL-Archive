[Class Suggestion] King Variants

Class/Lore/Fanfic
ThePlex May 21, 2017, 9:00pm #1

NOTE: This is all a WIP and will ask that if you think there is a better alternative to a name or ability to one of my King Variants, let me know below! This is also purely an idea I’m toying with and mostly for fun. I made this because it’s interesting to see what could happen too. Who knows? Maybe Xblade will take interest in something like this in the future of Throne of Lies! Enjoy!

All the Kings here would retain the ability to use Guards and Decide fate but not Point the Finger but will gain a unique ability, which would not be able to confirm them unless it’s like… special circumstances like Physician/Herbalist…

First off, rename the “Good King” and “Evil King” to:

Dragon King
Unseen King
Cult King
Neutral King (No idea what to use for this one)

Noble -> Dragon King
Night Ability: Send a Maid to spy on your target to hear whispers. (2 uses)

Aristocrat -> Unseen King
Night Ability: Send a Maid to spy on your target to hear whispers. (2 uses)

Prince -> Brave King
Night Ability: Get the exact Class of your target (3 uses)

Sheriff -> War King
Night Ability: Get the Alignment of your target (3 uses)

Mastermind -> <Should not be> King
Nothing. Please make it so that Mastermind cannot step up.

Assassin -> Shadow King
Passive: Reveals to you who is Converted (to stay up to date with Mastermind)

Princess -> Dragon Queen
Day Ability: Unleash a Will O Wisp tonight but will tell you everybody who visits. (1 use)

Duchess -> Unseen Queen
Night Ability: Get the exact Type of your target (2 uses)

Psychic -> Visionary Queen
Day Ability: See the Unseen/Cult chat tonight (1 use)

Illusionist -> Illusionary Queen
Day Ability: See the Prince’s jail chat as Prince and Prisoner talking (1 use)

Knight -> Fighter King
(Must have all Guard uses)Day Ability: Use up all uses of your Guards ability for 1 night of protection. If sucessful, you will kill the Killer Class. (1 use…)

Enforcer -> Thug King
Night Ability: Threaten your target to use their Night ability on somebody at random tonight. (2 uses)

Priest -> Holy Queen
Day Ability: Speak with the dead chat tonight (1 use)

Soulcatcher -> Unholy Queen
Day Ability: Able to read the dead chat tonight (1 use)

Court Wizard -> Magic King
Day Ability: Cast a flame ward on your target. If they use a Day Ability, it will backfire onto themself. (2 uses)

Sage -> Dark King
Night Ability: Cast a flame ward on your target. They will use their Night Ability on themself. (2 uses)

Physician -> Healthy King
Passive: All Royals will see how a person died.

Herbalist -> Poison King
Passive: All Royals and Unseen will see how a person died.

Observer -> Observant King
Day Ability: Send a Hawk to watch your target tonight. In the next Day Phase, Royals will be informed on who your target visited. (1 use)

Nightwatch -> Nosey King
Day Ability: Send a Raven to watch your target tonight. In the next Day Phase, the Unseen will be informed on who your target visited. (2 uses)

Butler -> Drunk King
Night Ability: Send the guards to drink with your target. They will be told Guards were sent to protect them but they were secretly occupied. (3 uses)

Servant -> Thief King
Day Ability: Send the guards to drink with your target tonight and steal their ability. You will be able to select their target tonight and it will tell them Guards were sent to protect them. (3 uses)

Mercenary -> Protector Queen
New Goal: Don’t let the Blue Dragon lose
Extra 2 Guard uses but cannot use it on yourself

Sellsword -> Hired Queen
Night Ability: Prevent your target from leaving their room. They will be told Guards were sent to protect them. (2 uses)

Fool -> Fool King
Goal and Reward: Get yourself executed - If successful, there will be no more Kings this game.
Day Ability: Buffed Trollbox: You can speak as your target but they will not know. (3 uses)

Alchemist -> Thaumic Queen
Same Goal: Survive
Day Ability: Saphire Potion: Your target will have their abilities refreshed tonight (2 uses)

Reaper -> Dead King
Same Goal: Win with Surviving Neutrals
Passive: Read what the dead are saying (Should be able to hide the dead chat too)

Possessor -> Puppet King
Same Goal: With with Surviving Neutrals
Day Ability: Hide your tracks tonight, your Possession target will not know they were Possessed. (2 uses)
