Baron

Neutral Investigative
Passive: Royal Blood - You can step up to become king. (You will become a king of the opposite faction as starting king
Passive 2: Private Guards - Night immune twice

Day Ability: Bribe - Force the first target to vote the second. You can force someone to vote for king. (3 Uses)

Night Ability: Courier Intercept - Get target players full night results (Unlimited)
Night Ability 2: Forged Lineage - Give target player royal blood. If they already have royal blood then they will lose it. You will know which happens. They won’t know until the king dies. (2 Uses)
goal: Live to see the King lose.
