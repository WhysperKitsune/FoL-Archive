Which is better? The Knight or The Physician?

NAOtheRitualist September 21, 2018, 11:27pm #1

The Knight

Pros :

  • Can protect others from dying.
  • Has a killing ability.
  • Can be confirmed with CS.
  • Can confirm others with CS , although you commit suicide.
  • Counters Reaper , Limits the Possessor’s Possess options and prevents a night kill by The Sorcerer.
  • The Best Duo to kill evils is : Butler(Cult Games)/Drunk(Unseen games) + Knight.
  • Can turn the outcome of the game.
  • Has a passive that can prevent you from dying when you successfully guard someone.

Cons :

  • Easy fakeable.
  • BD will usually exe all knight claims on sight.
  • Can harm BD more than the others factions when played poorly.
  • Not guaranteed to spawn.
  • Can easily be found once converted.

The Physician

Pros :

  • Can protect others from dying.
  • Can protect yourself from dying.
  • Can be confirmed with the help of an Observer or Noble.
  • Can make someone immune to bleeding/poison.
  • Prevents a night kill by The Sorcerer.
  • Can cure bleeding/poison.
  • The Best Duo to survive till the end of the game : Alchemist(Cult Games)/Knight(Unseen Games - With Mastermind dead) + Physician.
  • Can be clutch in 2v2 situations.
  • Has a passive that makes you immune to poison/bleeding.
  • Is a mini investigator that finds out what killed a player.

Cons :

  • Easy fakeable.
  • BD will try to execute the healers claims that didn’t heal poison/bleeds no matter how much you try to defend yourself.
  • Reaper and Possessor’s Possess can easily bypass healing.
  • One healer is guaranteed to spawn. This means alch can replace phys.
  • Can be easily found once converted , especially when another physcian is in the game.

So , in my opinion , Knight is better in some situations.
What do you think?
