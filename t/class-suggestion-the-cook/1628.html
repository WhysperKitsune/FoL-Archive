The Cook - Neutral Killing

Passive Ability: Immune to Conversion

Day Ability: Feed (4 uses)
Feed the King and his guards will be forced to protect you tonight.

Night Ability 1: Spoiled
Serve a plate of rotten meat to somebody at night. They will die in 1 day, from food poisoning.

Night Ability 2: Cook (1 use)
Serve food to the members of court. In 4 days, everybody that is night immune will die unless you are killed.

Goal: Win with Surviving Neutrals

Lore:

Page from the diary of The Cook-

"I spent years serving the King and the court. Pork leg here, chicken breast there. All of it was cooked to perfection but does the cook get any thanks? No. All they do is scoff it down like pigs. Even the Princess eats like a pig. Castle Adiart doesn’t deserve such luxury.

I’m going to do it. I’m going to kill them all. Over the weeks, I’ve saved meat that I’m going to mix in with a big feast. Maybe even serve it to them at night. Hell, they’re too tired to even think about what goes in there mouth at night.

I’m sick and tired of the King and feeding his royal scribble backside. Castle Adiart will perish."
