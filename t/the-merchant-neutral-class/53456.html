The Merchant

(Neutral Offensive)

Goal: Be alive on Day 8.

(You have to delay the game so it reaches Day 8 and be alive)

(When Day 8 begins, you will lose all of your abilities.)

Passives

Haggler:
You are occupation and redirection immune.

Insurance:
You are immune to death at night once.

Abilities

Stockpile: (D) (3)
Your abilities will bypass occupation and redirection immunity tonight.

Ransom: (D) (2)
Prevent all other visits to a player tonight.

Barter: (N) (oo)
Occupy a player.

Advertise: (N) (3)
Choose two players. You will redirect all players from your first target to your second target. (Can target self)

I am determined to include a balanced Neutral Offensive into the game. The Merchant is focused on delaying the game as long as possible, until Day 8, so that he can sell more stuff and make more money.

Plain Survive Goals are boring.

Merchants can be beneficial and detrimental to both sides as they are able to occupy and prevent visits, preventing Investigative classes from checking players or Physicians from healing bleeding or poisoned players.

However, they are also good for Blue Dragon, as they can prevent visits, meaning prevent conversions and attacks, and the fact that they want the game to be delayed means they do not want people to die.

EDIT:

Prevent a player from using Day Abilities for the rest of the day.
