Arete January 25, 2020, 8:46pm #13966

For what it’s worth, Priestess, I don’t see people replacing out in FM as necessarily comparable to replacing out in ToL

For one thing, replacing out is generally accompanied by someone else replacing in, which means that you’re not leaving your team with no recourse

Additionally, FoL is a much greater time commitment than ToL, and runs over a much longer period. It’s reasonable to expect people to have enough of a sense of their plans for the next half an hour that they don’t join a game of ToL if they’re going to have to do something urgent in 5 minutes; it’s far easier for you to get injured and land in the hospital, or be too sick to play, or have a family emergency, or be unexpectedly scheduled to work 60 hours at your job because it’s the holiday season, and given that you’ve spoken out against people sacrificing their personal wellbeing for FM I feel like ‘accept that sometimes people are going to have to replace out’ is a reasonable step towards accomplishing an actually reasonable balance

I’ve never replaced out of a game, but I also think that if we want to ensure that FM can be a place where people flourish, it’s important to protect people’s right to replace out, so that people with legitimate reasons don’t end up worried that they’re doing something wrong by replacing out
