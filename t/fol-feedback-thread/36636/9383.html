  • Butler:

Nightshade Wine (Day) - Poison The King, causing him to die tonight if not healed. The king will not be informed if he is poisoned. If the King is Good, you will commit suicide. - 1 use, available starting Day 2

Should say Mithras’ not Mithras.

  • Court Wizard:

Arcane Empower (Day) - The target of your night ability, this night, will bypass immunities (discounting conversion immunity) and be guaranteed to succeed with their actions. Their investigative abilities will always receive correct results. 2 Uses.

This sentence should follow what everything else says and instead say: “The player that you target with your ability tonight”

Majority of the things in the brackets don’t need to be specified on all classes.

Court Spies should have brackets around it.

Little Bird (Day) - Send a bird to track if target player leaves their room that night or not. In addition, you will learn if any players visited target player or not. You will be told the next morning. - 2 uses

Why not just say that you’ll be told at the end of the night like other abilities?
