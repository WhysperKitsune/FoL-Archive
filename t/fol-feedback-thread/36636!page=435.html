Summary:

Reaper can see the number of his souls now.
Reaper gains the potential to gain occupy/redirection immunity over manipulating logbooks which is more Possessor’s niche. This also makes NK’s almost universally occupy/redirection immune, which is the design we’d aim for. Reaper doesn’t have it by default, but makes it up for it by being flexible.

The Cult Alt Physician was one of the weakest. Taking bleed immunity is now stronger and will be kept through becoming Cult Leader/Devout King and will also stop poison. The main ability is now also able to heal, so converting bleeding players with Physicians alive is actually a viable strategy. A converted Physician should also still have healing in some capacity.

Maid can no longer find NK’s. To compensate they gain Rumor, which allows them more flexibility, and also gives them ways to bounce back from being blocked/jailed night 1. In addition the cult alt was very weak and has been given a frame and a stronger matchmake to put them in line with other cult alt investigatives. Cult also had no frames yet, which is bad for claimspace. Handmaiden gained a buff in Matchmake.
