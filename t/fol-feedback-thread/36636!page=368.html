NuclearBurrito May 1, 2019, 8:37pm #7393

Saying something is fun is both vague and impossible to reliably measure. And even if we could measure it that still wouldn’t justify not doing something we haven’t already tried.

Or in otherwords even if having both the scorned and devil is fun that wouldn’t mean that having just one isn’t more fun.

And just because a class is fun to play as doesn’t mean it’s fun to play against and vice versa.

That is why we use game theory and psychology to design games and not a series of polls.

Both of those can be backed up by math or science where a vague idea of “fun” cannot
