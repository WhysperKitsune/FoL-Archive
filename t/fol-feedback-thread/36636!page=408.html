The Devil

Neutral Social
Second Chance (Passive) - Immune to death at night, once.

Informant (Day) - You learn the faction of your target at the end of the day. - 1 use

Trickery (Night) - If the target player is executed tomorrow, they will appear to be a chosen class. - 2 uses

The Scorned

Neutral Social
Resolve (Passive) - Immune to occupation and target changing. Additionally, you are immune to death at night until you complete your objective.
Frame (Night) - Frame one of your targets. They will appear as a member of the Unseen or Cult to investigations tonight, depending on which is present in the current game. - 2 uses
Bait and Switch (Night) - Swap a player with one of your targets, redirecting all actions on one to the other. - 3 uses
Your objective is to survive to see one your targets executed, they are [X] and [Y].

The Scorned’s targets will always be non-unique members of the Blue Dragon (at the start of the game).

Neutral Special

Head of State (Passive) - Your vote counts as two if there are more than 5 players alive. Immune to occupation and redirection.
Grand Trial (Day) - Instantly put a player on trial for treason. They may provide a defense, and then the players alive may either vote to execute or pardon.
