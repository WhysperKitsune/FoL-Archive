The Pretender

Neutral Special
Royal Blood (Passive) - Votes for you to become King count as 2
No Allegiance (Passive) Immune to Death at night once. If you become King, you will become a Usurper King
Silence (Day) - Prevent Target player from using Day abilities if Royal, or the King (3 uses)
Blood Test (Night) Find out if the Target Player has Royal Blood, and their Class group (Special, Investigative etc) if they are (Infinite uses)
Kinslayer (Night) Kill target player if they are Royal, or the King (2 uses)

Usurper King

Neutral Special
Pass The Torch (Passive) - If you die, there will be a vote held to crown the next King.
Head of State (Passive) - Your vote counts as two when putting people on trial.
Royal Pardon (Day) - Make a player unable to be voted today. - (2 uses)
Decide Fate (Day) - You may choose whether the player on trial will be executed or pardoned. (1 use)
Guard? (Night) - Prevent all visits to you tonight - 1 use.
Disinherit (Night) - Permanently remove a player’s royal blood. If this is successful, you will gain two uses of Guard?. - (Infinite uses)
Your objective is to survive until the end of the Game, making sure the Prince dies

Okay. Here’s my alchemist rework, credit to @Livicus for going over it with me.

I did use some material from my other alch suggestion, so enjoy this one.

The Alchemist

Neutral Special
Experimentation (Passive) - Upon achieving your win condition, you will leave the castle the next night and create a clone to fake your death
Extract Essence (Day) - If target player dies tonight by someone else’s hands, you will gain another use of a potion of your choice. - Infinite uses - 1 night cooldown
Experimental Shield (Day) - You will be immune to death and occupation tonight. - 1 use
Concoct Potion (Night) - Choose from a variety of potions to use tonight. - Infinite uses

Crimson Potion - Heal targeted player, if you successfully heal a player from an attack that is not you. This use of Crimson Potion will not be used up. - 1 use
Misty Potion - Target player will appear as a member of the Blue Dragon to Investigations. - 1 use
Tar Potion - Occupy target player, they will not know they were occupied. Investigative classes will get generic Investigation results. - 1 use
Knife Potion - Bleed targeted player, sometime in the next day, you will regain a use of this potion if they bleed out. - 0 uses

Your objective is to use 5 potions throughout the game.

(You can target yourself with your potions.)
