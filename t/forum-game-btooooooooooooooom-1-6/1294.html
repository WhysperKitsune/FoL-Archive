##Welcome
To the forum game called Btooom
The idea is from a anime that Ive watched called, well, Btooom
In the game you basically get bombs and blow people up
Timing and planning is needed to get a kill

##Basic Mechenic
1.The attack weapon in this game are bombs and only bombs
2.You can either: throw a bomb into a room beside you or hide it in the room you are in currently.
3.Those bomb that were thrown out obviously will be noticed by anyone who enters
4.You can set the timer of the bomb to whichever round you want to bomb to explode
5.Everyone get 3 crackers at the start of the game
6.All bomb will explode on the start of the round, damaging all players inside
7.If the owner regret throwing the bomb, they can defuse it and take it back,Other player will have to use a defusal kit
8.There will be 6 players in a 5x5 arena

AP

How do you get bomb:
By searching room, you get 2-4 bombs of random type depending on its rarity, and 1/3 chance to get a special item
You can also loot dead player and get everything they have
Also theres supply drop

Supply drop
Every 3 rounds there will be an amount of supply drop depending on how many players there are
You basically find lots of stuff in it without needing to search the room
The coordinate of the supply drop will be announced in main thread

Health Problem
Everyone have 2 HP
Get hit by any bomb will decrease 1 HP
The only way to replenish HP is by finding Health Kit
