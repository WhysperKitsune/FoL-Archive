[Class Suggestion] Priest and Heretic (Social)

Feedback/Suggestions
orangeandblack5 August 23, 2018, 10:27pm #1

So people really seem to like Maid Spy.

However, putting it on Noble muddies an otherwise streamlined class. By bringing back the Priest as a Social Class, this problem can be helped significantly by dedicating the Noble to vote control and the Priest to whispers and social protection, two areas no Social class truly covers in any meaningful way.

Here’s my basic idea for that Priest. Linked below, you will find my Noble suggestion, which no longer has Maid Spy. I think this has a lot of potential to fix a bunch of issues in one go, so please let me know what you think of both of these suggestions. Thanks.

Priest

Blue Dragon Social

Confessions (Passive) - Each night you will hear the confessions of a random player. They may speak to you, but you will not know who they are or be able to respond.
Devout (Passive) - Your voice may not be tampered with. (Icy Touch, Distract, Trollbox, etc. will not work on you)
Contrition (Day) - You choose who you will speak to for Confessions tonight - 2 uses
Anoint (Night)- Protect your target from any Social manipulation tomorrow (includes Decide Fate) - unlimited uses
Intercession (Night) - You may choose a player to intercede with, allowing you to hear all whispers they send or recieve until you select a different target (you may not target the King) - unlimited uses

Heretic

Unseen Social

Hearsay (Passive) - As long as you are alive, the Unseen will have a seperate chat tab with which to talk during the day.
Dogma (Passive) - Your voice may not be tampered with. (Icy Touch, Distract, Trollbox, etc. will not work on you.)
Impersonate (Day) - Your next whisper will secretly appear to be from your target, they will secretly be unable to send or recieve whispers until this occurs - 2 uses
Inquisition (Day) - Your target will confess to you tonight - 2 uses
Indulgence (Night)- Protect your target from any Social manipulation tomorrow (includes Decide Fate) - unlimited uses
Trespass (Night) - You may choose two players to spy on, allowing you to hear all whispers they send or recieve until you select a different target (you may not target the King) - unlimited uses

Noble and Aristocrat

https://forum.imperium42.com/t/class-suggestion-noble-and-aristocrat-social/28119

Points of potential improvement:
-Confessions is unique and cool, but not very effective at any single task in most scenarios. Contrition and Inquisition, however, are much more likely to be useful.
-Do you prefer unlimited, single-target whisper reading, or limited, multi-target whisper reading?
-Open to further suggestions/improvements. Don’t be afraid to be harsh!
