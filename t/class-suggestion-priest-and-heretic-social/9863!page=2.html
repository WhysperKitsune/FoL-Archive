I’m adding my take on Priest just to offer some alternatives on the same thread.
I do agree with Boslov that confessions should be 100% anonymous. My take on Priest gives him some ways to reward or punish good/bad confessions afterwards. (So that the Noble ‘LMAO’ hearsays wont occur)
Also, the Unseen variant of Priest makes it so that it is really risky to out yourself on a confession, because a turned variant can easily disable you the next day or even kill you. So the confession should be insightful for BD Priest, but anonymous (in case of possible converted priest).

Priest

(Unique social class. Magic user)

Passive

  • Confessions: Each night, one random player can send messages to you at night. You won’t know who that other player is and cannot respond to those messages. (This is the old Noble’s Hearsay passive)

Daytime abilities:

  • Good Karma: The person that confessed to you last night becomes immune to death, occupy and conversion the next night. (1 use)
  • Piousness: You are immune to conversion next night (2 uses)

Nighttime abilities

  • Savior: If you die from an attack, you will be the only one to die from that attack. (unlimited uses)
  • Repentance: The person confessing to you this night cannot talk, accuse or vote tomorrow. He can still be voted upon. (3 uses)

Dark Priest (Unseen)

(Unique social class. Magic user)

Passive

  • Confessions: Each night, one random player can send messages to you at night. You won’t know who that other player is and cannot respond to those messages. (This is the old Noble’s Hearsay passive)

Daytime abilities:

  • Bad Karma: Target a player. If this is the player that confessed to you last night, he dies next night. (1 use)

Nighttime abilities

  • Embrace the Void: Target player will leave an empty will (unlimited uses)
  • Break Will: Target a player, if this is the player confessing to you this night, he cannot talk, accuse or vote tomorrow. He can still be voted upon. (2 uses)
