  • Replace Black Wine with…anything. It doesn’t do anything but allow for a few giggles. It confirms a Butler’s presence, but it also doesn’t accomplish much. If a Scum is Black Wined, then they have an excuse not to contribute to discussion or give a proper defense.

  • Remove Alchemist’s Blur because it is basically auto-win when you are a neutral who can confirm themselves 100%. BD never hangs Alchemist claims and evils never waste a night killing Alchemist claims. Easy ELO.

  • Add a discussion during King elections. If neither candidate confirmed themselves before the election, it’s basically a game of chance on who to pick. I’d like to hear them sell themselves on why they should be King.

  • If you send a message during a period of time where they aren’t allowed (Defense of the Accused, King Elections), please do not let it send upon hitting Enter! Just let it sit there so you don’t have to retype the same message once your time to talk comes again.

Just some random thoughts. That Soulcatcher suggestion is what really prompted this post. PLEASE CHANGE THAT.
