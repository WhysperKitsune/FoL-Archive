Rules, Gameplay and other info

Rules

The Gameplay
Days will be 2 IRL days long, or shorter if someone dies
Nights will be 1 IRL day long
Actions are done by messaging me, Scum actions can be done on the shared discord server

Last Will
Message me your entire Will, the last draft I receive before you die shall be the one displayed

Whispering
Removed because it sucks.

Mafia
It will be on a discord server, Mafia get day/NIGHT chat, Night Kills will be performed by any mafia that wants to kill they can do this alongside there night action, but keep in mind they will show up visiting both to following roles.

To Join
To join, simply express interest in joining by saying something like “I want to join” or “/join” or “/in” or "sign me up, Daddy <3 " or whatever you want

Tie breakers
-For example if 3 mafia are alive with 3 town members if they have chance of winning AKA having a Town Killer, Driver or Escort alive ect. The game won’t auto lose, but it will if they have No chance of winning.

Town Classes: [Goal Kill all of the Mafia and the Snitch to win]

Summary

Mayor - Town Leader
-During the Day, you may say "I am the Mayor! to reveal yourself, your vote counts will be 3, classes that can protect you and the Seeker may only target you twice once you have revealed yourself.

Cop - Town Investigative
-During the Night, you may select someone and you will learn if they are Suspicious, Mafia apart from Godfather will show up as Suspicious, others will show up as Not Suspicious.
-You may check if someone is Neutral or Mafia, this goes through the Godfathers interrogation Immunity, Frames still effect this though. (1 use)

Seeker - Town Investigative
-During the Night you may select someone to see there visitors
-During the Night you may select someone to see who they visit

Doctor - Town Support
-During the Night you may select someone to heal, if attacked you will save there life.
-You may self heal (1 Use)

Marshal - Town Support (Occupy Immune)
-During the night you may select someone you will prevent all visitors.
-You may self protect (1 Use)

Vessel - Town Support (U)
-During the Night you may use a action of dead Town Member, you may only use this once per corpse, you can talk to the dead during the night, while using a dead Marshal, Driver or Escort you will be Occupy
Immune, You can target yourself with actions.

Guard - Town Killer
-During the Night you can select someone to kill, you may only do this twice, if you attack a Town, you will not be able to kill again, you cannot do this until Night 3.
-You may watch over someone if they are attacked you will kill there attacker giving up your own life

War Hero - Town Killer (Unique)
-During the Night you can go on Alert, you will kill all visitors trying to attack you you will be Immune to the attacks, you may only do this three times.

Escort - Town Offensive (Occupy Immune)
-During the night you can select someone, you will prevent them from visiting that night.
-You may have a Dance party blocking all visitors (1 Use)

Driver - Town Offensive (Occupy Immune)
-During the night you can select two players and they will be swapped, you may select yourself twice

Mafia Classes: [Goal Kill all members of the Town] All Mafia get night chat.

Summary

Godfather - Mafia Leader (Night Immune + Unique)
-You will appear as not suspicious, to Cops, you may perform the factional night kill.

Enforcer - Mafia Leader (Occupy Immune + Unique)
-During the night you may select someone to visit someone else, using there night action on that person.

Consigilere - Mafia Investigative
-During the night you may select someone, you will learn there full class, Learning who visited them and who they visited.

Frauder - Mafia Offensive
-You may select someone during the night, if they get Investigated they will appear to be suspicious to cops, they will be seen as a Frauder to the Detectives, Seekers will not see anything if a Frauder frames the Seeker.

Consort - Mafia Offensive (Occupy Immune)
-During the night you can select someone, you will prevent them from visiting that night.

Maid - Mafia Support
-During the night you may select someone to clean there body, They will show up as “Cleaned” when dead with no will, this only has Three uses.

Trickster - Mafia Offensive
-During the night you may give someone a fake Night message, or blur out a Investigative classes message giving them nothing “No result”

Note: Mafia Get a factional kill, someone may peform this alongside your kill, be wary seeker will see you visit both targets.

Neutral Classes

Summary

Snitch - Neutral Investigative
-During the Night you may scout someone learning there class you will also learn who they visit and who visited them.
-During the day you may send out a Private message they will get a message from the “Snitch”
-You will be Immune to the first attack you receive.
-If all the mafia are wiped out you will be able to kill every Night
Goal: Town loses.

Survivor - Neutral Bystander
-During the night you may use one of your bullet proof vests to make you Immune for that Night.
-You have 3 vests so use the wisely.
Goal: Survive until the end of the game.
