The Kingdom of Adiart hath stood for many centuries. It’s security and power envied by all. Even after standing through many coups, the world is changing, people change.

How much more can the kingdom take?

The kingdom is getting undermined, the people getting unhappy. Dissent goes through the court, conspirators arise. Can the Blue Dragon quell the threat? Or will they finally fall?

The enemies are coming, will you stand up to them? Or will you join them?

Roleplayers will partake on important roles, all found in the original game! Roles are all up for the taking!**

Take your characters on an exploration of the castle! Also, find out the mindset of the people, and discover what they really are on the inside. Deception and Manipulation are expected in this RP. Plot and Scheme, or join the fanatical Cult!
