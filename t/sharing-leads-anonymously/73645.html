Solution?

It seems like the most straightforward solution is to provide BD with some mechanism to share information anonymously (their class may be displayed but not their number).

Currently, the Mystic can do this through telepathy and the prince can technically do it by asking their jailed player to relay info, but the mystic is not a required class and is capped at 1 per match and the prince method is pretty roundabout. Also both of these methods face the same big problem that king whisper meta had, they rely very heavily upon the skill of just one player.

The lightest touch solution would be to simply require a mystic in every game as it used to be. This way, each game would have a built-in mechanism for investigatives to share their important leads without outting themselves. Downside is that less experienced players may not fill the information sharing role very well.

Alternatively, a more radical solution that I could imagine is some sort of notice board that operates through a night ability allowing investigatives to anonymously post feedback results from one past night of their choosing. This solution would obviously have pretty far reaching implicaitons which I certainly have not thought through completely.
