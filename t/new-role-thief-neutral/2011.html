New Role: Thief (Neutral)

Feedback/Suggestions
Ashe June 19, 2017, 10:17am #1

I had an idea for a new role, the Thief. The Thief is neutral aligned, aka no faction, with the win condition to steal 5 gold bars, and live to tell the tale. (Survive)

To steal gold, you would use your night ability, Steal. Depending on the role, you will steal 1 gold bar, and occupy the player you stole from that night. You can only steal from a player once

However, occupy immune roles can’t be stolen from (with the exception of the king), and a few roles have unique interactions:

The King, Cult Leader and Mastermind will be notified of being robbed, but noone else will receive this message,
Visiting the King won’t occupy him, however it will steal 2 gold bars. If however the King has guards on him, they will kill you.

The Thief is immune to conversion.

So, what do you think? Are any of the unique interactions to harsh for what may be random chance (depending on the night)? Would it be too easy/hard to win as? Does it fit in Throne of Lies? Please leave feedback below.
