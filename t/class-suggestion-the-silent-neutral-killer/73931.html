The Silent

Neutral Killer
Passive 1: Survivor - You will heal yourself if attacked, poisoned or bleed. You are immune to redirect, anyone trying to redirect you will think they succeeded (not immune to occupy)
Passive 2: Infinite Blades - Kunai will gain an additional use at the end of each night, even if you are jailed

Day Ability 1: Evenom - Kunai will bleed your target’s in addition to it’s other effects. They will die the next night if not healed bypassing death immunity (2 use)
Day Ability 2: Duck and Roll - Prevent all visits to you tonight. You will know who is prevented this way (2 use)

Night Ability 1: Kunai - Kill target player. If they survive for any reason it will not consume the use. You may use this ability as many times as you have uses each night. Does not count as a visit or an attack(1 use)
Night Ability 2: Stalk - Learn who target player visits and who visits them (3 uses)

Goal: Eliminate the Primary faction members

Note: With Kunai you have a single box of all the players. You can select as many players as you have Kunai uses and will attack all of them at the end of the night and then refund wasted uses. Kunai ignores knight entirely
