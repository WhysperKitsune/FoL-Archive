Some suggestions

Feedback/Suggestions
TruthTeller October 27, 2017, 10:49am #1

I saw several suggestions around here, so I thought I’d make some of my own.

Social

  1. Make it so that when you mouse over a dead person’s name, you can see the night (or day) they died.
    It is useful in many cases to discredit some bad fake logs. Right now, you can, indeed, scroll up in sustem chat and see when someone dies. I just think it would be easier this way.

  2. In the endgame chat, when someone speaks, the game should show his number (just like the in-game chat).
    This might seem silly or unimportant to you, but I like talking in the endgame chat. However, it’s somewhat inconvenient to have to check the list to see who’s actually talking. Especially since I just spent the whole game getting used to referring to people using their numbers (or even their in-game names). I’m guessing this can be easily implemented.

Abilities

  1. Change assassin’s distraction to not notify the target.
    In my opinion, assassin’s distract is not really useful at this moment. I mean the target can just wait 10 seconds and then just announce he was distracted. Its only real use is if it’s used in the last 10 seconds of the day phase.
    Instead, I would like it to be pretty much the opposite of a trollbox. The target should not be notified he was distracted. In addition, if he says something in that 10-sec window, he would see it like he properly said it, but noone else would be able to see it. I think that keeps up with the spirit of the ability, but makes it better.

  2. Similarly to my previous point, change duchess slap, noble order, and psychic’s and illusionist’s misdirect.
    Make it so the target thinks he votes freely, while in fact he isn’t.

  3. Maybe marshal’s snoop could rewrite journals.
    Alright, this might be too strong and/or confusing in some cases. It would be really useful since it could write fake logs on a good guy or even erase someone’s journal completely. This could be amazing if you coordinate with the assassin to kill the target on the same night and give bad info to the rest of the players or even write something minor, but wrong, in his logs, so they appear fake if he has to defend himself.
    Perhaps, in order to weaken it a bit, the target should receive a notification next day that his journal has been rewritten, so if he survives the night, he could reveal there’s a marshal (or tell the priest if he doesn’t survive). Also, maybe reduce the snoop’s charges from 3 to 2 if that’s the case.

Thank you for reading.
