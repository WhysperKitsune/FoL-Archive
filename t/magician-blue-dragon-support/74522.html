The Magician
Blue Dragon Offensive

Passive - Focused: Occupy and redirect immune. You will keep this passive even if your class changes.

Night Ability 1 - Lightning Bolt: Occupy attacks or converts targeting a player, You will be notified if you occupied anyone. (infinite uses), (starts off as convert protection)
Night Ability 2 - Summoning Portal: Redirect all players targeting your first target onto your second target. (2 uses)

Day Ability - Channel Magic: Toggle Lightning bolt between occupying attacks or occupying conversions. (infinite uses)

The Mage
Unseen Offensive

Passive 1 - Focused: Occupy and redirect immune. You will keep this passive even if your class changes.

Night Ability 1 - Frost Bolt: Occupy all investigations and attacks directed to the unseen, You will be notified whether or not you occupied an investigation, an attack or nothing. (3 uses)
Night Ability 2 - Summoning Portal: Redirect all players targeting your first target onto your second target. (3 uses)

Not too sure if this should be a support or offensive, but leave your thoughts.
