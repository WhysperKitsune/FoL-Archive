The Baron
Neutral Wildcard
Goal: Destroy the Blue Dragon at all costs (even if you die)

Passive: Money talks: Immune to death at night.

Passive: Adaptive Morality- If converted, you will join the converter’s team as usual, but your role will remain the same (your goal will change to match)

Day Ability 1: Bribe: Give gold to someone. They will be unable to vote that day, as you now control their vote. You can make them vote for anyone, including themselves (nothing will show in that case, the required votes will just go down silently if that happens.)

Night ability 1: Coerce: 2 uses: Can buy a player drinks at night, occupying them. In his/her drunken stupor, your target taught you how to use their abilities.

Night ability 2: Capitalize: Infinite: Select out of the abilities you’ve been taught, and some of them you’ve learned previously (Some random abilities, and some abilities you’ve gained from occupying roles) and perform one tonight.
Abilities that cannot be gained: Any kills, Bribe
