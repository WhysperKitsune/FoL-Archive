Shrimpy June 30, 2018, 3:25pm #1

I do not like the new global maid spy. I understand it was intended to stop the whisper meta. But when Prince outs and demands claims, nobody ever refuses because someone might be spying them. It only works (somewhat) to dissuade early whispers to King. And it has the unintended effect of discouraging cooperation among evils and neutrals, which was already difficult to do but is now impossible until it’s gotten to the point where evils are winning anyway. (e.g. Alch can’t whisper Reaper D2, it’s suicide now, whereas before he could get away with it).

I propose that spy be reverted to single target, but usable on King. In fact it used to be single target and not usable on King, so just letting it be used on King should have been the obvious solution to the whisper meta (for those who didn’t like it). Making it be global was just overkill and takes away any skill involved the ability, since now every Noble is just mindlessly clicking spy on N1.

I do not like the new merc and sellsword. It feels like you just die and hope your team wins. Sellsword especially, you cannot communicate with your allies because of the noble spy. So they always accidentally kill you.

I love the new Court Wizard because his swap bypasses redirect immunity. But the empower could be greatly improved. I don’t like how empowering a social class makes them conversion immune, we already have a day ability to do that. It’s redundant and we don’t need a court wizard making two people conversion immune on the same night, conversion immunity is one of the most frustrating parts of this game.

There is no penalty for accidentally empowering a Cult Leader, Mastermind, Reaper, or Possessor if he’s using Possess. This is terrible … Looking at it another way, there is no reward for a Cult Leader, Mastermind, or Reaper convincing a CW to empower them.

I strongly dislike Possess bypassing heal. Bypassing healing was Reaper’s gimmick and I wish it were reverted.

I do think that Possessor should be able to jump out of order execution. Possessor is already killing his current body by jumping to a different one, so it doesn’t make sense that King helping him kill his current body should actually kill the Possessor himself. This would also make Possessor better at framing King which a lot of people want already (he could bait King into order executing him then facelift as a BD class to make it look like King killed a BD).

  • Noble spy is fine as is
  • Noble spy should be single target but usable on King to counter whisper meta
