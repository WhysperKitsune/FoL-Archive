The Shifter

Neutral Offensive
Dark Entropy (Passive) - From night 2 onwards, you appear as a member of the Unseen/Cult to all faction checks. You will die if Reform Body fails.
Sickly Body (Passive) - All limited-use abilities will have 1 less use when you gain a new class.
Last Chance (Day) - Bleed yourself but prevent all visits to you tonight and the following night. - 1 use
Seeking Bodies (Night) - Learn the possible class types a player could be. (basically just flirt) - 4 uses
Reform Body (Night) - Kill a player, wipe their journal, make them flip as Shifter and become their class. Fails if you are attacked that night. Does not steal class if the player doesn’t die. Heals you from Poison/Bleeding if it works. - 1 use, only usable from N4 onwards.
Become a new class and fulfill their objective.

Feedback:

Shapeshifter:
You have begun your own demise to increase your power! You have begun bleeding and will die in 2 nights, however all visits to you will be prevented on those 2 nights!
You have killed [player] to shift into their body. They were a [class], and you are now their class!

Everyone else:
NONE

It’s the closest thing to a ToL amnesiac that is actually neutral that I’d like to see. It solves the issues with conversion and faction size that a regular Amnesiac has and I beleive it is far more interesting gameplay than “wait for a class you want to die and become that class.”

Thoughts?

Based on The Double from Town of Salem Forum Mafia.
