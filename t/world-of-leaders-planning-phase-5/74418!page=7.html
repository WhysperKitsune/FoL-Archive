Reporter Interviewer Net Corner!

Interviewer Net: Hello, hello! Today we have an interview with one of the great leaders from our local communities, The Beast BlueStorm! Let’s hear what he has to say on some issues of today!

Interviewer Net: Thank you for making time off your busy schedule! For a starter, would you care to share your stance on the recently constructed Nuclear Bomb Creation Facility in Our Kingdom of Family Guy? The news, as a whole, is a big shock to everyone!

Beast BlueStorm: I believe this to be an attempt at a show of force by Pug Griffin, and I will let it be known that should they come to us with threats of war, that we will find allies and we will not back down.

Interviewer Net: Thank you. Mass Media Network is also concerned of this development. We have an obligation to spread information and to risk our life for it, but the death of an Acolyte is always undesirable.

Interviewer Net: Moving on to the next question, other than Our Kingdom of Family Guy, Anti-Revolution Commonwealth has also been actively improving their military. Do you see them as a cause of concern, or at least, consideration?

Beast BlueStorm: While the improvement of military units may seem like a possible concern, there is currently no reason for us to worry until these troops are mobilised. If or when that time comes, I assure the people of Suplex City that we will be prepared.

Interviewer Net: And for good reason! Your attention to public healthcare and public in general has been noted by your people and our Network. It is interesting to see someone who cares about their people.

Interviewer Net: Last question. Do you have any plans for Suplex City? Tell us how would you develop your community in the future.

Beast BlueStorm: While I cannot disclose anything specific at present, I can say that there are big plans to improve the health, morale, and wellbeing of our people. They are, after all, the most important part of this kingdom. We have big plans to create a community that is strong both in togetherness and physical prowess.

Interviewer Net: I see. Thank you for your time. Do you have any closing statements for our readers?

Beast BlueStorm: Just the one - if you ever see Advocate Nerbo in the streets again, don’t be afraid to give him suggestions on how to improve our community! We’re always looking for new ideas.

Interviewer Net: Thank you for your time!
