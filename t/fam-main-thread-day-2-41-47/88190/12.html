Vully

So little disclaimer, I went through Vully’s ISO and looked at a couple of their posts and thought about it for a while… and then proceeded to lose my ISO post on my notepad as my computer decided to freeze and crash for some reason. So this is my second time writing this so some things might look wierd, but hopefully not.

Vully’s ISO is complicated to say. Or atleast the first half is. At the beginning of the game, all Vully was pretty much doing was making low level flash reads, which are hard to evaluate.

On one hand, they’re not very well developed and most of them aren’t very touched upon later on, so it is kinda scummy.

But on the other hand, Vully seems like the type of person to do this regardless of their alignment anyway.

Their only real fleshed out reads early on were on Tan and Astand.

The Tangeld read is justifiable I think, but meh. Basically them going after a bad reaction from Tan.

The Astand read is meta stuff which I have 0 idea about.

Both of these reads are fine, but hard to evaluate (atleast for me).

Vully’s second half of their ISO is much better because it has less flash reads and actually starts developing some other reads. Most of the posts are decent (doesn’t stick out as particularly towny or wolfy) but there are actually some posts I’m noting.
