This class has to do with bounding to a target and helping them fulfill their goal. You can also bound with other players to see what they are claiming and to see if they could help you out in anyway

Ashen Communion (Passive) - At the start of the game, you will know the identity of all non-NK neutral players. If the NK remains the last neutral alive while you have still not won, then their identity will be revealed to you.

Grim Determination (Passive) - On days where no one die, you gain death immunity that night. You are immune to occupation, redirection and target changing

Peace Bond (Day) - [Unavailable on Day 1] You will bind yourself to a target player. While bound to that player, you can hear all whispers to and from them. If they are neutral then all whispers will be hidden from the public. You may only be bound to one player at a time and you will only be unbounded on their death or if you change to a new target.- [Infinite uses]

Peace treaty (Day) - Give your bounded target an extra use of all of their abilities, this includes day abilities. If they are a Mercanary or a Sellsword they will gain night immunity for the night. - [2 uses]

Showing your true colors (night) - Visit a player. If they are Neutral, they will be healed and protected from negative effects. If your target is immune for this night, then the ability cost is refunded. - [4 uses]

Conspire (Night) - [Unavailable before Night 2]. Create a group chat between yourself and a bonded player. - [Infinite uses]

Win Conditions - help 2 other neutral players complete their goals or win with the NK

Bonus gold - If you achieved your goal by helping 2 neutral players win then any other neutral player you help achieve their goal, you will gain 25 gold at the end of the game.
