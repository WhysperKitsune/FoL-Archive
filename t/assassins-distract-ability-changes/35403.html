psychoneirik January 8, 2018, 8:26pm #1

I think assassin’s distract ability is probably one of the weakest abilities in the whole game, 10 seconds without talking is not that useful, and you cannot use it while someone is on trial. I have some suggestions to make the ability better:

  • Suggestion 1: Make the person not know they have been distracted and instead of being unable to send messages, no one else will see the messages they send for its duration.

  • Suggestion 2: Make the distracted person unable to hear what others say as well as not being able to talk for the duration, like a mini reaper chill.

I understand this could be a buff for the assassin who might be in an ok spot right now, but I think distract should feel rewarding since it has a lot of potential, and if it turns out too powerful you could lower its uses or the uses of his other day ability to compensate.
