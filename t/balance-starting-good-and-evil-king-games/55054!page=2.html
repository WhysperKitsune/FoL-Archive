RawkHawk March 28, 2018, 4:50pm #34

We’re never going to balance evil king and good king like that. The big picture is that evil king will be a +1 to evils, and unless evil themselves kill him, bd have to use a whole day executing him.

I saw the idea of nerfing Order Execute to 1, but that almost only nerfs neutral king. Most games I’ve played with an evil king, ends up with him somehow getting prince up to the stand, using Decide Fate, then killing a known bd at night or just killing prince at night, then getting voted up by the court. More often than not he doesn’t get his second Order Execute off.

In addition to this, we currently have no way to deduce the faction of the king. He has no abilities that are social or investigative anymore, so we’re required to whisper meta. This leaves us in a hard spot where we have to whisper king to deduce his faction, but doing that gets the king closer to identifying the prince.

Tl;Dr we need to make the kings abilities not be able to auto kill the prince once he knows who he is, as that’s what makes evil king and good king so swingy.
