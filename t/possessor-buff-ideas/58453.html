Possessor Buff Ideas

Feedback/Suggestions
Livicus April 18, 2018, 2:52am #1

Possessor, was supposed to be the NK that got the biggest benefit from the removal of dead interaction. However, it’s still the worst NK, due to the worst win rate at 7% (confirmed).

And now with the Noble Changes coming most likely in the next patch, Poss loses another potential fake claim, the Noble.

I think we should think about buffing the Possessor, and I have some ideas here.

Disclaimer: These are not only mine, some are also from others, including @NuclearBurrito @NightX @orangeandblack5

IDEAS

  1. Increase the uses of Possess back to 3 from 2

Possessor was really versatile with 3, as you could jump multiple times, leaving your old body when you felt you were becoming suspicious. This just increases the flexibility of the Possess ability, and it doesn’t feel like it’s a late-game crutch.

  1. Make Possess bypass healing

This isn’t an implausible idea, as the Reaper’s only night ability, Reap, always bypasses healing. However, Possess is a cornerstone to the Possessor, and without it, the jumping mechanic is harder to use.

  1. Include “Hidden Occupation Immunity”

Like the Mastermind, Possessor could also hide its occupy immunity, so that it can use more fake claims than just the average Hunter or Butler claims that most NK fall to.

  1. Some Day Ability to Cancel or Silence Votes

Reaper, even though it doesn’t have night or occupy immunity, has a higher win percentage because of its special abilities: Icy Touch and Gather Darkness. I feel that Possessor could also take advantage of an ability like that, to disrupt communication or action during the day.

So a proposed rework could be as follows:

The Possessor

Passives

Blackened Shield:
Immune to death, occupation, and redirection.

Driven:
Can use both Day Abilities in the same day.

Abilities

Siphon Aura: (D) (2)
Choose a player. Their passives will be cancelled for today and tonight. (Works during trials and king election) (No notification)

Bind Aura: (D) (3)
Choose a player. They cannot use Day Abilities for the rest of the day. (Does not work during trials and king election)

Puppet Strings: (N) (oo)
Choose a player. Force them to attack your second target. Any player you controlled into attacking others will have their whispers visible to you for the rest of the game.

Possess: (N) (3)
Choose a player and a class to disguise as. You will possess their body and become them. Bypasses Healing.

Note: If the possessed player had any Passive or Immunity, such as Royal Blood or Poison/Bleed Immunity, you will keep it.

So what Siphon Aura does is remove someone’s Royal Blood, Occupation Immunity, Poison and Bleed Immunity, and most importantly Night Immunity, but also prevents them from using Day Abilities. It may seem a bit OP, but this sort of flexibility could be what Possessor needs to be buffed.

Note: King’s Guards will still make a player whose aura was siphoned to be night immune.
