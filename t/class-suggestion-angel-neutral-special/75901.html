Angel

Neutral Special

Angel Bless (Passive) - Immune to death once. This passive is lost after you have achived your win condition.

Angel Wings (Day) - Guarantee to visit your target tonight. [2 Use]

Blessing (Night) - Protect a player for tonight. Will not protect death from Bleed or Execute. [Infinite Use]
You have successfully Blessed [Target].
You have been attacked but you have been Blessed.
[Target] did not get attacked.
You attacked [Target] but Angel Bless stopped the attack.

Goddess Light (Night) - Prevent every attack tonight. If the attack is non-unlimited, then the attacker will regain the use. Annoucement will be made at the end of the night. You will get 1 success protected. Wont stop Prince from executing his prisoner. [1 Use]
Angel have release the Goddess Light and stopped any attacks for the night.
You attempt to attack [Target] but the Goddess Light have prevent you from it.

Win condition:
Successfully protected 3 people from dying.
