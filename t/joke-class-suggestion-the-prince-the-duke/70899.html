The Prince

Blue Dragon Killer

Passive (Unstoppable) - Cannot be occupied or redirected.
Passive (Royal Blood) - Can put your name forward for nominations to become The Next King.

Day Ability (Imprison) - Choose a player to jail tonight. Abilities targeting them at night will be prevented. Can only imprison the same player 3 times.(Infinite Uses)

Night Ability (Execute) - Execute the jailed player. If they are Blue Dragon , you will no longer be able to Execute. Not Usable until N3.(3 Uses)
Goal: Defeat The Unseen/The Cult and any Neutrals that seek you harm.

Converts into:

The Duke

Unseen Killer

Passive (Untouchable) - Cannot be occupied or redirected. Will not become The Assassin if the previous one dies. Won’t know who The Unseen is.
Passive (Royal Blood) - Can put your name forward for nominations to become The Next King.

Day Ability (Imprison) - Choose a player to jail tonight. Abilities targeting them at night will be prevented. Can only imprison the same player 3 times.(Infinite Uses)
Day Ability (Aid) - Check if a player is Unseen.(1 Use)

Night Ability (Execute) - Execute the jailed player. If they are Unseen , you will no longer be able to Execute. Not Usable until N3. (3 Uses)
Night Ability (Nightshade) - Poison the jailed player. They will be notified that they have been poisoned the next day.(2 Uses)
Goal: Defeat The Blue Dragon and any Neutrals that seek you harm.
