Rules

  1. Follow the Global Forum Rules
  2. If you haven’t posted content in 48 hours, you’ll be prodded for inactivity. If you don’t respond to that prod in 12 hours, you will be force replaced.
  3. No private messaging others in the game about the game.
  4. Do not quote private host messages
  5. Don’t throw the game. This includes any form of intent to make you or your faction lose
  6. Refrain from posting so many times in a row.
  7. Players are not allowed to use big letters. These are reserved for the host.
  8. You are allowed to leave the game but doing it will net you a temporary blacklist meaning you can’t join a forum of lies for a while. Further leaving may net with a permanent blacklist
  9. Lets all have fun and play together!
  10. Do not edit or delete made posts.
  11. Do not like posts in a thread if you are dead in that game.

Gameplay

  • Days will be 48 hours long and will start/end at 9 PM EST or when the gunbearer has shot.
  • Nightless
  • There are no lynches
  • Before the game starts, mafia must elect a townie to be the gunbearer.
  • The gunbearer has until day ends to shoot another player.
  • If the gunbearer hits scum, they keep the gun. If the gunbearer hits town, the gunbearer dies and the targeted townie becomes the new gunbearer.
  • If the gunbearer does not shoot then they die at EOD and mafia chooses a new gunbearer.
  • The gunbearer is announced and confirmed innocent.

Other Stuff

  • Do not talk after the gunbearer has shot.
  • You must post a minimum of 5 game-related posts per day phase. If the gunbearer shoots within 12 hours of day start this rule is nullified.
  • Shots administered by the gunbearer will be done in public using the /shoot (player) command. They must tag the host in the command.
  • There will be corn-themed flavors because it makes the game more fun. Flavor WILL NOT be alignment indicative. I REPEAT: Flavor isn’t alignment indicative.

To Join

To join, simply express interest in joining by saying something like “I want to join” or “/join” or “/in” or “sign me up, coach” or whatever you want.
*Game will start when filled or on Tuesday (November 5th). Balance can be adjusted if more than thirteen people show interest.
