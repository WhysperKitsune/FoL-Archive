1.1.7 Patch Notes

Official Announcements
Ellie August 3, 2018, 11:35am #1

THE CLASS CAP FOR BLUE DRAGON IN CLASSIC MODE HAS BEEN INCREASED TO 3 FOR NON-UNIQUE CLASSES.

Lobby:

  • Updated in-game rules 9 & 11.

Quality of Life:

  • Probe icon changed.

General:

  • Voting and voting abilities are now disabled for the first 15 seconds of the day.
  • The time left in the day after an execution has been increased to 20 seconds, and you can use abilities after the trial.
  • All abilities are disabled when there are 5 seconds left in the day.
  • Exhume is now usable at all points during the day.
  • Royal Blood has been reworked.
  • The votes for King are now hidden.

Royal Blood:

  • Players with Royal Blood will no longer be able to step forward before other players.
  • Votes for player with Royal Blood will now count twice compared to those without.

Minor Class/Ability Changes

Cult Leader:

  • Can no longer use Rupture during trials.

Court Wizard:

  • Clear Mind reduced to 3 uses.

King:

  • Guards are now hidden to Gossip & Intrigue.

Noble & Aristocrat:

  • Maid Spy reduced to 2 uses.

Princess:

  • Fixed interactions between Will-o-Wisp and Puppet Strings.
