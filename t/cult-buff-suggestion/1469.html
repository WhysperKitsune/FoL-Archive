ThePlex May 7, 2017, 5:46am #1

I posted this in discord and someone mentioned posting it here

If Cult hit a conversion immune target:

1st instance: Day ability disabled for target
2nd instance: Target dies (shows they suicide)

EDIT: >(Hitting the same person twice does nothing)<

If they hit Prince, prevents any jailing so that there is no chance of any Cult being jailed the following night.

If they hit Paladin, prevents any chance of their next target from receiving a blessing.

If they hit Reaper, prevents him from being able to use souls for Icy Touch or Gather Darkness.

If they hit Fool, can’t Troll Box.

If they hit Alchemist, can’t use Drug the Wine.

If they hit Possessor… well that just sucks.

To explain what happened:

1st Instance:

Prince:
“Mithras invaded your dreams. You are too afraid to go to the cells tonight.”

Paladin:
“Mithras invaded your dreams. Your blessing means nothing today.”

Reaper:
“Mithras invaded your thoughts. Corax ordered you to stand down today.”

Fool:
“Mithras invaded your dreams but you laughed at him.”

Alch:
“Mithras invaded your dreams and you forgot your Tar Potion.”

Possessor:
“Mithras attempted to convert you.”

2nd Instance:

Prince/Paladin:
“Mithras invaded your dreams. You were scared to death.”

Reaper (with soul):
“Corax fought off Mithras… for now.”

Reaper (without soul):
“Corax was unable to fight off Mithras.”

Fool:
“Mithras grew tired of your tricks.”

Alch:
“Mithras invaded your dreams and forced you to drink a Crimson Potion.”

Possessor:
“Mithras attempted to convert you.”

EDIT:
I would also suggest that the hitting of the immune would be reset if you convert the next night.

For example:

N1: Hit Prince and disable day ability
N2: Convert successful
N3: Sacrifice to kill the first immune to conversion
N4: Hit Paladin and disable day ability
N5: Hit Alchemist and kill
