What is Popcorn Mafia?

Right before the game begins, after alignments are rolled, the Mafia must choose one town player to be the Gunholder. Everyone is always aware of who is currently holding the gun.

The person who holds the gun can at any time shoot another player! If the player they shoot is mafia, that mafia goon will die! If the player they shoot at is town, the shooter will die and the town they shot at will become the new Gunholder.

  • The town wins when all mafia are dead. The mafia win when the number of mafia is greater than or equal to the remaining town.

  • The game is nightless and there are no lynches. After receiving the gun, you have 32 hours to shoot another player or you will die and a random town will get the gun.

  • The Gunholder shoots by typing /Shoot PlayerName.

  • Mafia will have a daychat.
