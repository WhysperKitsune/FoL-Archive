The Avenger

Neutral Investigative

Passive (Resolve) You are immune to death, occupation and target changing at night.

Night Ability (Lookout) - Select one of your two targets to see who they visit, and who visits them. (3 uses)

Night Ability (Avenge) - Attack a player. Bypasses death immunity. (1 use)

Goal: Kill someone who killed one of your targets. For example, you win if you kill Prince after he executes one of your targets. Can start with anyone as a target.

You will commit suicide if your Avenge ability gets prevented or you kill the wrong target.
