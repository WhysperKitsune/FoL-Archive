The following is the template I use for making class cards when I do my suggestions, which I believe makes them easy to read. (Note: I didn‚Äôt create the layout, I believe that honour goes to @Jazz)
Additionally, I have an automatic card maker that fills your choices in to the format automatically here. This was originally made by NuclearBurrito, with modifications to my format and extra bells and whistles by me.

> # [color=INSERTFACTIONCOLORHERE]The NAMEOFCLASS[/color]
> **[color=INSERTFACTIONCOLORHERE]FACTION TYPE[/color]**
**PASSIVE (Passive)** - PASSIVE.
**DAY ABILITY (Day)** - DAY ABILITY. - x uses
**NIGHT ABILITY (Night)** - NIGHT ABILITY. - x uses
*GOAL.*

This prints something like this:

The NAMEOFCLASS

FACTION TYPE
PASSIVE (Passive) - PASSIVE.
DAY ABILITY (Day) - DAY ABILITY. - x uses
NIGHT ABILITY (Night) - NIGHT ABILITY. - x uses
GOAL.

From there, you can give the class a name, faction and type, add colours, add in passives and abilities and set an objective.

These are the colours used for specific factions:
Blue Dragon ([color=steblue]INSERT TEXT[/color])
Unseen ([color=purple]INSERT TEXT[/color])
Cult ([color=darkred]INSERT TEXT[/color])
Neutral ([color=gray]INSERT TEXT[/color]), although you can give a neutral class any colour for it‚Äôs name

These are the objectives for BD/Unseen/Cult:
Blue Dragon: Your objective is to defeat the Unseen or Cult, and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.
Unseen: Your objective is to defeat the Blue Dragon and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.
Cult: Your objective is to defeat the Blue Dragon and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.

If the class is Unique or Guaranteed, you use an emoji:
:shield: for Unique
:crossed_swords: for Guaranteed

And when you put it all together, you can get something like:

The Butler

Blue Dragon Offensive
Dutiful (Passive) - Immune to being occupied at night and immune to target changing.
Nightshade Wine (Day) - You will poison the King, causing him to die in one night if not healed by a Physician or an Alchemist. If the King is a member of the Blue Dragon, you will take your own life. - 1 use
Serve Wine (Night) - Occupy a player by getting them drunk. The Butler will be notified if the target cannot be occupied, as they will refuse the wine. - Infinite uses
Concentrated Wine (Night) - Throw a party, occupying all players who visit you tonight. Bypasses occupy immunity. - 2 uses
Your objective is to defeat the Unseen or Cult, and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.
