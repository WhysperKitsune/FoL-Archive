What about a NK that is a Necromancer? You bring back dead people and you can talk to them at night and tell them to kill ppl. That would be a way to have ppl interact more when they are already dead.

Necromancer 1:

Passive skills:

Unholy Flesh: Immune to poison/bleed. (to be able to claim alch, stoneskin, why he doesnt die when saved by zombie)

Active Night Skills:
Resurrect Zombie (2days CD): Resurrect a dead player to your side with defensive skills.
Resurrect Skeleton (corpse must be Kill/Off) (2days CD): Resurrect a dead player to your side with offensive skills.

Zombie player night skills:
Guard Master: Guard your master and die if attacked.
Scary Scream: Occupy a Player at night.

Skeleton player skills:
Sword at Night: Kill a player, remove all immunties. Kill yourself if Off/Kill.

Necormancer Day Skill:
Dark Vision: Reveal Player Class. (3 days CD)

NK wins if Evil/BD are dead.
The Undead Chat would be a seperate like the Psychic to reveal the Necromancer early on.

Pros: Strong lategame, fun to play I would guess. Room to bring back The Priest (!) as counter to Necormancer.
Weak: Very bad early game, can‚Äôt kill anyone before others kill and even then only slow start.
