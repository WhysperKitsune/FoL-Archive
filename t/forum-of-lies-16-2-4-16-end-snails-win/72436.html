Gameplay

Days will be 3 IRL days long, or shorter if someone dies
Nights will be 1 IRL days long
Actions are done by messaging eevee/Luxy/Marcus
Unseen/Cult will talk through a special Forum Thread
Priest will speak with the dead through a separate Forum Thread
All classes will be used, and will be used as they are found in the Class Cards Thread 12
When you receive a card you will have 24 hours to reply, confirming you have received it

Executions

To accuse someone say “/accuse [player]” or “/vote [player]”
Once majority is reached to accuse someone, a trial will begin.
Either after 24 hours or once a trial reaches majority on pardon/execute, the trial will end.
After the trial ends, the King has 12 hours to choose to allow or veto the vote, if the king does not respond the vote is allowed
Executions end the Day

Flavor:

The snails down in Snailville liked Snailmas a lot. But the bugs that lived just north of Snailville, did not. The bugs hated Snailmas, the whole Snailmas season. Now please- don’t ask why. No one quite knows the reason. It could be their heads weren’t screwed on just right. Or maybe their little bug shoes were too tight. But I think, the most likely reason of all, was the long and brutal war that was waged mere generations ago between the two tribes. Despite all of the bugs’ valiant efforts to stem the slow, inexorable onslaught of the snail infantry, they were ultimately outmaneuvered and utterly decimated in an event now known in infamy among bugkind as Snailmageddon.

But to the snails down in Snailville, Snailmageddon is celebrated every year (though oddly not on the actual anniversary of Snailmageddon but rather on a day formerly set aside for a pagan celebration of the solstice) under a different name: Snailmas. With this year’s Snailmas fast approaching, the snails down in Snailville were slowly hurrying to get everything in order. If any bugs seeking revenge, vengeance, or revengeance were waiting for an opportunity to infiltrate the ranks of the snails and slay them all, well…

… now’s the time.
