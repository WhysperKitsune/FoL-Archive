Welcome to KT Cop Madness v1! It’s a h̶e̶l̶l̶s̶t̶o̶r̶m very fun game you will all enjoy!

Hosted by @KyoDaz and x.

This setup follows simple forum rules. I hope you are all aware of them by now.
The setup will start once 10 players have signed up. Backups, spectators and co-hosts are appreciated.

Gameplay

Days will be 48 hours long, or shorter if someone is lynched.
Nights will be 24 hours long or until everyone has submitted their actions.
Actions are done by messaging me. You may pre-queue night actions during the day.
Cops are unaware of their sanity.
Cop sanities are hidden upon flip. You will be able to determine if they were a Town Cop or an Assassin however.
Mafia do not have a meeting. If a meeting is formed it they will be able to communicate at all times.
Mafia win the game when their numbers are equal to that of the Town.
The Mafia do not have a faction kill.

Executions

To accuse someone say “/accuse [player]” or “/vote [player]”
Executions end the day.
Executions will be absolute majority (1/2 of alive players + 1) + plurality.
There must be a lynch everyday. If you can’t decide then random.org will. Any no-lynches will always result in a random town member dying.
If there is a tie between plurality lynches then one of them will be randomly lynched.

After The Night

You will be informed of feedback according to your action.

All
You slept peacefully. (No action)

Cop
Your target is a member of the Town!
Your target is a member of the Mafia!

Assassin
You successfully killed your target.
Your target is a member of the Mafia! You have joined a chat with them.
Player is an assassin and attempted to attack you last night. You have joined a chat with them.

KyoDaz’s Twist (KT)

KyoDaz’s Twist is a modifier where I put my own seasoning on the setup. It’s basically a modifier.

  1. Journals are not present however your investigations will be logged and shown upon death.
  2. Mafia Assassins are exempt from having journals but may leave behind an assassin’s note. (Sends a message to the other Assassin upon death provided they are not in a Mafia chat. The note will not be revealed to Town.)
  3. Assassin is an original role made by me. It’s very different as you all can tell. I didn’t want to go for boring Mafia Goons.
  4. This setup is originally balanced as one vanilla mafia v 4 different cop sanities. The setup has been doubled to fit more players in.

Role Cards

Cops

Town Cop

You are a Town Cop.
Every night you may choose to investigate a player. You will learn if they are Town or Mafia.
Defeat anyone who dares to oppose the Town.

Town Sane Cop

You are a Town Sane Cop.
Every night you may choose to investigate a player. You will learn if they are Town or Mafia.
Appears to self as Town Cop.
Defeat anyone who dares to oppose the Town.

Town Insane Cop

You are a Town Insane Cop.
Every night you may choose to investigate a player. You will instead learn their opposite alignment.
Appears to self as Town Cop.
Defeat anyone who dares to oppose the Town.

Town Paranoid Cop

You are a Town Paranoid Cop.
Every night you may choose to investigate a player. You will always learn that they are a member of the Mafia.
Appears to self as Town Cop.
Defeat anyone who dares to oppose the Town.

Town Naive Cop

You are a Town Naive Cop.
Every night you may choose to investigate a player. You will always learn that they are a member of the Town.
Appears to self as Town Cop.
Defeat anyone who dares to oppose the Town.

Assassin

Mafia Assassin

You are a Mafia Assassin.
You do not join the Mafia meeting. The Mafia in the meeting will be informed of your identity.
Every night you may choose to kill a player seperate from any other Mafia kills. If they are a member of the Mafia you will instead join their meeting.
Defeat anyone who dares to oppose the Mafia.
