Objective: Kiss 2 people (or maybe 3 I’m not the developer)

Ability 1 (Day): Entrance somebody to follow you to the lake (your base) where he/she will be occupied during the night. Since it is only the first night there, they will not kiss you yet! Only the second night will be person be ready to kiss. The player can also decide if they want to visit the mermaid again. If yes: you get a kiss. If no: the mermaid will place a curse on you. The mermaid also won’t be able to entrance some else that night, so she’ll be all alone.

Night Ability 1 (Kiss): once your target is ready to be kissed, they will get a blessing from the mermaid. The blessing gives the player night invincibility for 1 night (or more or less up to you guys) and now their votes count for 2 instead of 1, but still 3 for the king (lasts for 3 days). Also they will not be able to talk for 1 day because they are so stunned about the kiss.

Night ability 2 (Mermaids curse): If the player does not return for a second “date” with the mermaid, the mermaid will place a curse upon you, which will not allow you to talk for 2 days, and not allow you to write in your will for 2 days. Also since you broke the mermaid’s heart, you can never kiss her again this game. You jerk! (Curse is placed at the end of the night you didn’t want to go on the date)

“Ultimate ability”: Once 2 players have been kissed, you are night immune. You won the game. Still, you can impact the game. You have 2 spells to choose from now! (All chosen the day before).
1: make all players not be able to use a will for the following 2 nights, except the ones you have kissed
2: identify the roles of your lovers

(Each have a charge of 1)
So don’t get on her bad side if you know what’s good for ya
