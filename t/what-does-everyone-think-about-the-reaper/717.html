NO matter the number of players, I think there should be only 1 Reaper per game. It’s currently the strongest of the Neutral killers based on multiple factors.

  1. Target doesn’t show dead until 2 days later. This allows 2 days in hiding with no knowledge of an NK at all. If two reapers in a game, this gets crazy, as they can potentially kill 6 People before anyone knows that they exist!
  2. Reaped players are unable to be revived by the Priest. While this does help the Reaper’s end-goal, his ability to go undetected and even survive death by consuming souls means that this ability is literally a cherry on top of a 6 layer chocolate cake with icing. Also, for those unfamiliar with the game, this just makes playing the Priest frustrating. I’ve seen numerous players already confused why they couldn’t resurrect someone who had been Reaped.
