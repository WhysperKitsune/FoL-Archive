SFoLSomething: Conspiracy

Disclaimer:

Game’s Bastard, yo
Your class and faction card will not explicitly lie to you

Setup:

You will be sent a PM with a Class Card and a Faction Card
Your class card will be a Blue Dragon Class, probably
The “Good Guys” faction is still called the Blue Dragon

Mechanic: Splinter

Splinter refers to two separate things:
A Splinter Class is a player with multiple classes, you will not start the game as a Splinter Class
A Splinter Faction is a faction that is created by a member (or several members) of a pre-existing faction

Mechanic: Ever-Changing Loyalty

A player isn’t necessarily only a member of one faction

Mechanic: Testing the Waters

You can do things at night and during the day, those things aren’t necessarily the actions your class card entails, there are limits, but it is up to you to find them

Mechanic: Ideas

Every night, between 3 and 5 players will have an idea about something or someone, at least 1 idea each night is true and at least 1 is false

Mechanic: Secrets never told

A player’s faction is never revealed upon death

Game End:

The game ends when every living player belongs to the same faction, at which point some, most or all of the members of that faction will win

Note:

You must join the Conspiracy Discord server and remain a member for the duration of the game -> https://discord.gg/4Zyh2vw
