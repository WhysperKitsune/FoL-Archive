Damafaud February 19, 2018, 4:11pm #1

A normal peaceful day. There wasn’t anything special. No heroes triumphing over evil. No sudden discovery of a new planet. No major breakthrough in technology. No explosion of a failed inventions. Nothing.

And yet, through a series of unlikely coincidence, it happened.

A collision, a twist of fate.

The creation of a new realm, which later would be called Nexus Hub.

A realm that was an intersection between four other realms. Velonia, the Arcana Realm to the north. Grand Peninsula, the Steampunk Realm to the West. Callinety Falls, the Meta Realm in the South. Elouis-12, the Techno Realm in the east.

Scholars, scientists, philosophers, inventors, all sought the answer yo one question: What caused the creation of Nexus Hub?

Even if they won’t believe it, it was just a coincidence.

The roleplayers will play the characters from one of the four respective realms; Arcana, Steampunk, Meta, and Techno. Connecting the four realms, Nexus Hub, is a mix of vegetations, geography, laws, flora and fauna from all four realms.

Travel through the newly birthed realm to discover pristine landscape untouched by civilised creatures. Or your characters might be more interested to travel another realms? Gain acquaintances from different realms, discover mysteries of nature, and explore all the five realms can offer.

Will you?

Show interest by posting in this thread! If there is a lack of supporters, our GM Team will work harder to create a better concept in the future!
