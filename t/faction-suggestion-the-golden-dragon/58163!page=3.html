psychoneirik April 16, 2018, 6:42pm #56

Okay, thank you for the feedback, here are some changes I made:

  • I changed the mortician’s useless ability (oops).
  • I completely changed the executioner and hangman, there is no jailing in the Golden Dragon anymore. Made the hangman a “risky” convert on purpose, can be really powerful but if the unseen doesn’t play their cards right it will be obvious there is a hangman in the game.
  • I removed political pressure from the Elder and the Old Man and added another “social” ability that doesn’t involve vote manipulation.

Stuff Im still thinking about:

  • Im not really sure how to change the Maid. A part of me really wants an ability that can hear night conversations, but I agree it could be really underpowered since evils will most likely just don’t talk at night if they know there is a risk of a Maid listening. Also, the ability to read whispers will still make her easy to confirm since there is no other class that can read whispers except maybe Possessor. One change I have in mind is to give her an ability that notifies the maid if a player speaks at night, but won’t reveal their messages.
  • I really want to make the Old Man able to choose the class he will reveal his accuser as, but there is no way to do that without removing one of his day abilities which I believe are more important.
