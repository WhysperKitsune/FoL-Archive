[Class Sugesstion] Director (Neutral Offensive)

Class/Lore/Fanfic
Ashe September 19, 2017, 2:19am #1

I love the Control Freak in Camp Cadaver, so I thought I’d make a class based on that.

Objective: Cause three deaths. (This can be redirecting or bait and switching a killer into a different person, or an execution that gained majority due to your personal vote and/or your micromanage vote, but not a vote that would have successfully executed regardless of your votes)

Passive - Focused
Immune to Occupation.

Day Ability - Micromanage (2 uses)
Force a player to vote execute or pardon on a current vote. They will be locked into this choice (meaning they can’t change to a different option).

Night Ability 1 - Redirect (Infinite uses)
Control a player, causing them to target another with their chosen night ability. There will be no effect if the ability they were using can’t be used on that target, or they didn’t use an ability.

Night Ability 2 - Bait and Switch (2 uses)
All players who visit you tonight will have their chosen night ability redirected to a target player.
