Blue Dragon Offensive

Passive: Skeleton Key - Cannot be prevented from targeting players

Day Ability: Break in - Target player will be occupied bypassing immunity’s if they attempt to use a limited use ability tonight (2 use)

Night Ability 1: Reinforce - Prevent visits to target player. You will know if anyone tries. Cannot target the same player twice in a row (Unlimited)
Night Ability 2: Deconstruct - Remove all of target players immunities, they will be unhealable tonight. Anyone attempting to visit them will be guaranteed to succeed ignoring prevention, occupy and redirection. (3 uses)

Night Ability 2: Pick lock - Remove the target’s immunity’s. Visits to target player cannot be prevented. Unseen trying to visit target player won’t count as visiting and cannot be prevented, occupied or redirected (3 uses)
