The Occultist / The Curator v1.1

The man walked through the gallery feeling the objects at his hands. He knew that they had history, memories, and feelings all locked within. The world was more than what we saw and the items he held were proof of that. While others couldn’t feel the emotions coming off of an old dagger, a talisman or book, he could and every day it was his strength and his burden. Hearing the trouble in Castle Aldiart, the Occultist found some of his favourite items and went into the castle, for he alone could find the evil lurking within.

The Occultist - Blue Dragon Social

Passives
None

Day Abilities
Aura Reading (2 Uses)
For the rest of the day, when a player is Pardoned or Executed after being on trial, you will learn how many Unseen, Cult and Neutral players Pardoned them (if they were Executed) or Executed them (if they were Pardoned). You will not learn who.

Scrying (1 Use)
Tonight you will be able to hear the messages of the Unseen and Cult to each other. Their names and actions will be hidden. The Unseen and Cult will be given a message that they are being Scryed Upon by an Occultist.

Night Abilities
Psychometry (Unlimited)
Target a dead or living player other than a King. You see the results of their final night’s actions if they are dead or die this night. If targeting a body that was switched with a Possessor you will instead become prevented.

Prognostication (2 Uses)
You become immune to death tonight.

Converts into…

The Curator - Unseen Social

Passives
None

Day Abilities
Aura Sense (1 Use)
For the rest of the day, when a player is Pardoned or Executed after being on trial, you will learn which Pardons are Neutral players (if they were Executed) and which Executions are Neutral players (if they were Pardoned).

Shared Seance (2 Uses)
Today all Unseen members will be able to whisper each other without their whispers being seen by others.

Night Abilities
Psychometry (Unlimited)
Target a dead or living player other than a King. You see the results of their final night’s actions if they are dead or die this night. If targeting a body that was switched with a Possessor you will instead become prevented.

Ghastly Scrying (1 Use)
Tonight and tomorrow you will be able to hear messages between a target and whoever they talk to or whisper. You cannot target the King.

The Occultist fills a Social Role with a splash of Investigation with their Psychometry and Aura Reading abilities. They gain only snippets of information, though with clever thinking one can easily start to narrow down where the Unseen, Cult and Neutrals are located. Through their Scrying they can also see the Unseen and Cult night chats, or force them to talk less openly once they are warned about a scryer. This allows them to have a unique source of information that many other classes are not privy to.

The existence of the Curator also allows the Unseen to talk during the day using Shared Seance, letting them plan new strategies during a time when many people wouldn’t think they are capable. This allows them to escape not being able to plan due to the Prince and their Jail, or even set up fake claims during the day on the fly.

Due to the Occultist existing, the Scum factions must play smart with executions, though due to the Occultist having no provable abilities, it grants Scum another role to claim and hide in. The presence of the Occultist’s Prognostication can explain away the lack of dying from Masterminds or for classes like Alchemists, Scorned and Night Killers.

Ultimately while the role has some power it provides more claimspace for Scum players and provides Blue Dragon with a strange, yet usable source of information.

Thank you for your time and any comments! I mostly do this for myself as a creative outlet but figured its fun to post them up anyway.
