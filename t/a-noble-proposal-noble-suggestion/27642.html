A Noble Proposal [Noble suggestion]

Feedback/Suggestions
Longshot405 December 13, 2017, 10:21pm #1

Pondering on it a while, I came up with an idea for how to make a Noble useful and interesting enough to be brought back. The aim is to add some depth and counter play to discovering a king’s allegiance. May be a bit too much, but I’ll let you all take a look and decide for yourselves:

Noble:

  • Passive: Royal Blood

  • New Passive: Trusted Successor
    If you become a king, gain 2 uses of the Allies ability.

  • Day Ability: Overthrow
    Put the king on trial and attempt to become the new king. If the king is executed and was a good king, guards will kill you at night. Cannot be used the first 3 days (1 charge).

  • Night Ability 1: Royal Informants
    Discover the king’s actions tonight (3 charges).

  • Night Ability 2: Rig the Trial
    Tomorrow, you may vote an additional time during a trial including your own. This vote will appear as anonymous (Infinite uses).

Reasoning and thought process:

Trusted Successor

Normally, deciding who becomes king is based on which class is least important when trying to become the next king. This ability aims to change that a little. Now there will be a class that is an appealing choice for the next king, and also gives evil/neutral classes an interesting class claim when trying to become the next king. It is independent from the Noble’s Overthrow ability so as to not encourage using Overthrow too hastily.

Overthrow

Makes an announcement in the chat box saying ‘A Noble is attempting to overthrow the King!’ This skips the process of voting up the king and immediately puts them on trial. This might be the most imbalanced part of the ability. Guards killing the Noble at night if a good king is executed is a means of adding risk to the ability.

Alternative Suggestion: Put the king on trial and attempt to become the new king. If you fail to execute the king or if the king is a good king, guards will kill you. Cannot be used the first 3 days (1 charge).

^ Increased risk to the Noble, should the king be pardoned.

Royal Informants

Helps the noble determine if the king has discovered evil players or taking suspicious action with their guards. Given limited charges since it is a powerful ability.

Rig the Trial

A new default night ability for the Noble, when Royal Informants is out of charges. When a trial happens the next day, a second pair of voting options appears beneath the others with a title ‘Anonymous Vote.’ Anonymous votes will appear at the end of the list of player’s votes during a trial saying ‘An anonymous vote was made for Execution.’

Might be easier to make this a day ability instead of night, but Noble would still need one more night ability.

Alternative Suggestion: Rig the Trial instead reduces the number of necessary votes to start a trial tomorrow by 1.

Please, give this a look over and let me know what you think
