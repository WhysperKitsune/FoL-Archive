Welcome to SFoL 38 - Abyss

Hosted by Marluxion!

Abyss?

The main addition to this Special Forum of Lies is a new scum faction. The Abyss.

These twisted mind flayers have risen from the shadowy depths and seek to claim Adiart for their own vile purposes. Our scouts have displayed an abnormal fear upon witnessing such creatures…

Bending the minds of mortal men and slaying our kind without remorse… Some even whisper that they are trying to awaken some kind of Dark God that sleeps beneath the tides…

Their leader was dubbed ‘The Dreamer’ by one particularly… eccentric… scout. We have reports that he can steal the faces of the men he tortures… So… for all we know, any one of us could be one of these twisted creatures.

Rules

Forum of Lies

Forum of Lies

Hosting Forum of Lies, derived from "Throne of Lies" online social deduction game on Steam @ discord.gg/tol

Gameplay

Days will be 3 IRL days long, or shorter if someone dies
Nights will be 1 IRL day long
Actions are done by messaging me
Unseen/Cult will talk through a Forum PM
Priest will speak with the dead through a separate Forum PM Chain
All classes will be used, and will be used as they are found in the Class Cards Thread
When you receive a card you will have 24 hours to reply, confirming you have received it

Executions

To accuse someone say “/accuse [player]” or “/vote [player]”
Once a player reaches a majority of the accusations, they are put on trial.
Trials last 24 hours (ended early if Majority is reached on execute or pardon (or the king decides their fate))
Trials do not extend the day.

Journals

Message me your entire journal, the last draft I receive before you die shall be the one displayed

To Join

To join, simply express interest in joining by saying something like “I want to join” or “/join” or “/in” or “sign me up, coach” or whatever you want

Starting Abyss Members

The Dreamer

The Dreamer

Abyss Special
Unearthly (Passive) - You are immune to occupation and target changing. You will be informed if someone attempts to occupy you.
Endless Dream (Passive) - If you die, all living blue dragon will become afraid.
Facestealer (Day) - Choose a class. You will appear as this class to any investigations. This effect lasts until you use this ability again. - Infinite uses

Cycle of Madness (Passive) - If you die, the next non-Dreamer Abyss in line will become The Devout. If there exist no other non-Dreamer Abyssians, the next successful convert will instead become The Devout.

Sacrifice (Night) - Kill a player. - Infinite uses

Your objective is to defeat the Blue Dragon and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.

  • Members of the Abyss CAN inflict other Abyssians with fear.

  • The Faction cap for the Abyss is 3

  • You aren’t notified if you are afraid. Only certain special investigative abilities can find that out.
