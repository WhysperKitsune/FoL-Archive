1.0.15 Patch Notes

Official Announcements
Ellie January 4, 2018, 10:42am #1

General:

  • Noble and Aristocrat have returned.
  • Improved the wording of occupy immune passives to include immunity to target changing.
  • Players will no longer receive messages saying someone tried to occupy them or change their target if it was blocked by the Court Wizard’s barrier.
  • Added the fact that someone could be protected by the Court Wizard’s barrier to the reasons an ability failed
  • Added King to the list of classes newer players can’t get.

Apostle:

  • Reworked to behave like the new Noble.
  • Day Ability Cult Pressure - Force a player to vote for another and prevent further voting from them. 2 uses
  • Day Ability Cult Numbers - Your vote for execution or pardon will count for two votes. 2 uses
  • Night Ability Cult Spy - You will see all whispers to and from target player. Cannot target King. 3 uses
  • Night Ability Snoop - Read a player’s journal. 2 uses. (This has been improved from the Marshal’s version. There are now two journal buttons on the right that will display the snooped players’ journals).

Cult Leader:

  • Starting Cult Leader starts with 3 uses of Eradicate.

Herbalist:

  • Defile now properly works on reaped players.

Invoker:

  • New passive Fanatic - Immune to occupation at night.
  • Chains of Corax now has 3 uses.

Marshal:

  • Snoop and Listen In removed.
  • New Night Ability Probe - Determine a player’s class if they are part of the Blue Dragon.

Noble & Aristocrat:

  • Social Class
  • Day Ability Political Pressure - Force a player to vote for another and prevent further voting from them. 2 uses
  • Day Ability Noble Twin - Your vote for execution or pardon will count for two votes. 2 uses
  • Night Ability Maid Spy - You will see all whispers to and from target player. Cannot target King. Infinite uses
  • Night Ability Snoop - Read a player’s journal. 2 uses. (This has been improved from the Marshal’s version. There are now two journal buttons on the right that will display the snooped players’ journals).

Psychic:

  • Now unique.

Ritualist:

  • Brotherhood now has 2 uses.

Seeker:

  • Gaze now has 3 uses.
