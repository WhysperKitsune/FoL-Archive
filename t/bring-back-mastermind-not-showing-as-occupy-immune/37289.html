I’m starting to feel that the Butler should flatly never get feedback on whether his target was occupied successfully or not. He’s not supposed to be an Investigative class.

…however, that might weaken him too much, so we might also want to consider removing occupy immunity from some classes, or weakening it slightly (eg. a Mastermind is only Occupy Immune when converting, but can be occupied when using Foresight - although really, if you’re a Mastermind and you’re being occupied repeatedly, you’re already screwed.)

To be honest… how necessary is the Mastermind’s occupy immunity in the first place? It would lead to occasional feelbad moments where you’re occupied N1, but unless you’re completely outed it’d probably only slow you down for one night. The Assassin’s lack of Occupy Immunity doesn’t seem to be catastrophic (and rarely gets them caught).

The problem is that giving Occupy Immunity to almost everyone important risked making the Butler pointless… unless you gave the Butler the ability to see occupy immunity, which is actually often worse for evils than missing a night. As he is now, just flatly removing occupy immunity from the Mastermind would probably be a buff, especially if paired with removing all feedback from occupies.

I mean occasionally an obsessive Butler will occupy you from the start despite having no evidence or reason to do so, but a jailor could do that anyway, and it doesn’t really happen that often. Why does Throne of Lies give Occupy Immunity to so many people, basically? The lack of occupy immunity only occasionally seems to be a problem for the Cult Leader or Reaper.
