Witch Hunter . (Neutral Investigative)

Passive
Determined : The Witch hunter is driven to route out the witch and see her burnt , he cannot be occupied .

Night Ability 1 : WitchHunt : Discover if someone is possibly a witch ( possible witchs are female)

Night Ability 2 : Burn the Witch (3 uses ) Kill someone you believe to be a witch , if the target is a witch this bypasses night immunity .

Win condition : See the witch Dead .

Notes : Each charachter would now in addition to everything else have a gender . If a game starts with a witchhunter , 1 random female charachter will in addition to their normal class , be a witch.
Witchs are night immune to death and receive a notice at the start of the game that they are also a witch .
