Now why did we choose the things we did? Well, there were a few reasons. First, we outright said we would not use lunatic, as the demon has to know the target that the lunatic chooses, but that makes things more difficult when we are in a forum setup and do not have a set night order in which everybody goes, as people are on at different times.

We didn’t want no outsiders in the game (this was when it was only 13 people in-game), so we added godfather, and then when zone re-inned, we added another outsider to the list. We decided to put goon in at first because people wouldn’t really trust it, and then added tinker once zone came in.

Minstrel was a no, with 3 minions in game, having everyone be drunk potentially 3 times would be a huge pain and drag the game on for a while. Same reasoning with zombuul, we knew y’all would get burned out if the game lasted for a really long time.

We put DA in to sow doubt into any pacifist saves, and potentially make the pacifist misclear someone they think they saved from their ability

We decided to add both Exorcist/Courtier to sow doubt into people trying to metagame, and exorcist isn’t that great against Po anyways, so we didn’t feel bad including it.
