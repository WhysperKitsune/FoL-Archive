The Agent (Blue Dragon Social)

DAY ABILITIES:
Watchful Eye
See who the Unseen/ Cult visit. Cannot be used until Night 2.
(3 uses)

Hearing Ear
Listen in on the Unseen/Cult’s conversation tonight.
(2 uses)

NIGHT ABILITIES:
Spy
Hear whispers to and from a certain player. Cannot target the king.
(Infinite Uses)

Follow
You will be able to see what happen to your player this night and the next day.
(Infinite Uses)

Goal:BD

The Traitor (Unseen Social)

DAY ABILITIES:
Expose
Reveal to the Unseen who the Prince jails tonight.
(3 uses)

Listen In
You will be able to hear the Prince’s conversation tonight.
(2 uses)

NIGHT ABILITIES
Silence Prevent a player from speaking tommorow.
(Infinte Uses)

Goal : Unseen

The Priest (BD Social)

Priest: (Magic) (Unique)

NIGHT ABILITIES:
Channeling
Choose a dead Blue Dragon. They will be able to speak the following day.
(4 uses)

Blessing
Choose a dead player and find out how they died.
(Infinite Uses)

Goal : BD

Soulcatcher - Unseen Social

PASSIVES:
Dark Words
While the Soulcatacher is alive , dead Unseen members can talk to living ones.

NIGHT ABILITIES:
Catch Soul
Choose a player. If they die , they cannot talk to a Psychic, or be revived by a Physician.
(3 uses)

False Truth
Choose a dead player. You can pretend to speak as them if you right-click their name before you type your message the following day.
(4 uses)

Goal:Unseen

Archer (BD Killer)

DAY ABILITIES:
Sharpshot
Kill a player in the day. If they are Blue Dragon , you will commit suicide this night.
(Infinite Uses)

NIGHT ABILITIES:
Last Breath
Choose a player. When you die , they will be attacked.
(Infinite Uses)
