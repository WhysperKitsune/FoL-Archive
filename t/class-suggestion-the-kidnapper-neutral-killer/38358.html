Hi Maximus,
I’ve been thinking a bit about this class but I just haven’t had the chance to update this page properly.
One idea I’ve been messing with is a completely different approach to the occupation game that takes ideas from Sorc and sets the Kidnapper further apart from Reaper.
Rather than having a day ability to bypass occupy immunity, beginning on night 3 or 4 Kidnap is upgraded to be able to bypass night immunity, including on the King. This combined with the possibility of an In The Shadows-like passive makes the Kidnapper a much more stealth-oriented NK that becomes more powerful the longer he’s left alive (going so far as to kill death immunes), while still retaining the weakness of the delayed/interruptable kill.
