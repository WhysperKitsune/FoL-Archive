FadeBlade:

this means everyone will know there are no royals alive making knowing what classes alive a lot easyer.

Hmm, I realized I worded it awkwardly – it’s not that the royals are necessarily dead, it’s that they did not step up to become King. To become King as a Royal is actually an ability to click called “Royal Blood”. This trumps all slots and they become King. We haven’t decided if we will announce that a royal stepped up (because a royal could be quiet to see who wants to be king, and THEN trump it, as if they won the vote). There’s a few mechanics to test during Alpha. But the TL;DR is that royals can OPTIONALLY step up at any time when the king is dead, but they don’t have to, and can wait to use it.

FadeBlade:

and how quick is the voting for new kings anyway?

We don’t have a determined time yet, but we’re taking suggestions. The thing that we want to avoid is voting TOO OFTEN, which could lead to boredom. Perhaps the king’s voting could take place before night instead of the morning to allow for extra conversation … this part isn’t coded in yet, so now is a good time to hear your voice.
