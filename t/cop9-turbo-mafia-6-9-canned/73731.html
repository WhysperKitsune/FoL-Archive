Gameplay

Days will be 15 minutes long, nights will be 5 minutes long.
Mafia will have a day and night chat.
Mafia will have a factional kill, it is not assigned.
The cop receives the feedback ‘Not Suspicious’ or ‘Your target is a member of the Mafia!’
Lynches are majority and plurality. No lynch is not an option.

Roles

Town Citizen

You are a citizen of the town! You have no night action, however, you still have your vote and your intellect!
You win when the Mafia is eliminated.

Town Cop

You are the town cop! Each night you may check a player, determining whether they are a member of the mafia or not.
You win when the Mafia is eliminated.

Mafia Goon

You are a member of the Mafia! You have a day and night chat with your partner. Each night you and your partner may vote on who to kill.
You win when the mafia equals or outnumbers the town.
