Happy Easter and Passover Everyone!

It’s time for another contest! This one is for the more artsy people out there, but everyone has a chance to win. I present to you…

https://i.imgur.com/wANWNia.png

For the month of April, in this thread, post your ORIGINAL death note illustrations. May Adiart see the talent of our fine courtiers.

Prizes:
5 x 40.00 USD Steam Gift Cards (yes, TWO 20.00 USD Steam Gift Cards) to the top 5 artists of the contest.

You will be judged based on creativity and content. Be original! The judges will be announced later this month.

Rules:

  1. All content must follow the game & forum guidelines.
  2. No discrimination.
  3. You may post as many deathnotes as you like, but you can only win once. Quality > Quantity.
  4. Don’t gamethrow by focusing ONLY on the deathnote in game. Make sure you help your team.
  5. All death notes drawn using automated software will be ineligible for prizes.

Bonus points!
This contest will involve 3 potential themes that you can follow to be judged better. While these are not mandatory for your entry, relevance will increase your chances of winning. The themes are:

  1. Fool’s Day - The Fool loves this day of levity. What hilarious shenanigans is he preparing for the court?
  2. Springtime - The flowers bloom and the birds sing in the courtyard of Castle Adiart while the screams of it’s denizens echo. Spring is here!
  3. The Mithras Bunny - In our glorious Kingdom, the Mithras Bunny gives out darkness and despair in the form of chocolately goodness.

Don’t forget to use the new colors that were recently added. These will really make your entry stand out!

If you have any questions, message me @cbman on Discord or on the forums.

Frequently Asked Questions:

  1. When does the contest end?

April 30th, judging will take 5 days after the end.
