[Class Suggestion] The Dishonored (Cult)

Class/Lore/Fanfic
Foxx March 11, 2018, 12:01am #1

Class: The Dishonored (Cult) (Offensive)

Backstory: An impeached royal that has fallen into insanity, their grudge against the Blue dragons grew and their insanity lead them to warship mithras.

Royal Blood: Yes

Converts from: Royal bloods (Noble still converts to Apostle)

Day ability: Royal Threat (x2) - Make someone unable to talk for the rest of the day, if they do talk they will be killed from an arrow, the message"You have been threatened by a fallen royal" will appear when this happens. To prevent accidentally talking when this is used on the player, they are timed out for talking 5 seconds, this gives the CL 1 more use of eradicate.

Day ability: Kingdom Heist (x1) - Restock 1 of every person in the cults night abilities (For example, seeker would get +1 gaze and +1 identify) this does not work for the cult leader’s abilities.

Night ability: Royal sacrifice (x3) - Sacrifice blood to mithras to gain night immunity for 1 - 2 members of the cult (1 member if there are 2 members of the cult, 2 members if there are 3)

Night ability: Spiritual protection (x4) - Summon demon spirits to make anybody who visits you bleed, the person bleeding will die in 2 nights, they will get the notification they would get if a hunter sent wolves after them.
