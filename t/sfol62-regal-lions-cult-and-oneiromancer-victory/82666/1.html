The Castle of Regalia. A magnificent castle, one surrounded in riches and grandeur. The inhabitants of the castle all live in relative harmony, as even the commonfolk have enough money to stay out of poverty. The castle is also known for quite a few popular individuals, ones who have extraordinary powers and talents. They all band together under the banner of the Regal Lions. But it just isn’t enough for some, or that’s what the Lord’s wife said… before she was found dead in her quarters. A well known topic amongst the citizens of Castle Regalia is the revolutionaries that once plagued Castle Adiart, an ally of the Lions. However, the Blue Dragon of Adiart managed to defeat these revolutionaries… or so was thought until the heinous act of regicide was committed. It’s clear that the Unseen and the Cult are not completely gone… in blood on the wall of the quarters spells “Your riches and your talents cover up the true corruption within this castle. We will liberate this castle and spread your coin across those who deserve it!” Can the Regal Lions silence these familiar foes, or will they consume the castle within their iron fists? Only time will tell…

Follow the https://forum.imperium42.com/t/global-forum-discord-game-meta-rules-please-read-first/244, and the https://forum.imperium42.com/t/official-forum-mafia-and-forum-of-lies-rules/78926.

Gameplay

  • Days are 48 hours long or until an execution ends it, Nights last 24 hours. The first day is an exception, it lasts only 24 hours and has no execution.
  • You will be provided a DM with your classcard by the host, send your actions there.
  • Unseen/Cult will talk through a special Discord server or forum message.
  • Commoners, Serfs, and Commoner!Apostles will talk with their guests in a separate forum message.
  • Those in The Oneiromancer’s Dream Sphere will talk in a separate forum message.
  • If the Lord uses Trial By Council, this will be publically announced.
  • Alts will be allowed this game, however I must be notified of your identity before the game starts and you must apply through the alt mod, aka DatBird.

Executions

  • To accuse someone say “/vote [player]”. Please make the life of the hosts easier by bolding it (by putting ** ** around it)
  • Should the votes reach the majority, that player will be executed and the day will end.
  • When the day ends, the player with the most votes will be executed.
  • Voting “/no execution” is an option.

Picking a New Lord

  • If there are less than 4 players a new Lord will not be selected.
  • When the Lord dies a 12 hour period starts. In this, players may vote in their class PM for who they want to become the next Lord.
  • Whoever receives the most votes in this period will be crowned Lord. If votes are tied, a Lord election will be held the next day to determine it.

Journals

DM the host what you want on your journal. When you die, the last update to your journal will be revealed to everyone. It has a character limit of 1000 characters.
