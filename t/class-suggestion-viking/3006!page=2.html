Guess I’ll explain why I made him “underpowered”.
Basically when I used to play ToS, the werewolf would be one of the worst roles to get. But he was scary to go up against too. Him being the only NK to not have night immunity, yet being able to pierce it was great imo.

BMG tried fixing the Werewolf problem by making him night immune, but that just meant it’s almost impossible to go up against.
So here’s my take on it.

The Viking can only pierce night immunity with his passive. It’s very rare you’ll end up needing to pierce night immunity. Only when you against Possessor, yet then you’ll both die unless you’re on a cooldown.

His semi immunity means he can be safe the days he’s not rampaging. I might make rampage give him some form of immunity too, but basically he’s not supposed to be too OP.
