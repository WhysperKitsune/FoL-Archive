[Class Suggestion] The Leper

Class/Lore/Fanfic
Livicus February 5, 2018, 6:24am #1

So, we’ve been without a Neutral Offensive class for, forever now.

I have a proposal, which is somewhat of a merge of Pestilence and Arsonist in ToS, but has different abilities, different playstyles, and a different win effect.

So here is the outcast peasant, the Leper! He has leprosy.

The Leper

Neutral Offensive

Goal: Survive to infect all players.

Passives

Diseased:
Anyone you visit will become infected. Anyone who visits an infected player becomes infected. Anyone who visits you will become infected.

Epidemic:
When everyone is infected, the next day nobody may talk, vote, or use abilities.

Abilities

Infect: (N) (oo)
Visit someone and infect them.

Plague House: (N) (3)
Prevent all visits to you tonight. If you are bleeding or poisoned, cure yourself.

Please leave your feedback, I would really like to see an interesting Neutral Offensive join the game.
