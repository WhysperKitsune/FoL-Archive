also i felt like #forum-matches needed more non-fol/mafia games, so here is one

How 2play:
You will be put in a fight with up to 3 other players, each player will have 1000 HO
each round fighters must send a move they wish to do
moves can be basically anything, however more complex moves are harder to pull off
after i receive all the moves they will be announced and it will be determined if they were successful
success is done by RNG, I decide the rolls and required results but not the outcome of the moves
Moves can do stuff they may even inflict relevant status ailments
if you lose all your HP, you are KO’ed
if you take considerably more damage than your remaining HP you will die, this means you’re out of the league

The league:
this will happen, you can drop in or out at any time, and you get points depending how you fared in each fight

The spirit league:
if you’re dead, your ghost can fight other dead people in the spirit league, you can’t kill the dead though

The aim of the game:
The game has no set end point, but you want to be the league champion, right?

How battles are set up:
ou can request fights, if your target says yes then you fight, also there will be random ones occasionally

Points:
If you KO a player you get 1 point
If you Kill a player you get 2 points
If you win a match you get 3 points
