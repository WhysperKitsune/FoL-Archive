Time Reaper

Every night, either steal an hour from someone, if you steal 2 hours from someone they die, or lose 3 hours to kill anyone who you’ve stolen an hour from.

If you die but have at least one hour, you will prevent yourself from taking action, lose an hour, and be brought back to life. You start with one hour.

Kill off all members of the White Primrose and Yellow Poppy.

Sandbox Turbo Thread - Mass Class Thread

O24

Candlemaker

Every night you may kill someone.
The candle’s size starts at 6. If a candle reaches past size 12, the candlemaker will start a new candle on size one. If a candle reaches below size 0, the new candle will start on size 12.
You are informed of the candle’s size on each daystart.

If the victim is Yellow Poppy, the candle rises by 1.
If the victim is White Primrose, the candle rises by 2.
If the victim is Orange Lily, the candle melts by 3.
The candlemaker gets an extra life if a candle reaches size 9.
The candlemaker dies if a candle reaches 3.

Bring your candle’s size to exactly 12 or be one of the last two players standing.

You may decide to eat someone during the night and guess their role.

If correct… strongman the player and kill them. You gain immunity to the lynch tomorrow.
If wrong… you are mod revealed as the Werewolf.

Ensure you are one of the last two players standing.
