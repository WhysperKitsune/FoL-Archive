[Class Rework Suggestion] Alchemist/Physician

Feedback/Suggestions
anon29151597 July 5, 2018, 4:25pm #1

I have seen a lot of discussions about the power of Alchemist and Physician lately so I decided to post my suggestion here.

This rework idea is an attempt to make Physician more enjoyable and engaging to play without making it a weaker copy of the Alchemist. Below are the basic concepts that I had in mind:

The Physician

Type: Support;
Faction: BD;
Is Unique?: No.

Passive Abilities:
‘Remedy Studies’ - You are immune to Nightshade poison and bleeding.

Day Abilities:
‘Inoculate’ [1] charge - Make a player permanently immune to Nightshade and bleeding. Does not cure it.
‘Quarantine’ [2] charges - You will be immune to death tonight.

Night Abilities:
‘Heal’ unlimited charges - Heal a player, preventing them from dying tonight, and cure their poison/bleeding.
‘Malpractice’ [1] charge - Poison a target player with a lethal dosage, instantly killing them. You can not target The King with this ability.

Notes: I would also propose to make Physician a guaranteed BD class would be a good change. That way you do not have to rely on Alchemist (who is inherently neutral) if they spawned as the only healer in game.

The Alchemist

Type: Support;
Faction: Neutral;
Is Unique?: No.

Passive Abilities:
‘Coagulate’ - You are immune to Nightshade poison and bleeding.

Day Abilities:
‘Stoneskin Potion’ [3] Charges - You will be immune to death tonight.
‘Contaminate’ [2] Charges - The first person that visits you tonight will become poisoned with Nightshade/will start Bleeding (depending on the game type) at the start of the next day. They will be notified and will die in 2 nights unless healed.

Night Abilities:
‘Heal’ unlimited charges - Heal a player, preventing them from dying tonight, and cure their poison/bleeding.
‘Emerald Potion’ [2] Charges - Assist your target for the night, making their attacks bypass Death Immunity. You can not target The King with this ability.

Notes: The notification for ‘Contaminate’ would be identical to the notifications you get when you are poisoned/bled by an Assassin or Cult Leader (to avoid making the Alchemist instantly confirmable).

All and any feedback is appreciated.
