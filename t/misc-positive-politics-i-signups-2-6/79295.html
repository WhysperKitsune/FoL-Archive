Experiment: Positivity Modifier

This is an experimental modifier. It may be subject to change during the game, after the game or prior to the game.

The positivity modifier is a modifier that restricts you from voting for the lynch if what you have done is deemed as not positive or against the community rules.
This will be announced publically and you will not count towards majority.
Insulting of the person will result in immediate restriction.
Community rules will be more strict than normal here.

Experiment: Public Votecount Modifier

This is an experimental modifier. It may be subject to change during the game, after the game or prior to the game.

The first vote in this thread will be a wikiposted votecount. You are allowed to edit it.
Editting another player’s vote is strictly prohibited (provided you aren’t fixing it). You are only allowed to edit your own and the numbers.

Executions

A tie in plurality votes will result in a coin toss.
To accuse someone say “/accuse [player]” or “/vote [player]”
Please make sure you put votes on a seperate post.
Executions do not end the day as the day is infinite.
Executions will be majority + plurality.
