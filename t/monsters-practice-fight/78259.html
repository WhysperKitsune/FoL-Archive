Combat Menu -
FIGHT - Must be specified when performing an attack. Attack your opponent.
ACT - Perform an ACT against your opponent, or to yourself or a friend. Actions will be developed as you learn them. You only know the following actions at the moment:
CHECK - Counts as an ACTion. Learn about your enemy: ATK, DEF, HP, a short description, and (in the case of a Human) LV .
DEFEND - Counts as an ACTion. Defending will increase your own DEF to 50%, thus cutting all damage taking in half. Adding to this, you also gain Mana.
ITEM - Use an item. This can include consumables, or changing weapons or armours.
MERCY - Show your enemy MERCY, sparing them.

Turn order will be determined, in the order of the lowest EXP to highest, before switching to the other team. Traditionally, in a battle between Humans versus Monsters, the Humans side will go first, but there are exceptions.
If players have the same EXP, order will be randomised.
