All Neutral Reworks from the Master Rework of DOOM

NOTE: All Neutrals who complete their goals become convert immune. i.e. Scorned, Merchant, Thief, etc.

Converted Neutrals are purposefully stronger than Blue Dragon converts because Neutrals do not have to fight against the Unseen, but can choose to for a certain duration.

All Unseen share the same win-condition: to beat the Blue Dragon and any threatening Neutrals.

Mercenary

(Neutral Support)

(Converts to Sellsword)

Goal: Keep your target alive until the end of the game.

Passive: Contracted: If your target dies or you have no target by night 3, you will commit suicide. You can not accuse your target and must always vote to pardon them.

Day Ability: Take Contract: Target player becomes your contract. Only usable if your previous target is dead. Unlimited uses.

Night Ability: Stand Guard: Prevent all visitors to your target. Your target will not be notified. 4 uses.

Night Ability: Rebound: Same as now. 1 use.

Forsaken

(Unseen Social)

(Converted from Scorned)

Passive: Vengeance: You keep your targets from when you were a Scorned. If one of your original targets is executed, you gain unlimited uses of Frame.

Passive: Marginalized: You do not count towards the Unseen maximum limit and do not become the Assassin by default.

Apothecary

(Unseen Killer)

(Converted from Alchemist)

Passive: Coagulate: Immune to bleeding and poison.

Night Ability: Violet Potion: Choose a player and a class. The next night your target will start to bleed, and will die the following night, showing up as the class you selected. 2 uses.

Night Ability: Scarlet Potion: Heal all members of the Unseen. Infinite uses.

Passive: Haggler: Occupy and Redirect immune.

Night Ability: Advertise: Anybody who targets you will be redirected to your target. Unlimited uses.

Thief

(Neutral Offensive)

(Converts to Rogue)

Goal: Rob all living Royals

Royals = People with Royal Blood, People with Royal Blood who become King, Not Starting King

Passive: Keen Sense: At the start of each morning, the number of remaining Royals that need to be robbed will show up to the Thief. When the number hits 0, the Thief will have ‚Äúrobbed‚Äù the Palace Treasury and wins. He will then be convert immune.

  • Keen Sense goes down by 1 if Thief uses Robbery on a Royal, or Royal that is not robbed dies.
  • Keen Sense goes up by 1 each time Baron or Duke gives Royal Blood to a player.

Passive: Royal Hatred: You cannot become a candidate for a New King.

Day Ability: Drugged Dagger: Prevent a player from seeing names before messages, accusals, and votes for the rest of today. 3 uses.

Night Ability: Spying Eyes: Choose two players. You will be notified if at least one is a Royal and if at least one is a Royal, gain a use of Robbery. Unlimited uses.

Night Ability: Robbery: Choose any player. (Including the King) If they are a Royal, you are guaranteed to remove their immunities and occupy them. 0 uses at start.

Accomplice

(Neutral Investigative)

(Convert Immune)

Goal: Survive to see the Blue Dragon fall.

Passive: Trickery: Immune to death at night once.

Day ability: Snitch: Target player will receive your night results tonight in addition to you. Unlimited uses.

Night ability 1: Stalk: Choose a player. You will know their exact class, who visited them, and who they visited that night. Unlimited uses.

Night ability 2: Sabotage: Choose a player. They will not receive any feedback from their abilities tonight. 4 uses.
