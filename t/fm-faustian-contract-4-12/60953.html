Faustian Bargains

At any time, a Mortal or Priest may make a Faustian Bargain with me. They can ask for basically anything (see things I can’t do below). I will ask for the permission to do something in turn. If both of us agree, then I will enact both parts of the bargain.

Things I can’t do:

  • I cannot change someone’s alignment or win condition.
  • I cannot gain permission to communicate with the Demons.
  • I cannot not lie about mechanics.
  • I cannot lie about action processing or the vote count, unless granted the power by a deal.
  • I cannot kill the dealmaker in a deal, unless they are a Priest.
  • I can’t tell anyone who the Exorcist or Priest is, unless it’s part of a deal.
