{Class suggestion} The Inspirer (remade)

Class/Lore/Fanfic
NOBLE1 February 19, 2018, 2:14am #1

Name - Inspirer
Faction - neutral
Win condition - survive until the end

Passive 1 - My bright side - inspire anyone who visits you, healing them the night they visit you_

Day Ability 1- True to your Kingdom - make a player conversion immune{1}_

Day Ability 2 - Hideout - have your followers meet you in a meeting tonight(simialar to cult\unseen meetings){2 uses}_

Night Ability 1 - Convincing argument - Make a player realize that everyone should live together in harmony.(Convert a player into a neutral of their class, with one goal to protect the inspirer){2 uses}_

Ex. If a Noble is converted Neutral then the Noble will keep all of his day and night ability but his win condition will change. He will also appear Neutral from there on out.

Night ability 2 - Pep talk - Convince your followers that they are unstoppable. Make your concerted followers death immune and occupy immune for the night. {2 uses}

The plan here was to make a Neutral that can convert and also have an impact on the game

Notes: I realize that there is no real reason to try to harm the Inspire as he is a neutral trying to live. Maybe making him Neutral evil and changing his win condition could possible help make this an interesting class. Read below for my alternate idea for this class
