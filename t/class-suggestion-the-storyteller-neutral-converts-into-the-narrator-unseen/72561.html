The Storyteller

Neutral Investigative

Passive (Aura) - If someone attacks you , you will survive but if they attack you again , you can no longer be healed and you will die.

Day Ability (Beginning of Story) - You will become immune to Mindwarp and Frame tonight.(2 Uses)
Day Ability (Change Tone) - Change your night abilities’ results.(Infinite uses - [2 Days Cooldown]. This only affects one ability. After you have used an ability , the results will be switched back.)

Night Ability (Narration) - Target 2 players. You will see if they are the same faction.(Infinite Uses - You will see if they are in the same group of class types [Killer/Offensive] , [Support/Social] , [Special/Investigative] if you have used Change Tone.)
Night Ability (Impersonation) - Target up to 4 players. You will learn the name of the faction that has majority. Does not count as visiting. (3 Uses - You will see the exact classes of the players you have selected but their results will be mixed randomly if you have used Change Tone - Not usable until N3 - You will need to select atleast 3 players.)
Goal: Survive to see The Unseen/The Cult lose.

Converts into:

The Narrator

Unseen Investigative

Passive (Loud Voice) - The Unseen will be able to receive your abilities’ feedback.

Day Ability (Change Tone) - Change your night abilities’ results.(Infinite uses - [2 Days Cooldown]. This only affects one ability. After you have used an ability , the results will be switched back.)

Night Ability (Rewrite the Story) - Target 2 players. You will see if they are the same class type. If they are , get their class type.(Infinite Uses - You will get a target’s class type. You will not know who it belongs to. This will only be available if you have used Change Tone.)
Night Ability (Ballad) - See the exact classes of 2 players. Does not count as visiting.(1 Use)
Goal: The Unseen’s goal.
