Btw 2 vines per night increases the KPN too much. Here’s my version for ToL/FoL from now on:

Druid

Neutral Killer

Wall of Nature (Passive) - You are immune to death, poison, and bleeding. You are permantly rooted.

Root Network (Passive) - The roots will prevent you from visiting someone if it would lead to your death. All players whoose rooms have been rooted will investigate as ‘Your targets room is damaged by roots!’ to all investigatives.

Judgement of the Wild (Day) Publicly mark someone, you will gain a use of Constricting Vines if they aren’t dead by the next day. (Infinite uses, 1 day cooldown)

Rune of Growth (Day) - Spreading Vines can target two people tonight (Infinite uses, 1 day cooldown)

Speading Roots (Night) - Curl roots around a player’s room (Infinite uses)

Constricting Vines (Night) - Kill all people who are vined (0 uses)
