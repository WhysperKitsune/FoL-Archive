https://forum.imperium42.com/t/court-wizard-contest/54572?u=htm

Court Wizard, BD, Support

Passive 1: Light Magic - You will be occupy/redirection immune the first time someone occupies or redirects you

Day Ability 1: Empowerment - Empower a player’s abilities to go through attack/occupation/redirection immunity, this will not inform you’re target, (example, butler occupies someone occ immune going through his occ immunity, it will just tell him he served wine to him), 2 day recharge time, unlimited uses

Night Ability 1 Magic Shield - Protect a player from all negative effects, if someone tried using a negative effect on him a message will come up, saying you’re target has been visited by a negative effect, this will not come up to your target unlimited uses

Night Ability 2: Sphere Of Lightness - Add a magical sphere protecting this player from all visits, 2 use

Sage, Unseen, Support

Passive 1: Dark Magic - You will be occupy/redirection immune the first time someone occupies or redirects you

Day Ability 1: Ally Empowerment - Make unseen immune to all negative effects at night, 2 use(idea by SabreOverdrive, slightly changed by RawkHawk)

Night Ability 1 - Swap a Snake - Same as Tornado

Night Ability 2 - Sphere Of Darkness - Make only the unseen able to visit the target, 2 use

Ritualist, Cult, Support

Passive 1: Will Of Mithras - Same

Passive 2: Crimson Magic - You will be occupy/redirection immune the first time someone occupies or redirects you

Day Ability 1: Brotherhood - A chosen Cult member appear as a member of the Blue Dragon tonight, 2 use

Night Ability 1: Cult Strength - Same as Empowerment can only be used on cult, 2 use

Night Ability 2: Ritual Swap - Same as teleportation, 2 use

Magic Shield Protects Target From:

Conversion
Redirection/Occupation
Frame/Mindwarp
Bleeding (Hunter Bleed)
Ritual Swap/Swap a Snake

Ally Empowerment Protects Unseen Target From:

Gather Darkness/Possess
Redirection/Occupation
Bleeding (Hunter Bleed)

Feedback/suggestions are appreciated

Corruption Protection Spell is a bad ability. Permanently making someone conversion immune is not a fun mechanic, and really should not be an ability to give out, ever.

Empowerment is interesting. No real problems.

Magic Shield is barrier, which I think is fine as is.

Sphere of Magic is basically Ice Ward, right? I assume it prevents visits. It’s fine, depending on what you determine Magic Shield to be protecting.

Slither and Crawl just seems to be another “add on more abilities to make the character intersting”. Sage isn’t really seen as boring, and I don’t agree with buffing him, along with changing poison mechanic for BD with no warning.

Ally Empowerment is simple enough. I think “conversion (edit: I mean occupy, not conversion) immune” rather than “negative effects” would be better, since I don’t think the assassin is going to be converted that night.

Swap a Snake is Tornado and Sphere of Darkness is Wall of Fire, so no complaints.
