This is a very interesting conversation! Here are my thoughts.

I think the intent behind Psycho King is it’s very difficult for the Neutral Killer to get elected as King, but if he somehow manages to do so, he should be heavily rewarded and everyone else heavily punished. So I disagree that Reaper should have Royal Blood, but I agree that he should keep Reap as Psycho King since otherwise he will get outed for not reaping anybody. And so smart Reapers would never want to become King.

The proposed solutions for the n1 deb problem seem to be convoluted and make Reaper overpowered so I think it’s best to simply not notify the Drunk of an attack if he debauches the Reaper. Of the current counterplays Reaper has to a night one debauch, none are viable in practice. He can simply do nothing night one, but this isn’t worth it since he probably won’t get debauched anyway. He can’t empower reap since that isn’t available night one. He can chill the Drunk but as everyone knows that just prolongs the inevitable. So in my opinion, not notifying the Drunk of the Reaper’s attack is the best solution to this problem.
