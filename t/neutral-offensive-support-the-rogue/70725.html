Class/Lore/Fanfic

Only Appears in Unseen Games

  • Mastery of the Arts (Passive) - Immunity to Poison
  • Got your back (Night - Infinite uses) - Guard a player, but only from occupation. Does not protect from an attack
  • Bet you didn’t see that one coming (Night - 2 uses) - Guard a player, if the player gets attacked, the attacker gets killed in their place. Displays the player’s death note.
  • Shadow Form (Day - 2 uses after D2) - Target a player (you may target yourself), they become immune to occupation and redirection until tomorrow.

Win Condition

  • Survive

The idea behind this guy was kinda like a mercenary that can chose his own side; he can chose to either keep protection on the BD or keep his kill guard on the assassin or nk if you think they’re about to get CS’d
