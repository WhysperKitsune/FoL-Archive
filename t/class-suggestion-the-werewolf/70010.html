The Werewolf

Neutral Killer

Passive (Ferocious) You are immune to death, occupation and target changing at night. While poisoned or bleeding, your attacks will bypass healing and death immunity.

Passive (Canine Sense) You will not attack players defended by a Knight or the Hunter if he bears.

Night Ability (Full Moon) - Attack two players. (1 day cooldown - Infinite uses)

Night Ability (Maul) - Bleed someone. They will die in one night if not healed. Generic notification shared with Hunter wolf. (2 uses)

Goal: Defeat the Blue Dragon, Unseen/Cult and any neutrals who seek to do you harm.
