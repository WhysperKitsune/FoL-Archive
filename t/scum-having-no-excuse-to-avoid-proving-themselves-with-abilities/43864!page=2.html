Aquillion February 11, 2018, 10:04am #24

I still don’t like giving it to the Butler or CW. Not only is it outside the scope of what we’re trying to solve (BD day abilities being used to prove), it potentailly makes things worse because we’re adding a new ability that could potentially be used to prove.

Even with a wide variety of people with the ability, it could still narrow down possibilities a bit, and I just don’t think we should ever give a BD role a Day ability with an immediately-obvious effect unless it is absolutely necessary. And beyond that, I just don’t understand why you want Butlers or CWs to gain this ability at all - what purpose does it serve for them? As I understand it, the only purpose of this ability is so evils can use it to prevent people from using day abilities to prove.

As for having them work similarly… it’s not essential, no, but it’s good because it makes the game slightly less complex. Complexity is a resource, so to speak; there’s a limit to how complex the game can become before it becomes impenetrable for new players. And Throne of Lies is already a hugely complex game where a single player failing to know the exact details of any one ability can lead to catastrophic failure for an entire team. So all else being equal it’s better to go for a simpler solution.
