Electrocutioner (Neutral Killer)

Class/Lore/Fanfic
Unoski February 2, 2017, 6:20am #1

DAY ABILITY 1:

NIGHT ABILITY 1:
Charge: Apply a static charge to somebody. This person will die if they come into contact with another charged person or if they are charged a second time. This is silent.

NIGHT ABILITY 2:
Electric Discharge: Should a charged player visit you, they will die. (Passive)

NIGHT ABILITY 3:
Electric Field: Immune to death at night (Passive)

LORE:
There is a rumor of a man whose immense power came from lightning itself. Believing this was a sign from above, he went on a mission to prove his godliness.

Idea sparked from http://www.blankmediagames.com/phpbb/viewtopic.php?f=27&t=23872&hilit=Electrician
