Buff ideas for non-CL cultists:

Ritualist

Cult Support
Passive: Will of Mithras - Sacrificing you will give the cult leader 3 uses of eraticate instead of 2

Day ability 1: Guidence - Target player will be occupy immune and bypass visit prevention tonight (1 use)
Day Ability 2: Exhume - Learn what class killed target dead player (1 use)

Night ability 1: Teleport - Redirect all visitors between 2 target players. Bypasses redirection immunity. (3 uses)
Night ability 2: Brotherhood - Prevent anyone targeting target cultist. (2 uses)

Seeker

Cult Investigative

Day ability: Expose - You are told the next player to use a day ability besides King. (2 uses)

Night ability 1: Identify - Learn the targets faction and class type (3 uses)
Night ability 2: Gaze - Learn who the target visits and who visits them (2 uses)

Invoker

Cult Offensive
Passive: Fanatic - Occupy and Redirect immune

Day ability 1: Bewilder - The next day ability target player uses will fail (1 use)
Day ability 2: Enrage - Your night ability’s will bypass occupy immunity tonight (1 use)

Night ability 1: Chains of Corax - Occupy target player (3 uses)
Night ability 2: Strings of Mithras - Redirect your first target to your second. (2 uses)

Apostle

Cult Social

Day Ability 1: Project - Send an anonymous message to all players. (2 use)
Day Ability 2: Mind control - Force one person to accuse another and prevent further voting. (2 uses)

Night Ability 1: Mind Warp - Target player receives incorrect feedback tonight (3 uses)
Night Ability 2: Cult spy - Hear all whispers tomorrow (2 uses)

Acolyte

Cult Killer
Passive: Blood shield - Immune to death once

Day ability: Curse - Anyone who is bleeding cannot be healed tonight. (1 uses)

Night ability 1: Judgement - If the Cult leader fails to convert his target then they will start bleeding. They will die in 1 night. (3 uses)
Night ability 2: Rampage - Eradicate will bypass night immunity tonight (2 uses)

Note: Judgement gives the same message as the hunter wolf. Shroud is just a defile that only works during the day.
