Thundie85 November 18, 2017, 11:42am #1

I have had a few games where i ended up being Prince or King, and was too tired, and too overwhelmed to actually play the roles as needed. Missing key information, or going against agreed tactics, because i did not have the energy to pay the needed attention to the chat going on. I have on a few occasions ended up making it a loss for my faction.

You can still be a good MM while being tired, and you can still be a good Neutral Killer as well.

Just throwing my idea out there. Maybe make it a feature under the account management, or in the game settings.
