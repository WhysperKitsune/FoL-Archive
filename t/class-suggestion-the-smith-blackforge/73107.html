The Smith

Blue Dragon Offensive

Iron Crate (Passive) - Occupation and Redirection immune.

Flame Smoke (Day) - Make a player talk to themselves for 10 seconds. They wont notice. Cannot use during trials. 3 Use.

Weapon Distract (Night) - If your target do somethings that isnt investigate, you will occupy them. You will not know if you successfully occupy someone. Unlimited Use. You wont know it they are occupation immune.

Iron Armor (Night) - Give player death immune for the night. Regain an use if target didnt get attacked. 2 Use. Cannot target King.
(Special announcement: You have destroyed Smith or BlackForge Iron Armor.)

Win condition:
You must defeat the Unseen or the Cult or any Neutral that stand in your ways.

BlackForge

Unseen Offensive

Enchanted Dagger (Passive) - When you are alive, your Assassin attack will be bypass death immune. This passive is lost when you succeed bypass someone immunity

Iron Crate (Passive) - Occupation and Redirection immune.

Violet Smoke (Day) - Make a player talk to themselves for 10 seconds. They wont notice. Cannot use during trials. 3 Use.

Armor Distraction (Night) - If your target do somethings that isnt investigate, you will occupy them. You will learn their class and make Unseen NS to them if they used Investigate ability. Unlimited Use.

Harden Armor (Night) - Give wll member of Unseen Death Immune tonight. Regain an use if no one was attacked. 2 Use. (This ability > MM Immune)
(Special announcement: You have destroyed Smith or BlackForge Iron Armor.)

Win condition:
You must defeat the Blue Dragon or any Neutral that dare to stand in your way.
