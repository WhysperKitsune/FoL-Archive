[Class Suggestion] Lich

Class/Lore/Fanfic
Tormental June 11, 2017, 12:53am #1

Lich (Neutral Killer) - An undead wretch who seeks the throne of Adiart.

Passive 1:
Royal Blood - You may put your name forward to become the King.

Passive 2:
Gallows Humor - You are immune from death on the night following an execution.

Day Ability 1:
Spectral Whisper - Your target will hear nothing but your anonymous chat for a period of 10 seconds. (Like anonymously whispering to them, but also blocking out information others are saying.) 2 uses.

Night Ability 1:
Casket Breath - If you are killed, you will take the name/place of your target. Single use. (Note: If you move more than once, the dead person you previously swapped with then has their true class announced and shown. ie: “We discovered PlayerName was actually ClassName!” in chat, and correct class next to their name. Common sense stuff.)

Night Ability 2:
Devour Flesh - Kills targeted player (if they’re not immune, etc.). Successful kill gives you one additional use of Casket Breath if you have zero. (You will never have 2 or more uses on hand.)

Lore:
The Lich is a member of the royal family who was ousted for “unkind behavior toward royal livestock”. Exiled to a diseased land, the Lich became an undead, infectious fiend capable of projecting his life essence into others.

Comments:
Once you become King, your class is actually converted to…

Lich King (Evil) - An evil king bent on causing his subjects pain.

Passive 1:
Pass the Torch - If the King dies, a Royal can use his day ability to step forward. If not, a new king will be voted upon.

Day Ability 1:
The Royal Finger - The King’s next vote for treason will count twice. (1-Day cooldown)

Day Ability 2:
Blood Nectar - If you use this after being poisoned and on the day of the poisoning, you will instead gain immunity from murder at night. Single use.

Night Ability 1:
Brain Parasite - Your target’s votes related to you will not be shown to anyone but them from that point forward. Infinite use. (Makes it appear they’re not voting you, etc. - Still counts though. Do it to enough people, and it will look like no one is voting you!)

Lore:
Same as Lich, but now you the KING, baby!

Comments:
Please note he does NOT have all the same powers as the king. No immunity to occupation, no guards, no decide fate.
