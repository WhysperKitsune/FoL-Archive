Tournament for the Throne

TLDR
I am holding a tournament on Twitch .tv/ lunamink and am looking for participants. The first round will be held 12/2/17 5 pm (PST) and cash prizes will be distributed for 1st, 2nd, and 3rd place.

Introduction:
Hey, fellow Throne of Lies players! I am excited to announce the first ever (maybe) unofficial Throne Of Lies Tournament: Tournament for the Throne. Throughout my gaming career, I have always had a love for watching and participating in the competitive scenes of the particular games I was playing. Unfortunately for games like Town Of Salem, Mafia, Werewolf, and now Throne Of Lies, there has never been a highly competitive online scene. I am hoping this tournament allows the best of the best to compete at a high level, on a fair playing field. So if you think you got what it takes to win it all, you can join below!
