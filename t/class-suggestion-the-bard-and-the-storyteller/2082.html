[Class suggestion] The Bard and The Storyteller

Class/Lore/Fanfic
Magnasword2 June 25, 2017, 1:26pm #1

Role name: Bard

Alignment: Blue Dragon

Class type: Investigative

Goal: Win with the Blue dragon

Abilities:

Passive: Inspiration. If occupied at night the Bard’s speech will contain tiny musical symbols at the start and finish for that entire day (These should provided as copy/paste-able in game and on the wiki)

Day Ability: Fate or fiction, Target one player and input a 4 letter word. Should the player speak that word during the day they will instantly say their class out loud.

Night ability: Tale of love, Choose two players, This night those players will occupy each other unless one is a killing class at which point the killer will attack the other chosen player with their relevant conditions. (E.g Knight will suicide if forced onto another Blue dragon.)

Unseen Variant: The Story teller

Class type: Support

Passive: Cannot be occupied at night

Day ability: Propoganda, Input a 4 letter word. Any class who speaks this word will be unable to use their day abilities and all previously chosen day abilities will no longer be active (Including the prince) They will not be notified should this happen.

Night abilities:

Tale as old as lies. 1 use only, Replace one players will with a will of your choice erasing all previously written down information.

Infinite uses: Protect one unseen from being occupied this night.
