As town you have many tools at your disposal in order to find scum by reading their intentions and motivations behind their posts.

Scum have an agenda they need to push in order to win, and they know what everyone is.

Town are trying to find the other town and find the scum. Being natural is how they roll.
Scum want to stop the town from finding the other town and want to prevent town from finding the scum. Scum additionally want to look town (or have their partners look town), so they may vote eachother to keep up being natural and/or throw eachother under the bus.

If someone is natural in their posting, and you can agree with their thought process and progression, you probably found a villager.
