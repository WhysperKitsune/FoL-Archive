Neut Class Suggestion (The Bastard v.3)

Class/Lore/Fanfic
NOBLE1 July 6, 2018, 7:20pm #1

Lore: You slowly realized that you’re father was caring less and less about the war between BD and Unseen. As you accuse him of staying neutral in the war, he gets furious and exiles you from the kingdom. You swear that you will come back and kill your family for what they did.

Name - The Bastard (Fallen Prince)
Faction - neutral
Type - offense

Passive 1: hidden in the chamber - you hide in the jail the first time someone tries to attack you, making you immune to death once.

Passive 2: taking what’s mine - when the original prince dies then you may choose to kill anyone with your night ability “execute”
You will also gain 3 more uses of imprison

Day Ability 1: imprison - jail a player at night, when you speak, you will appear as prince (3 uses), gains 3 more uses when/if prince dies

Day Ability 2: - Disturbing thoughts - pick a player and tell them about the prison, they will be occupied tonight (4 uses)

Night ability 1: execution;

  • execute the player in jail. (1 uses).
  • (If the prince is dead) kill anyone (3 uses) [if you kill someone without royal blood then you are not able to execute anymore, but is still able to jail]

Night ability 2: lit candle - lite a candle revealing the faction of the person who is jailed, (3 uses)

Mechanics - this class can be jailed interrupting any day abilities. This class cannot jail the prince. You will be given a message that "[player name] is the prince". If both the prince and bastard jail the same person then the prince will succeed and the bastard will get a message saying “[player name] was taken away before you could jail them”.

Win condition - Kill anyone with royal blood and the starting king.

Extra lore: you are not given royal blood because your family doesn’t consider you one of their own.
