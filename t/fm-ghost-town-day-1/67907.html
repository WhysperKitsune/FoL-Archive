FM: Ghost Town

Rules

  1. Follow the Global Forum Rules
  2. Be respectful to the moderators and your fellow players.
  3. Don’t be inactive. If you do not post meaningfully on any given day, you will be prodded. Receiving two prods will result in your removal from the game.
  4. Do not quote/screenshot private host messages.
  5. Don’t throw the game. Always play with the intent of helping your faction win.
  6. No editing or deleting posts without express moderator permission.
  7. No posting after majority is reached or at night. We won’t lock the thread, and instead the honor system will be used. Repeated or egregious failure to comply will result in your removal from the game.
  8. It’s a game - have fun!
  9. Do not talk about the game outside of officially sanctioned channels. This applies even if you’re dead, even if the person you’re talking to isn’t playing, and even if you think that there’s no way it could possibly have any effect on the game. Just don’t do it. At all.

Gameplay

  • This is a mostly open game. The exception is that there is one bastard mechanic that is closed. I will tell you only that it concerns the dead.
  • Due to this mechanic, there will be no spectating allowed.
  • Days will last up to 24 hours.
  • Nights will last roughly 12 hours.
  • Actions are performed by sending myself and Icibalus a message on the PM I send you with your role card.

Executions

  • To accuse someone, say “/vote [player]”.
  • To rescind your vote, say “/unvote [player]”.
  • To vote for no lynch, say “”/vote NoLynch".
  • Making your vote obvious (bolded, on their own line, etc.) will make it less likely to be missed by us.
  • Vote counts will be given whenever the moderators are available to do so. You may request one by pinging both Icibalus and myself.
  • Days 1 and 2 will have a deadline lynch - whoever has the most votes when the timer expires will be lynched.
  • Days 3 and on will have a plurality lynch - if majority is reached on a player, the day will end immediately with their lynch, but if majority isn’t reached whoever has the most votes when the timer expires will be lynched.
  • If majority has been reached, stop posting immediately. Repeated or egregious failure to comply may result in a modkill.

After The Night

  • You will be told what happened to you in your PM, and day will begin.

Journals and Death Notes

  • There will be no journals or death notes.
