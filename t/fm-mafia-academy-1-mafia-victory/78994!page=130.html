Honestly, for whether or not it’s likely derps is telling the truth, you have to ask yourself, what are the odds a Mafia derps would fake the checks.
We know by math that there’s roughly a 20 percent chance a town derps misses all the Mafia with 5 checks
This may seem to indicate that derps has an 80 percent chance of being scum, however this is misleading
Instead, we have to look at this back-to-front. What kinds of worlds have derps telling us this? A world where derps is town, and a world where derps is Mafia. For this I’m going to assume the prior probability is 50 percent in each of the town and scum worlds, because it’s basically between us at this point, and I really don’t want to deal with Monty Hall probabilities here.
Now, in each of these worlds, what are the odds derps tells us this.
In the town world, it’s 20 percent, and in the Mafia world, I’ll leave that to yall to show I’m not being biased, but I’ll use x%
Now, we add these two together, and divide into that total to see which is more likely:
20 / (x+20)
Or
x / (x+20)

If the two worlds weren’t 50/50 we would put in another multiplier but we’re not dealing with that for now. Just plug in your value for x and you have a more accurate relative percentage for scum derps and town derps
