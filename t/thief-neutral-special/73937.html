Thief (Neutral Special)

Class/Lore/Fanfic
Charrlous November 20, 2018, 8:00am #1

Thief
Neutral Special
(has an item based system similar to mercenary’s brilders or reaper’s souls called jewels)

Passive 1 - Jewels!: If you are attacked at night, lose 2 jewels and continue living, starts off with 3 jewels.

Passive 2 - Code of Honour: If you are killed or executed during the day, your logs will be wiped and you will not be able to speak after you are sentenced for treason

Day Ability 1 - Greedy Pressure: Force someone to vote someone else (including them self), Can be used as many times in the day, pressured players will follow your execution or pardon vote, lose 1 jewel. (Infinite uses)

Night Ability 1 - Steal: Make a player lose 1 use of all their limited use abilities, Gain 1 jewel for every use of an ability you stole. Players stolen from will be notified (Infinite uses)

Night Ability 2 - Supply: Give a non blue dragon player +1 use for all their abilities, Gives Reaper +1 soul and Sorcerer can use 2 day abilities in the same day. You will be notified if your target was a member of the blue dragon. Lose 1 jewel if they are a member of the blue dragon, Gain 1 jewel if they are not (only gives alch +1 stoneskin) (After using this ability, you may not supply your target for another two nights.) (Infinite uses)

Win Condition: Survive to see the defeat of the blue dragon.
(Stalls the game cause of their pressure mechanic)

I currently think that supply is more of an investigative mechanic then a support mechanic, I’m also not too sure if it should a be a social, offensive, support or special class type.

My suggestion would have it function as an investigative action, where supply will give either an evil/NK or neutral (besides alch) an extra limited use and if an ability is given in such a fashion, you receive 3 jewels back so +2 jewels, the drawback being you may only supply (or attempt to) each player once per game.

The reason this is investigative is you will be given feedback if your supply did anything or not, thus you are informed if your target is evil or not regardless of frames/warps.
