Due to circumstances, there have been less people to look over games, hence the amount of FMs are decreasing. To combat this, I’m going to offer up to three people a chance to become reviewers and help review setups that have laid dormant for who knows how long in the Queue. How does the process work? It’s quite simple.

First, you must make a post about your interest in wanting to become a reviewer. Once I have the time to do so, I will contact you via DMs or Discord (Whichever you prefer. If you prefer Discord, please post your username) and ask you a series of questions. After the flow of interests for the position have slowed down, I (And possibly Eevee if he can respond to me) will select those that I believe are the most suitable to be a reviewer.

There is no time limit for this selection process, but I would like to get it done within a couple of weeks so that things don’t go stale. Good luck to all and I hope to talk to you soon.
