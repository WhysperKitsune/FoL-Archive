Too Much Power (Passive)

Hero Shooter (Passive) - For every member of the Town you detonate, you gain Unfortunate Towncred (1-3 points, depending on how townread your target was and determined by the host). Upon accumulating 5 points of Unfortunate Towncred, you are revealed in the thread as Unfortunately Town.
THE GAMESOLVE SHOT (Day) - Switch your detonator to another player who has been townread by at least a quarter of the living players today. Quote the townreads in your role PM as you use this ability. If they are Mafia and you successfully detonate them, you become a Vanilla Town.
Bad Justification (Day) - You can only use this ability after detonating a member of the Town, and you may use it on the same day you detonated someone and/or used THE GAMESOLVE SHOT. If Hero Shooter has already triggered, then nothing will happen.
