The general rule of thumb is that back-ups will always be allowed to become spectators if they wish. But spectators usually cannot become back-ups.

There is more flexibility in being a back-up so even if there is a remote chance that one could and wish to play the game, they can. However, once one decide to spectate, there is no going back.
