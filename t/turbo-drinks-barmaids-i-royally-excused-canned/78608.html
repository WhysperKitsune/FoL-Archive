Welcome to [TURBO] - Drinks & Barmaids I

Hosted by @KyoDaz

This setup follows simple forum rules.
The setup will start once 7 players have signed up.
The setup will start on Night 1.

Gameplay

Days will be 10 minutes long.
Nights will be 5 minutes long.
All actions must be done in your rolecard except for the faction kill.
The town alignment is the Yellow Poppy.
The mafia alignment is the White Primrose.
The White Primrose automatically win once majority is reached.
The White Primrose can only communicate during the night.
The White Primrose do not share rolecards and will be in seperate threads therefore any feedback is personal.
The White Primrose have a faction kill.
The only role that will receive ability feedback is the Constable.
Items grant players a day ability.
You will be told if you receive a Flintlock at the beginning of the day.

Executions

To accuse someone say “/accuse [player]” or “/vote [player]”
Executions will be plurality. Players are not lynched until the end of the day unless all votes are received. Any ties in votes will be determined a no-lynch.
If a lynch target has been decided via all votes being received but a player has not decided what they will do with a day ability (ect. /nolynch Flintlock or /flintlock KyoDaz) then the lynch will be delayed.
Feel free to ask any scenarios with lynches.
