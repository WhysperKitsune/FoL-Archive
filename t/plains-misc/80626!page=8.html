Theories
If we die In this game, we simply respawn, in the same world with everything as it was at the moment of our death (as if, instead of dying, we teleported back to the spawn, except the previous bodies are still there). Note that our character-memories remain intact - all but confirmed imo
We cannot issue commands arbitrarily (i.e. no “repeat all previous commands” or anything else with 1 or more degrees of separation between the submitted command and the desired effect), however we can issue multiple commands in a sequence (i.e. go to x and do y), albeit with the note that should a command not apply, it will not submit (unconfirmed whether further commands in the chain will submit or not after one of these failed commands) - semi confirmed imo
Commands issued in multiple posts will be attempted to be submitted simultaneously, with prior posts taking precedence - unknown. Most likely either this or commands In multiple posts will just be submitted as if they were in sequence
Upon such a state where it is deemed impossible to win/progress, the world is reset as well as our character. Notable in that we seem to lose our character-memories. That is, if we establish which direction North is in one life and then die, we can still submit “go north” and go to the same place, same for locations in general, whereas if the world resets, we can no longer go north or to specified locations. Unknown as of yet whether the resets world is the same be as the previous one or if things are changed, and if so to what degree - sorta confirmed imo. Uncertain as to the specific circumstances that would cause this effect
There is no randomness when not provoked by commands (like in the most recent results), either something will work or it won’t I’m a given situation - I’d say so, but I have no reasonable degree of certainty of it
