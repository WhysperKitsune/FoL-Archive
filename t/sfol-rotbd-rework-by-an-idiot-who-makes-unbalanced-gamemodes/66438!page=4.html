The Alchemist

Neutral Support
Experimentation (Passive) - In the start of Day 1, you choose your win condition of 3 options.

Neutrality: Survive to the end of the game
The Evils: Ensure that the Blue Dragon is defeated
The Town Ensure that the Blue Dragon is victorious

Experimental Shield (Day) - You will be immune to death, occupation and redirection tonight. - 1 use

Concoct Potion (Night) - Choose from a variety of potions to use tonight. - Infinite uses

If “The Town” win condition is chosen then these abilities are gained:

Crimson Potion - Heal targeted player, if you successfully heal a player from an attack that is not you. This use of Crimson Potion will not be used up. - 3 use
Tar Potion - Prevent all players from visiting your target. - 2 use

If “Neutrality” win condition is chosen then these abilities are gained:

Tar Potion - Prevent all players abilities from visiting your target. - 3 use
Crimson Potion - Heal targeted player, if you successfully heal a player from an attack that is not you. This use of Crimson Potion will not be used up. - 2 uses

If “The Evil” win condition is chosen then these abilities are gained:

Misty Potion - Target player will be framed to Investigators tonight. - 3 use
Tar Potion - Prevent all players except attacking and conversion abilities from visiting your target. - 2 uses

Ensure the win condition that you have chosen is successful.
