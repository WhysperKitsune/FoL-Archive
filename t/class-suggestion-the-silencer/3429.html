The Silencer (Neutral Offensive)

Lore:
You’re some crazy bard/singer or something that thinks their own voice is so beautiful that you think you’re the only person in the world that’s allowed to speak.

Objective:
You win when everyone is dead or silenced. (Once everyone has been silenced that is alive you will win, even if you die later.)

Passive 1: Compulsive singing
Your voice is so divine, it would be a crime not to use it. Everytime you stay in your room you will sing.
If you do not take any actions at night (and you’re not occupied or jailed) everyone who visits you will know who you are.

Day ability 1: The sound of Silence
Nobody can hear others speak for 10 seconds. But they will hear themselves speak. (they won’t get a notification about this) 2 uses.

Night ability 1: Silence
Remove a target’s tongue. They will not be able to speak until they die. infinite uses

Night ability 2: Silen… oops
You try to remove a target’s tongue, but you accidently kill them. Oops. 2 uses

Notes:

Maybe silenced people and silencer should be able to talk with psychic since that’s some mind reading voodoo. Dunno.
Should probably not spawn with both reaper and possessor in one game.
