Welcome

Rules

No cheating, gamethrowing, angleshooting, etc.

Gameplay

Days will be 72 hours long, or until a Kingmaker is chosen.
Nights will last 24 hours.
Each day, there is no lynch. Instead, whoever gets the most votes becomes a Kingmaker.
When half the players are Kingmakers (rounded down), the King’s election will start.
The game ends when a King is chosen.
Loyalist Kingmakers break ties in the King election.
If this does not resolve the tie, or the tie is for electing a Kingmaker, it is resolved by RNG.
This is a no flip gamemode.
Traitors have day and night chat, and may even speak with their dead.

Classes

Loyalist

You have no powers.

You must ensure that a Loyalist is elected King

Assassin

Loyalist

You may kill someone once per game, bypassing immunity.
If you become a Kingmaker, you will not be night immune.

You must ensure that a Loyalist is elected King

Traitor

You may vote on the factional kill.

You must ensure that a Traitor is elected King

Kingmaker

Loyalist or Traitor

You may vote for the King.
You cannot be killed, or be voted. You cannot vote for a Kingmaker.
You cannnot be elected King.

Your must fulfill the win condition of your previous role.

To Join

To join, simply express interest in joining by saying something like “I want to join” or “/join” or “/in” or “sign me up, coach” or whatever you want
