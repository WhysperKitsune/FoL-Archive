The Pretender King - A new king class idea for Pretender!

Class/Lore/Fanfic
TheVideoChipmunk August 17, 2018, 10:24pm #1

Whenever a saw the class for pretender, I’m sure I’m not alone when I say I was disappointed. I’m not saying the developers aren’t doing a good job because they really are, with the constant updates. I’m just saying the Pretender is not really something we needed. I want to show my idea for a new King class that is Pretender exclusive.

The Pretender King

Passive (Pretend) - You are one-time death immune. This does not bypass empower reap and similar abilitys, except for butler wine, which affects your one-time death immunity.

Day Ability (The Royal Finger) - Your next nomination for treason will count twice.

Day Ability (Decide Fate) - Decide an accused player’s fate.

Night Ability (Fake) - Choose a player. They will get the notification for being attacked but immune tonight. 1 use.

Night Ability (Guards!) - Send a guard to protect a player. You may not target yourself. 2 uses.

Goal: Survive

My plan with making this king class is its a neutral king with 2 guards and 1 fake guard, which total to three “guards”, just like a regular neutral king. They are also 1-time death immune to prevent the pretender king from losing too fast.

With how disappointed or maybe even angry I may have sounded at the start of this, I ensure you I’m not mad and I trust the developers to lead this class in the right direction.

As always, suggestions for change are always acceptable and appreciated!
