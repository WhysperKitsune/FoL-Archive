Possessor Guide

NuclearBurrito June 29, 2018, 6:20pm #1

This guide should help you play Possessor more effectively. These tips partially apply to other NK but this is tailored to the Possessor in particular.

There are a few things you need to keep in mind when playing Possessor. There is no magic strategy that will win you every game but you can be a larger threat if you optimize your play.

Tip 1 - Have a planned endgame from day 1

Obviously you need to be flexible. However you need to know what you are working towards as soon as possible. Have a backup plan of course but don’t lose sight of the goal just to make a quick play that gets you spotted.

A few different examples of win states:

1: Being a trusted psycho king

2: Dead king. Dead Prince. Possessed confirmed sheriff.

3: Possessed King. 1v1

Ect. You don’t need to get creative for this. Just take a state where you are likely or guarenteed to win and get there.

Generally if you can control the day phase you win.

Tip 2 - Don’t be afraid to speak up

Talking draws night kills. However it also makes you seem more trustworthy. This is always worth it. You are night immune so you don’t need to worry about the Unseen or Cult.

The bigger thing to keep in mind is that the more you talk the easier it is to take control of the day phase and also let’s you gather claims

Tip 3 - Plan your jumps in advance

You should already know when and why you are jumping a few nights in advance. If you are jumping due to suspicion then you should accuse someone the day before. If you are trying to frame someone then whisper a trusted BD that they might kill you.

If you are trying to possess a confirmed BD then you should know what the protectives are doing.

Know who you jump to!

Nothing kills a Hunter frame attempt more than possessing the Hunter you are trying to frame. Possessing the Prince is usually good but requires good timing to make the most out of it. And if you possess a scum then you are in danger of being immediately discovered

If there is anything you should take away from this post it’s that:

Planning is key!

If you know what you are doing. Why you are doing it and what everyone else is going to do in response beforehand then you win. You CANNOT wing it as Possessor. There is no randomly winning because you have majority.

On the thread here are a few of my own Possessor stratagem.

Defile any class that doesn’t indicate it’s a Possessor game and possess the King N1

It seems risky and stupid to do this, but it typically pays off as long as you don’t stay too long in the King. The reasoning here is to rely on the King meta and get players to start claiming to you so you can begin drafting long-term plans for the late game. Also you’ll be typically safe for the first few days as nobody will ask you to point the finger as nobody expects the King to be possessed so early, or that it’s even a Possessor game at all. To reduce suspicions on you, you can also announce to the court that you have an Inquisitor claim to dampen any suspicion that it’s a Possessor game.

Feign reaped and jump twice early game

Essentially, on the second or third day Facelift into another class, say nothing, vote nobody, and abstain during trials. At night make sure your log only contains information up to the previous night and then jump after deleting your death note. Do the same thing with the next body and then you can either save your last jump or use it to feign reap again. For added effect, claim that you’ve been chilled. This strategy is far more reliable than claiming Inquisitor as if suspicion starts to pile on that it’s not a Reaper game then people won’t instantly execute you in response, additionally people find it easier to believe a fake Reaper than a fake Sorcerer game. This tactic essentially allows you to jump into people who have been mechanically confirmed in the past without any suspicion at all and is less risky than faking Inquisitor.

Stay in the same body, have a solid fakeclaim, gain trust and become a Psycho King

This works only if you are skilled in creating fake logs, know how to scumread, and avoid being scumread in response. To put it simply, meld into the Blue Dragon, don’t antagonize people, create a convincing fake log, gain everyone else’s trust, and once all Royalty barring the Prince dies you kill the King. This will secure your place in the next day’s election and nobody will even believe that you’re a Psycho King. The fact that it’s a Possessor game will make people believe that the Possessor must have jumped at least once, but since you can provide evidence that you did not get possessed, as in having your whisper logs and being able to point once King, you’ll basically be set for the rest of the game. To summarize, it’s using WIFOM as in a Possessor game people will expect you to forgo a solid claim and rely on your jumps not to get caught, and not do the complete opposite.
