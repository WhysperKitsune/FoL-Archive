Matchmake: (N) (2)

To make it fair for Cult Leader and Mastermind however, Mastermind’s In The Shadows Passive should make Mastermind appear as an Investigative class. Prince should also be changed from a Blue Dragon Killer to a Blue Dragon Special, which makes sense overall for the Prince in my opinion.

And the most important thing to note is that this change would not make the Princess more confirmable than she is right now.

ShadowKnight June 6, 2018, 4:16am #19

I like the change to Will o’ Wisp letting you determine exact class type, but I think “if they targeted you” is not enough utility, since most of the time you will know if you’ve been targeted (at least as far as Special/Killer/Offensive/Physician goes).

Instead, I’d like to continue supporting the idea of giving the Princess some survivability and add my previous suggestion to yours:

"If the target attempts to target you with an ability, the ability will be prevented."

This covers all attacks, not just those that visit (and it covers Possessor as well since they are targeting you with the second half of their ability), it covers conversion attempts and it covers Sellsword trying to prevent others from visiting you and Sorcerer Circle of Death’ing you. It, however, also prevents healers from healing you, Court Wizards from Barriering/Swapping you, and Investigative classes from confirming you as Princess. Whether it protects from Empowered Reap or not is up for debate.

For the Duchess, you can have her Dark Wisp straight up prevent visits to her when used (like a Knight/Sellsword/Merc), which is not exactly the same as a proper Will o’ Wisp, instead of disguising investigative results.
