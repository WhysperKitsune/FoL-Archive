NuclearBurrito December 19, 2017, 1:54am #1

People seem to like the maid spy version of noble the most. But that isn’t happening and that’s a problem for adding back the Noble. So the question is: What could he be?

A few questions that we know the answers to:

1: What class type is he? Social

2: What passives does he have? Royal blood (possibly another)

From listening to others speak this seems to be what we have so far

Noble - Blue Dragon Social
Passive: Royal Blood - Can step up to be king
.
Night ability 1: Private matter - Whispers to and from the noble are hidden the next day (Unlimited)

Obviously not enough to work into a class. There is however 1 version of the noble that might be fun in this meta.

Noble - Blue Dragon Social
Passive 1: Royal Blood - Can step up to be king
Passive 2: Noble Twin - Meet with the other Noble at night. You can speak with each other and are both night immune.

Again not enough for a class but it has potential given a few changes to how it was in beta.

Noble - Blue Dragon Social
Passive 1: Royal Blood - Can step up to be king
Passive 2: Noble Twin - Meet with the other Noble at night. You can speak with each other and are both night immune. You will never lose this passive under any circumstances other than losing night immunity if you become king. You cannot see what the other Noble does.
.
Day ability: Signal - Send a message to the other noble. No one else will be informed. (4 uses)
.
Night ability 1: Royal sway - It take 2 more votes to execute the other noble. If you target self it takes 1 more vote to execute you instead.(Unlimited)
Night ability 2: Shared sources - The other noble can hear whispers to and from you and knows what happens to you tonight. (Unlimited)

Note: If one noble is converted then the other stays BD. The Aristocrat is the same as noble except that the second night ability also lets the unseen hear whispers to and from each other and that the unseen will see the message sent from Signal.

If someone makes a suggestion that is better it will be placed here:

Noble - Blue Dragon Social
Passive: Royal Blood - Can step up to be King.
Passive 2: Noble Twin - Meet with the other Noble at night. You can speak with each other and are both night immune. You will never lose this passive under any circumstances other than losing night immunity if you become king. You cannot see what the other Noble does.
.
Day ability 1: Signal - Send a message to the other noble. No one else will be informed. (4 uses)
Day ability 2: Private Matter - All whispers to and from you will be hidden. (2 uses)
.
Night ability 1: Royal sway - It take 2 more votes to execute the other noble. If you target self it takes 1 more vote to execute you instead.(Unlimited)
Night ability 2: Shared sources - The other noble can hear whispers to and from you and knows what happens to you tonight. (Unlimited)

Another idea for those who liked the vote manipulation

Noble - Blue Dragon Social
Passive 1: Royal Blood - Can step up for king
Passive 2: Bureaucracy - See’s all votes in real time
.
Day ability’s are the same but with 3 uses instead of 2
.
Night ability 1: Regroup - Check how many BD there are alive (3 uses)
Night ability 2: Power Play - If less than half of the living players are BD then you gain 2 extra votes for the rest of the game in all votes and you cannot be voted.(1 use. Is consumed even if it fails)

The Aristocrat is the same but he has order instead of regroup and doesn’t have Power Play.
