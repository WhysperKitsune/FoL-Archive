Court Wizard Rework

Design Philosophy:
-Court Wizard should be hard to confirm (requires other BD as CW can‚Äôt confirm himself), but potentially incredibly powerful
-Abilities should have huge potential impact
-CW needs self preservation

Passive:
Crystal Ball - You cast your spells from afar through your magical crystal ball, as such, your night abilities do not count as visits.

Day:
Cloak of Invisibility, 2 Charges
Tonight you will be immune to all visits.

Night:
Magical Barrier, 2 Charges
Target will be protected from attacks. Target will be notified they were attacked and shielded by a barrier. Court Wizard does not know if target attacked or not.

Illusionary Decoy, 2 Charges
Target will be immune to all visits, Target will be unaware they were visited - Visitors will be convinced their visit succeeded (Target Occupied, Attacked Successfully, etc). Court Wizard will know who visited the decoy, but not what for.

Sage would be reworked to be in line. I‚Äôm still debating adding another day ability with either minor social and/or investigative abilities.
