Night ability: “Loyalty Rules” - Cause a player to target the king with their selected ability (2 uses)

Day ability: “Powerless” - If you would be jailed today, you can’t be executed (the prince still loses an execute) OR if you would be jailed during the night, you break free. (separate ideas)

“Royal Support”: If you cast this on a Killer class, their attack will bypass night immunity (either for their next attack or for the selected night). If you castit on an Investigative class, they get correct feedback (no occupies, no framing, no target switching, surpasses the Mastermind’s passive)

“I can do it better”: Gain 1 use of a player’s main ability (1 use)

“Chaos” - Cause anyone targeting you to target themselves.

Day ability: “Not so fast” If your target gets poisoned / bled AFTER the use of this ability, the caster gets the same effect on themselves.

Day ability: “Winds of Time” - Reverse time for everyone by 1 day. (e.g. if the following night is night 4, it will be night 3 again), causing all bleeding, poison, and ability cooldowns to last another night. (1 use)

“Switcheroo” If a player has 2 abilities and is using one, cause them to use the other, (if they’re both targeting, target the same person. If ability 1 isn’t targeting and ability 2 is, don’t switch. If a player has expired uses of the other ability, don’t switch.

“Recycled Failure” - If a conversion fails tonight, the Mastermind gets an extra use of “Foresight”

“One last crime” - Make an Unseen member not appear as Unseen to all Investigative abilities for the night (1 use)

Court Wizard, alright.

Court Wizard will now be a neutral class.

During the day, he now has the ability to take one night ability from a player during the day, and give that ability to another player. For instance, let say you know a player is a fool. You click your swapping ability, there will be 3 boxes, one with the player you want to take from, one with the player you want to give it to, and a box stating “1” and “2.” If you click 1 and take it from the fool, and give it to a sheriff, he will be able to hide for only 1 night, then this taken ability will be removed and given back to the player it was taken from. The player with the given ability can also use his other abilities. Court Wizard can only use this ability 3 times. Court Wizard cannot use this ability during execution or during a trial. The player that is being taken from is notified, but the player that is being given the ability is not notified. You also aren’t notified of what the ability is that you are taking.
Lets call this ability “Shrouded Robbery.”

Let’s say 1-16 are all targets. (x symbol) Court Wizard, during the night, is now able to frame any set player. (1-16.) If a Court Wizard is successful in a frame, he gains 1 use of “kill a player for 1 night.” This frame has an infinite amount of uses.

His goal is to kill or execute all neutral classes.
Once the goal is done, he does not die.

My idea is for Court Wizard to be a neut destroyer, and to out them to either help BD or Cult/Unseen.
