The Break Gauge is present for each and every enemy, as well as a hidden gauge for each and every one of you when fighting another human enemy. When this gauge is depleted, said enemy/ally will not be able to act the following turn, is delayed and every attack/craft that hits them will guarantee a follow-up attack.

Follow Up Attacks are made by unbalancing the enemy or by dealing critical damage. Strike them with attacks they’re weak against for another ally to also strike at them for additional damage. This will earn you Brave Points in which you can use to Rush to strike at a group of enemies near the first attacker, or Unite for all enemies to be damaged by all party members. Each of them Cost different amounts of Brave Points, so be careful.
