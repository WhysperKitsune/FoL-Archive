Throne of Lies Screenshot Challenge

Official Announcements
Thunder April 11, 2018, 1:47pm #1

Now that our biggest patch yet has been released, there have been plenty of visual changes to Throne of Lies. From the user interface and character customization screens to animated emotes and multiple deathnote colors, Castle Adiart looks a whole lot different.

The facelift is complete and we need some excellent new photos to show off and share with all of our friends and family on the website, Steam store and social media pages. And this is your opportunity to be featured in them and get rewarded for your efforts!

We want to see your best, high-definition screenshots featuring all of the different aspects of the game, from the menus and lobbies to the main hall, prison cell, haunted hallways and individual class rooms.
This will be an ongoing contest until we find the perfect shots and each screenshot selected will earn you 3,000 GP and a free Throne of Lies game key to give to a friend.

The only rules are that all screenshots must be 1080+ quality, feature only family-friendly chat and get posted in the #pics-screenshots section of our official Discord server https://discord.gg/tol.

Our trusty leader, Xblade, will personally select the winners as they come in and won’t stop giving out GP until he is satisfied!

EDIT: Hacked! Xblade here! The contest is still going and have already picked out some below.

Still seeking more! Specifically looking for…

  • Class Night rooms hovering over a night ability – especially looking for Mystic room.
  • Cult room at night with interesting chat + hovering over a night ability
  • More variety executions
  • Class cards while hovering over your favorite class portrait (extra points for Illusionist, my current favorite portrait)

1920x1080+ resolution on high gfx with high fps! These will go on the store page.

Have a deathnote in your screenshot?
