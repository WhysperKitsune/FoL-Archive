Erik March 23, 2018, 7:48pm #79

Soric made a good point that butler is an offensive class and should feel offensive. To me, that means the scum should worry about what happens if the butler finds out they’re scum, but the butler should not himself be able to detect if they are.

Butler can already tie up cult, assassin, and reaper with occupation (at least for a while), but what could he do against possessor, sorcerer, and mastermind?

What if, instead of concentrated wine, the butler gained a night ability with just one charge. Thematically, name it something to reflect a weakening poison slipped into a glass of wine. It permanently removes all of the target’s passive abilities.

Now the butler’s main ability is still weaker than the drunk, but you are a hard counter to the king AND the scariest enemies. You still can’t occupy an enemy into oblivion because the three-times-occupied immunity isn’t a passive, but at least you can open them up for the cold steel or just future occupy visits from yourself. Plus, you have to use it carefully, or you could cost a BD player their own occupy/bleed immunity, etc.

Additionally, it opens up fake claims a bit because if you claim an occupy immune class and get occupied, you could try to argue that a butler drugged you. Will that save you? Probably not, but it opens up the social deduction gamespace.
