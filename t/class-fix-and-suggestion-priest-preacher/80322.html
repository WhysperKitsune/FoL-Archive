[Class Fix and Suggestion] Priest/Preacher

Class/Lore/Fanfic
Magnus February 16, 2020, 9:49pm #1

These classes were a collaborative effort between Magnus and DirectorHail.

Priest

BD Support
Holy Resurgence (Day Ability) - Once the Soul passes through this world to the next, they’re never coming back. Select a recently killed member of the Blue Dragon to revive. They will receive a notification that they’re being revived and cannot talk to or hear other dead players. Their chat history of dead players will be removed for the duration that they’re alive. The revived target will die in two days. - 1 use
Familiar Blood (Day Ability) - Hold hands, and wait for a sign. Within the first 30 seconds of the day, you may select a dead member of the Blue Dragon. The dead player will be notified and can send an, up to 30 letter, message. At the end of the following night the Priest will receive the message as well as the player’s updated logs. - Infinite uses
Blue Fire (Night Ability) - The flames of the gods burns through your veins. If attacked by an anti-Blue Dragon class, you will be immune to death. - 1 use
Your objective is to defeat the Unseen or Cult, and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.

The Priest will convert to either the Preacher or the Ritualist.

Preacher

Unseen Support
Purple Flames (Passive) - That which was once benign, is now a cruel and twisted fate. When attacked, you will be immune to death. This passive can only take effect once.
Demonic Chant (Day Ability) - The end is nigh! Prepare yourselves! Select an alive player. They will be silenced and unable to use their limited use day abilities, and you can talk in their place. - 2 uses
Fearing Retribution (Night Ability) - Why show mercy when you can twist the blade in deeper? Select a player. If they are attacked, the attack will bypass death. - 2 uses
Your objective is to defeat the Blue Dragon and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.

Clarification

Both classes are intended for Throne Of Lies; heavy edits would be needed for it to work in a Forum Of Lies environment
Holy Resurgence - Select a recently killed member of the Blue Dragon to revive. By Revive, it means killed the night previously.

Familiar Blood - Within the first 30 seconds of the day you may select a dead member of the Blue Dragon . The dead player will be notified and can send a 30 letter message. At the end of the following night the Priest will receive the message and the players updated logs. This fixes the problem of forced to stay. It means you need to use it quickly, yes. And you will receive the update logs of the player as it was when the BD received the notifications. This is to counter multiple messages via logs.

Blue Fire - If attacked by an anti- Blue Dragon class, you will be immune to death. This means, if you use this ability, you will be immune to death, but only against those who are not BD (of course, death bypassing abilities will still work). You will be immune if you attacked from multiple non BD.

Purple Flames - When attacked, you will be immune to death. This passive can only take effect once. Unlike Blue Fire, you do not have to actively use it, and it will protect you from anyone. However, if you are attacked by two people on the same night, you will die.

Demonic Chant - Select an alive player. They will be silenced and unable to use their limited use day abilities, and you can talk in their place. Originally, this wasn’t going to affect day abilities, but Hail convinced me. (Thank him for making the classes good and balanced, actually.) Once this ability is used, you cannot cancel it for the day. This means you cannot talk as yourself again for the day (bad news if you’re VFRed), but you can vote from yourself. Admittedly, the target can spam vote, but you can talk it off. Even if no one is convinced, there’s no link back to you.

Fearing Retribution - Select a player. If they are attacked, the attack will bypass death. No, no, NO! This is not like the Frenzy ability of the Enforcer. This doesn’t bypass jail, but it does bypass protection. In addition, if anyone kills the target, they will always die. (Although you’ll still rely on the Assassin, probably.)

anon97870008 February 16, 2020, 7:20am #17

Dead interaction was a really bad idea, it got removed for good and won’t come back.

"Why remove dead people interactions from living abilities?"

Dead chat/interactions has always been random and unreliable. There’s no fun factor in forcing people to stay after dead to claim your gp. Died night 1? Still get some gp out of it without waiting til the end.

We now offer a % gp after the match ends if you left while dead , which discourages players to stay even-more if they don’t want to (most players left or afk’d immediately after death, anyway)

We want dead chat to be more of a spectate/optional thing than being a core part of the game. It’s one of those things that’s “cool at first”, but you realize very fast is both useless and frustrating~ for example, we couldn’t reward a % leaving while dead while these features existed.

TL;DR: We don’t want to hold your gp hostage by making you stay (if dead). Dead interactions were unreliable and too random (mostly bad-random). Died n1? np - quit, queue back up, get gp later when the old game ends.
