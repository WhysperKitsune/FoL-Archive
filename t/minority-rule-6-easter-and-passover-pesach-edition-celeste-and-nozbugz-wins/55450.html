Minority Rule: Easter and Passover (Pesach) Edition

Welcome to Minority Easter and Passover (Pesach) Edition

The rules are pretty simple. I will present you a choice of several roomsâ€”just choose one to enter! If you pick the room with the most people, you will all meet a horrible demise!

How to play?
Every round, you will be given a list of rooms to choose from. PM me your pick privately. if you pick the room a majority of the players pick, you will be eliminated. If there is a tie, then both rooms are considered a majority and players who picked both are eliminated. The game will end once there are two or fewer players left. Do /Join to join. Feel free to PM or DM each other as long as you include me.

Potential Rooms

Air - A random picker becomes light.
Betrayal - If this room is tied for the minority, it gains infinite picks and becomes the majority.
Crucifixion - Everyone who picks this dies, even if it is not the majority.
Dice - Everyone who picks this is given a random status.
Splitting the Sea - Everyone who picks this counts as half.
Earth - A random picker becomes heavy.
Evil - All pickers becomes infected with the 10 Plagues.
False - This room does nothing, but it takes the name of another random room.
Flux - If this room is the minority, half of its pickers will change to the room with the second-most number of pickers in it.
Force - A random person who did pick this will change to a random room in the current pool.
Gravity - A random person who did not pick this will change to this room.
Testament with God - A random picker becomes a Moses. All other pickers become infected with the 10 Plagues
Illusion - This room takes the effect of another secret item before the start of the round.
Insanity - A random picker will be Blending In.
Life - If nobody picks this, everybody dies.
Luck - If this room is the majority, then one random picker will survive. If this is not the majority, then one random picker will die.
Majority - If this room is the majority, the items with the second most votes will be the majority.
Multiply - Everyone who picks this counts as two.
Mundane - This room literally does nothing.
Murder - One random picker becomes a Barbaric Roman.
Mystery - Everyone who picks this is given a random status. They are not informed which status they received.
Permanency - If picked by anyone, this room will also be in the pool for the next round. Once this room is the majority, it is removed from the pool for all future rounds.
Protection - All pickers are immune to death this round for any reason other than the majority. A random picker becomes a bodyguard.
Purity - Everyone who picks this loses all statuses.
Rejuvinate - Everyone who picks this becomes healthy.
Spite - A random picker will extract the 10 Plagues.
Secrecy - All pickers become cultists.

Potential Statuses

Bodyguard - Nobody who picks the same room as you can die for any reason other than the majority. If anyone who would die without your intervention is saved, you will die instead.
Cultist - If there are any cultists, then there will be an item that only its members may pick. It will be denoted in [brackets].
10 Plagues - You will die next round unless your status is removed or your death is prevented. This status is removed afterwards.
Heavy - Your pick counts as two picks for the next round. This status is removed afterwards.
Healthy - You cannot die for any reason except for the majority. Once your death is prevented by this status, this status will be removed.
Blending In - You must pick the majority or you will die.
Light - You do not count as a picker for the next round. This status is removed afterwards.
Moses - You are immune to death. Once your death is prevented by this status, this status will be removed.
Barbaric Romans - A random person who picks the same item as you will die.

Order of application goes:

  1. Effects of the Room
  2. Existing Statuses
  3. Majority calculated
  4. New Statuses

  2. Every round one room will be labelled as FESTIVE aswell as having itâ€™s normal effect. If you choose this room you will receive an egg :. When it gets to the last two players alive (assuming they are not each others buddy in which case they would instantly both win), whoever has the most eggs wins, along with their buddy of course. This is to reduce the chance of a tie which would result in 4 people winning. If the last two players have the same number of eggs they both win, along with both of their partners.
  3. You can share eggs with your partner but only when you are both alive. If your partner gains an egg in a festive room but then dies that same round, the eggs they gained that round and any other tangerines of theirs are lost when they die. If you wish to trade you must say so so in a PM including both of you and myself, or publicly here. Both of you must agree.
