Aquillion January 14, 2018, 1:11am #12

…anyway, putting the pointless quibbling above aside, yeah, it should probably be edited into the racism / homophobic entry; or that could just be changed to “hate speech”, although I tend to interpret that as more extreme than some things that would still fall under racism / homophobic.

The wording of the report options matters because people sometimes won’t report things that they don’t think will be enforced (especially since invalid reports can backfire). So the “it makes it seem like this is acceptable” problem is real and serious, even though I think it’s clear that that wasn’t the intent.

I’m pretty sure the full TOS makes it clear that it’s not acceptable - but few people are going to read this, so you’re gonna get people clicking “report”, reading the options, and saying “oops, I guess it isn’t reportable.”

(Also, perhaps “sexually explicit” needs to be an option, since I’m pretty sure that’s against the ToS, too. Again, an ‘other’ option would be ok, but I think it’s best to have the report system itself make it clear what is and isn’t reportible to discourage invalid reports and encourage good ones.)

EDIT: Going over the rules, they say:

  1. Swearing is permitted. However, keep it classy – don’t overdo it and no targeting people.
  2. No hating, bullying, harassing, stalking, or being overly creepy (esp. sexualizing).
  3. No hate speech.
  4. No spamming.
  5. No spoilers.

…I cut out some that aren’t relevant to the reporting system. Comparing them, “Targeted Harassment” covers 1 and parts of 2, while the “Racism / Homophobic” option seems to cover 3. So perhaps Hate Speech is the correct term, since that’s what the rules actually forbid. Maybe something like “Hate Speech (Racism, Homophobia, Misogyny, etc.)” to make it clear that it’s not all-inclusive and only lists the most common report reasons.

Also there’s no option to report spoilers, although I don’t know how frequently that’s needed. Clearly the intent is that going all “SNAPE KILLS DUMBLEDORE” for a recent movie is reportable, though, and IMHO the list of report options should reflect what’s reportable so even people who haven’t read the ToS know that.
