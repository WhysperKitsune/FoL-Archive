[Class suggestion] The adventurer + the explorer

Class/Lore/Fanfic
RopeStringFace September 12, 2017, 8:42pm #1

Inspired by Tormental’s treasure hunter

Upon the stumbling across a large network of tunnels below the castle, the sheriff hired the adventurer to clear it out of any valuables; he planned to block it off so no conspirators would be able to use it to get around undetected, but didn’t want to risk blowing up any thing valuable.

The adventurer
Blue dragon investigative
Passive: Dungeon crawler
The prince will be unable to execute you; he will be told “Your prisoner escaped through a secret passage!” He will still expend a use of execute
Night ability: open chest
infinite uses
Open a chest below the targets room. You cannot open a chest below a targets room you have already opened. Something will happen based on the target’s class type (see below class card)

Types of chests:
-Killer class: Trapped chest! You were poisoned with deadly nightshade!
-Support class: You found an elixir and drank it.
If poisoned or bleeding: “You drank the potion, and suddenly your (wound disappeared/poison was cured)!”
-Social class: “You found a magical transcript, filled with all the whispers that happened the previous day!” (you will be shown a letter looking like the old noble letter, filled with “x whispered to y: message” for all the whispers from previous day.
-offensive class: “You found a stockpile of booze! This will take a few days to drink” (you will be occupied the next two nights)
-Investigative or special: “You found a letter sealed with a symbol of a (black rose/eye depending if unseen or cult). It say that the (class that was converted this night) was successfully initiated!”
-Wildcard/framed: “The chest is full of sponges. They aren’t edible.”

Converts into the explorer!

The explorer
unseen support
Passive: Dungeon crawler
same as adventurer’s
Day ability: Map
infinite uses
Find a way through the network of tunnels for a secret entrance into your targets room; the assassin will be able to bypass their visit immunity the following night, and if he selects to assassinate them, will be able to do so with occupy immunity.
Night ability: Open chest
same as the adventurer’s
