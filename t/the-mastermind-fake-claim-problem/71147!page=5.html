Sketch July 30, 2018, 9:36pm #94

I’ve been reading along with this thread for a while and want to throw in my 2 cents about fakeclaiming.

The reality is that BD get occupied all the time. Sometimes you are unable to visit somebody. Things go wrong where your action doesn’t go through, and that is perfectly normal. However, when you are claiming Butler or Drunk and cannot claim “occupied”, you remove a vital tool in your kit to obfuscate. So you must either write fake information or claim you could not visit them. You lost 1 of your 3 tools to work with when creating a fake will, and be forced to input fake info that could catch the eye of BD. The biggest mistake you can do as scum is to never include “occupied” or “unable to visit” in any of your journals.

Claiming any occupation immune role like Butler/Butler is incredibly limiting and is strictly a -EV play in most games. Not only is your ability one that gives feedback and is generally easy to prove, the passive of real Butlers/Drunk prevent you from writing “occupied” in your journal.

Drunk is definitely worse of a claim than Butler because of the royal blood passive. Almost all Drunk claims are polarized to Drunk/Alcoholic/Assassin. Even Assassin’s may be wary of claiming their previous role of Alcoholic if they haven’t debauched anybody in a while.

Claiming an Investigative as the MM is your best bet in the grand picture. It allows you to claim occupied, AND you can turn things in your favor by mislynching in MYLO with false accusations. Using your claim as an Investigative gives you a 4th tool in your toolbox. It’s more difficult to mislynch somebody when you are claiming Physician/Butler than when you are claiming Princess/Sheriff as calling a BD out as scum with fake results.

I personally claim Observer as MM. Princess and Sheriff are fine claims, but I enjoy the challenge and flexibility of the Observer. Observer is the hardest role to play in the game, and it is the hardest to fake. But if you pay enough attention to the game, make educated guesses with where people are going, are good at lying and CC’ing, and have a little bit of luck as well, Observer can be a grand slam claim for MM in any game.
