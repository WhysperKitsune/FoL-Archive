Class/Lore/Fanfic
Shrimpy July 25, 2018, 9:13pm #1
  • Knight

If you don’t get flirted or coldsteeled by the real Knight, this is a good fake claim.

  • Obs
  • Princess
  • Sheriff

You’d think these classes would be decent fake-claims because like MM, they are Special/Investigative and not occupy immune, but Obs is impossible to fake. Princess gets outed as soon as King dies, and Sheriff gets outed because there’s usually two real Sheriffs.

  • Phys

Very easy to write fake logs for, but almost every game has two real Phys.

  • CW

As impossible to fake as Obs, since you can’t fake the N1 swap that every good CW performs.

  • Butler
  • Drunk

Impossible to fake and you have nothing in common with these classes.

  • Hunter

Unique, you can’t fake claim it when there’s a real Hunter and you have no way of knowing if there is before it’s too late.

  • Mystic
  • Noble

Impossible to fake.

Conclusion:

Knight is the only well-rounded BD fakeclaim for Mastermind. All the others require more luck than social manipulation to maintain your cover, like there not being 2 real Sheriffs or you randomly converted one N1 and he changed his claim upon becoming Assassin.

I believe this is another illustration of why we need new BD classes in the game.
