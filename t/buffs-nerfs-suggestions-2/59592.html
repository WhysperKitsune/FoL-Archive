Solic April 24, 2018, 12:02pm #5

Herbalist

  • Support, current Herbalist sucks

Alcholic

  • Not support. Prince should be infallible. It is typically better for the alcoholic to debauch protectives of confirmed BD, simultaneously framing those protectives. It is actually sometimes smart to debauch a sheriff, because it takes heat off MM and MM can just reconvert the third slot, so this would buff the alcoholic’s survival, but not the unseen’s winrate.

Nightwatch

  • Not support. Observers/Nightwatches are super strong already and I feel this change borders on OP.

Alchemist

  • Not support. Attacks going through night immunity exist for a reason. Prince should remain infallible in this regard as well. Stoneskin could be lowered to 3 uses. Bombs should not go above 2. Imagine the kill per nights of 2 yolobombing alchemists and the regular scum. 2 uses makes sure that Alchemists have to play the game and have a good fakeclaim or confirmed heals, before they can just end out the game immediately by killing everyone.

Merc

  • Not sure, it might be difficult to coordinate with a possessor that jumps to different players.

Duchess

  • Support, more day abilities are nice and finding neutrals is a huge benefit to the unseen. Not more than 1 use in my opinion though, because it can quickly turn overpowered.
