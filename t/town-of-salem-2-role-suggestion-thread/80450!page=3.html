JakeTheWolfie March 14, 2020, 3:53pm #47

Triplet of Peace

Neutral Benign
Description: The survivor of the Witch Trials, this innocent twin wants to prevent any other people from getting harmed, just like their other family.
Ability 1 (Night) - Prevent one player from dying to the trial tomorrow. (1 day cooldown)
Ability 2 (Day) - Prevent another player from being lynched, instead revealing their role. Anyone on the wagon loses their vote for the rest of the day, and Majority will be lowered to compensate. (1 use)
Investigative Results:
Sheriff: Your target is not suspicious.
Investigator: Your target has lost family in these trials. They must be one of the Triplets
Coven Leader: Your target wishes to stop judicial killing, because of those they have lost. They must be the Triplet of Peace

Prevent the lynch of a Town member and a Coven Member

Triplet of Magic

Coven Power
Description: Damn every little one of them! How could they just rip your family from you and call them servants of the dark arts? You’ll show them the truest of dark arts. Then they’ll all pay.
Ability 1 (Night) - Use a fellow coven member’s ability, whether alive or dead. You cannot use the same ability twice in a row. (Inf uses)
Ability 2 (Night) - Kill one player. Anyone visiting them will be roleblocked. (2 uses)
Investigative Results:
Sheriff: Your target is a member of the Coven!
Investigator: Your target has lost family in these trials. They must be one of the Triplets.
Coven Leader: Your target wishes to end those who have harmed their family, because of those they have lost. They must be the Triplet of Magic.

Triplet of Leadership

Town Power
Description: You didn’t want to. But it happened, and you couldn’t save them. The damn witches poisoned their minds. After years of political gain, it’s time for us to take down the dirty servants of evil.
Ability 1 (Day) - Choose two people. They will be the lynch candidates today. No-Lynch is also an acceptable option. (3 uses)
Investigative Results:
Sheriff: Your target is not suspicious.
Investigator: Your target has lost family in these trials. They must be one of the Triplets.
Coven Leader: Your target wishes to end those in the judicial system. They must be the Triplet of Leadership.

Additional notes: Only one Triplet may appear in a game, but they do not have to spawn.
