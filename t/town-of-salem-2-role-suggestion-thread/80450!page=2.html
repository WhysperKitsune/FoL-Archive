Citizen

Town Citizen

You are a Citizen. Your only weapons are your voice and your vote.

You must kill the Coven

Power

Jailor

Town Power

You are the Jailor, responsible for locking up criminals and executing the worst of them.
With the town in disarray, you’ve decided to use your jail and execution room to return order to this place.

Every night you may jail and potentially execute somebody.
To Jail somebody, you must message your Host during the day telling them who you would like to Jail.
If you do not do this before the day is over - you jail no one.
The prisoner will be allowed a 1000 character limit to share a defense with you.
(NOTE: Jailed Coven will lose the ability to communicate with other Coven, and they will be unable to see the Coven Chat.)
If you jail somebody they will be roleblocked and cannot use their night ability, even if they are normally roleblock immune.
However, all night actions fail against your target.
An execution will kill anybody, even roles immune to other forms of night killing.
If you execute a Town member, you can not execute anybody else for the remainder of the game. You will still be able to jail people though.
You can execute up to 3 times.
You can’t execute on the first night.

You win with the Town
You must kill the Coven
You must kill Arsonists
You must kill Serial Killers
You must kill Stalkers
You must kill Bombers
You may spare anyone else

Mayor

Town Power

You are the Mayor of this town, forced to into hiding because of the Coven invasion.

You have the ability to reveal your Mayor’s badge, showing everybody who you are and giving you three votes instead of one.
However, doing so will make it impossible for the Doctor to heal you and you will be a top target for the Coven.
To reveal your role, simply say I am the Mayor! in bold during the daytime, and your Game Host will officially reveal your role to the rest of the town.

You win with the Town
You must kill the Coven
You must kill Arsonists
You must kill Serial Killers
You must kill Stalkers
You must kill Bombers
You may spare anyone else

You may bring one dead town member back to life.
You may not bring back a Player whose role was not revealed, even if they are town.
You may only use this ability once, so use it wisely.
Should you die on the night you decide to bring someone back from the grave, the powers that be have decided to finish the job you started and make sure that he comes back.

This can also kill people who are normally night immune, like the Serial Killer or the Arsonist.
You are immune to attacks, douses, stalking, and any other harmful night actions while on alert.

Strength.

Neutral Killing

You are nuts! Psychotic! Crazy! Mental! Insane! Over the edge! Uncontrollable! Irrational!
You were once in an asylum, in solitary confinement for first-degree murder. Recently released, you have emerged with a new look at life: not to kill just anybody, but to kill everybody!
Your thirst for blood and murder will never be satisfied and although the Coven Leader approached you, you declined and said that you will kill the Coven too!

Every night you may kill one person.
You are unkillable at night. If somebody tries to attack you, they will receive a message saying that you were immune.
If someone tries to roleblock you, you will attack them instead of your chosen target.
You win if it is just you and the Coven Leader remaining.

You win with Coven

Stalker

Neutral Killing

You are a creepy person who hides in the shadows and follows people around them until they die.
You are an incredibly feared individual who can instill paranoia in even the most hardened of people.

Each phase, you may stalk one person.
Each of your stalked targets will commit suicide four phases after you begin stalking them.
Your targets will not know they are being stalked until they commit suicide.
You may stalk a new target each phase, and your past targets will all commit suicide over the paranoia that you have caused them.
Your method of killing ignores night immunity, and any Town Protectives trying to stop this attack will fail to do so.
You are night immune. You are also immune to stalking.
If someone tries to roleblock you (including the Jailor), you will stalk your roleblocker and your original target.
Once you begin stalking a target, nothing can save them unless you die before the night they decide to commit suicide.
You win if every other living Player is Stalked.

You must kill the Coven
You must kill the Town
You may spare anyone else
