Gifts:

Extra Deny- grants an extra deny to deny a challenge (see Challenge Phase) (each player starts with one deny)

Extra Switch- grants an extra pokeball switch (see Pokemon centre phase) (each player starts with one switch)

Fishing Rod- grants the player an extra fishing rod. You may use this during the Pokémon Centre Phase to have 3 attempts to guess a players role. If any of those guesses are correct, you are told you have a ‘bite’ otherwise you get nothing. (You can use fishing rods on players and roles that have guess immunity or are otherwise unguessable. You start with one fishing rod)

Free Tip off to Team Rocket. - during the upcoming Pokémon Centre Phase, you may make a guess as to a player’s Pokemon. If you are correct, they will have it stolen by Team Rocket and eliminated. You may only choose one of this gift per round.

Visit from Officer Jenny - The presence of this Police Woman will stop Team Rocket stealing your Pokémon during the next Pokémon Centre phase.

Visit from Nurse Joy- you are not a valid Rotom or Delibird target during the Voting Phase - Nurse Joy will also prevent anyone switching with your ball

Gift Reserve - This gift can be redeemed at any point later in the game for a gift of your choosing. You can only hold up to 3 of this gift at a time.

Lotto ticket- if you are the only player to select this gift, you receive double gifts this round.

Voting Phase:

The Voting Phase will still occur during the first round, but no players will be voted and no judgement will occur.
Any player who lost a battle or contest during the past Challenge Phase is a valid vote target.
The player with the most votes at the end of the phase will be eliminated.
In the event of a tie, the first player who reached the tie amount is eliminated
The Magmortar, Tsareena and Delibird may act during this phase
Players may also select gifts during this phase.
For each challenge participated in (no matter if won or lost) the player earns one gift. Gifts are revealed at the end of the Voting Phase.
This phase will last 72 hours or until a majority vote is reached and all gifts have been claimed. The first voting phase, at my discretion, may end early if all gifts have been claimed.
