This was my original Baron and Duke

The Baron

Blue Dragon Support

(Converts to Duke)

Passives

Royal Blood:
(Everyone should know what this is)

Entitlement:
If you become the next King, you will keep your abilities alongside the abilities of succeeding Kings. (Not Starting King)

Abilities

Royal Loan: (N) (4)
Choose another player. Any limited night ability they use tonight will not lose a charge when used.

Not included for both Baron and Duke:
Prince’s Execute if Prince killed Blue Dragon
Reanimate (In whatever form)

Kinship: (D) (2)
Choose any player and give them the Royal Blood passive if they do not have it. If that class already has two passives, they may not inherit Royal Blood.

The Duke

Unseen Support

Passives

Royal Blood:
(Everyone should know what this is)

Entitlement:
If you become the next King, you will keep your abilities when you become King alongside a succeeding King’s abilities.

Abilities

Kinship: (D) (1)
Choose any player and give them the Royal Blood passive if they do not have it. If that class already has two passives, they may not inherit Royal Blood.

Coup: (N) (1)
Make Assassin’s attack tonight bypass heal and night immunity if it targets the King.
(Not part of Entitlement passive when you become the King)

Royal Loan: (N) (3)
Choose another player. Any limited night ability they use tonight will not lose a charge when used.

It’s the “Royal” Support class, being the only Royal who wouldn’t have to give up his abilities to become King, but the other abilities are still different from a Starting King’s abilities.
