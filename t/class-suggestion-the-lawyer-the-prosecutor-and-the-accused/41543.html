[Class Suggestion] The Lawyer, The Prosecutor and The Accused

Class/Lore/Fanfic
psychoneirik March 28, 2018, 7:36pm #1

Hello, here is an idea for three neutral classes that always spawn together: The Lawyer (Neutral Social), The Prosecutor (Neutral Social) and The Accused (Neutral Offensive).

The Accused is the main of these classes, it spawns with a random alignment (similar to King, but it is either BD or Evil, cant have neutral alignment) and can either help or hinder the BD.

The Lawyer and The Prosecutor must convince the court that the Accused is either on their side (The Lawyer) or against them (The Prosecutor). Each of them has their own tricks that can help them win over the court.

To avoid the court from either ignoring or auto executing the Accused, he can either reward or punish the BD depending on their choice, which forces them to pay attention and be careful.
