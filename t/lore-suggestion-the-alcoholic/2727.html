[Lore Suggestion] The Alcoholic

Class/Lore/Fanfic

Deep in the light of the castle, layed a tavern. A tavern were they held wines of low - high alcohol levels. There was the brother of the prince, one that was of the older age then his. The Younger prince would always go out to the tavern and drink, drink, drink. And due to his royal heritage he was able to do as he wanted, for to him he had the most money in the world. Until one day, one day that changed his live forever…

Younger Prince: Ey! Ima go down to the tavern and have a drink
Butler: What ever you say my master…

The Younger Prince walked down the stairs to the tavern

He opens the door

Everyone in the tavern looks disappointed

The Younger prince walks over to the bar

Younger Prince: Ey! Bartender could I have a drink?
Bartender: sighs Alright, here you go.
Slides a glass of wine to the Younger Prince

The Prince Quickly Picks up the glass

He drinks it all down

Younger Prince: Another one!

The Bartender Sighs, and gives the Younger Prince another drink

This continues until about the 20 glass.

Younger Prince: Ey! Youbettergivesmeanotherrdrink!
Bartender: No sir, you are way to drunk
Younger Prince: Whatareyoucallingmedrunk? Iaintnodrunk!

The Younger prince throws his glass at the bartender

Bartender: Alright! That’s it!
Bartender: Until you can control your drinking habits I here by ban you from this tavern!

Younger prince is shocked

Younger Prince: Whatdidyousay?Whatdidyousaytome?Iamtheprince!

Bartender: No, you are just a good-for nothing drunk!
Bartender: Hey! However escorts this drunk out of this tavern gets a free drink!

Multiple Men stood up

They all grabbed hold on the drunk/Younger prince

They then proceeded over to the door and threw the drunk/older prince out

The Drunk-prince fell onto the ground and blacked out

He a-woke in his quarters

Drunk/Younger Prince: Eh what happened?
Drunk/Younger Prince: Oh wait, I was banned from my favorite tavern!
Drunk/Younger Prince: I could never go sober!
Butler: Um Sir?
Drunk/Younger Prince: Yes?
Butler: The king has revoked your rights to be prince, but you are still a member of the royal family.
Drunk/Younger Prince: WHAT! I am his soN!

Throws something at the Butler

Butler: I am just going to leave now

Butler Leaves the room

Nothing happens for a while, the Drunk Royal is now just sitting in his bed

Then something happened

???: Hello?
Drunk: What who are you?
???: Well, I personally think that them kicking you out of the bar was a bad decision, as well as them revoking your rights to be prince.
Drunk: I agree! Why would they ever kick someone like me out of a tavern! And It is just plain stupid that they revoked my rights!
???: I have a deal for you, I will supply you with as much alcohol as you desire? And, treat you as the prince you deserve to be.
Drunk: Already sounds like a good deal!
???: In exchange you will join a group that seeks to destroy the wicked blue dragon.
Drunk: Alright! Ill join.
???: You will?
Drunk: Ya, I never liked my father anyways, always, “Don’t drink its bad for your health” and all the stuff like that!

–
From this day forward in time the Younger Prince/Drunk was no longer Either a Prince or Drunk. For he was a alcoholic seeking to destroy all of the blue dragon and their wicked deeds, whether it be to kill the father that betrayed him. or Seek the bartender that denied him. The drunk was a member of the group known as the unseen.
