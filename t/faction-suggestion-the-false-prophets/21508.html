[Faction Suggestion] The False Prophets

Class/Lore/Fanfic
psychoneirik November 30, 2017, 1:40am #1

Lore: Gifted with the ability to see the future, the prophets used to be strong allies of the Blue Dragon, however, their power and reputation tainted them, and made them corrupt and unreliable by tricking the people with fake prophecies. The king executed most of them, but a few of them hid in the shadows. Now, they formed a faction destined to use their powers to destroy the Blue Dragon, and rise to power once again.

Description: The prophets are a faction with great conversion power but they can be easy to predict since they announce their targets. Their killing power is situational, but most of their roles have killing abilities. As the name suggests, Prophet games would involve a lot of prediction, both from the Prophets themselves and from the BD, since they can trick players by making false prophecies. Prophets are specially vulnerable to observers, and the BD investigative on Scholar games is more similar to an observer than a sheriff. The prophets always start with two roles, ‚ÄúThe Oracle‚Äù and another random prophet.

Note: Every single ‚ÄúProphecy‚Äù ability will make the prophet that used it visit its target at night (when the prophecy becomes true) even though they are day abilities, unless it is fake.

The Oracle:
Unique Special, Prophet
Passive ability 1
When you die, other roles will still be able to target you. All of your prophets can use their night ability ‚ÄúGather power: Visit the Oracle tonight to gather his powers, turning you into the new Oracle. If more than one prophet uses this ability the same day only the first to use it will visit the Oracle.‚Äù
- Day ability 1: Advent
At the end of the next night, you will visit your target and convert it to the False Prophets. When you use this ability, a message will be shown to the public telling them who will be converted. There can be a max of 3 other prophets at the same time. If you convert someone while you have the max number of prophets the oldest non-oracle prophet will die. No cool-down, infinite uses.
- Day ability 2: Fake advent
Select a player and send a public message telling the chat that said player will be converted this night. You will not visit said player and they will not be converted. Infinite uses.
- Night ability 1:
Convert someone who visits you tonight. 3 uses. If several people visit you only the first convertible player will be converted. If you convert someone while you have the max number of prophets the oldest non-oracle prophet will die. If you are dead, you can still use this ability but only once.

The Oracle has great conversion power with advent but can be predicted easily if you are not careful, since it announces who you will be visiting to convert. Fake advent can be used to trick players into wasting their abilities in someone else, or to think someone has been converted when they weren‚Äôt. You can also use fake advent on yourself and then use your night ability to trick investigative roles into visiting you and then convert them. The Oracle is not immune to death at night and if he dies the prophets can easily replace him, but an observer can easily find who visited the Oracle. To counter this, the oracle can use his night ability once after he dies, so he will have to try and predict if any investigative roles will try and check his corpse.

Prophet of death
Killer, prophet. Converts from killing roles.
Day ability 1: Prophecy of death
Select two targets. Announce to the public that one of them will die tonight. You will visit only the first target and kill it. 2 uses.
Day ability 2: Fake prophecy
Select two targets. Announce to the public that one of them will die tonight. You will not visit any of them tonight. 3 uses.
Night ability 1: Deadly countdown
After three days, your target will die. This effect goes through night immunity and cannot be healed. 1 use.
Night ability 2: Gather power
Gather power: Visit the Oracle tonight to gather his powers, turning you into the new Oracle. If more than one prophet uses this ability the same day only the first to use it will visit the Oracle. Can only be used if the Oracle is dead.

Prophet of war
Offensive, prophet. Converts from offensive roles.
Day ability 1: Prophecy of war
Announce to the public that one player will die if they leave their room tonight. They will die if they visit anyone. 2 uses.
Day ability 2: Fake prophecy
Announce to the public that one player will die if they leave their room tonight. They will not die even if they leave their room. 3 uses.
Night ability 1: Treason
The first player that visits your target will attack them. 2 uses.
Night ability 2: Gather power
Gather power: Visit the Oracle tonight to gather his powers, turning you into the new Oracle. If more than one prophet uses this ability the same day only the first to use it will visit the Oracle. Can only be used if the Oracle is dead.

Prophet of plague
Support, prophet. Converts from support roles.
Day ability 1: Prophecy of plague
Poison a target. They will die in two nights. 2 uses.
Day ability 2: Fake prophecy
Make a target think they are poisoned. The poison will disappear automatically the next night, and the target will believe they were healed. 3 uses.
Night ability 1: Faulty medicine
Your target will be immune to attacks tonight, but if they are healed they will die. 1 use.
Night ability 2: Gather power
Gather power: Visit the Oracle tonight to gather his powers, turning you into the new Oracle. If more than one prophet uses this ability the same day only the first to use it will visit the Oracle. Can only be used if the Oracle is dead.

Prophet of light
Investigative, prophet. Converts from investigative roles.
Day ability 1: Prophecy of light
Announce to the public that a player will have their class revealed to the prophets tonight. At the end of the night visit your target to reveal the exact class and faction of the player to the prophets. 2 uses.
Day ability 2: Fake prophecy
Select two targets. Announce to the public that a player will have their class revealed to the prophets tonight. The target will not be revealed to the prophets. 3 uses.
Night ability 1: Curse of the firstborn
Select a class from the list of classes (similar to defile). Any targets of said class that visit the prophets tonight will die. 1 use.
Night ability 2: Gather power
Gather power: Visit the Oracle tonight to gather his powers, turning you into the new Oracle. If more than one prophet uses this ability the same day only the first to use it will visit the Oracle. Can only be used if the Oracle is dead.

Prophet of death
Social, prophet. Converts from social roles.
Day ability 1: Prophecy of silence
Select a target. Announce to the public that if said target sends more than 3 chat messages or whispers for the rest of the day they will be unable to talk the next day. 2 uses.
Day ability 2: Fake prophecy
Select a target. Announce to the public that if said target sends more than 3 chat messages or whispers today they will be unable to talk the next day. They will still be able to talk the next day. 3 uses.
Night ability 1: Dysphonia
Target a player. If your target is put on trial tomorrow, anyone who votes execute will be unable to accuse anyone of treason the following day. 1 use.
Night ability 2: Gather power
Gather power: Visit the Oracle tonight to gather his powers, turning you into the new Oracle. If more than one prophet uses this ability the same day only the first to use it will visit the Oracle. Can only be used if the Oracle is dead.

The Scholar
Social, prophet. Converts from social roles.
Day ability 1: Skepticism
You will become immune to conversions tonight. Infinite uses, but 2-day cooldown.
Day ability 2: Scientific Method
Find out how many prophecies made today will become true. 1 use.
Night ability 1: Field Research
If a player is visited by a member of the Prophets tonight, you will be notified and you will get a list of everyone who visited your target. If you used skepticism, the player you visit tonight will also be immune to conversions. Infinite uses.

The scholar is different than investigative classes like the sheriff and the paladin, instead of finding if a target is part of the Prophets directly, he can find them by checking their targets. Since prophets usually announce their targets the scholar can easily find the prophets, however, since other BD roles will probably try to visit said target as well, you will have to figure out who is a prophet from your list of suspects. Skepticism is a powerful ability that can help you survive conversions if the Oracle tries to bait you into visiting him and to prevent conversions on someone else, but you will be vulnerable for 2 whole days after you use it. Scientific method can also help you find out if a prophecy will become true to help you avoid baits, but you can only use it once and it becomes less useful if there are many prophecies being made at the same time, since you will only know how many will become true, but not which of them.
