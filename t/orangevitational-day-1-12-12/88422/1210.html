Frequently Asked Questions

Does the Sympathizer count towards Alien majority?

No, but they also don’t count against it - due to the game being flipless, there may be situations in which a game would be definitively over with flips but it is not definitively over without them. Once there is no way for the Aliens to lose majority, the game will be called as they have won - this includes situations where the Sympathizer not voting would result in an effective tie, but not situations in which the Sympathizer not voting would result in an effective Town majority. For example, if no roles remained that could change the numbers, a 3/2/1 would not automatically end the game while a 2/2/1 would. If you would like clarification on a specific hypothetical situation, please ask in your role card.

Can Aliens carry out the factional kill alongside their night action?

Yes, Aliens may perform any night abilities their role has alongside performing their factional nightkill.

Can passives be blocked by the Cryogenics?

No. Passive abilities will not be affected by the Cryogenics.

How long are nights?

Nights will nominally be 24 hours, with actions being able to be received up until 1 hour before daystart. The exact timing of day start may be moved slightly based on host availability - we will make every effort to avoid having to do this. Somehow I deleted this from the OP, my bad.

Can the Student back up roles that were an Alien or Sympathizer?

Yes. The wording of the card was somewhat ambiguous, but the Student can back up any deceased role, regardless of their alignment.

Can the Sympathizer win without any Aliens alive?

No. The Sympathizer must survive to see an Alien win in order to win. If both Aliens die, they cannot do this, and so the game with end immediately in a town victory.
