The Possessor

Neutral Killer
Driven (Passive) - Immune to occupation at night and immune to target changing. Also allows the user to see the contents of all whispers during the day.
Blackened Shield (Passive) - Immune to death at night.
Marionette (Day) - Force a player to accuse another player and prevent them from voting further. - 3 uses
Ethereal Leap (Day) - Choose a target, and if the user is executed at daytime they will use posses on their target, bypassing healing, prevention, and night immunity, while also consuming a charge of Possess if they still have one. Ethereal Leap is usable during the night if the user is jailed, taking effect regardless if the Possessor is executed by the Prince or not. - 1 use
Puppet Strings (Night) - Control a player and force them to kill another. You will also see whispers to and from the controlled player the next day. They will not be aware that they were controlled. Note that they still act normally that night; this does not replace their action. - Infinite uses
Possess (Night) - You will take over a person’s body, leaving your old one behind. Your target can not be healed. The old body will keep the class and logbook you had before. Your first victim will appear as a class of your choice to everyone. The second will appear as the first victim’s class. Additionally, when you possess a player, you will receive their deathnote. Additionally, you will gain your target’s passive while in their body. - 3 uses
Your objective is to defeat the Blue Dragon, Unseen, Cult and any neutrals that dare stand in your way.
