Alcoholic Change

Class/Lore/Fanfic
Yattien February 11, 2018, 5:54pm #1

I’d like to suggest an alteration to the Alcoholics Debauchery.

Right now it works the exact same as the Drunks. However, this can prove very dangerous for the Alcoholic, who might (like I did) Deb a Sheriff and get outted instantly.

I suggest changing the Alcoholic Deb slightly and make it so that the Alcoholic appears BD if they deb a Sheriff. That way the risk of doing it by accident is removed. The Alcoholic wouldn’t KNOW they debbed a Sheriff either so might not do it again (and be found) and if they do know then a Sheriff would become suspicious as to why the same Drunk is forcing them to change their target over and over again.

I find this change would be most useful, as Alcoholic deb is less useful than the Drunks in terms of finding killers. The Assassin isn’t going to be debbed (nor would they want to do this), so all you can possibly find with it is the Reaper or a yolo alchemist.

It might be personal bias but I found myself useless as Alcoholic when I got it. I think I debbed a Sheriff instantly, one that was not out, so I didn’t know to avoid them. Hope this suggestion is considered
