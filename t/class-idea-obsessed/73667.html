Obsessed
Neutral Special

Notes:
Starts off with a target, they will not be notified that you exist, however you will be notified that they’re your target, you win once you and your target is alive at the end of the game.

__
Passive 1 - Shy: You can’t whisper to your target.
Passive 2 - Persistence:Death Immune at night, additionally, if your target dies, you will commit suicide the night after they die.

Night Ability 1 - Stalk: Stalk your target, you will learn some information about them every night in this order
(Learn their class type> Learn their faction> Learn their class)…
If they are attacked or converted, instead prevent their attacker/converter (Infinite uses)
Night Ability 2 - Envy: Kill a player, bypassing death immunity (3 uses)
__
Day Ability 1 - Playful Pressure: Force a player to vote for someone and prevent further voting from them ( 2 uses)

Win Condition: Survive to see your target win.
Additional Notes:
Your target may not be the starting king, the prince, the fool or the neutral killer
