The Psychic

Unique Blue Dragon Social

(Converts to current Illusionist)

Passives

Astral Medium: Anyone connected to the Astral Network may talk to each other at night anonymously. If they are in jail, they cannot hear or speak. Passive remains even if class changes.

Abilities

Focus Link: (D) (2)
If Alter Link is on a cooldown, you may reset the cooldown and use Alter Link tonight.

Alter Link: (N) (oo) (1C)
Choose a player and connect them to the Astral Network. If they were already in it, remove them from the Astral Network. The Network has a maximum of 4 players at once. There is a 1 day cooldown.

Killer Rework

The Acolyte

Cult Killer (Lower chance to spawn as Underling than Seeker, Invoker, Ritualist)

Passives

Blood Offering:
If you die because of bleed, the Cult Leader will gain an extra use of Eradicate.

Abilities

Hemorrhage: (D) (1)
The Cult Leader’s next Rupture will kill someone that night, not in two nights. The player will see that they are bleeding and hemorrhaged.
The Cult Leader will be notified when Hemorrhage is activated.

Transfusion: (N) (3)
Choose a player and protect them. Their attackers will die and you will begin to bleed at night. The attack bypasses night immunity.

Crimson Curse: (N) (1)
Choose a player. If no one is currently bleeding, you will make them bleed. They will die in one night. Bypasses night immunity.
