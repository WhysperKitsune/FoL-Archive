Class/Lore/Fanfic

The Snitch

Unseen Special

Passive (Pay Attention!) - If you get occupied/redirected , you will convert the person who occupied/redirected you.(This only works on The Butler’s Serve Wine and The Drunk’s Debauchery. If you get occupied with Concentrated Wine or Happy Hour , this passive will be lost. The higher number takes priority). You are Immune to Death at Night.
Passive (Membership) - You will appear as a member of The Blue Dragon every other night.(Until N6)

Day Ability (Deathwish) - If you die at night/get executed in court , you will empower one of your Unseen member’s abilities.(1 Use - Only the starting Snitch has this ability. The other Unseen members will not be notified when you use this.)
Day Ability (Expose) - Send a message to all living Unseen members.(2 Uses)

Night Ability (Convert) - Convert a player to The Unseen.(Infinite Uses - [1 Day Cooldown] - Member limit : 3.)
Night Ability (Documents) - Reveal the classes of the players you select.(0 Uses - The More Charges you have , the more people you can select to find out their class | Charges are gained upon successful conversions.)
Goal: The Unseen’s goal.

Buffs

The Alcoholic next 2 uses of Debauchery will bypass Redirection Immunity. They will gain 1 extra use of Happy Hour.
The Assassin next 2 uses of Assassinate will bypass Night Immunity. They will gain 1 extra use of Nightshade.
The Aristocrat next use of Noble Twin will not use a charge of the ability.
The Duchess will gain 1 extra use of Dark Wisp. They will become Occupy Immune and it will not be shown that they are Occupy Immune.
The Enforcer next use of Frenzy will protect The Unseen from Death tonight.
The Herbalist will gain 1 extra use of every ability.
The Illusionist will frame the person that they visit.
The Marshal will determine the exact class of a player when using Probe. If they find a Neutral , Probe will be on a 2 day cooldown.
The Nightwatch will gain 2 extra uses of Watch Carefully.
The Poacher will gain 1 extra use of every ability.
The Sage will become Occupy Immune when using Tornado.
The Servant next 2 uses of Serve Wine will bypass Occupation Immunity. They will gain 1 extra use of Concentrated Wine.

(Optional) When another Unseen member becomes The Assassin , they will not gain The Assassin’s buffs.

Feedback is welcome , as always.
