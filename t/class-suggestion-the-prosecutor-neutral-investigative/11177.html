[Class suggestion]The Prosecutor (Neutral investigative)

Class/Lore/Fanfic
NuclearBurrito November 8, 2017, 9:16pm #1

The prosecutor - Neutral investigative

Passive: Diligent - Occupy immune

Day Ability: Background check - Learn your targets class immediately. (Unlimited)

Night Ability 1: Tampler - Your target will have thier invest results flipped (BD <-> Unseen/Cult Neutral -> BD) (3 uses)

Night Ability 2: Silver toung - Make your first target target the second. Ignores occupy immunity. Will fail if they did nothing or if it wasn’t a valid target. (Unlimited)

Goal: Ensure that there are more executions than night kills when the day ends.

(Unique)

Notes: I mostly wanted to make a neutral invest who’s ability’s directly help his goals (forming a basis to accuse people on and learning who they need to control to slow night killing) while having a goal other than “Find X classes”.
