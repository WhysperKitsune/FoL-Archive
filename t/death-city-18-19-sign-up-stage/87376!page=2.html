Sup.
Here’s the map of Chapter 1.

Here are the locations:

Emmanuel Dormitories: Big building in the top left side of the map. Basically has the dorms and the trial rooms where you can start trials that will last 24 hours, and the accused cannot leave the trial until the verdict is given. To vote a player must be present in the trial hall.

Emmanuel Plaza: The plaza in front of the dormitories. Works similarly to the cafeteria in Danganronpa.

Emmanuel Annex: The small building besides the Emmanuel Dormitories.

Emmanuel Stairway: A large set of stairs right below the Emmanuel Plaza.

Emmanuel Watchtower: The tower E of the plaza.

Moringtal Pedestrian Street: The lower-leftmost place on the map.

Centennial Library: Large building close to the pedestrian street.

Fairway Railnet: Right NE of the Library.

Sheridan Bridge: Bridge N of the Railnet.

Sewage Treatment Plant: Leftmost building on the eastern section of the map.

Red Dragon Diner: Red building on the NE of the map.

Camille Park: Green area in the E.

Glory Plaza: Bottom right of the map. Where you may find the Gates of Glory.
