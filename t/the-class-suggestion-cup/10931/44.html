[Class Suggestion] The Outcast / The Vagrant

[The Outcast]

[Neutral Offensive]

The Outcast is a Unique Neutral “Offensive” class. They must live to see two to five classes with “Royal Blood” die or be executed for treason. The Outcast cannot become King, even when converted. The Outcast’s converted class is [The Vagrant]. Not able to be converted if win condition is already met. If a member of the Evil Faction contains someone of “Royal Blood”, you will slay them the night you are converted.

Passive Ability 1:

[Revenge of the Outcast] - If found guilty of treason, kill the current king before your execution. (Can provide a win if current king has “Royal Blood” and previous “Royal Blood” classes were killed.)

Passive Ability 2:

[Scouting the Kingdom] - Unable to be visited the first three nights.

Day Ability 1:

[Royal Seal] (3 Uses) - Seal a target’s Night Abilities for one night if they have “Royal Blood” (does not inform the Outcast if it succeeded or not, and only informs target at night start).

Day Ability 2:

[No Kings Live] (1 Use) - Only available for use the day a new King is elected. You will slay the new King tonight without fail. The Court will be told who the Outcast is the next day.

Night Ability 1:

[Search for Royalty] (4 Uses) - Occupy a target. If the target has “Royal Blood”, the Outcast will be informed and also frame the target, but will not be occupied and cannot be visited the next night. If the target has “Royal Blood” and is “Evil”, you will attack the target.

Night Ability 2:

[Barricade] (2 Uses) - Barricade the door, occupying all players who visit you. You will know who visited you, and if they contain “Royal Blood”, you will be informed but not know which visitor contains it.

Lore:

The Kingdom exiled you after you accused the King of being evil for slaying your family. You vowed you would ruin him and his kin. Seven years later, the opportunity arose.

Win Condition:

The Outcast must live to see two to five classes with “Royal Blood” die or be executed for treason.

Comments:

This class, although primarily seeming against the Blue Dragon, also will definitely kill Evil Royal Blood, helping the Blue Dragon. A true Neutral.

[The Vagrant] (Converted Outcast)

[Unseen Offensive]

The Vagrant is an “Offensive” Class that belongs to the Unseen faction. It is converted from the Outcast.

Day Ability 1:

[Coin Toss] (2 Uses) - Choose a target, one of their Night Abilities are sealed for one night.

Night Ability 1:

[Beg For Coins] (Infinite Uses) - Occupy a player. Can only be used once per target.

Night Ability 2:

[Redirect] (2 Uses) - Cause all players targeting a player to target a different player instead. You will be immune to occupation tonight.

Win Condition:

Generic unseen win

Comments:

A Neutral that can be converted is always nice. Not too overpowered abilities, and punishes the unseen or cult as a result.

Please let me know what you think!

My entry. The Outcast is a Neutral Offensive class, with a win condition of Royal Blood dying. Depending on how many classes in the current game have “Royal Blood”, the Outcast needs to survive see two to five of them die. The Outcast cannot instantly kill Blue Dragon, but can instantly kill Evil classes who have “Royal Blood”. The Outcast can be converted, but will kill any evil faction member if they contain “Royal Blood” when he is converted. (I.E. Princess gets converted, original assassin dies, Outcast gets converted, Outcast kills Princess the same night, becoming the Assassin)

The Vagrant is the Unseen version of the Outcast, and functions primarily as an occupier, but also has an inverted debauchery.
