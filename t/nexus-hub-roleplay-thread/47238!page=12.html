Callinety Falls

The Entrance to Nexus Hub from Callinety, is what could be mistaken as a normal tunnel. With concrete floor and walls, with luminous orange light dimly lighting it. Attempts to put a regulation to enter the tunnel, are useless. The tunnel itself seems to resist any attempt to regulate passage using it.

Many people pass through it everyday. Today is not an exception. However, many of them provide a wide distance between themselves and a tall old man with blood dripping down his coat. People whisper his name behind his back.

Dragon.

He himself, does not care of the attention. When he arrive in front of the tunnel, he calmly lets a bullet off into the air.

Murmurs turn to silent. Caution turns to tension and fear.

He doesn’t care. What matter is, it becomes silent.

“Anyone coming here to answer my call, raise your hand. If there isn’t any, I’ll kill everyone here for failing my expectation.”

His mood is still sour.
