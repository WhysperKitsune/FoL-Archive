For a while, apart from the glowing temple, no visible change can be seen on Arthas. Suddenly, the priest lets out a shout and forcibly presses Arthas chest. Ignoring the writhe of pain, she utters a stringof words, while her hands perform a series of movement above his body.

With time, the movement turns more subtle, and her words even fainter. Arthas breathing becomes more relaxed with every passing minutes.

A circle mark appears on Arthas temple, radiating a blue hue. The priest takes a deep sigh and tries to stand up. She wobbles toward a chair in the room and sleeps on it, exhausted.

The whole process takes some time over three hours.
