#Note: Just because I said this class is “silly” doesn’t mean I don’t think it could work in Throne of Lies, the mechanics just seem a little silly, and perhaps it would have to be restricted to a more chaotic or silly gamemode

#The Diseased
At Death’s Door(Passive) - You will die the night after receiving this class
Horrid Stench(Passive) - You are immune to occupation
Sneeze(Day) - Sneeze on a player, they will not perform their day ability - Infinite uses
Infect(Night) - Convert another player into The Diseased, your class will cease to be The Diseased once you have done this, unless your original class was The Diseased

Objective: See the disease continue to the last 2 players

Notes: You can only win if your disease reaches the final 2, the other Diseased player will also win
If you die and you did not infect anyone, the disease has lost and so have you
The “Hot Potato” aspect of this class may make it less fun for the original Diseased, so something could need adding there
