Hmm, I was going to base it off the current CW lore, but I wasn’t feeling it. What I’m trying for here is a Court Wizard who’s still suspicious as all hell, still causes chaos for the BD.

Night Abilities
Induce Visions: Your target will see a confirmation of their ability as if nobody could inhibit them. If occupied, redirected, or prevented, they will not receive a message. They still attempt to perform the action.
Tornado: (3 uses) unchanged

Notifications:
Induce Visions: target was given a vision of their actions.

Sage:
Tornado: unchanged
Induce Doubt (3 uses): Your target receives a message of being prevented from visiting their target. If occupied or redirected, they will not receive a message.

Notification:
Induce Doubt: target was given false visions of their actions.

Induce Visions from the target’s perspective: No notice that you were the target. This bypasses everything (occupied, redirected, merc guard, jail, frame/hide) to show you the message you’d get. This does not give immunity, so if occupied or prevented, your target will not be healed, attacked, occupied, etc.
Induce Doubt simply does the same effect, but the message will always be prevented from visiting.

squidgyxombie March 28, 2018, 11:06pm #5

The idea was keeping CW still horribly suspicious. It only screws with messages people receive. Make CW something bad guys can still claim, but if you put multiple logs together… you can prove a person was having visions.

If Butler does an Occ on 2, and 2 is in jail. The butler will not occupy the jailed person and see the message
“You were prevented from visiting 2.”
If Butler does an Occ on 2 with Induce Visions and 2 is in jail. The butler will not occupy the jailed person, but the Butler now sees “You occupied 2” or “2 was immune, this could be due to blah blah” based on 2’s class.

The biggest thing this does is make a CW have synergy with both real and fake investigators. If the CW visions a person faking logs, they will know when the person claims they were prevented or occupied. If they vision a real investigator, then the information is gathered regardless of occupy, swap, etc.
