Livicus:

Starting Day 2, at the start of each day, the Rigelian Army and the Deliverance will meet to engage in battle. The winning side will gain useful prizes each day that sometimes could swing the tide of the game. All day abilities, including day attacks, can still be used during battles.

This is based off of the classic “Weapon Triangle” from Fire Emblem, where Axes beat Lances, Lances beat Swords, and Swords beat Axes, much like Rock, Paper, Scissors.

How It Works

  1. Members of The Deliverance will give a fake claim of what they will choose for the battle the night before: either Swords, Axes, or Lances. This will be displayed in bold text alongside some descriptive narrative at the start of the day.
  2. The Deliverance will also give the real choice. There may be abilities that a class can use which allows them to choose Bows, forcing a tie the following day regardless of the Rigelians’ choice.
  3. There will be 1 day in real time for all alive members to come to a vote in the thread by choosing Swords, Axes, or Lances. Some classes may have special abilities that affect this phase.
  4. The Deliverance will come to blows against the Rigelian Army and the choices of both sides will be revealed.

If the Rigelians win, all members of the faction will gain Conversion Protection for that night. It’s similar to conversion immunity, but this is only temporary, and even then, can be bypassed by some abilities.
If the Deliverance wins, the faction will be given the choice of removing a single cooldown that night from any ability that their members have. It’s quite powerful.
And if the two sides come to fight and find themselves at equal strength, then nothing will happen as draws are so anti-climactic, and thus don’t deserve any specialties.
