Preview of Patch 1.1.0

Official Announcements
Ellie January 26, 2018, 6:20pm #1

Patch will be out in the next few days.

General:

  • New simplified game mode called Casual. (If it is popular enough, new players will be required to play X number of games of it.)
  • Old game mode now named Classic.
  • Everyone can now draw in their deathnote.
  • Spy/Cult Maid no longer counts as a visit.
  • Disabled the early game over checks for the time being. (Games would end when they shouldn’t, even when things like DK’s and abilities were accounted for.)
  • New female armor and crossbow execution added.

Assassin:

  • Distract now usable during trials.

Enforcer:

  • Removed Armored passive.
  • Protection now prevents visits to targeted Unseen member.

King:

  • Removed Allies from starting King.
  • Added Night Ability Order to starting King - Prevent a player from voting tomorrow. 4 uses.
  • Abilities no longer count as visiting.

Knight:

  • Changed Sacrifice icon.

Mastermind:

  • Into the Shadows now hides the Mastermind’s occupy immunity again.

Princess:

  • Flirt now show Killer/Support and Offensive/Social.

Psycho King:

  • Now permanently death immune at night.
  • Abilities no longer count as visiting.

Ritualist:

  • Tornado reduced to two uses.
  • Brotherhood changed to single target, uses increased to three.

Sorcerer:

  • Magic Dust uses reduced to 2.
  • Circle of Death uses reduced to 2.
  • New Day Ability Magic Cloak - Hides your visits at night. 2 uses.

Bug Fixes:

  • Fixed a bug with some abilities not being usable during trials.
  • No longer shows multiple healing messages.
  • Fixed a bug where sometimes people wouldn’t show up as visiting. The only abilities or classes that shouldn’t show up as visiting will say so in the class card.
  • Some others.

Casual Game Mode:

Core Differences:

  • Changed almost all Blue Dragon classes and their conversions.
  • Prince remains the same.
  • The max number of each non-unique Blue Dragon class is 3.
  • Only the Unseen can spawn.
  • The Sorcerer and Inquisitor cannot spawn, but all other neutrals still can.
  • There is no Royal Blood.

Magistrate - Investigative Blue Dragon

  • Spawns every game, is unique, and is unconvertable.
  • Day Ability Scout - Send a scout to watch a player. You will be alerted if they are converted.
  • Night Ability Expert Investigation - Determine if a player is part of the Unseen.
  • Night Ability Surveillance - Prevent visitors and learn the name of those that try. You will be alerted if an Unseen member tries. 3 uses.

Inspector - Investigative Blue Dragon

  • Night Ability Examine - Limit a player’s class type down to two options. Works the same way as the Princess’ Flirt.
  • Night Ability Window Peek - Discover a player’s faction. 2 uses.

Champion - Killer Blue Dragon

  • Passive Armored - Your armor will protect you from the first attacker blocked by Sacrifice.
  • Night Ability Sacrifice - Protect a player, giving your life to kill their attackers.
  • Night Ability Entrench - You will become death and conversion immune, and will kill any attackers. Bypasses night immunity. 2 uses.

Steward - Offensive Blue Dragon

  • Passive Dutiful - Immune to occupation and target changing.
  • Night Ability Serve Wine - Occupy a player by getting them drunk.
  • Night Ability Debauchery - Cause a player to target you. You will be immune to all attacks redirected. 2 uses.

Mystic - Social Blue Dragon

  • Passive Call the Dead - Communicate with the dead at night.
  • Day Ability Telepathy - Telepathically send a message to everyone, they will not know who sent it. 2 uses.
  • Day Ability Private Matter - All whispers to and from you will be hidden from the public. 2 uses.
  • Night Ability Read Mind - See whispers to and from a player tomorrow. Cannot target King. Does not count as visiting.

Medic - Support Blue Dragon

  • Passive Remedy Studies - Immune to Nightshade poisoning.
  • Night Ability First Aid - Heal a player back to life if they were attacked.
  • Night Ability Quarantine - Heal a player’s poison and prevent all visitors. Can only be used on a player once.

Sleuth - Investigative Unseen (Converted from Inspector)

  • Night Ability Study - Discover a player’s class type.
  • Night Ability Watch Carefully - Discover a player’s class. 2 uses.

Gladiator - Support Unseen (Converted from Champion)

  • Night Ability Protection - Protect an Unseen member and prevent others from visiting.
  • Night Ability Frenzy - Guarantees the Assassin will kill their target. 1 use.

Attendant - Offensive Unseen (Converted from Steward)

  • Passive Dutiful - Immune to occupation and target changing.
  • Night Ability Serve Wine - Occupy a player by getting them drunk.
  • Nigh Ability Drugged Wine - Drug a player and then force them to target someone. 2 uses.

Seer - Social Unseen (Converted from Mystic)

  • Day Ability Telepathy - Telepathically send a message to everyone, they will not know who sent it. 2 uses.
  • Day Ability Private Matter - All whispers to and from you will be hidden from the public. 2 uses.
  • Night Ability Read Mind - See whispers to and from a player tomorrow. Cannot target King. Does not count as visiting.
  • Night Ability Mind Warp - Cause a player to receive incorrect feedback from their abilities.

Sawbones - Support Unseen (Converted from Medic)

  • Night Ability Abscission - Make an Unseen member appear as Blue Dragon tonight.
  • Night Ability Drug - Causes a player to believe they were attacked and healed. 2 uses.
