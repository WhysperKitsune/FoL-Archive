Priest and Soulcatcher Comeback

Class/Lore/Fanfic
thepigeonnyc February 11, 2021, 4:59pm #1

The Priest

Blue Dragon Special
Godly Info (Passive) - You will learn the class of everyone who dies no matter what, but you will not know that the flip was tampered with. Possessor jumps will flip as Possessor.
Limited Power (Passive) - You may only use the ability of a player once.
Pray (Day) - Use target player’s main ability on yourself tonight. Can target dead players. You will only receive feedback if your target is dead. Two uses.
Living Souls (Night) - Use target player’s main ability on another player. Can target dead players in the first slot. You will only receive feedback if your first target is dead. Cannot target self in either slot. Does not count as visiting. Unlimited uses.
Speak with Dead (Night) - Learn what feedback your target received on the night they died. Does not count as visiting. Three uses.

The Soulcatcher

Unseen Special
Godly Info (Passive) - You will learn the class of everyone who dies no matter what, and you will also know what they flipped as to everyone else. Possessor jumps will flip as Possessor.
Limited Power (Passive) - You may only use the ability of a player once.
To Darkness We Hail (Day): Use target player’s main ability on all of the Unseen tonight. Can target dead players. You will receive feedback. Costs 1 soul.
Collect Souls (Night) - The Assassin’s kill target will instead be reaped and you will gain one soul. The unseen will be aware of their class. Unlimited uses.
Living Souls (Night) - Learn all actions and results of your first target and use their ability on your second. You will receive feedback as if you used the ability. Cannot target self. Can target dead players. Does not count as visiting. Costs one soul.

Cult conversion: If someone wants to design a Cabalist, go ahead, but I’ll currently have it as Seeker

Clarifications and Exceptions

Drunk = Happy Hour
Hunter and Prince = Normal Kill
Kill Knight = Defend
Non-knight Killer and Offensive abilities will only target the Soulcatcher
NK = Normal Kill
MM = Foresight as if the game just started (ie, Sage would not show up as conv immune)
CL = Normal Kill
Cult convert = Ability with more uses
Ritualist = Brotherhood
