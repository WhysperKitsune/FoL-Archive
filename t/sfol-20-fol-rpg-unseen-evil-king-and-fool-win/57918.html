Gameplay

Days last 96 hours, or until a majority is reached for execution. Nights last 48 hours.
All actions must be sent through the class card DM.
Unseen/Cult will talk through a special Discord server. Night Chat only.
The priest will speak with the dead through a separate Discord server. Other dead speakers will get messages through their class card.
All classes will be used, and will be used as they are found in my Class Cards Thread
When you receive a card you will have 48 hours to reply, confirming you have received it
Works like normal FoL but choosing /noaction will cause you to level up, and allow you to choose new abilities

Executions

To accuse someone say “/accuse [player]” or “/vote [player]”
Votes saying Execute or Pardon before the target is on the stand will not be counted.
Should the votes reach the majority, that player will go on trial. They can give a defence, and the rest of the players must vote Execute or Pardon on the thread.
Players can discuss while the target is on trial.
No one is able to use day powers when a trial is in session. The only exception is the king’s Decide Fate.
Every trial will go on for 24 hours. However, if The King uses decide fate it will be cut short.
Trials pause the counter.
If the option with the most votes is what happens to the target.

Picking a King

If there are less than 4 players a new king will not be selected.
After a king’s execution the day does not end, an election shall be held right after.
When the king dies 24 hours is given for any player with royal blood to step up and take the throne.
After that 24 hours anyone is free to step up.
If only 1 person steps up when the day ends that person becomes the king
If 2 people step forward everyone votes on who should be the next king. Whoever has the most votes at the end of the day becomes the king. If the votes are tied whoever stepped up 1st becomes the king

Journals

Message me your entire journal, the last draft I receive before you die shall be the one displayed
