Deliberation Deck

This game requires between 5 to 8 Players. - Inspired by Jackbox Push that Button and Fakinâ€™ It.

  • Roles & Alignments.
    • Survivors - The uninformed majority of the game. They have no special abilities. Their objective is to Locate the Darkeners and eliminate them.
    • Darkeners - The informed minority of the game. They have limited use abilities to help them in their team during the game. They have no private chat. Their objective is to Banish the Survivors and reign victorious.

Each round begins with a Player being Mission Leader. They will select from a menu of Mini games, and the Players to represent it. In these Mini games, the Players will be typically asked a question; however, the Darkeners will have a twisted variant, which can reveal an odd answer and give their alignment away.

Mini games - I only have two right now, suggest more mini games if you want!
Deliberation Deck - The Mission Leader selects three Players for this. They receive a fictional scenario with five possible decisions and must pick the decision they would most likely make. Darkeners will receive a completely different scenario.
Detection Ward - Two Players are chosen for this. The ML will know how many Darkeners are among them, but not know who is which. This can only be used twice in the game.
