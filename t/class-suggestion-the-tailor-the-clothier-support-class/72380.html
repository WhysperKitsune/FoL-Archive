[Class Suggestion] The Tailor/The Clothier (Support Class)

Class/Lore/Fanfic
BlueStorm September 13, 2018, 4:05pm #1

The Tailor (Unique Blue Dragon Support) - Information-based Support class.

Day Ability 1: High Society (One use)
Permanently grant a player the effects of Royal Blood.

Night Ability 1: Reality Cloak (Infinite uses)
Visitors to your target will receive accurate feedback from their abilities.

Night Ability 2: Alarm Netting (Three uses)
Your target will be informed of who visited them.

The Clothier (Unique Unseen Support)

Day Ability 1: Secret Society (One use)
Permanently grant a member of the Unseen the effects of Royal Blood.

Night Ability 1: Sensory Cap (Four uses)
Prevent the Assassin from visiting a target that would result in their death.

Night Ability 2: Invisibility Cloak (Two uses)
Hide the Unseen’s visits for the night.

(First time I’ve tried to create a BD/Unseen class/convert, so there might be a fair amount here that needs reworking, please review this with as much depth as you can, the more you tell me, the more I can improve!)
