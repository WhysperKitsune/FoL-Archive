1.1.3 Patch Notes

Official Announcements
Ellie May 17, 2018, 5:09pm #1

Lobby:

  • Casual Mode is now disabled for public games.
  • Improvements back end. Should improve reconnection.

Store Content:

  • Three new executions - Oracle’s Earring, Lightning Rod, Bowl of Nails
  • Three new Deathnote colors - Fiery Orange, Sky Teal, Stone Gray

Quality of Life:

  • Changed Probe Icon
  • Added a tips section the class cards with basic tips for each class.
  • Changed the order of the colors in the DN
  • Mute function fixed.

General:

  • Increased amount of time to step forward to become King.
  • Increased the money you get from betting on deaths.
  • Occupy and redirection immunity now carries over when your class changes.
  • The Mystic is now the only class immune to Mind Link & Conduit at the start, but this immunity carries over when their class changes.
  • Occupation and visit prevention are now merged into one message saying you were unable to use your ability.
  • Mercenary has been reworked and the Sellsword has been revived as a Neutral Offensive class.
  • Added MVP system. See below for details.

Minor Class/Ability Changes

Knight:

  • Will now always attack their target, even when they are Blue Dragon.
  • Will now only commit suicide after they successfully attack a Blue Dragon player. (This means if the target is night immune, they will not commit suicide)

Observer:

  • Follow is now affected by Mind Warp.

Paladin:

  • Smite usable Night 3.

Possessor:

  • Possess now prevents the target from being healed.

Reaper:

  • Players can no longer emote when icy touched.

Major Class/Ability Changes:

Mercenary:

  • No longer has a contract. Now must see the Unseen/Cult defeated in order to win. Does not have to be alive at the end.
  • Remains conversion immune.
  • Removed Connections and Rebound.
  • Stand Guard is now a targetable ability, but you cannot target the same player two nights in a row. Unlimited uses.
  • New Ability Shield - Shield a player, dying in their place if they are attacked at night. Will stay on the target until changed. Unlimited uses.

Sellsword:

  • Brought back from the dead as a Neutral Offensive that must see the Blue Dragon defeated in order to win. Does not have to be alive at the end.
  • Occupy, redirection, and conversion immune.
  • Stonewall prevents all non-attacking visits to your target. Cannot target the same player two nights in a row. Unlimited uses.
  • New Ability Intimidate - Occupy a player.

Sheriff:

  • Scout will no longer tell you if a player was converted, and will instead tell you if the Unseen visited targeted player.
  • Added new ability Surveillance - Determine a player’s class if they are a member of the Unseen. 3 uses.

MVP System:

  • At the end of the match, before the end screen, players can pick the top 3 players they think contributed to the game.
  • The top three players will receive gold.
  • 1st place receives 250 gold.
  • 2nd place receives 125 gold.
  • 3rd place receives 75 gold.
