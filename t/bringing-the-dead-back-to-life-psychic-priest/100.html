Ellie October 11, 2016, 3:25pm #7

Whenever the King dies, the following day there will be a chance for people to step forward to become the new King. If only one person steps forward they’ll become King. If two people step forward, voting will start. People choose one of the two that stepped forward. If no one steps forward that day, then it’ll see if anyone wants to the next day. If there is still no one after that, it just stops checking.
