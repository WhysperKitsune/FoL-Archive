CHARACTER INFORMATION
Name/Alias: Dinara Senade
Age: 25
Gender: Female
Plane of Origin: Velonia
Species:

Night Elf

The origin of Night Elves was said to be an elf clan who wished to achieve immortality in order to fully taste the full bearing of life. They performed a ritual described in an ancient manuscript told to be older than the world itself, hoping it would fulfill their wish. Many versions appeared on how the grand ritual failed. A sabotage by their own member, a false instruction in the manuscript. Nevertheless, the ritual failed and left the elves as shadows, stripped from their enchanting appearance that charmed mortals far and wide.

In vain, the first of Night Elves tried to reverse the damage, but it was too late.They covered their shadowy form with a magic augmentation that will change their appearance into figures as imagined by others. It was not enough, but it was all they have. The Night Elves hid from other elf clans in shame and blend with humans, only dare to tread the world under the cover of the moon and ink black sky. Yet they continued to live, having learnt their lesson. Life is a good thing, and one won’t cherish a good thing unless it has an end.

If a Night Elf is seen without his glamour, he will be seen as a shadow figure, similar to those of a silhouette. He would be as tall as the person who sees him with a pair of glowing yellow eyes on their face. The shadow themselves are dense and trying to run a hand through one will be a useless struggle.

With his glamour on, a Night Elf can take the appearance of what a person imagine. The glamour that covers a Night Elf tricks all senses, changing the Night Elf skin tones, muscles build, intonation and melody of voice, fragrance, and other physical attributes to fit the onlookers thought.

A Night Elf is always shrouded by illussion, but able to suppress it, avoiding unneeded attention on daily life activities. A Night Elf’s shadow form normally can only be seen by elves and witches. Other species will only see them as humans. However, there were cases when an injured Night Elf turns into his shadow form as his magical energy focused itself on keeping the Night Elf alive.

They lost most of their elven heritage, including their affinity with nature magic. They do keep their enchanting charm and their meddles with spells left them with a unique bit of magic of their own. They call it trust magic, although they are unsure of the reason they earned it.

Magic:
Beauty Glamour: Dinara’s illussion allow her to turn into the image of beauty from another person’s mind.
Favour Coins: By exchanging favour or request from another person, Dinara is capable of casting magic otherwise restricted from herself.
Skill: Persuasion and acting, brewery and potioneering, negotiation
Appearance:
Backstory: A runaway from an aristocrat family, she was picked up by the Archmage who perceived her ability in magic, and heritage as night elf. Dinara spends her time mostly as his apprentice, helping him facing problems in Velonia.
Others: Her parasol is a magic artifact that is capable of controlling wind.
