Character Rule

Velonia:

  1. Your character, should they be a magic user, should have a method of controlling magic, what this is is up to you, it could be a wand or potions or harnessing mana through leylines
  2. Depending on how your magic works, it might not work on other planes, or it may be limited.

Callinety Falls:

  1. Your character, should they have metahuman powers, may only have 1 power or 1 type/style of power
  2. Callinety Falls is basically present day earth but with crazy powers, so keep it modern and save the crazy technology for the sci fi world
  3. Remember, your character may be a metahuman, but they‚Äôre still human, so don‚Äôt make them an immortal supergod when their actual power is making fireballs

Grand Peninsula:

  1. Steam power has its limit, even though on this plane those limits may be seen as unrealistic, they still exist
  2. Steam power is not infinite, it needs water and fuel, sometimes you may not be able to get these things, in this case your machines won‚Äôt work, deal with it
  3. You‚Äôre only human, any extraordinary abilities your character may possess are due to machines and invention, not any sort of special power.

Eleos-12:

  1. Your character, should they be from an alien race, must have purely defined abilities and shortcomings, you don‚Äôt have to state what they are, but remain consistent
  2. You may have all sorts of crazy technology, but the whole nexus thing is pretty new, so you can‚Äôt communicate or teleport between dimensions using your technology.
