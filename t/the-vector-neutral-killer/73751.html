The Vector

Neutral Killer

Immunity (Passive) - You are immune to poison and death at night.

Infected (Passive) - You infect anyone you visit or whisper, and anyone who visits or whispers you becomes infected. You also infect anyone who bleeds you. Infected targets are not notified and they will die in 3 nights unless cured or delayed.

Adapt (Day) - Render a player susceptible to infection, poison, and bleeding (this is the opposite of Innoculate).

Infect (Night) - Infect a player.

If you die, all infected are cured of infection. Infection can be cured, and deaths due to Infection can be delayed by Chronomancer.
