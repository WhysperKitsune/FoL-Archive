Class Suggestion - Courier / Forger

Class/Lore/Fanfic
SweetyCake September 26, 2018, 4:25pm #1

Courier

Blue Dragon Social

Paper Work(Passive) - Immune to most occupation and redirection. This passive is keep even if your class change.

Paper Madness(Passive) - You will prevent the first attempt attack on you. You will known if you used this passive.

Log Reader(Day) - You will recieve a copy of target first page Log Book at the start of the night. 3 Use.

Log Reminder(Night) - Visit the target, you will know what they have added into their Log Book at the end of the night. Unlimited Use. You will receive what they added during that night,

Secret Note(Night) - See a target Deathnote. 1 Use

Your objects is to defeat the Unseen and Cult or any Neutral that seek to your harm.

Forger

Unseen Social
Paper Work(Passive) - Immune to most occupation and redirection at night. You will keep this passive even if your class changed.

Paper Disguise(Passive) - If you die at night then you will show up as Courier

Log Checker(Day) - You and Unseen will recieve a copy of target first page Log Book at the start of the night. 2 Use.

Note Checker(Night) - Get a player Private Note. 2 Use

Replace (Night) - If the target die tonight, he will appear as chosen class and will have their log writen by you (Start writing after choosing target.). 2 Use. Regain an use if target didnt die.

Your objects is to defeat the Blue Dragon or any Neutral that seek to your harm.
