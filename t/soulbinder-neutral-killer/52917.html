Soulbinder

Neutral Killer

Passive: Bound Soul: Any negative effects (death, bleeding, occupation, etc.) will be redirected to your bound target. Prince executes bypass this. Bypasses Immunity. People aren’t informed that they were redirected.

Day Ability: Bind Soul: Bind yourself to someone. Unlimited uses, 1 day cool down.

Day Ability: Manipulate: Force your target to vote for someone, and prevent them from voting for the rest of the day. Unlimited uses.

Night Ability: Hex: Kill everybody who visits your target. Unlimited uses.

Night Ability: Soul Shock: Kill your bound target. Bypasses immunity. 1 day cool down.
