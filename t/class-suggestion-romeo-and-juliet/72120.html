[Class Suggestion]Romeo and Juliet

Class/Lore/Fanfic
Qwert September 4, 2018, 6:05am #1

Romeo

Neutral Offensive

Passive (Way of the Sword) - You are night, occupy and redirection immune.
Night Ability (Wine) - Occupy a player.(Infinite uses)
Night Ability (Propose) - Propose to a player, will only work if the target is Juliet. Can not be interrupted. (1 use)

Juliet

Neutral Investigative

Day Ability (Hide) - Tonight you will hide from all visits by other players.(3 uses)
Night Ability (Check Horoscope Compatibility) - Check love compatibility between 2 players. Male and female classes are compatible with each other. Fool, Mastermind and Cult Leader are not compatible with any classes. Prince is compatible with all other classes.(Infinite uses)
Night Ability (Accept) - Accept a proposal, will only work if the target is Romeo. Can not be interrupted.(1 use)

Goal : Survive to marry each other at the wedding.

The couple have 5 days to find love. After night 5 they will both suicide. If Romeo or Juliet dies before Day 6, the other lover will enter a frenzied state (lose all passives, abilities and gain Night Ability (Poisoned dagger) - Attack a player (1 use)) with a new goal to seek revenge on the killer before committing suicide. If one lover is executed during the day, the killer will be the first accuser.

If the proposal is successful at night, the couple will marry each other next night at the wedding (provided they survive the proposal night and the following day). All surviving (BD?) players are invited to the wedding.(Skip the night?)
