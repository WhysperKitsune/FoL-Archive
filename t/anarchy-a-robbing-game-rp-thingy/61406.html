Anarchy

You all are specifically selected to rob banks,criminal murders,raids and cause the beauty of anarchy.
Greetings for being selected to play my game. now all of you will be criminals and we will cause Anarchy…

So what do you have to do?

Now then whenever you do a mission chosen by me you all have 2 ways to do it either do it silently or go loud and cause lots of Anarchy. Your choice…

Also whenever you want to do some action please make it clear by either going in detailed RP or by doing Example or Example
Some of your actions will have luck based on it so hope you get lucky just think of this as a game of Dnd.
Also pick a class I will give you a selection fear not…
Also please give me a nickname for yourself so you do not get caught and gender and also the reason you joined my quest on Anarchy.
Also please refrain from killing citizens last thing we want is all the nations on us.

Now since you have got the memo please pick your class.
Leader- Leaders are known to lead everyone and help everyone they also do most of the hostage taking and normally come out with quick escape plans in the case of major problems and support alot.

Gun slinger- These are the people who normally do most of the violent and dynamic things of a mission they are used to using a variety of guns and are rather violent which can help alot in the case of large police assaults and attacks.

Phantom-Phantom…most of the time are really silent knowing the secret art of silence and stealth these people even when not in stealth can be really good with their underhanded tricks and skills which can take down a rather strong officer.

Engineer-These people are nearly always the main people to help in robberies and are known to have machines to help the team out in both silent and loud ways these people if given time can take out a massive assault of cops with their tactics and machines.

Once everything is ready and set in place come at the spot I gave you all the coordinates on.
