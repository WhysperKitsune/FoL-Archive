Crow (Blue Dragon Special Social)

Class/Lore/Fanfic
Crow_is_dead April 19, 2021, 2:20am #1

Win Condition: Defeat all who oppose the Blue Dragon.

Passives:
Mimicry - Your vote counts twice. It still shows up as 1 next to the name but actually will count twice, this is so it is harder to prove yourself.

Day Abilities:
Crow’s Mark - Remove someones ability to vote for the day (3 uses)
Helping Hand - Give someone else the ability to vote twice. Similar to Mimicry to where it will still show up as 1 vote but actually counts twice. (2 uses)

Night Abilities:
Crow on the shoulder - Pick a target, you will then see any whispers that include them (Inf uses)
Trust me - Pick a target, the next day they will immediately be executed (1 use)
