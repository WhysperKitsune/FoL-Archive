Ok so, the whisper king meta. Nobody really likes it but it’s always kind of been there. How do we solve it.
First:
Turn off whispering to king.
Well now, that’s a solution all in itself. But we still need a way to give info to king. How? (Keep in mind, the King can still whisper you.)

Add this fella:

The Messenger

Blue Dragon Social (Unique)

Passive (Manipulative) - This class has a 10% chance to be evil. Meaning, it will still be in the BD class form, but it will need to see the Blue Dragon lose. It will only be able to see it’s fellow evils if it is converted. If the evils try to convert the 10% evil messenger, it will work and it will now just be the converted class. Otherwise, it will not know who their evils are. Also, this class will always be in a game that has more than 11 players. <11, and this class will be random if it spawns or not. If the class is evil, it will take up a neutral slot.

Passive (Connections) - You may whisper the King. All whispers to the King will be hidden.

Day Ability (Private Matters) - Hide all whispers to and from you for the rest of the day. 2 uses.

Day Ability (Open the gates) - The court will be sent a notification, and they will be able to send whispers to the king for the rest of the day. 1 use, not usable until d4.

Night Ability (King’s Connection) - Select a player. The king, once day time is started, will be given the message “The messenger’s selection is a member of the ____.” The messenger will not know the faction of who they selected, and the king will not know the number of the person the faction belongs to. 3 uses.

Night Ability (Private Eyes) - Select a player. You will know if they have been visited by a royal in the past 3 nights, including the night this is used. King counts, if they were jailed, that will count too. Infinite Uses.

Goal: Same goal as BD (unless the messenger is the 10% evil)

The Mediator

Unseen Social

Passive (Evil Connections) - You may whisper the King, and all whispers from you to the king will be hidden.

Day Ability (Watch the People) - The court will be sent a notification, and they will be able to send whispers to the king for the rest of the day. All unseen will be able to see these whispers. 1 use, only usable if “Open the Gates” was not used as a messenger.

Night Ability (King’s Connection) - Select a player and a faction. The king, once day time is started, will be given the message “The messenger’s selection is a member of the ____.” The king will not know the number of the person the faction belongs to, and he will be shown the fake faction you selected. You will know the real faction of who you selected. 2 uses .

Night Ability (Unseen’s Connection) - Select a player. The mastermind, on day startup, will know their exact converted class. 3 uses.
