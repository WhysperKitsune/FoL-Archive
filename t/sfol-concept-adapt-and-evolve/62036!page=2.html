Its in the post but let me copy it here:

The Collector
Neutral Killer
Perseverance (Passive) - Immune to death once.
Analyze (Day) - Select a player, you get a pairing of class types. Their class type will be one of the two. The pairings are Killer/Offensive, Support/Social and Special/Investigative. - Infinite uses
Exploit Weakness (Day) - Guess a player’s exact class. If you guess it right, they will be killed immediately. (Can’t be used on Leader or any of its evolutions). - 2 uses.
Collect (Night) - Kill a player. - Unlimited uses.

  • Kill an offensive role: You will become immune to occupation and redirection. People that try to occupy or redirect you will believe they succeeded.
  • Kill a support role: You will become immune to poison/bleeding. Perseverance now makes you immune to death permanently.
  • Kill a killer role: “Collect” now bypasses healing and death immunity.
  • Kill an investigative role: Analyze now only gives the real result, and will also tell you the target’s faction.
  • Kill a social role: Gain 1 extra use of “Exploit Weakness”. When you use “Exploit weakness” you can guess two classes instead of one. If the target player is any of those classes they will die.
  • Kill a special role: When you first get this evolution, choose a class. You will appear as that class to investigators. Also hides your visits.
  • Complete all other quests: “Collect” can now target two players.
