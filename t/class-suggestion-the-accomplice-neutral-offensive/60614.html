The Accomplice is designed to give a boost to NK win rates, but also to boost evil win rates when the NK dies early (particularly from leaving), which tends to give BD extra time to find evils. The first day and night abilities are meant to help the Accomplice find the NK, while the second day ability and both night abilities can help protect the NK.

Watchful allows the Accomplice to know who is in jail. This can help piece information together and maximize the effectiveness of Interfere and Carbon Copy. It also gives the daring Accomplice the opportunity to claim Prince, which could shake up the meta a bit and have some situational uses.

Carbon Copy is a modified day version of the Nobleâ€™s Gossip night ability. Unlike Gossip, CC can detect attacks. The ability is not usable D1 to (1) provide some cover to MM/CL and (2) prevent the Accomplice from finding and outing the NK d2 to get themselves aligned with evil.

Once the NK is identified, Accomplice can use Level Playing Field to prevent king from pointing finger or Prince from jailing, buying valuable space for the NK in crunch time. Do Not Disturb can protect the NK from Order Execute, Cold Steel, Debauch, and Attacks.

While the Accomplice does have powerful abilities that can help the NK, the Accomplice is vulnerable to death (including at the hand of the NK) and struggles to have a convincing claim as a K/O that is not occ immune.
