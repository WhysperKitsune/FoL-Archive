I was tasked to make a Neutral Killer that utilized Bleeding effects to kill their targets. I am operating under the assumption that Bleed and Poison are interchangeable and that the Unseen/Cult divide will eventually be collapsed into one status effect.

The Exsanguinist can utilize Hemorrhage to cause targets to Bleed, mistaking the attack as Unseen/Cult, but more importantly can speed up the deaths and make it so each poison and bleed acts much quicker if he himself is bleeding. He can up the speed by using Flesh Wound on himself (to trigger Crimson Revelry and punch through a Physician‚Äôs Inoculate or natural Immunity) or use Flesh Wound to divert attention from Physicians. With the fast acting effects of Bleed, even a Mastermind (who is normally not worried about most night killers) may need to worry about such threats and will have to make themselves known and speak up. The pain of the bleeding also puts additional strain on the Prince and their jailing as well as other redirecting roles.

Using Lamb to the Slaughter the Exsanguinist can lock a target down, letting them drip out overnight without any help from others. Alternatively, they can target themself to protect or stop effects that may be targeting them this night (for whatever reason). If a target dies under the effects of Lamb to the Slaughter the body will be found in a gruesome and unrecognizable state, making the killer‚Äôs presence widely known while destroying a lot of information.
