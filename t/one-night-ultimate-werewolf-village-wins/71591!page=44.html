NuclearBurrito August 20, 2018, 1:19am #866

Most of the roles are the same but some aren’t. For example the Drunk equivalent sees his new card. Tanner win is also a werewolf win and the werewolfs see the Tanner. Lone minion looks at a bottom card and the Revealer doesn’t see the card if he can’t reveal it. There are also roles unique to AHGW

Negotiator can reveal his own card at any time

Detective can compare alignments between 2 players

Snoop is a scum that can check 2 bottom cards

Intimidator is a scum that can block someone from doing their action

Guard can stop target player’s card from being swapped (but not from being viewed)

Tracker knows where his card is at the end of the night

Witness knows how meny bank robber’s there are and which players used a night action on them.
