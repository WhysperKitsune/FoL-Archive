Town ITA Master

Goal: Defeat the Mafia and all neutrals who seek to do you harm

Town Friendly Neighbourizer

You are the Friendly Neighbourizer, aligned with the Town! You have the following ability.
The Friendly Neighborizer may each night target a player, which will recruit them into a Neighborhood, allowing them to communicate with each other outside of the game thread. Everyone who is added to the Neighborhood will receive confirmation from the host that the Friendly Neighborizer is town-aligned. Once added to the Neighborhood, a Neighbor will stay there permanently (until they die).
Goal: Defeat the Mafia and all neutrals who seek to do you harm

Town Probate Lawyer

You are the Probate Lawyer, aligned with the Town! You have the following ability.
The Probate Lawyer - The Probate Lawyer will, for as long as they are alive, passively allow dead townies to leave behind a last will, a post-death message. When the Probate Lawyer dies, all last wills are published.
Also makes it so dead townies can’t speak on the chat until you die.
Goal: Defeat the Mafia and all neutrals who seek to do you harm

Town Blackjack

You are the Blackjack, aligned with the Town! You have the following ability.
The Blackjack may once per game during day choose to randomize all roles in the game. This will cause everyone’s roles (not alignments) to be randomly switched around.
To clarify, all townies will remain townies, and all mafia will remain mafia-aligned.
Goal: Defeat the Mafia and all neutrals who seek to do you harm
