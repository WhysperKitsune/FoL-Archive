Court Wizard Rework

Class/Lore/Fanfic
NightX February 18, 2018, 2:34am #1

Court Wizard is a pretty boring class. After discovering its roots, I have come up with this:

Court Wizard (Unique Blue Dragon Support)

Night Ability: Tornado: Swap two players, making anyone who targets one of them target the other. Unlimited uses. Bypasses occupation immunity.

Night Ability 2: Magic Barrier: Prevent all negative effects on two targets besides death tonight, and the next day. 2 uses. Does not prevent death, poison, or bleeding.

Sage would remain the same.

Change log:
1: Barrier does not stop bleeding.
2: Made Unique
3: Formatting

Non-unique court wizards with infinite tornadoes was a thing a while back and wasn’t very fun, especially since back then occupy immunity didn’t stop the redirect and being redirected 3 times didn’t give immunity.
