Sam17z April 9, 2018, 10:22pm #111
Ellie:

Fool:

Hide now grants death immunity instead of preventing visitors.

I do not like this. I feel that this now allows for king to order execution on the fool when it would be prevented and not work. The fool should be able to escape order and exe be voted the next day. This will significantly decrease fool wins in my opinion. Although some may say that the fool prevent is bad because of people knowing if prevented then they are fool, overall this is a negative change to fool. Players should be able to use there own skill to avoid the statement “oh I was prevented and you were not jailed you must be fool”. Through claiming a sage wall of fired you or a hunter put up a trap on the door this takes away a key social deduction skill from players which should not be the intended goal.

Ellie:

Knight:

Will no longer attack players when using Cold Steel if they are redirected.

I heavily agree with this change. The reason behind this is that if you are a knight and you are debauched by a drunk you die because you cannot kill a fellow BD member. (I thought this needed to be changed but did not create a forum post) Same thing will a court wizard swap, invoker redirect or other redirect actions.

Ellie:

Paladin:

Smite can’t be used until Night 4.

Very good change. This takes away from the N1 smite meta where the paladins have a 4/15 chance of killing the cult leader. And then we have the mercenary …

Ellie:

Can no longer get a second contract.

I dislike this change. The one reason I do not like this change is because of the prince. The prince can execute a mercenary’s target and the mercenary cannot do anything about it. I feel like this is ridiculous because how can you play a game where you have NO control whether or not your target is executed. I understand that you want mercenaries to be loyal to their OG target but this is a flawed method. The mercenary can do nothing about this and if the prince exes their target they auto lose. There is 2 situations that could happen that I think of that could cause the mercenary to lose. One is a merc getting an Unseen/Cult as a target and the prince jails and exes them, which causes the merc to lose. The second is the merc’s target is BD and the prince exes a BD. These 2 situation show how the merc has no control when jailed whether or not the prince exes them. What I propose, is that the mercenary contracts a second target, but once their first target dies they will lose all the charges to their abilities. This causes mercenaries to use their charges on the first target before they die and recieve a second target with nothing. Furthermore, this allows the mercenary to have some room if the first target gets exed by a prince. 2 times is unlikely but happens and if your target gets exed 2 times tough luck.

Ellie:

Can no longer be converted.

I do not like this because we do not need more targets that the unseen and cult are not able to convert. I understand some mercenaries get frustrated but this causes more social deduction between the target and the merc and the BD.

Ellie:

Passive Contracted - Occupy and redirection immune.

This is necessary if you have 1 target.

Ellie:

Day Ability Connections - Force the court to pardon your contract. 1 use.

This would have to make them not able to be voted up the same day like decide fate pardon, but this will also cause the target to be jailed which leads to the same problems as mentioned above.

Ellie:

Night Ability Stand Guard - Works just like before, but no longer notifies target. 4 uses.

Creates more social deduction which is nice. So good change. Probs going to see more merc claims.

Ellie:

Night Ability Rebound - Prevent and kill everyone visiting your contract.

Understandable if you are only going to have 1 target, but I like my solution to the problem really good.
