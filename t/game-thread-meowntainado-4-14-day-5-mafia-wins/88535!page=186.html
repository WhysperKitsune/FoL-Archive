I thought your reads were pretty good this game – both in the sense of ‘were they accurate’ and in the sense of ‘were they made for reasons that actually indicate the person being indicative of that alignment’. Your read on Gorta had a similar issue although in his case I don’t think it would be easy to get there on him without meta.
