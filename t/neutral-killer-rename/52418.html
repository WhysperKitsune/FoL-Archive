I’ll be honest, the fact that the term Neutral Killer dates all the way back to SC2 Mafia is, to me, a good reason not to change it. I get the desire to make ToL feel unique and fit its theme (hence the insistent terminology on a number of other renames, like class vs role), but at the end of the day changing names that are commonly-accepted within the genre serves to increase the game’s learning curve a bit, so it should only be done if there’s a good reason. I don’t feel there’s enough of a reason here.
