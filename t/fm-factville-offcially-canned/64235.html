Gameplay

  1. Days will be 72 hours long, or shorter if someone dies
  2. Nights will be 48 hour long, or shorter if everyone sent their actions in
  3. Actions are done by messaging me
  4. Mafia have a night chat and dead members may speak to the living mafia
  5. All classes will be used, and will be used as they are found in my class card section
  6. When you receive a card you will have 24 hours to reply, confirming you have received it

Executions

To accuse someone say “/accuse [player]” or “/vote [player]”
When someone is on Trial they will be able to post a 1000 character defense through me, during this time no-one should post, everyone will then vote Guilty or innocent by messaging me
After the votes are revealed a player will be decided as innocent or guilty, Executions end the Day
Trials pause the counter

Broadcasts

Everyday there will be things called broadcasts. The first 3 people at day to /broadcast in their PM will talk through me. There will be special effects from a player depending on their class. This effect only lasts for the day and night after you do the broadcast. You may choose to activate it when your broadcasting but it is only 1 use, though there maybe a way to get an extra one…. There is also another broadcast event that I shall not reveal.

Journals/Deathnotes

Message me your entire journal/DN, the last draft I receive before you die/kill shall be the one displayed
