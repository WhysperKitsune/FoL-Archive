The Marksman

Blue Dragon Investigative

Passive (Ghillie Suit) Your abilities do not count as visits and you appear to be unconvertable, neutral, and support to evil investigative abilities. You can still be converted however.

Passive (Focus) You are immune to occupation and redirection (credit to @NuclearBurrito) .

Day Ability (Take Aim) - Set your sights on a player to make them your target. (Infinite Uses)

Night Ability (Scrutinize) - Determine information about your target based on how many days you have had them in your sights (counter reset by imprisonment and prevention). Day 1: Whether they visited anyone last night. Day 2: Narrows down their class type to one of two possibilities. Day 3: Their exact class type. Day 4: Their faction. Day 5: Allows Headshot to kill without needing to predict who they visit. (Infinite Uses - credit to @Kirefitten)

Night Ability (Headshot) - Lead your target, predicting who they visit tonight (selecting your target will count as predicting them not visiting anybody). (credit to Firekitten) If correct, kill them and prevent their night action. Bypasses everything (even Reaper’s souls). Permanently disables your Ghillie Suit passive. (Infinite Uses) (Not usable before N3 - credit to @ShadowKnight and @santana )

Goal: Defeat the Unseen/Cult and any neutrals who seek to do you harm.

The Scout

Unseen Investigative

Passive (Camouflage) Your abilities do not count as visits and you appear to be Blue Dragon to investigative abilities. You will keep this passive even if your class changes, but it will be lost upon your first attack.

Day Ability (Scope Out) - Target a player. When night begins, you will be informed of their exact class type and how many uses of each ability they have remaining. (Infinite Uses)

Night Ability (Track) - Exactly like Observer’s Follow except you get to choose which night you get your results from. For example, you can choose Night 1 to find who they visited and were visited by on Night 1, even if it’s currently Night 4. May be used on dead players. (1 use)

Goal: Defeat the Blue Dragon and any neutrals who seek to do you harm.
