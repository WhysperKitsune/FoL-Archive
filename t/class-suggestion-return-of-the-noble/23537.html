[Class Suggestion] Return of the Noble

Class/Lore/Fanfic
Ulkesh December 3, 2017, 10:12pm #1

Noble was removed because of a Hearsay that did not work as intented and because people thought Noble was too primarily focussed on vote manipulation.

My suggestion is to have him return with his old (but slightly nerfed) Spy Maid ability instead of 1 vote manipulation ability.

Noble

(Social class)

Passive

  • Royal Blood
  • Inquiries: When found dead in the morning, increase the trial limit for that day by one.

Daytime abilities:

  • Political Pressure: Force a player to accuse someone. You cannot force the King to accuse someone or force a player to accuse the King. (3 uses)
  • Spy Maid: Choose a player. You will see all whispers from and to that player today. Can target the King. Cannot be used before day 3. (3 uses)

Nighttime abilities

  • Royal Decree: Protect another player from being accused next day. Cannot target the King (3 uses)
  • Send Order: Target player cannot accuse or vote next day. Cannot target the King. (3 uses)

Aristocrat (Unseen)

(Social class)

Passive

  • Royal Blood

Daytime abilities:

  • Political Pressure: Force a player to accuse someone. You cannot force the King to accuse someone or force a player to accuse the King. (3 uses)
  • Spy Maid: Choose a player. You will see all whispers from and to that player today. Can target the King. Cannot be used before day 3. (3 uses)

Nighttime abilities

  • Royal Decree: Protect another player from being accused next day. Cannot target the King (3 uses)
  • Send Order: Target player cannot accuse or vote next day. Cannot target the King. (3 uses)
