[Class Suggestion] The Shadow (Neutral Killing)

Class/Lore/Fanfic
Tormental February 5, 2018, 5:13am #1

The Shadow (Neutral Killing) - A dark boogeyman from beyond the veil.

Passive 1:
Tainted Blood - If you are killed by any means other than Prince’s execution or execution by vote, your killer will become the new Shadow on the 3rd night after your death. If your killer is a member of the Cult or Unseen, they will not change unless or until they are the final living member of their faction.

^ Example: Hunter bleeds you and kills you. On the 3rd night following your death, the Hunter becomes The Shadow. If the game is over before then, naturally nothing happens.

Passive 2:
Bitter Victory - You will win if any Shadow wins, even if you are dead.

Day Ability:
Altered Silhouette - Choose a player. You will have their investigative results, and they will appear to have visited your target instead of you, if anyone is watching. (3 uses.)

^ Observer, Wisped Princess, etc. will see your target incorrectly visiting.

Night Ability 1:
Bad Luck - Kill a player who was voted up for treason and pardoned the previous day. You gain occupation immunity and your target cannot be changed when using this ability. (2 uses.)

Night Ability 2:
Sudden Darkness - Choose a player to pull into the night, killing them.

Should those three nights pass, however, we may run into another issue - as they will be the most likely to kill you, converting an Assassin or Cult Leader to a new Shadow means they know who and what all of their teammates were (and, somewhat less importantly, vice-versa). This may not play out very well if the Shadow decides to claim Sheriff and start outing his teammates, and in general the remaining Unseen or Cult will probably bus the shit out of any of them that kills a Shadow on accident.

Not sure how you’d fix this, but it’s kind of a cool concept.

Silhouette Slaughter - Kill target player and anyone that target player visits. (2 uses)

Shadow should be able to become unseen/cult before they’d be last member in my opinion or his killing power is too low.
