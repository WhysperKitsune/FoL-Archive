General Gameplay Infomation

Rules

  1. Follow the Global Forum Rules
  2. Respect the host and the players
  3. If you haven’t posted content in 12 hours, you’ll be prodded for inactivity. If you don’t respond to that prod in 18 hours, you will be force replaced.
  4. No private messaging others in the game about the game.
  5. Do not screen-shot private host messages
  6. Don’t throw the game. This includes any form of intent to make you or your faction lose
  7. You are allowed to leave the game but doing it will net you a temporary blacklist meaning you can’t join a forum of lies for a while. Further leaving may net with a permanent blacklist
  8. Let’s all have fun and play together!
  9. We may add rules as we go…

Gameplay

  • Days last 24 hours, or until a majority is reached for execution. Nights last 12 hours.
  • All actions must be sent to the mod via Discord
  • Unseen/Cult will talk through a special Channel in the Server.
  • The priest will speak with the dead through a special Channel in the Server.
  • Trials are automatically done via commands:
    • !vote @user: Votes the mentioned user.
    • !execute/pardon: votes for the accused to be executed or pardoned.
  • You will have your own little channel where to use your abilities, your journal and your deathnote.
  • The King elections will be done during the day. If you have Royal Blood, announce yourself with Bold Letters saying I will be King (include the emote) and Ping @King so we can see it.

Classes:

All ToL and FoL Classes will be involved along with 2 Exclusive only to Discord of Lies classes!

The First Discord of Lies Exclusive Class

The Virtuoso (Neutral Support)

Immune to Conversion

Passive: Musical Soul - If the music dies, you will die (Being Occupied will kill you).

Passive: Inmate Harmony - You will play a sweet harmony for the Prince when jailed, thus, allowing the music to keep playing as if you were not Occupied.

Day Ability 1: Passionate Composition (3 uses)
Your music will be loud enough to block out visits to yourself tonight.

Night Ability 1: Romantic Serenade (Infinite uses)
Sing a romantic song to somebody tonight, they will be immune to Occupation.

Night Ability 2: Royal Melody (2 uses)
You will play a sweet Melody to the court during the next day. All Royals will be immune to Day Abilities the next day.

Goal: Don’t let the music die.

The Second Discord of Lies Exclusive Class

The Baron (Neutral Offensive)

Immune to Conversion

Passive: Royal Revenge - If you are executed for treason, the King will die the next day.

Night Ability 1: Royal Decree (2 uses)
Take Royal Blood from them if they have it. Give Royal Blood to them if they don’t.

Night Ability 2: Fraudulent Shaming (Infinite uses)
If your target is voted for Treason, all the trial votes will be reversed. Execute will be Pardon and Pardon will be Execute. Abstains will be normal.

Day Ability 1: Proclaim Throne (1 use)
Reveal the King’s true alignment to you.
Note: Usable when King is voted to Trial

Day Ability 2: Prison Ordain (2 uses)
The Prince will not be able to execute tonight.

Goal: Survive to see the Blue Dragon perish.

New/Reworked Commands

Admin-Only commands

  • class playerNumber className (DM command, no prefix)
    Description: Sets the player with this position number to the class specified.

  • addHost @Username#tag
    Description: Adds people to the hosts (in the config.json file). Only hosts or the owner can use this command.

Public commands

  • prison:
    Description: Sends the rest of the message to the prison channel (can only be used by the Prince).

  • imprison playerID
    Description: Automatically sets your target ready to get imprisoned. You can change targets as well.

  • execute
    Description: Sets the player in prison ready for an execution.
