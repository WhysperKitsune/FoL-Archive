Gameplay

  • You will be provided with a DM containing your class card. You will send your actions there.
  • Days will be 48 hours long, and Nights will be 24 hours long.
  • There is an execution Day 1.
  • Executions are both majority and plurality.
  • The Blue Dragon and Hand of Byzantium will speak through private forum messages or discord servers
  • The Soulcatcher/Blasphemer/Priest/Saint will speak with the dead through a private forum message.
  • The Exiled will speak through a private forum message.
  • The Archivist/Princess/Librarian‚Äôs log network will be held in a private forum message.
  • Both evil factions will cap at 4 members.
  • If both evil factions attempt to convert the same player in the same night, the faction with less members will convert that player. If both factions have equal amounts of members, the Blue Dragon will convert that player.
  • The maximum amount of non-unique duplicate classes is 3, barring those with the Prestige passive.
  • There may be up to 3 classes with Prestige in the game. All Unseen classes with Prestige besides the Exiled are unique. If a starting member of the Blue Dragon or Hand of Byzantium has Prestige, then the Unseen equivalent with Prestige cannot spawn.
  • If the King ever chooses to use Royal Pardon, it will be publicly announced.
  • Any time the word ‚Äúantithetical‚Äù is used, it means that redchecks become greenchecks, greenchecks become redchecks, and class types change based on:
    Killer = Social
    Offensive = Investigative
    Investigative = Killer
    Support = Offensive
    Social = Special
    Special = Support

Executions

  • To accuse someone say ‚Äú/vote [player]‚Äù. Please make the life of the hosts easier by bolding it (by putting ** ** around it)
  • Should the votes reach the majority, that player will be executed and the day will end.
  • When the day ends, the player with the most votes will be executed.
  • Voting ‚Äú/no execution‚Äù is an option.
  • You are allowed to vote yourself.

Picking a new King

  • If there are less than 4 players a new king will not be selected.
  • When the king dies a 12 hour period starts. In this, players may vote in their class PM for who they want to become the next king.
  • Whoever receives the most votes in this period will be crowned king. If votes are tied, a king election will be held the next day to determine it.

Journals

DM the host what you want on your journal. When you die, the last update to your journal will be revealed to everyone. It has a character limit of 1000 characters.
