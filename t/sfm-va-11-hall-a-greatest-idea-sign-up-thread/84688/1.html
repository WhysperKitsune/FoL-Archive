VA-11 HALL-A GREATEST IDEA

Greatest Idea Mafia is a semi-open setup designed to be flexible while adding quite a few extra layers of WIFOM and a creative UPick-esque role choice aspect.

Mechanics

  • Pre-game is 24 hours, Day is 48 hours, Night is 24 hours.
  • In this setup, each player is dealt three cards from a deck (shown below for convenience).
  • Two of these three cards are combined to produce the player‚Äôs role and alignment (more on this below) and the final card is discarded.
  • Two cards must be used, so no player may choose a single card to indicate both role and alignment.
  • Once every player has chosen their two cards, all players inform the moderator of their role/alignment combination and any roles with partners are informed of their partners.
  • At the start of Day 1, every player‚Äôs discarded card is publicly revealed.
  • If a player does not pick their role combination, I‚Äôll randomly sort it out.
  • Roles are assigned to players via the Random.org List Randomizer.
  • Post minimum is 20*day. However if your faction members complain that you are inactive you‚Äôll be proded earlier. After a prod you have 24 hours to answer before a replacement is sought.
  • There is no Day 1 lynch.
  • Votes are both majority and plurality.
  • Balance is explicitly not guaranteed.
  • Alts allowed.

Blacklist

Because of the special ‚Äúchoose 2‚Äù mechanic, many new and bastard roles are possible given certain role combinations.

Non-town Innocent Children and Masons of different alignments may not spawn.

Greatest Idea Modifications

Different from traditional Greatest Idea, read with care

  • If only one Mason or Lover is chosen, then they are essentially a named Vanilla Townie.
  • If more than 2 Masons or Lovers are chosen, then all the Masons and Lovers form separated pairs.
  • The One-Shot PGO is manually activated.
  • I‚Äôll only run setups that start with at least 50% Town roles
  • The Alien faction has only an one-shot factional nightkill for the whole game.
  • The Innocent Child can choose to reveal itself at whatever moment it chooses. It is not revealed automatically at the start of Day 1.
  • The Enabler affects one randomly mod-selected other player. If the Enabler dies, its Enablee can no longer use any role powers other than a factional nightkill.
  • In Greatest Idea games, Judas and Saulus‚Äôs alignment-change-on-elimination powers are an ALIGNMENT effect, not an ABILITY. Think of the card as ‚ÄòVanilla Judas‚Äô and ‚ÄòVanilla Saulus‚Äô.
