THE WRAITH OF OUTPOST 420

FADE IN

INT. GUARD ROOM

A dimly lit, relatively small room only illuminated by two hanging lamps. Two guards (#1 and #2) are playing checkers on a grey table, sitting on two uncomfortable looking chairs in the center of the room. One guard (#3) is in a corner, playing solitaire near the door into the adjacent storage room. In the opposite corner is a door that connects to the rest of the outpost. Guard #1 looks older and more decorated than the other guards. They appear to not be talking until Guard #1 says something to Guard #2.

GUARD #1

So you heard the story of the wraith, right?

GUARD #2

I can’t say I have, no.

GUARD #1

Well, can’t fault you too much for that. You’re new here. Also, side note, you’re terrible at checkers.

GUARD #2

Yeah, yeah. So what’s the story about?

GUARD #1

Story goes that there’s a spirit that can possess the bodies of people, and that it’s contained somewhere in this outpost.

GUARD #2

Yeah, that can’t be right. The only thing this outpost contains are objects from medieval times. Why do we do that, anyway? I forgot.

GUARD #1

They say it’s for preservation, but I don’t buy it. The spirit’s supposedly ALSO from medieval times, and (leans forward) I believe it’s trapped in the iron maiden. (he points to the door to the storage room) That’s why they moved all the old junk here, to get the spirit away from the continents where it can actually harm people. That’s also why they tell us we can’t open the maiden.

GUARD #2

Nah, I think it’s for preservation. Spirits existing, and HERE of all places? You gotta be joking. Also, it’s still your turn.

GUARD #1

Maybe I’m just superstitious, but I feel like I’m right. Hell, you’re usually in the room with the objects, I can’t be the only one who’s unnerved by the things.

GUARD #2

…actually, I have heard the story of the wraith before. Most of what you said is the same as what I know, but you got one little detail wrong.

GUARD #1

And what’s that?

GUARD #2

I go by “possessor”, bitch.

Suddenly, Guard #2 reaches across the table and grabs Guard #1 by the throat. Guard #2 collapses into a bloody pile and Guard #1 is seemingly fine. The whole time, Guard #3 watches in shock. Guard #1 slowly turns around to stare at Guard #3.

GUARD #1

Don’t mind me, I’m okay now.

Guard #3 rushes through the door he was next to into the room which stores the medieval objects.

INT. MEDIEVAL ROOM

The medieval room is a very dimly lit and large room, lit by only a couple hanging lamps. The room consists of a catapult, a cannon, an iron maiden, a suit of armor, many antique swords, and several other miscellaneous medieval objects. Guard #3 notices that the door to the iron maiden is open, even though it isn’t supposed to be. He then rushes to hide behind the cannon by crouching. He hears the door open and footsteps walk into the room. After three minutes pass, Guard #3 hears the footsteps close to where he is, and peeks out. Guard #1 is dangerously close. After one minute passes, Guard #1 leaves the area close to Guard #3, and Guard #3 slinks to behind the catapult. He picks up a nearby antique shield and tosses it between the catapult and the open iron maiden. He hears footsteps rushing to where he threw the shield.

GUARD #1

GOTCHA-wait, huh? (picks up the shield, inspecting it)

GUARD #3

I’m WHEELy sorry about this!

Guard #3 forcefully shoves the catapult into Guard #1, which rolls right into him, causing him to stumble into the iron maiden. Guard #3 hastily slams the door of the maiden shut with the possessed Guard #1 inside.

GUARD #3

Neuts out? More like…(looks at camera) neuts in. (Slowly puts on sunglasses)

THE END
