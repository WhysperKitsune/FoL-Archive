Sorcerer

Neutral Offensive

Passive: Mage Armor: You are immune to death, occupation, redirection, poison, and bleeding.

Day Ability: Magic Dust: Make everyone appear as the Sorcerer tonight. 4 uses.

Night Ability: Curse: Occupy a player. Unlimited uses.

Night Ability: Circle of Death: Kill all visitors to someone. 1 use.

Live to see the Blue Dragon lose.

Inquisitor

Neutral Investigative

Passive: No one expects the Spanish Inquisition!: You are immune to death once.

Passive: Failure: If the Sorcerer is killed by someone other than you, you will commit suicide.

Night Ability: Find Magic: Detect if someone is the Sorcerer.

Night Ability: Ruthless Efficiency: Kill someone. Bypasses Sorcerer’s immunity. If the Sorcerer is alive after you use both charges, you will commit suicide. 2 uses.

Kill the Sorcerer with Ruthless Effiency
