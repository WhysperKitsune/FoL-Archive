The Time Warper

Blue Dragon Support

Passive (Delay) - All actions against players will be delayed by 1 night. Actions will be shown as they’ve succeeded.

Day Ability (Back To The Present) - Make a player Immune to Death tonight.(1 Use)

Night Ability (Denounce) - Make a player Immune to Delay’s effects.(Infinite Uses)
Goal: Defeat The Unseen/The Cult and any Neutrals that seek you harm.

Converts into:

The Clock Master

Unseen Support

Passive (Delay) - All actions against players will be delayed by 1 night. Actions will be shown as they’ve succeeded.

Night Ability (Denounce) - Make a player Immune to Delay’s effects.(Infinite Uses)
Night Ability (Shattered Time) - Only The Unseen will be able to visit your target tonight.(1 Use)
Goal: Defeat The Blue Dragon and any Neutrals that seek you harm.

The Technician

Blue Dragon Support

Day Ability (Hard Work) - You will be Immune to Death tonight.(1 Use)

Night Ability (Fuse) - Anyone attempting to occupy/redirect yourself or your target will be killed.(Infinite Uses - Will not work if the target is Occupy Immune/Redirection Immune - Can NOT Target the King)
Night Ability (Fearless) - If you die tonight , your killer will be occupied the next night.(1 Use)
Goal: Defeat The Unseen/The Cult and any Neutrals that seek you harm.

Converts into:

The Hacker

Unseen Offensive

Day Ability (Hacked!) - Disable a player’s abilities tonight.(1 Use)

Night Ability (Deflected) - Make a player target themselves.(3 Uses - Does NOT Bypass Redirection Immunity.)
Night Ability (Watchdog) - If your target visits you tonight , they will be occupied.(Infinite Uses)
Goal: Defeat The Blue Dragon and any Neutrals that seek you harm.
