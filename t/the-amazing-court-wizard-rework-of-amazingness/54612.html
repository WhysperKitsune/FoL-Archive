Court Wizard

BD Support

Day Ability 1 - Overcharge - Your target will be able to use their night action twice tonight (this does not affect multi-target actions or actions that can only target one player e.g. execute) - 2 uses

Night Ability 1 - Double Up - You will copy the ability of your first target and use it on your second target, unless it is a harmful ability such as killing - Infinite Uses

Night Ability 2 - Ricochet - All players using an action on your target will instead use that action on themself - 1 Use

Sage

Unseen Support

Day Ability 1 - False Promise - Your target will be told they are able to use their night action twice tonight (as though they had been Overcharged), however they will be occupied - 2 uses

Night Ability 1 - Copycat - You will copy the ability of your first target and use it on your second target, you cannot copy the abilities of the Assassin or Mastermind this way - Infinite Uses

Night Ability 2 - Bounce Back - All players using an action on your target will instead use that action on themself, if that action is a kill, it instead does nothing - 1 Use
