Version 1

Princess

Blue Dragon Investigative

Night ability 1: Flirt(Uses: Unlimited)- limit your target’s class type down to 2 options(Same as current ones)
Night ability 2: Will-o-Wisp(Uses: 2)- Determine your target’s faction
Day ability 1: Charm(Uses: 2)- Flirt will determine your target’s exact class type.

Notes

  • To prevent power creep, snoop should be removed from Marshal before this is added.

  • To view a copy of a players logs, , the logs will be displayed next to the player’s name(where the logs are shown upon death) instead of being displayed above the name list. The icon for snooped logs will remain the same, however.

  • If you snoop the same player multiple times then the icon will be put next to their previous logs and a number will appear on it that identifies which is which.

  • Forge and Shred can only be used on players whom you already have a copy of their logs. For forge, when you edit it, you will edit the copy that was there at the time you snooped it.

  • If you forge a player and you have several copies of their logs then you may decide which copy to forge.

  • Forge, Shred, and Snoop may not target the King

Duchess

Unseen Social

Night Ability 1: Bat Eyelashes(Uses: Unlimited)- Determine your target’s class type and faction
Night Ability 2: Snoop(Uses: 3)- Gain a copy of your target’s logs. You will be able to view this copy at any time
Day ability 1: Shred(Uses: 1)- Erase your target’s journal and prevent them from writing in it further. You will gain a copy of it
Day ability 2: Forge(Uses: 1)- Gain a copy of your target’s logs. You will be able to edit this copy at any time. If your target dies, then their logs will be replaced with your edited version.
