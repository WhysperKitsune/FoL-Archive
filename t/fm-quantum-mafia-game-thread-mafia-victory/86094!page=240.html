fun fact im seeing a lot of similarities between this game (at this stage in particular but an extension at previous points) and another social deduction game i know of

its called Timebomb

Game Explanation

everyone has 5 cards in front of them face down. out of all the cards in front of all players, a number equal to the number of players are Successes, one is a Boom!, and the rest are duds.
at the start of the round, each player learns how many of each type of card they have in front of them, but not which is which. (so they essentially just know the chances at flipping over each kind of card)
1 or 2 players are scum and the rest are town. the scum do not know who their allies are
scum’s goal is to prevent the town from revealing all of the successes, or to reveal the bomb at any point before that.
in a round, one player has the clippers. They go first, and choose to to reveal a card from in front of another player. they then hand the clippers to that player and they do this again. play repeats until a number of cards equal to the number of players have been revealed
at that point all the cards are collected, revealed successes are set aside, revealed duds are discarded, and then the cards are redistributed, now with 4 cards in front of each player. they are again told the amounts of the different types of cards they have, but not which card is which. this continues until after the round where each player has 2 cards. if after that round at least one success is still hidden, or if at any point before the bomb is revealed, scum wins. if at any point all successes are revealed, town wins

its a game where neither side has more information than the other, but where scum can still hide and deceive the town. its a game where one can use the numbers to make an intuitive guess as to what kind of strategy scum might be using. and its a game where its possible to figure out where the things you want to flip might be hiding, but you cant be fully sure if it will work or not, and where there just might be a bomb waiting to be flipped alongside a success.
i feel the similarities from there should be obvious.
