The Warlock

Unique Neutral Offensive

Goal: Survive
(Starts with 0 Souls)

Passives:

Voidlord: Immune to Occupation and Target Change

Deathseer: Can talk to Ghouls at night

Abilities:

Bloodbloom: (D) (2 Souls)
Heal yourself tonight.

Shadow Essence: (D) (1 Soul)
If you have a ghoul, gain an extra vote today in accusing.

Soul Tap: (N) (Unlimited)
Occupy a player. If successful, Take a soul.

Mortal Coil: (N) (2 Souls)
Convert a dead Neutral player into a Ghoul. If that Neutral has already won, you cannot convert them into a Ghoul.
(I.e. Lynched Fool, Inquisitor, Scorned)

The Ghoul

Neutral Offensive

Goal: Make sure the Warlock succeeds.

Passives:

Undead: Player will appear dead to all living players. May not accuse or vote, and will speak in dead chat.

Soul Steal: If you are the first Ghoul, any Neutral players that the Warlock converts into a Ghoul will become a dead Ghoul, and you will recharge all of your abilities.

Abilities:

Grave Chant: (D) (1)
Have the next Nightmare bypass occupy immunity.

Hellish Wall: (N) (2)
Occupy all visitors to the Warlock tonight. Bypasses occupy immunity.

Nightmare: (N) (3)
Occupy a player tonight.
