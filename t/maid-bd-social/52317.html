Here is a suggestion for another social class, based on some of the old noble’s abilities like hearsay and secret message, which I thought were really fun and interesting abilities but sadly got removed.

The Maid
Day Ability 1: Eavesdrop
Select a player. You will be alerted if said player speaks tonight. (Prince, Jailed player, Gossip, Psychic, Linked players, Unseen/Cult, etc). 2 uses.
Day Ability 2: Expose
The next whisper from anyone will be made public. 3 uses.
Night Ability 1: Gossip
Ask all players for gossip, up to three people will be able to send an anonymous message to the Maid. Unlimited uses.
(This is my attempt to make Noble’s hearsay come back. It would work like this: As soon as you click the ability, everyone would get a message saying “A Maid is looking for Gossip!” and all living players would get a box like the one for whispers. Every player can write one message in the box, and the maid will be able to read only the first three messages sent. The Maid will not know who is sending her the messages).
Night Ability 2: Secret Message
Allows a player to write an anonymous message that will be displayed at the beginning of the day. 2 uses.

The Housekeeper (Placeholder name)
Day Ability 1: Trollbox
Select a player and whisper a short phrase into the trollbox. It will appear as if that player said it. 2 uses.
Day Ability 2: Expose
The next whisper from anyone will be made public. 3 uses.
Night Ability 1: Gossip
Ask all players for gossip, up to three people will be able to send a message to the Maid. You will know who exactly sends a message. Unlimited uses.
Night Ability 2: Cleanup
Select a player, if they die tonight their class and logs will be hidden. 2 uses.
