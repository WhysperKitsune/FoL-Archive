Guys I had this amazing idea for how we can make ToL have a ranked mode.First we rename Throne of Lies to Town of Salem.Then we rename the classes.Hunter would become Veteran who shoots everyone.Knight would become Bodyguard and Vigilante.Prince would become Jailor.Observer would become Lookout.Sheriff would stay the same.Princess would become Investigator.Drunk and Butler would become escort.Noble would become mayor.Physician would become doctor.CW would become transporter.Psychic becomes medium.Fool becomes jester,scorned becomes executioner.alch becomes survivor.Also we add amnesiac.arsonist,werewolf,spy and serial killer.And we remove
King,Reaper,Possessor,Sorcerer,and Inquisitor.After that we rename Imperium42 to Blank Media Games.Also we make minute long queue times to get into ranked because no one wants a leaver or AFK. We also should rename the factions.Blue Dragon would become town and Unseen would become mafia.
Also we need to get rid of conversions because that’s dumb.We should just make a mafia role instead of a long list of conversions.My suggestions are a blackmailer,consort,framer,forger,disguiser,consigliere,janitor,godfather,and a mafioso to round out the mafia.
Then we change it so that all deaths are announced and we know the cause of death.
Also we should change it so that we know if a target is jailed or not and when you are in jail you know if you are attacked or not.Also the targets should be alerted when transported but not told who to or if they’re were redirected.If we do all of these changed I think Throne of Lies or Town of Salem as we are calling it with this rework will have a really good ranked mode.
I forgot a couple of things-We should add a retributionist to revive one dead Town.
Paladin would become Vampire Hunter and Cult would become Vampires which can can convert anyone town or anyone without basic defense.I also forgot that we should completely change the death immune mechanic.We should have 4 tiers for defense None,Basic,Powerful,Invincible. People with powerful defense don’t die to people with basic attacks.Also we should have 4 tiers of attack None Basic,Powerful,Unstoppable.The way this would work is that defense will always beat attack.So a basic defense will stop a basic attack but not a powerful one.Also we should rename classes to roles because classes is dumb for naming something where as roles tell you what your job or role is.

Side note though this is literally what some people are proposing in ToL to add a ranked mode.Conversions just wouldn’t work in a ranked environment as you could be doing really well but still end up with a loss.
