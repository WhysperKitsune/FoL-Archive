[Class Suggestion] The messenger

Feedback/Suggestions
NagoGiovanna January 29, 2018, 9:35pm #1

Right now, with the new king changes, information is scarce in the land of the blue dragon. BD can’t trust each other, they risk too much and gain too little. but what if there was a class that could safely convey the information, without risking his life instantly?
in comes in THE MESSENGER

type: social
goal: convey info between the secretive BD without calling them out

passive the perks of the job: the messenger will be able to see all the messages he conveys, but not who sends them.

Day ability
royal chatter: allows a member of the royal family (prince, princess, …drunk? and king) to talk to the others safely, without revealing who they are. they will only talk to each other as their titles, so there won’t be a need to reveal who they are. only a few charges, its a pretty strong ability.

utmost secrecy: the messenger allows for a secretive meeting between 3 players he chooses at night. he can hear what is being said, but not who is saying it. allows to organize tactics or call out someone without needing to reveal them to the whole world. can be turned against him if he uses it with the wrong people.

Night ability
mail routes reveal the whispers of the selected character to the royal character of his choice the next day.

while this is obviously a very rough concept, I just wanted to bring it up to see how we can shape this class up together. I think BD has a lack of safe routes for communication, as psychic is the only one currently filling that position and its hard to convey messages through the psychic, due to the links being only between the psychic and one other, and the fact that the psychic can’t really trust if the man who claims to be prince really is one.

there could obviously be an unseen version that actually tampers the communication between the BD, being able to pretend to be whispering someone as a different character, changing who receives the whispers of X character, or even making public the previous whispers of someone.

thanks for reading.
