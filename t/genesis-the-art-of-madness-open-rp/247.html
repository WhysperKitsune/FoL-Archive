One important thing I must state before anything else…

You do not have to read the entire game to start playing!

I’m making this because of a roleplay thread we have going on at the other forums I’m currently active on. I decided to bring the madness here, so have fun.

To join, all you have to do is make a character (Basic needs are Name, Sex, and Race. You can make up addition info as you go along if you want to.). Your character does not have to be a human, and you can control multiple characters at once if you’d like.

Also, rules of course:

  • Everyone does not have to be in the same place at once. You can be in different places from other characters
  • Don’t be a Fade with your characters
  • No demi-gods, Gods, or anyone who is godly in anyway. You cannot be all-powerful, all-versatile, and you cannot erase anyone from existence and no all-powerful demons. Nothing wrong with demons though.
  • You cannot attack souls.
  • No genocidal maniacs. Seriously, the last thing we want is someone killing everyone the moment they come along.
    Actually put effort into your characters.
  • This is a game with limitless possibilities. You can have your character face off 50 flying devil bunnies, or tame ghost dragons. You can even kill off your character and have a dramatic revival. Anything is possible in this world… HOWEVER This rule does not interfere with other rules, and you must consider them while using this knowledge.
  • Your character cannot do OP moves (such as the ability to kill 100 enemies per second or something)
  • Killing off other characters without permission is strictly prohibited.
  • Your formatting isn’t restricted, but it is recommended that you type OOC comments in parenthesis, actions can be whatever you feel is comfortable. Personally, I use bold and italics, but you can do whatever you’d like. For characters, you can make a unique color for them, or you can just type “Character: Text”. Whatever you feel is best.
  • Anyone can continue the story. The first post made for it will always be canon (unless they take it back). Any other post that would contradict it is not canon (unless agreed on).
  • And finally, the most important rule, have fun!
