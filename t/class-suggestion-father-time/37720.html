[Class Suggestion] Father Time

Class/Lore/Fanfic
Solic January 17, 2018, 11:26pm #1

Father Time - (Neutral Killer) This NK manipulates the aspect of time in ToL in interesting ways.

Win Condition: Same as reaper/possessor.

Passive 1:
Groundhog Night - Father Time will upon being attacked, reset the night timer back to full, having every player choose their action again. They will obtain their original night results, but their actions will reset. Whoever attempted to attack Father Time cannot attack Father Time again that night. Father Time’s original action will not reset and he can attack again after the reset.

Passive 2:
All the Time in the World - Father Time is immune to occupation, retargeting and conversion.

Day Ability:
Skip to the Good Part - The day timer will lose 80 seconds. If it is 80 seconds or less night time will immediately begin. (2 uses)

Night Ability 1:
Your Time is Now - Attack targeted player and rapidly age him towards his death. (Unlimited uses)

Night Ability 2:
Chronic Corruption - Corrupt past selves of targeted player, attacking every player the target has visited in the game so far, including the player the targeted player visits tonight, bypasses night immunity. (2 uses)

Lore: Pending

Comments: Father Time’s most unique trait is Skip to the Good Part that ensures BD must act soon if Father Time is found out. Father Time can however easily frame someone by using this too. Skip the day when someone is found out as neutral and that person shall surely be executed. Groundhog Night gives him the needed night immunity in an unique form that does give him away entirely to his attacker, much like reaper’s night immunity. These both have great synergy with Chronic Corruption. There will be more nightly visits if days are skipped and the abilities used prior to a Groundhog Night activation still count as a visit. This makes targeting players who have visited many alive players the most important deduction step for Father Time. Father Time shouldn’t target players like knights or physicians that generally visit the same key player with this ability. The mere existence of Chronic Corruption also makes having a log full of dead people less suspicious, giving scum an easier time to fake-claim in a Father Time’s game. Conversely this also makes BD players amazingly less credible. “This drunk log only contains dead people and he didn’t step up for royal blood, exe!”. Lastly he has the standard attack targeted player this night ability.

I took your comments to heart and made some changes addressing them. I think it’s one of the core things that makes him unique, so I’m reluctant to completely take it out. Players now do get their results. This is only still possibly tedious for prince. The rest of the players receive feedback from the night before it resets. So a sheriff can investigate another target for instance, or a butler hitting an occupy immune, can choose to occupy someone else. This is obviously bad for Father Time, but to compensate for that he gets to use a kill ability twice. Also buffed Chronic Corruption slightly for that extra framing ability. The player that is targeted, will show as killer to wisps/watching observers now, similar to possessor.
