2 new King night abilities

Feedback/Suggestions
Shrimpy January 16, 2019, 10:38pm #1

I brainstormed a couple new King abilities, what do you think? They both utilize the voting feature but are night-time.

Outlaw (Night) - Instantly announce to everyone by a system message that a particular player has been made an outlaw for the night. The first person to click their name (in the same way one would accuse them of treason) will attack them, instead of using a night ability.

The King’s deathnote will appear on the outlaw if he is killed in this way.

Grand Jury (Night) - Instantly announce to everyone by a system message that the court’s opinion on a particular player is being surveyed. Simply click on their name (in the same way as one would accuse them of treason) in order to anonymously vote against them, or abstain otherwise.

The results of the survey are published the next morning.

Allies (Night) - Publicly announce you are seeking an ally. Every player is given the option to vote another. King will learn the classes of the three players with the most votes but not know which is which.
