Gameplay

Days will be 72 hours long, or shorter if majority is reached. Nights will be 24 hours long.
Lynches are majority and plurality, no lynch is an option.
Mafia have both a day and night chat.
The Mafia have an assigned factional kill.
If one of the members of the mafia dies, another one will assume their element as well as their own, and they may choose between the two elements they have to attack with.
When you receive your classcard, you have 24 hours to confirm you have received it.
There is a day one lynch.
There are no journals or logs of any kind.
Elemental Doctors can only heal an attack if it is of the same element as their chosen element that night.
The element of a goon is not revealed upon their flip. If a goon dies, they will be revealed as Elemental Goon.
The one-shot death immunity only takes effect if the sk would die otherwise, if he is attacked using his element that he chose, he will keep the oneshot death immunity

Lynches

To vote someone to be lynched type /vote [name]. To unvote type /unvote.

Class Cards

Town Citizen

You have no night abilities, however, you still have your vote and your intellect. Put them to good use!
You win when the Mafia and the Serial Killer are defeated.

Town Tracker

Once per night, you may target a player to see who they visit, if anyone.
You win when the Mafia and the Serial Killer are defeated.

Town Elemental Doctor

Once per night, you may select an element, fire, water and earth, and a target. If they are attacked using your chosen element, you will heal them, preventing their death.
You win when the Mafia and the Serial Killer are defeated.

Town Elemental Nurse

Once per game, if an Elemental Doctor dies, you will assume the role of Elemental Doctor.
You win when the Mafia and the Serial Killer are defeated.

Fire Element Goon

You are a member of the Mafia! You share a day and night chat with your teammates. Once per night, if you perform the factional kill, you will attack them using the Fire element.
You win when the Mafia equals or outnumbers the town, and when the Serial Killer is dead.

Water Element Goon

You are a member of the Mafia! You share a day and night chat with your teammates. Once per night, if you perform the factional kill, you will attack them using the Water element.
You win when the Mafia equals or outnumbers the town, and when the Serial Killer is dead.

Earth Element Goon

You are a member of the Mafia! You share a day and night chat with your teammates. Once per night, if you perform the factional kill, you will attack them using the Earth element.
You win when the Mafia equals or outnumbers the town, and when the Serial Killer is dead.

Elemental Serial Killer

You are a crazed madman, seeking to kill everyone in the town. Once per night, you may select an element and a target, you will attack that player using your chosen element. You are night immune once. You are also immune to the element you attack with each night.
You win when you are among the last two players standing.
