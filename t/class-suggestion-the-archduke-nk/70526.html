[Class Suggestion] The Archduke (NK)

Class/Lore/Fanfic

The Archduke

Neutral Killer
Megalomaniac (Passive) - As a Royal, you can put your name forward for nominations to become the next King. Becomes the Tyrannous King instead of the Psycho King.
Paranoia (Passive) - Allows the Archduke to listen on whispers and grants immunity against poison, bleeding, and death at night.
Tyrant’s Pressure (Day) - Force a player to accuse another player and prevent them from voting further. - 3 uses
Tyrant’s Informant (Day) - You will know the class type of the target player at the beginning of the night. - 3 uses
Political Murder (Night) - Kills a player without counting as a visit, but has a one-day cooldown period and cannot target the King. - Infinite uses
Coup d’Etat (Night) - Kills the King while bypassing night immunity. Can only be used in the fifth night or later. - 1 use
Your objective is to defeat the Blue Dragon, Unseen, Cult and any neutrals that dare stand in your way.

Crowns into:

The Tyrannous King

Neutral Special
Reign of Terror (Passive) - The Tyrannous King’s cruel guards are guaranteed kills against players who did not vote for him during the ballot phase of an election bypassing preventions, night immunity, and healing.
Tyrant’s Paranoia (Passive) - Grants immunity from occupation, redirection, bleeding, poison, death at night, and allows the Tyrannous King to read whispers. For the first time the Tyrant King is accused of treason and the majority vote for an execution, the Tyrant King will overrule the vote, pardon himself, and kill his first accuser. The self-pardoning does not work on the same day that the Tyrant King steps up and if this triggers then his cruel guards will only be able to kill one player at night.
Tyrant’s Finger (Day) - The King’s next vote for treason will count twice. - Infinite uses
Tyrant’s Decision (Day) - After the final vote, the King can veto the votes and decide if the fate of the person defending themselves. If the King decides to execute, the player on the stand is executed. If the King decides to pardon the player on the stand, they can’t be accused of treason again that day. - 1 use
Cruel Guards (Night) - Allows the Tyrannous King to send guards to two players at once, killing them both. - Infinite uses

Theme: A neutral killer that has a weak KPN potential but can meld in with the court as a Princess or Noble claim, and then becomes an extremely dangerous NK if he becomes King.
Survivability: Non-visiting attacks allow him to survive bears and Knights, meanwhile he is by default immune to poison and bleeding.
Late-game: Becoming King late-game is essentially GG for the Archduke.
