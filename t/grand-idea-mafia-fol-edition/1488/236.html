The Herald

Blue Dragon Social
Influential (Passive) - Your votes secretly count as two.
Noble Agreements (Passive) - You may speak with Nobles, Aristocrats, and Noble Apostles at night. They will see you as the Herald.
Announce (Day) - Send a message to all players. They will know that the Herald spoke. (3 uses)
Improve Presence (Night) - Choose a player. Their vote will secretly count as two tomorrow.
Private Talk (Night) - Open a night chat with someone for the night. Both of you may use it freely until the night ends.
BD victory condition.

Converts into the Harbinger or the Apostle.

The Harbinger

Unseen Social
Influential (Passive) - Your votes secretly count as two.
Noble Agreements (Passive) - You may speak with Nobles, Aristocrats, and Noble Apostles at night. They will see you as the Herald.
Announce (Day) - Send a message to all players. They will know that the Herald spoke. (3 uses)
Improve Presence (Night) - Choose a player. Their vote will secretly count as two tomorrow.
Private Talk (Night) - Open a night chat with someone for the night. Both of you may use it freely until the night ends.
Unseen victory condition.

Note: “Votes secretly count as two” means that, for example, in a 7-player scenario, if a Herald accuses a player, only two more votes will be needed to put them on trial. This way, no one knows whose vote counts as double, because in FoL, it is not said how many votes there are - only how many players have voted.
