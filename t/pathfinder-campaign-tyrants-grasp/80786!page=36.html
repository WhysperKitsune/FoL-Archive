For explanation, most races see normal, have low-light vision or darkvision 60 feet.
With normal sight you can see fine in normal light, in dimlight you can still somewhat see but get penalties, and in darkness you are effectively blind.
For example, a torch or a light spell has 20 feet radius normal light, and until 40 feet it gives dimlight.
With low-light vision you can see double as far.
With darkvision you can see 60 feet around you as if it would be normal light, but this vision is colourless.
