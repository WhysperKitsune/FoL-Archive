Dead Interactions - Splitting the Difference (Simple Proposal)

Feedback/Suggestions
TinyDeer March 19, 2018, 8:42pm #1

If there will be zero dead interactions, here is a simple proposal:

  • Allow the dead to see all whispers.

Here is what this would accomplish:

  • Allows dead to better observe/comment on gameplay choices and to openly discuss the game between factions.

  • Something to look forward to after dying. (“Damn, I got killed - let’s see what’s going on behind the scenes!”)

  • No risk of this knowledge impacting the game, since there are zero interactions between living and dead.

This would make death more interesting as well as increase the opportunity to analyze and discuss gameplay.
