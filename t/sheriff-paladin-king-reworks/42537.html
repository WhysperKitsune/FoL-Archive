Sheriff, Paladin, King Reworks

Class/Lore/Fanfic
Livicus February 5, 2018, 2:17am #1

Okay, the problems we did need were fixed, but it’s my opinion that the game could be even better if polished and honed. I don’t like the awkward way that we have two different investigatives that are just soft counters for different factions. And the King, in his current state, wields too much direct power that letting him loose, even at a 20% chance, is a liability.

Both the Sheriff and the Paladin should be able to appear in all games.

At least one Sheriff or Paladin will appear in every single game. (Similar to how at least one Physician or Alchemist will appear in every single game.)

So, these are my rework ideas. (I do not expect everyone to agree with this, I’m just experimenting and see what ideas people may have on this topic.)

The King

Passives

Divine Right:
You are immune to occupation, target change, and have two votes for all accusals of treason.

Royal Grief:
If executed during the day as a Good King, the following day, Royal Blood will not work for all players.

Abilities

Decide Fate: (D) (1)
Decide the outcome for the accused player.

Private Matter: (D) (3) *
All whispers to and from you will be hidden today.

Guards: (N) (4)
Send a guard to make a player night immune tonight.

Royal Scribe: (N) (3) *
Choose a player. That night, you will see all actions that happen to them. Tomorrow, you will see all whispers to and from that player.

*- Only applies to Starting King

The Sheriff

Blue Dragon Investigative

Converts to The Marshal

Passives

Constable:
You are immune to conversion to the Unseen starting from Day 7.

Abilities

Scout: (D) (oo)
Choose a player. You will be notified if they were converted.

Case Trial: (D) (2)
For that day, you will see all votes for pardoning or killing an accused person cast in real-time.

Expert Investigation: (N) (oo)
Determine if a player is evil or not suspicious.

The Paladin

Unique Blue Dragon Investigative

Converts to The Templar

Passives

Righteous Zeal:
You are immune to conversion to the Cult starting from Day 8.

Abilities

Consecration: (D) (3)
Choose a player. They cannot be targeted by Day Abilities today. If affected by Distract, Silence, or Icy Touch, cancel it.

Holy Fervor: (D) (2)
When you use Test Faith tonight, you will attack if they are evil. Does not bypass night immunity.

Test Faith: (N) (oo)
Determine if a player is Evil or Not Suspicious.

Smite: (N) (2)
If anyone attempts to convert you or your target tonight, you will attack them, not bypassing night immunity. If they survive, they will not be able to use their Day Abilities tomorrow.

The Templar

Unique Unseen Support

Passives

Abilities

Consecration: (D) (2)
Choose a player. They cannot be targeted by Day Abilities today. If affected by Distract, Icy Touch, Silence, etc, cancel it.

Silence: (D) (3)
Choose a player. They cannot talk for the next 20 seconds. Cannot be used during a trial. (Distract should be buffed so it works for 20 seconds)

Dark Fervor: (N) (3)
Choose a member of the Unseen. Any limited abilities they use tonight will not lose a charge.

PLEASE LEAVE FEEDBACK
