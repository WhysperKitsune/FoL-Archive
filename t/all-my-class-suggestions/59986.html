Golden Dragon

I was thinking about having a second ‚ÄúGood‚Äù faction, with different classes than the Blue Dragon to help make games more diverse. This class would be an alternative to the Blue Dragon, and games would have either Golden Dragon or Yellow Dragon. Here is a suggestion for the faction called ‚ÄúThe Golden Dragon‚Äù.
The main differences this faction has over the Blue Dragon are:

  • The Queen is a more ‚ÄúSocial‚Äù version of the King.
  • The only guaranteed role the Golden Dragon has is ‚ÄúThe Bailiff‚Äù which works as a nonconvertible Sheriff/Paladin.
  • There are no classes with royal blood, instead the Queen is able to give royal blood to whoever she wants.
  • All royals are able to speak to the Queen and each other at night as long as the Queen is alive.
