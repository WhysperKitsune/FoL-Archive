Vulgard May 21, 2020, 2:11pm #512

Removed invest immunity from Harbinger because it felt pointless. Arbiter bypassed it anyway, and the only other HoB class with a direct alignment check is Socialite, who has a 1-shot parity check.

Also, Elder now loses their immunities upon joining the factional chat. The reason is because learning who your teammates are is enough of a benefit to offset this – plus, it felt weird to me to keep things like “Syndicate attacks you, you gain X” when you’re already in their chat and they know who you are. This makes Elder weaker mechanically after it joins the chat, by a significant amount; however, their individual power beforehand is probably the highest among all the wolf classes, so it balances itself out. You could actively try not to find the wolves, but you will be at a strategic disadvantage if you do this and you also won’t get sweet, sweet dayvigs.
