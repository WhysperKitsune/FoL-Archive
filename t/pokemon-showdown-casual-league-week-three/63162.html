My basic idea is a first phase of League:-

Rules

  • Play each team once
  • Matches will consist of a best-of-three series of battles
  • Teams will consist of 9 pokemon - 6 mainboard/3 sideboard
  • At the start of each match, you must have your 6 mainboard pokemon making up your party
  • Before the start of battles 2 and 3 in each match, you may swap out any member of your team for any pokemon in your sideboard
  • Each team must have no repeat items or repeat pokemon
  • Pokémon levels are set to 65
  • The following Pokémon are banned
