Good King Reworks:

Good King Rework (1) - Swear Fealty.

Summary

Good King (1)
Passive(1): Tenacity, Occupy and Redirection Immune, Votes are doubled.
Passive(2): Loyalty, If the starting Good King is executed, then the court will be unable to accuse anyone of treason for a day.
Day Ability(1): Decide Fate, Decide an accused player’s fate.
Night Ability(1): Guards, Select someone or yourself they will be Immune tonight. Targets will not know if they where attacked or not and attackers won’t know if they where immune. (3 Uses) [Starting King gets 1 Extra Use.]
Night Ability(2): Swear Fealty, Make a player permanently conversion Immune, cannot use night 1 [Starting king only] (1 Use)
