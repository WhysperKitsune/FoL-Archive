Welcome to The Resistance! (Also known as Avalon)

Summary

The Resistance is a social deception game in which the objective for the uninformed majority, the Resistance, is to see three(3) missions succeed while preventing the informed minority, the Imperials, from seeing three(3) missions fail. The game is played in 5 missions, each with a team agreed upon by the players. During the mission, the players must decide the outcome of the mission by choosing to either succeed or fail the mission. Failures should be deemed as suspicious as only Imperials can fail a mission.

Rules:

  • Established community rules must be adhered to. https://forum.imperium42.com/t/global-forum-discord-game-meta-rules-please-read-first/244
  • Standard forum games etiquette should be observed.
  • Do not post role cards or DM anyone but the host.
  • Should the Resistance successfully complete their objective, do not reveal your roles immediately because the Assassin can still try to kill the Commander.

Gameplay:

  • This game will be nightless, but actions will still be submitted through direct messages.
  • Team selection will be 48 hours.
  • Voting and mission decisions will be 24 hours.
  • Discussion will be allowed at all times.

Team Selection:

  • At any point during discussion, the Leader may select a team by posting:

@cbman
/choose @Player_1 @Player_2 @Player_3 etc.
/confirm

  • If a selection is not made by the deadline, it will be considered a failed vote, and the next person will become Leader.
  • You may only reject four(4) teams without consequences. If a fifth team is rejected, the Imperials immediately win. This count resets every mission.
  • Uncasted team votes will be considered accepted.
  • Uncasted mission actions will be considered as a success.

Voting/Mission:

  • Everyone will vote after a team has been selected. When requested, respond in direct message with /accept or /reject.
    – Votes will be decided by majority.
    – Should a vote fail, the next Leader may select a team.
    – Should a vote pass, the team members will respond in direct message with either /succeed or /fail, when requested.
    – Resistance members can not /fail.
