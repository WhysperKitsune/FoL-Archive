yeah welcome to the internet. Though you are also misinterpreting how things work in general. The owner of any kind of server obviously gets the IPs of anyone connected that isn’t going through a proxy or a VPN. More or less that information doesn’t narrow someone’s location down to more than their local service provider (so roughly the area of a city).

From that information if something was significant enough that authorities were needed, they’d likely contact the authorities for that city, then the authorities would contact that ISP to find which residence has that IP address. In short… you need the co-operation of the ISP to get a useful address, and most likely they won’t co-operate with anyone other than law enforcement.
