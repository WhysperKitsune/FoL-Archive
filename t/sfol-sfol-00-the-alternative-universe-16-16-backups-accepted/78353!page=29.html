The Mind Flayer

Neutral Killer
Insanity (Passive) - You are immune to death at night, occupation and redirection.
Mindstorm (Passive) - Once per night you may guess a player’s class. If you are correct you will empower your attack via Mind Read. You will gain a one-night buff depending on who you kill with this passive. This passive will be disabled if you guess incorrectly twice. Cannot Target the King.
Insight (Day) - Gain a clue to your target’s class type. You receive the same results as princess. [4 Uses]
Mind Read (Day) - You will see who your target visits tonight. [4 Uses]
Brain Destruction (Night) - Kill a player. You will be able to choose who they target tonight, bypassing redirection immunity. You may not redirect the King. [Infinite Uses]
Whispers of Insanity (Night) - Cause a player to commit suicide, destroying their logs and allowing you to determine what they flip as the next day. You will receive their real class and their logs. [1 Use, Cannot Target the King]
Possible Buffs Gained:

  • Killer: Gain an addition use of Whispers of Insanity.
  • Offensive: You will occupy everyone visiting you, bypassing occupy immunity.
  • Support: You will heal yourself tomorrow night, curing infection and bleed.
  • Social: You will be guaranteed to visit your target.
  • Investigative: All uses of Insight and Mind Read will be replenished. Your visits will be hidden from Investigators.
  • Special: You will appear as the class of your choice to investigators.

Goal: Destroy both the major factions and any neutrals who may stand in your way.

Jack the Ripper
Neutral Killer

One With The Fog (Passive) - You will be immune to death and will gain additional powers for every successful kill.

  • 1 kill - Your visits will be hidden and will be prevented from visiting anyone that would lead to your death. You may also target 2 people with Stalk.
  • 2 kills - You will be immune to redirection and occupation. Additionally, gain a use of an ability of your choice.
  • 3 kills - You will be immune to all forms of delayed death and Eviscerate now bypasses immunity and healing and cleans the flip for other players. You will secretly receive their real classcard in your PM.
  • 4 kills - You can no longer die at night and Leer also prevents your target from voting.
  • 5 kills - Day abilities no longer take any uses and you will secretly have a double vote or take 1 more vote to lynch. You may switch between the double vote/loved passive at any time.
  • 6+ kills - You may use 2 day abilities every day.

Leer (Day) - Prevent a player from using day abilities today and occupying them tonight. [3 Uses]
Stalk (Day) - Learn who your target(s) visit tonight. [3 Uses]
Cutthroat Tactics (Day) - Your target will be bled at the end of the night, killing them in 1 night if not healed. [1 Use]
Eviscerate (Night) - Kill your target. This will bypass all forms of protection and prevention if your target has claimed at least one ability that they have. [Infinite]
Ambush (Night) - You will attack all players visiting your target tonight. [1 Use]
Win Condition: Survive to the final 3 or defeat all that would stand in your way.
