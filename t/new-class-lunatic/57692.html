                     •Lunatic

Faction - Neutral
Class - Killer

Lore: The lunatic is a killer that grows stronger as he lives on. He likes to observe his pray learning more about them

Notes:
You will start out the game as a level 1 killer. When you get the amount of kills needed to upgrade then you will atomaticly upgrade into the next level.

Level 1 - starts game. Needs 2 kills to got to next level
Level 2 - needs 5 kills to go to the next level
Level 3 - final level.

•Level 1

Passive 1:
Back stabber - kill anyone who attempsnto occu or redirect you, You are also night immune.

Day Ability 1:
Close watcher - discover a players class type (unlimited uses)

Night ability 1:
Drawing smiley faces - Select a player to draw a smile on, killing them.(unlimited uses) [the lunatic prefers to draw smiley faces on the victims hearts]

• Level 2

Passive 1:
Back stabber - kill anyone who try’s to occu or redirect you, You are also night immune.

Day Ability 1:
Close watcher - Find a players class type (unlimited uses)

Day Ability 2:
Quick learner - Discover a players faction (unlimited uses)

Night ability 1:
Drawing smiley faces - Select a player to draw a smile on, killing them.(unlimited uses) [the lunatic prefers to draw smiley faces on the victims hearts]

Night ability 2:
Craving blood - make 2 players bleed. If any of them die then if will count as a kill towards your progression towards the next level. (2 uses)

•Level 3

Passive 1:
Back stabber - kill anyone who try’s to occu or redirect you,You are also night immune.

Day Ability 1:
Stalk - discover a players faction and class type. (Unlimited uses)

Day Ability 2:
Quick learner - When you kill tonight, gain that players passive,If they have one. (2 uses). When successful once, you cannot use this ability again

Night ability 1:
Multi stabber - Kill a player and anyone who visits that player will bleed (unlimited uses)

Night ability 2:
silent footsteps - Kill a player in silent,meaning you will not be noticed by an observer. Also you will appear as class type investigative and BD to all you investigates you. ( 2 uses)

Win condition: murder all the main factions

Strategy - there is a lot of ways to blend in and disguise yourself. So use that to the best of your ability.
