Also, to make a game more about deception and less about keeping good logs. choosing the right king and not ust randomly getting an evil king.

The idea:
At the start of the game, there will be 2 kings, one of them is “fake”, and the other is good (1,16 posiotions)
This could be in every game of some of them.

Abilities:
passives:

  1. immune to deaths first 3 nights
  2. all the current king’s passive

day abilities:
defile (starting evil king only): when a player die at day, change the way he his apeared to the court. (2 uses)

Night abilities:
Allies: check if a player is cult/unseen (1 use, only useable on first 3 nights)
Speech: talk with 2 players at night

Election:
At the beggining of day 4 (can be day 5 too) the town will have an election, and vote for the king who stays at the game. The losing king will be executed. His identity won’t be revealed until the new king dies. the day won’t end after the election.

The new king abilities:
passive: current king’s passive, the new king can’t be voted out of the game, can’t be poisioned by butler, for the first 3 days after the election unless he executed a blue dragon player.
day: royal finger, defile (evil only)
night: 4 guards: 2 order execution.
