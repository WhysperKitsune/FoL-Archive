Monokuma appears with a long stick, it had fire on the end, and Celeste looked happy as she was going to go out in the most elegant way possible. Monokuma lit the hay below her on fire as a castle built itself around Celeste, she couldn’t think of a better way to go out of this life. Several Monokuma’s were watching.

The fire began to rise to Celeste, who was still smiling, suddenly the entire hay stack went into flames as it rised even closer to Celeste. Even the castle around her was setting on fire. Soon enough, everything but the giant concrete stick she was on was on fire. She was smiling, looking up. Until…

A Fire Truck was heard, and it came full speed towards Celeste, a ramp for good measure. The firetruck went onto the ramp and threw itself towards Celeste… until… Hifumi, out of the flames, appears and the firetruck smacks into him, the force of Hifumi’s weight sends Celeste flying out of the Concrete Chains and out of the execution grounds, the firetruck bouncing of Hifumi, smiling, is still alive, and turns to run back out of the execution grounds…

But, it’s far too late, the flames have blocked the exit, Hifumi, with no where else to go, sits down, thinking about anime. Hifumi knew he was the culprit, had to make a last ditch effort like Sakura did. He did a good deed, He thought, as the flames burnt his clothes, then him. Soon enough, all you could see was ash in his place. Then a Monokuma holding a fire hose, puts out the fire.
