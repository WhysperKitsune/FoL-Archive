The King's Allies - They Gotta Go

Feedback/Suggestions
Livicus January 17, 2018, 3:20am #1

I know that a while back, Allies was changed to the ability that enabled King to find Royal Blood classes. This was perfectly fine, as Allies, the most overpowered Investigative ability, just doesn’t fit the King and makes all early games almost the same (unless the starting King dies early and then things get interesting).

There were a lot of whiners, and Allies was brought back and edited.

But I think that Allies should go for the King to become a more Support and Social focused class who uses leads from others to make judgements.

There have been lots of us talking about this issue, and plenty of ideas that could replace this.

I think Private Matter is probably one of the best choices to give to the King. Make the Royal Finger as part of a Passive, or make it so that Private Matter becomes a Night-activate ability for the next day with a lot of uses. This would shift the starting King away from the Investigative-heavy position he has and turn him into the whispers and deduction champion that he’s meant to be.

I have here a possible idea for a reworked King.

The King

Passives

Tenacity
Immune to Occupying, Target Change, and all accusals for treason count as two

Justification
If a Good King is executed, then all Blue Dragon will be unable to use abilities the next night. The following day only, Royal Blood will not work for Blue Dragon. Unseen or Cult with Royal Blood can still step up, although not recommended in most cases.

(Prince’s Imprison and Sheriff’s Scout will still work and activate, as they are day abilities that take effect at night.)

Abilities

Private Matter* (2) (D)
Make all whispers to and from you invisible today.

Decide Fate (1) (D)
Choose to execute or pardon the accused player.

Royal Decree* (3) (N)
Prevent a player from being accused of treason tomorrow.

Guards (4) (N)
Make a player immune to attack that night.

Original Good King - Make a player immune to attack and conversion that night.

(*) - Abilities only the Original King has

And if this does become the new King, then I think the Invoker’s Bewilder should be changed so that the King’s Passives and Abilities are disabled that day.

And to balance it out, I think the ratio at which type of King spawns should be changed, favoring a Good King spawning more.

Possible:
Good King: 66%
Evil King: 33%
Neutral King: 1%
Psycho King: 0% (Still Mad King)

My thoughts, in short:

King

Allies is completely removed, never to return.

The starting King gains a social or support power, and the starting Good King gains a few things that the BD don’t want to lose to friendly fire. This should keep the King out of the central investigative role, but also prevent the staring King from being an instant kill from the BD.

Allies Replacement: All whispers to and from the starting King are silent.

BD Boon: Starting Good King’s guards protect against conversion as well as kills.

BD Punishment: If a Good King dies to public execution or Butler, the royal bloodline is useless and royals may not step up early.

These are all subject to change, and should be a big talking point. Investigative King is bad, it needs to go.
