The Paranoid (Neutral Killer)

Steel Door (Passive) - You are overly protective of your room and have made sure that no Killers can get to you. Immune to death at night.

Solid Mind (Passive) - If you are Occupied, you will be immune to it the next night but the Occupier will not be told.

Schizophrenic (Day Special) - If you’ve killed somebody using Paranoia, your mind will start playing tricks on you. Your next attack will kill anybody else visiting your target. If you are jailed, you will kill the Prince if you are not executed.

Panic Attack (Night) - Attack your target in a fit of panic. If they are an Investigative, you will destroy their journal. - Infinite uses

Paranoia (Night) - You will stay in your room tonight and over-think all possibilities and will kill any visitors. - 2 uses

Goal: Generic NK win condition

Note: This Class has received great feedback in the contest and the by FoL team and I never really published it on the Forum so here it is.
