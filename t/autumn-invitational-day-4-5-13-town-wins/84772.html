Welcome to the Autumn Invitational!

Hosted by @Chloe and @astand

Rules

Follow the Global Forum Rules as well as the FM/FoL Rules

Post Requirement

This game has a post requirement of 20 substantive, on-topic, game related posts per in game day.

Posts that are explicitly or obviously made solely for the purpose of meeting this requirement do not count towards the minimum, nor do posts containing solely a vote. Failing to meet this requirement a single time will result in a force-replace without warning. Prods will be given at the hosts discretion if they consider it likely that you will not hit this minimum. This requirement may be reduced or waived if an execution is hammered early by majority.

Gameplay

  • Phases are 48/24, with the exception of Day 2, which will be extended by 24 hours. (To accomodate for American Thanksgiving)

  • The factional kill is mandatory and assigned, but may be done alongside other actions. If the member performing the factional kill is not specified, the last member to submit a kill will be assumed to be performing it. If no Mafia submit a kill, a player will be picked at random from the living non-Mafia players, and a random Mafia member will perform the action.

  • The Mafia will have access to a 24/7 chat and may communicate at any time.

  • Both majority and plurality are in effect.
    All votes must be in the form **/vote [name of player]** to be considered valid, and all unvotes must be in the form **/unvote**. This is to enable the smooth processing of votes.

  • You may self-vote. Along with this, no-elimination is a valid vote, and may be hammered to end the day early. In cases where no-elim and another vote are tied at the end of the day, no-elim will have priority in the rand.

  • Tied votes will result in a player being eliminated at random from among the tied players.

  • Votes are locked in LyLo, but not MyLo.

  • Roleblock feedback will only be given to investigative roles - when they would normally receive results. (Voyeur, Motion Detector, Role Cop, Tracker, Watcher, Parity Cop)

  • Action resolution system prioritizes scum PRs in instances of direct conflict.

  • If two players of the same alignment roleblock eachother, the actions cancel, and neither player will be seen as visiting by a Watcher or Tracker.

  • Flavor is not alignment-indicative.

The uninformed majority are the Town and the informed minority are the Mafia
