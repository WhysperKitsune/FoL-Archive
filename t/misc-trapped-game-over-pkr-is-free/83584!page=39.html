Game rules

  • There are 8 locations - Post box, middle, Left chimney, Right chinney, large pile, small pile, left cage and right cage.
  • Players start in the middle of the room and can move to any other and search.
  • You can stack searches until the stack is exhausted or a invitation is found
  • When found, it is announced that invites are found.
  • They must be taken to the post box, stamped there and then posted.
