@PoisonedSquid if you ever get out to Japan hit up Fuji-Q highland

Top Thrill Dragster does 0 to 120mph in 3.8 seconds. Do-Dodonpa does 0 to 110 in 1.56.

Maverick drops at a 95 degree, steeper-than-vertical angle with two inversions at a top speed of 70mph. Takashiba drops at 121 degrees with 7 inversions and a top speed of 62mph.

Magnum drops 205 feet at 60 degrees, reaching a top speed of 72mph and lasting 2 minutes. Fujiyama drops 230 feet at 65 degrees, reaching a top speed of 81 mph and lasting for 3 minutes and 36 seconds.

Gatekeeper drops 164 feet, reaching a top speed of 67mph, going through 6 inversions, and lasting 2 minutes and 40 seconds. Eejanaika drops 249 feet, reaching a top speed of 78mph, going through 14 inversions, and lasting for 2 minutes and 10 seconds.
Oh, and the seats are facing backwards.
And they also spin you head over heels.
In short, this is an easy pick for third favorite, if it doesn’t beat out Maverick for second.

In short: all 4 of their big coasters are unique, fun, and well worth riding. Especially Eejanaika.
