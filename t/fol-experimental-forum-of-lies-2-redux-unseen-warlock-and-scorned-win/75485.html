Gameplay

Days will be 2 IRL days long, or shorter if someone dies
Nights will be 1 IRL day long
Actions are done by messaging me
Unseen/Cult will talk through a special Discord server
Priest will speak with the dead through a separate Discord server
All classes will be used, and will be used as they are found in the Class Cards Thread
When you receive a card you will have 24 hours to reply, confirming you have received it

Executions

To accuse someone say “ /accuse [player] ” or “ /vote [player] ”
Once majority is reached to accuse someone, that player will die. There will be no trials.
Executions end the Day

Special Experimental Changes

  1. Trials have been removed.
  2. Noble has been reworked.
  3. The Kings have all been reworked.
  4. Mastermind has been updated.

All class cards that have had minor interactions changed are not noted here. Any denotations of voting for execution instead refer to accusations due to the removal of trials.

Converts into The Aristocrat or The Apostle

Kings

The Good King

Blue Dragon Special
Pass The Torch (Passive) - If you die, there will be a vote held to crown the next King. If you are executed for treason all players will lose their Royal Blood passive permanently.
Head of State (Passive) - Your vote counts as two. This passive is disabled while there are 4 or less players alive.
Royal Pardon (Day) - Make a player unable to be voted today. This will be publicly announced. - 1 use, shared with Royal Execution
Royal Execution (Day) -Target a player. That player will require 2 less votes to reach majority today. This will be publicly announced. - 1 use, shared with Royal Pardon
Guard! (Night) - Choose a player. They will be immune to death tonight. - 4 uses
Swear Fealty (Night) - Make a player permanently immune to conversion. - 1 use (Starting King Only.)
Your objective is to defeat the Unseen or Cult, and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.

The Evil King

Unseen Special
Pass The Torch (Passive) - If you die, there will be a vote held to crown the next King.

Royal Pardon (Day) - Make a player unable to be voted today. This will be publicly announced. - 1 use, shared with Royal Execution
Royal Execution (Day) -Target a player. That player will require 2 less votes to reach majority today. This will be publicly announced. - 1 use, shared with Royal Pardon
Guard! (Night) - Choose a player. They will be immune to death tonight. - 3 uses
Scheme (Night) - Make the target player night immune and look not suspicious to The Sheriff tonight. - 2 uses (Starting King only)
Your objective is to defeat the Blue Dragon and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.

The Devout King

Cult Special
Pass The Torch (Passive) - If you die, there will be a vote held to crown the next King.
Head of State (Passive) - Your vote counts as two. This passive is disabled while there are 4 or less players alive.
Royal Pardon (Day) - Make a player unable to be voted today. This will be publicly announced. - 1 use, shared with Royal Execution
Royal Execution (Day) -Target a player. That player will require 2 less votes to reach majority today. This will be publicly announced. - 1 use, shared with Royal Pardon
Guard! (Night) - Choose a player. They will be immune to death tonight. - 3 uses
Dark Gods Crown (Night) - Select a dead Cultist and a living player. Depending on the Cultist’s class a different effect will occur. Each corpse can only be used once.
Your objective is to defeat the Blue Dragon and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.

Dark Gods Crown Effects:
Seeker - You learn of the players class type.
Ritualist - You will prevent visits to the player.
Invoker - The player will be occupied.

Devout King - Receive an extra Royal Pardon/Execution.

New Mastermind

The Mastermind

Unseen Special
Can’t Touch This (Passive) - Immune to death at night, occupation and target changing. Occupations and target changes on you will appear successful and You will know if someone tries to occupy you or change your target.
Lurking Presence (Passive) - You will appear to be a Blue Dragon Investigative class to investigations for the first 3 nights. When you die, the Starting Assassin will take your place if they are still alive.
Dirty Work (Day) If there are no more Unseen members left, you can become The Assassin at the beginning of the night. - Infinite Uses
Convert (Night) - Change target player into the Unseen version of their class. If you have failed to convert a target twice already and your next conversion would fail it will instead target a random convertible player. - Infinite uses, 1 night cooldown
Foresight (Night) - Determine a player’s converted class type, or if they cannot be converted. - 4 uses
Your objective is to defeat the Blue Dragon and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.
