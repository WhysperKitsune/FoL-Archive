However, if the Prince’s second exe is on BD, then the Prince will lose the remaining uses anyway. In a way, Prince Exes are simply lynches but with more risks. That’s why scumreading is as important for Prince jails as for public lynches. Do not treat Prince jails as an easy hack and slash role as that’s not what it’s meant for. Meteoro is Dead No Matter What - Yes, Meteoro is the highest priority to get lynched and executed.
