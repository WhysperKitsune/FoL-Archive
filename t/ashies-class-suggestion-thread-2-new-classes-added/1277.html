Class/Lore/Fanfic
Ashie May 25, 2017, 8:36pm #1

MORTICIAN (NEUTRAL KILLER):

Passive Ability 1:
Dark Aura: If you are killed at night, the player who used said ability on you will become a Mortician.

Passive Ability 2:
Magical Combo: You may talk with other Morticians at night freely, however only one Mortician may use an ability at a time. There may be a maximum of 3 Morticians (for balance).

Day Ability 1:
Curse: Cast a magical spell on a non-Mortician player, they will become a Mortician in two nights if they are not healed by a Physician or Alchemist. They will not know they are cursed until they have one night left on their count. See below for immune classes. Uses: 2

Night Ability 1:
Spell of Despair: Attempt to kill target player. Uses: Infinite

Night Ability 2:
Bribe: Attempt to convert target player into a Mortician. Uses: 1

Immune Classes:
These classes are immune to being a Mortician:
Prince
King
Unseen
Cult
Possessor
Reaper
Paladin

GUARDIAN (BLUE DRAGON SUPPORT):

Passive Ability 1:
Fearless: Immune to being occupied at night.

Day Ability 1:
Lock Eyes: Give another player a threatening stare, disabling their Night Ability for the next night. Uses: 2

Night Ability 1:
Redirect: Stand guard at your door, anyone who visits you tonight will instead visit your target player. Uses: 2

Night Ability 2:
Guard: Stand guard at another player’s house, if someone attempts to attack, collect the soul of, possess, or occupy your target your presence will scare them off. Uses: Infinite

Unseen Equivalent: The Watchdog
Cult Equivalent: The Ritualist

Changelog:
4/22/17: Guardian and Watchdog are born!

THE WATCHDOG (CONVERTED GUARDIAN, UNSEEN SUPPORT)

Passive Ability 1:
Fearless: Cannot be occupied at night.

Passive Ability 2:
Eagle Eyesight: On even nights, you will know who visits the Assassin at night. On odd nights, you will know who visits the Mastermind at night.

Day Ability 1:
Threaten: Verbally threaten a player, they will use their Night Ability on a player of your choice. Uses: 2

Night Ability 1:
Guard Dog: Summon a guard dog to prevent the death of the Assassin tonight. If anyone visits the Assassin, the guard dog will scare them away. Uses: 1
