Pyromancer

Neutral Killer

Passive 1: Psychopathic - Immunity to Death, Occupy, Redirect, Poison and Bleed.
Passive 2: Recharge - You gain a use of killing spree at the start of every night.

Day Ability 1: Out of sight - Your Ability’s will not count as visiting and will Occupy the targets tonight. (3 uses)
Day Ability 2: Scar - Disable Target players day Ability’s for the day (2 uses)

Night Ability: Blaze - Kill target players. You may target as meany players as you have uses. Costs a use for each successful kill. (0 uses)

goal: eliminate the main factions
