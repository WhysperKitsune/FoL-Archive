Here’s a few rules:
1 : use common sense ! This means, no metagaming (using OOC info in character), etc.
2 : don’t interact with others character whithout their permission while they are offline. (the king IS a character)
3 :If everyone involved in a fight agrees, you can resolve combat only using RP actions. Otherwise, you should use rolls, applying the roll modifier in your character sheet.

It’s another morning at the castle, and the door open to the crowd… people swarm in the main hall… many persons hope to speak to the king. Others are here to entertain themselves, as shown by a little circle of people around a bard. In the middle of the main hall is a big statue of the king magnus lightwood. it describes every single one of the king’s achievement: they include defending the kingdom against countless invaders, and exterminating the threats that were vampires. It also looks like guards are everywhere, guarding the stairs to the second floor, where royalty lives. The door isn’t the only access to the castle, and in the undergrounds of the castle, a number of scum plan their next plot…
