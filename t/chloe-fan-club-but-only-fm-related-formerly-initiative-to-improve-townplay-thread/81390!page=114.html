I don’t think anyone is specifically in the wrong or in the right, I came in to this conversation thinking I specifically was right and that arete was just being a nerd, but after hearing their side I realized that they had been dealing with the exact same thing I was, just that, because it was never communicated, I didn’t realize they were unable to provide those reads, or how they felt when we didn’t answer their questions.

This lead to not only a big argument betweeen us 2 in the game where both people think they are right and the other uncooperative, but also post-game. This hadn’t really been fleshed out so I just… never knew.

This is why it is important to communicate things with people. A lot of things are often left unsaid that make people misunderstand circumstances.

If there is anything effecting the way you are playing or making cases, it’s important to say that so people don’t think you are purposefully doing negative things.

If you are going to post lower amounts, tell the game that.
Otherwise, people might think you are flaking or dodging questions becuase you are scum.

If you have not read a chunk of the game, say that before you make your readslist or your read on a person.
Otherwise people are going to think that you purposefully left out things from that part of the game from your read

If you are making a metaread on a person, but haven’t read their past 3 or so games, note that you haven’t read those games before making your meta case
Otherwise, people will think you are purposefully ignoring it.

If you don’t feel comfortable reading someone, say that.
Otherwise, people will think you are dodging reading someone.

this is overall a small thing and probably doesn’t effect too much, but it is just important to communicate with your fellow townies about things in the game.

Don’t leave out small important details about your mindset or how you did something, because they can turn in to bigger misunderstandings and eventually incorrect reads.

I would like to apoligize to arete for not making it clear that I didn’t want to defend my slot, as it lead to a lot of unintended frustration.

just

communicate, people, it’s a team game and you should make sure that your team knows important things.
