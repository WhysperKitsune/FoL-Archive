I am proud to present…

The Mad Artist (Neutral)

Passive: Ability details Immune to being reaped as lack of a sane soul.

Night Ability 1: Mosaic Betrayal. Murder someone and place a drawing on their corpse. Using rainbow colors, gain 3 artistic fame points.
Ability details
Optional notes 2 uses, can be role blocked.

Night Ability 2: Sketching Bones. Practice your artistic talent on an already dead corpse. Gain 1 artistic fame points it successful.
Ability details
Optional notes Unlimited uses but tricky to use because you have to target someone who dies the same Night. Useful if you know the prince is going to kill someone.

Day Ability 1: “Canvas” the Area. See if anyone is to be killed tonight by day or last-night abilities.
Ability details
Optional notes ^

Day Ability 2: Daytime Doodles. Practice your artistic skills by drawing some simple doodles.
Ability details
Optional notes increases the amount of skill points obtained by your night time abilities the next night if successful.

Goal: Murder people at night and post art on their corpses. Die in the public eyes as a famous artist. You must have at least 5 artistic fame points at the time of your death to be recognized as a famous artist and win.

Optional Lore: As a former Cult member, and banished Unseen member, the Mad Artist has seen it all. This was unbearable for his mind, and it drove him insane. He now murders people to publish his art, which is all he truly remembers from his old life. His passion for art.
