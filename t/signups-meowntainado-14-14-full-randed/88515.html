Rules

Follow the Global Forum Rules as well as the Forum Game Rules

All players must post a minimum of 10 substantive and game-related posts per 48h Day phase. This requirement may be waived in the instance the Day ends due to majority or you die mid-Day.

What doesn't count?
  • Posting for the sole purpose of meeting the post requirement.
  • Posting about the fact that you are making posts, without adding any new content.
  • Posting about non-game-related topics (ex. IRL, news, homework, etc), or posting fluff such as images or gifs.
  • Posting that you are reading, will read, will provide content in the future, etc.
  • Posts containing non-substantive edits to a prior post (e.g. “I think Chloe is mafia because she is greenchecked” → “I meant redchecked” - this only counts as one post).
  • Posts containing solely a vote.

Alts are allowed. PM Chloe if you would like to play this game on an alt.

The hosts may or may not utilize WOTM to prohibit players from joining.

THIS GAME HAS A POST CAP. Players can only make up to 200 posts per in-game day. This is lifted 2 hours before EoD. (Shooting the gun does not count towards the cap)

EXCESSIVE AtE IS NOT ALLOWED. Appealing to emotion can result in a toxic environment that ruins enjoyment. We want this game to be somewhat competitive and taken seriously. As a result, we will be monitoring the thread for heavy AtE. If you aren’t sure if what you’re posting breaks this rule, feel free to run it by us. If you find yourself unable to play the game without it affecting you emotionally, please let us know so we can help you. Feel free to message either of me/sulit at any time if you need to vent, or just want to talk.

The Setup

Role List

11x Vanilla Town
3x Mafia Goon

Sample Role PMs

Vanilla Town

You are Vanilla Town. Your only weapons are your voice and your vote.

Defeat the mafia.

Mafia Goon

You are a Mafia Goon. You share a 24/7 private chat with your teammates. Each Night, the Mafia must kill a member of the Town.

Reach parity with the town.

Special Mechanics

On Night 2, the Mafia must select a member of the Town. This player will be given a gun publicly, and must shoot it on Day 3 by typing **/shoot [player]** in the thread. If they shoot a member of the Mafia, the Mafia member dies. If they shoot a member of the Town, the gun holder will die.

If the Mafia fail to select a target by the final hour of the Night, it will be given to a random living villager.
The gun cannot be given to the target of the factional kill.
If the gun holder fails to shoot by the final hour of the Day, they will die.

Voting

  • Both majority and plurality are in effect.

  • You may not self-vote. Asking other players to vote and/or hammer you breaks this rule and goes against the spirit of the game.

  • No-elimination is a valid vote, and may be hammered to end the Day early. In cases where no-elim and another vote are tied at the end of the Day, no-elim will have priority in the rand.

  • Tied votes will result in a player being eliminated at random from among the tied players.

  • MyLo (mis-eliminate and lose) and LyLo (eliminate or lose) will be announced, and votes will be locked. This means that your first vote is final and cannot be changed.

  • All votes must be in the form of **/vote [player]** and all unvotes must be in the form of **/unvote**. Please ping the hosts (@Chloe and @sulit) and put your votes on a separate line in order to make it easier for us to process them.

  • On Day 3, majority is disabled until the gun is shot, after which votes will reset.

Gameplay

  • Phases will begin and end at 14:00 PDT

  • Days are 48 hours, and Nights are 24 hours.

  • In the instance the Day ends early due to majority, the Night phase will be extended to end at a consistent time.

  • The Mafia factional kill is mandatory. If no factional kill is submitted by the final hour of the Night, the kill will be determined randomly from among living members of the town.

  • Mafia will communicate through a private Discord server.

  • Flavor is NAI
