[Class suggestion] The double agent / The traitor

Class/Lore/Fanfic
RopeStringFace October 23, 2017, 1:21am #1

The Double agent
Blue dragon investigative
Passive: Infiltration
If the mastermind or cult leader attempts to convert you, you will become the traitor.
Passive: Reliable sources
You know if the cult or unseen are present at the beginning of the game
Day ability: In plain sight
Choose what class you appear to be to the evil classes if you are converted tonight*
Night ability: Informants
(2 use) learn how much of every faction is currently present in the game, ignoring the king.

The Traitor
Blue dragon Investigative
Passive: Intelligence
Hear the chat of the unseen/cult at night, with their classes as names instead of names. You do not meet with them the first night of your membership, as though you were imprisoned by the prince. After one night of membership, you learn the identities of the cult/unseen.
Passive: Convincing
You appear to be the class you choose with “In plain sight” as the double agent. You are so convincing that you appear suspicious to investigators.

*For example, if you choose physician and are converted into the unseen, you will appear to be the herbalist to the unseen. If you were converted to the cult, you will appear to be the ritualist.

Just an outline, this is the basic idea
