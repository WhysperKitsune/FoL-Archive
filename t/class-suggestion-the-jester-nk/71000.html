Class/Lore/Fanfic
Anglasut July 21, 2018, 9:09pm #1

The Jester.

Neutral Killer

Abilities:

Feline reflexes (Passive) - Immunity to occupation, redirection, bleeding, poison and death at night.
Deceptive steps (Passive) - Appears as a Support class for the first 2 nights. Your true visits are hidden.

Puppet play (Day) - Silence a player and impersonate him for the rest of the day. You can vote as him, but won’t be able to speak/vote as yourself for the rest of the day. Not usable until day 3. 3 uses. (Shared with Dramatic ending)
Deception and betrayal (Day) - Chose a player, make it look like you visit him tonight. Infinite uses

Cheerful stab (Night) - Kill the targeted player. Infinite uses-
Dramatic ending (Night) - Attack a player thrice, killing him. Not usable until night 3. 3 uses. (Shared with Puppet play) (Always kills the target unless something prevents the visit)

The Fool’s brother, any feedback is appreciated.

Edit 1:

Changed Deceptive steps to only work for 2 nights, pretty meaningless passive that will just save you really early in the

I personally don’t think that a NK with hidden visits is that OP, since all Nks are kind of hard to catch as Obs early in the game.
