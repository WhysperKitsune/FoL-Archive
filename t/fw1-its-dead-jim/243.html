Roles and what they do
Werewolves - Eat a player every night - win if they have equal number to Village
Villagers - No Abilities - win if they kill all the Werewolves
Seer - Check a player every night to see if they are a werewolf - wins with villagers
Hunter - Kill 1 player immediately if executed - wins with villagers
Tanner - If executed, the next day does not happen - wins if executed

How the game will work
Each day phase will be 72 hours (3 days) long, each night will be 24 hours
Everyone will be sent a role via Private Message and be given 24 hours to reply, confirming the receipt of the role, or they will be replaced
Werewolves will be able to talk to each other at night

Executions
Vote a player by saying “/vote [name]”
Once a player reaches 51% majority votes, they die, the day ends and the night begins
If they day runs out of time, the player with the most votes dies, if there is a tie, the player who reached that number of votes first will die
