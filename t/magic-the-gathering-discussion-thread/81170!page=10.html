My reveiw of the Ultimatums, by the way:

Inspired Ultimatum - It’s cool, it’s 5 cards, it’s removal, and it has lifegain tacked on to it. Nowhere near as good as a value card as Cruel Ultimatum, though.
Emergent Ultimatum - Welcome to Combo City: Population, Sultai Midrange. Super strong card, and its’ ability is not really that hard to circumvent.
Eerie Ultimatum - Basically an autoinclude in every Abzan singleton deck from now until the end of time. Expensive, but powerful.
Genesis Ultimatum - “hey we’ve got a temur ultimatum to print what do we do boss” - “just print a card that makes it worth it to just put in a pile of good temur cards into your deck” - “thanks maro”
Ruinous Ultimatum - Okay, I’m going to be serious on this one again. It has an incredibly strong effect and it’s absolutely worth including it if you can. I just don’t think there’s any Mardu decks it’s worth including this into.
