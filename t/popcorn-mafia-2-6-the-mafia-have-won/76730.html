Popcorn Mafia - Gameplay

  • Right before the game begins, after alignments are rolled, the Mafia must choose one player to start as Gunholder.
  • Everyone is always aware of who is currently holding the gun.
  • The person who holds the gun can at any time shoot another player.
  • If the player they shoot is mafia, that mafia goon will die.
  • If the player they shoot at is town, the shooter will die and the town they shot at will become the new Gunholder.
  • The town wins when all mafia are dead. The mafia win when the number of mafia is greater than the remaining town.
  • The game is nightless and there are no lynches. After receiving the gun, you have 10 minutes to shoot another player or you will die and a random town player will get the gun.
  • The Gunholder shoots by typing /Shoot [Player Name] in thread.
  • Mafia will have a day chat where they can use all the time.
  • Mafia cannot hold the Gun.
