I do not support this. Neutrals and scum tend to have night immunity, because they have no big team supporting them. They also usually have to survive until the end to win. That is why MM, the core piece of the unseen, whom without they’d usually lose, has night immunity. All NK’s, scorned, fool, alchemist, inquisitor have at least limited night immunity too. It is necessary for their win condition. Having a surviving prince is not a win condition for BD. Outing as Prince is the way to go if you want to have the guaranteed survival. BD has protective roles for that very reason. The prince also has the ultimate counterplay to any kills to him, simply jail the assassin/NK/CL. He does not need to be THIS unstoppable.
