The Bishop - Unique Neutral Investigative

Passive 1: Holy Meetings
You may talk to everyone that you have invite to your Church. You are immune to death if you have nobody in your Church.

Passive 2: Sinful
If you kill 3 people with Purge you will commit suicide the following night.

Day Ability 1: Invitation
Invite someone to your church. If they are Holy, they can accept or decline. If they are not Holy, they will decline. (infinite) (1 day cooldown)

Night Ability 1: Purge
Discover if someone is Holy. If they are not Holy, you will kill them. If they are Holy you will be notified. (infinite uses)

Night Ability 2: Prophecy
Select one person that is dead. They talk to you and your people in the church for the night. (1 use)

Holy Classes

All counterparts are holy.
The Paladin
The Sorcerer
The Inquisitor
The Cult
The Mastermind
The Reaper
The Physician
The Alchemist
The Psychic
The Court Wizard
The Princess
The Assassin
The Possessor
The Knight

Win Condition: Successfully Invite 3 people to your Church.
