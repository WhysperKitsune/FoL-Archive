Aquillion February 21, 2018, 5:52am #12
DustoffKay:

Guarding your last confirmed BD just to vote the final evil player to find out that your confirmed BD was converted through your guard seems really broken.

What’s broken about it? I honestly do not see any problem with it at all. It makes it harder to trust someone 100%, which is important for gameplay, and prevents BD from having “easy” strategies where they can perfectly protect everyone important.

I’m just not seeing any real benefit to this suggestion. King doesn’t need a buff. BD doesn’t need a buff. Conversions don’t need a nerf. And I don’t see how this would improve gameplay.

It seems like you’re expressing a vague feeling of “I don’t like how this feels”, but I have to say that my reading is just that you’re annoyed that it happened to you in-game - this suggestion feels like a “plz nerf scissors. signed, paper” sort of thing. Conversion being the weakness in death-based protection seems to me to be a valuable design choice that forces people to plan for more potential scenarios.
