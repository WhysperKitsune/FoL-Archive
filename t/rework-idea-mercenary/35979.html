The Mercenary

Neutral Support
Looking For Work (Passive) - You have three days to find a contract, or you will commit suicide. If that contract dies, you will become The Rogue.
Contact Contract (Passive) - At night, you can talk with your contract, if you have one.
Offer Contract (Day) - Offer a contract to a player, if you do not already have one. - Infinite uses
Stand Guard (Night) - Protect your target and prevent others from visiting and make yourself immune to death for the night. - 4 uses
Rebound (Night) - Kill a player that accused your target of treason during the previous day. - 1 use
Contracted player survives.

The Sellsword

Unseen Support
Keeping in Contract (Passive) - At night, you can talk with the contract you had as a Mercenary, if you had one.
Stonewall (Night) - Protect an Unseen player and prevent other visitors from interacting with them. - 3 uses
Revenge (Night) - Kill target player that accused an Unseen member of treason the previous day. - 1 use
Defeat the Blue Dragon and any neutrals that seek to harm you.

The Rogue

Neutral Killer
Hardened (Passive) - Immune to death at night.
Contract Killing (Day) - Offer to kill for a player. They can choose a player for you to kill, or can deny the contract. - Infinite uses, can be used on the same day if denied
True Strike (Day) - Make your attack tonight pierce death immunity. - Infinite uses
Exterminate (Night) - Attack a player that you have been contracted to kill. - Infinite uses
Kill three contract targets. If you became a Rogue after Day 6, kill two contract targets, or if you became a Rogue after Day 9, kill a single contract target.
