The Realityseeker - Blue Dragon Social

Warp Perception (Passive) - Both you and your target is immune to occupation, Redirection, Conversion and death in a Alternate Reality night.

Alternate Reality (Day Ability) - Create a reality where you and your target can talk. You can not use this ability on The King, Unseen or cult members, Prince, A Jailed target or the mystic. Target can still use their ability. (2 uses)

Bend Reality (Day Ability) - Disable all day abilities and all current uses in effect. The only exception to this is Nightshade and Rupture. All whispers will be then directed to you and the court will not know it. (3 uses)

The Realmfinder - Unseen Social

Trance (Day Ability) - Make a player only hearable to the unseen. Whispers will be shared to all of you and they will still think they are talking to the court. Telepathy will still go out to everyone. Day Abilities will target you. Except scout. The king can not be targeted (Unlimited uses)

Shatter Reality (Day Ability) - It’s Bend Reality but also disabling investigative abilities.

The Cult Converted Class Is The Apostle.
This Class Is Non-unique, Not conversion immune. Not death immune, Not Occupation immune.
