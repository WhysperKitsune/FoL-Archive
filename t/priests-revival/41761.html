I think the Priest has been gone long enough and deserves to be brought back about now. The game has room for her.

But with the Priest, I hope to bring her back, so please leave your feedback. We bring back the glory days of the Priest!

This is currently a blank slate. Let’s all contribute to the Priest.

The Priest

Unique Blue Dragon Support

Passives

Sabbatical:
You cannot be converted starting from Day 7.

Call the Dead:
Communicate with the dead at night. (TAKE BACK FROM THE PSYCHIC WHAT IS RIGHTFULLY HERS)

Abilities

Consecration: (D) (3)
Choose any player. You or they cannot be targeted by day abilities today.

(Does not work on Day abilities that work at night i.e. Prince’s Imprison, Sheriff’s Scout, or other ones where you are not the direct target of an ability i.e. Private Matter, Decide Fate, Royal Finger)

Bind Soul: (D) (1)
Choose another player, making them immune to death once.

Revive: (N) (1)
Bring back a non-Unique Blue Dragon player from the dead.

Pray: (N) (oo)
Choose a player. If anyone targets only you with a supportive ability tonight, you will also have it so it affects your target as well.

Single-Target Support Abilities:

  • Guards
  • Heal
  • Sacrifice (Knight will not lose Armored nor die if your target is attacked, but will lose it or die if you are attacked)
  • Barrier
  • Stand Guard
  • Crimson Potion

The Priest is looking for a convert.
