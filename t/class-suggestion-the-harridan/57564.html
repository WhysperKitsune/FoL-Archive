The Harridan

Neutral Support
A young magician, who is helping inviduals like her with their destiny.
Natural Instinct (Passive) - You know an idenity one of the neutrals.
Balance Keeper (Passive) - You’re immune to death once from each source (BD/Neutrals/Unseen/Cult).
Sudden Adjustment (Day) - Force pardon the player on trial if he’s Neutral. He can’t accused of treason again that day. - 1 uses
Rune Of Cloak (Day) - Hide target’s visit and make them appear as Blue Dragon tonight,if he’s Neutral. - 2 uses
Rune Of Safety (Night) - Make a barrage that preventing anyone from visiting your target. - 4 uses
Rune Of Power (Night) - Give a pernament buff for player. It will fails, if the target is not Neutral. - 1 uses(Not usable Until Night 3)
Help more than half of neutrals acomplish their objective.

The Buff’s that a Neutral class can get:

  • Fool: Made them suspicous to investigative classes, hide their occupy/redirtection immunity.
  • Scorned: Frame will make a target suspicious for 2 nights to investigative classes and night immunity for 1 night.
  • Alchemist: He can heal reaped players who were attacked by Reaper same night, occupy/redirection immunity.
  • Mercenary: Knowlegde about contact’s class and alerting if they got converted. Night immunity once.
  • Inquisitor: Find Magic always determine if the target is magic user can’t be affected by Magic Dust/Magic Cloak and Frame.
  • Sorcerer: Appear as Blue Dragon to investigative classes, if she wouldn’t kill her target she will know the reason (Example:Target was protected by Knight or was member of the Unseen/Cult).
  • Reaper: Hide his visits, occupy/redirection immunity.
  • Possessor: A possesed player will be same class but instead he’s goal will turn into “Help The Possessor acomplisgh their objective” and also gave a separate night chat. They will die next night, if the Possessor would be killed.
  • Neutral King: Benefits of Starting King.<---- Looking for replacement.

Note:
She can spawn only with at least 2 other Neutrals in game.
If the Neutral with “Rune Of Power” will become King he will got a benefit’s of Starting King(Except Psycho King).

Any feedback/suggestions to upgrade/change this class is always apprieciate.

Have a nice day!
