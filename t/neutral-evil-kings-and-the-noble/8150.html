Neutral/Evil Kings and the Noble

livejamie October 31, 2017, 4:01pm #1

Had a game as Mastermind yesterday where we were way behind but I had a hunch the King wasn’t good. I went all in and whispered him who I was and what my plan was.

The problem was a Noble then immediately reported everything I said to the table.

This felt pretty bad and made me realize that an Evil or Neutral king is completely destroyed if there’s a Noble listening in every day on what they’re doing. Even if they find evil allies they’re crippled by the Noble and there’s no counter-play.

My backup plan was asking my other evil-doers to whisper other people to try to distract the Noble but by that point, it was too late.

Perhaps it would be balanced if the evil or neutral king had an ability to whisper in secret temporarily?
