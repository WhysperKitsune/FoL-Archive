Cult Leader receives additional incorrect message from empowered Eradicate

Bug Reports
Cryotron9000 October 23, 2017, 5:51pm #1

What happened?

This all occurred on the same night:
-Player A (me) was the Cult Leader
-Player B was a Ritualist who used Strength of Corax
-Player A Eradicated Player C
-Player D (I believe a Physician) visited Player C
-Player A receives the same message that Player D received. I don’t recall exactly what that message said (sorry!) – it was whatever message pops up when you visit a player affected by empowered Eradicate – something to the effect of “You were attacked while visiting…”

What was SUPPOSED to happen?

Player A should not have received the message that they were attacked, because they were not actually attacked.

Steps to reproduce:

-Have a Ritualist use Strength of Corax on a target
-Have the Cult Leader Eradicate that target
-Have someone else visit that target the same night
-Compare the messages that the Cult Leader and the visitor receive.
