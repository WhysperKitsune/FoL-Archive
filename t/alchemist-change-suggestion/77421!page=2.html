Technically true but practically false. But not for the reason you would think.

Prince is actually somewhat close to not being a viable prince claim, since losing the Prince is so problematic that outing is too dangerous if it can possibly be avoided.

Now the real reason why Knight needs to be a viable Knight claim is because short of being so powerful when hidden as to singlehandedly win the game it’s just better to claim your real class when pressured as BD. Why can’t we make a BD claim unviable for themselves like we can for neuts?

Not because making a BD who’s best option is to fake claim is problematic, there are plenty of examples of that such as the Hunter in One night ultimate werewolf and the Seer in resistance. But instead because in throne of lies, making it so that a Knight claim is unviable for knight would mean that the Knight itself is negative utility, which is a balance and game health issue.

For neutrals, helping the BD isn’t part of their wincon so being anti-BD isn’t an issue like it is for the BD. Same obviously applies to scum.
