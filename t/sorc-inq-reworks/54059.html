Sorc/Inq Reworks

Feedback/Suggestions
NightX March 23, 2018, 11:41pm #1

Current Inq/Sorc dynamic isn’t great.

  1. It takes no effort as Inquisitor. The Sorc has no great claim, so will often get lynched without intervention.
  2. Swinginess, if the Inq yolos with Ruthless efficiency they have a 1/14 of simply winning and removing the NK.
  3. A sorc game is a huge advantage for Unseen/Cult, since the NK isn’t after them.

So I thought I’d draft a bunch of suggestions.

Nuclear's Rework (Tweaked)

Sorcerer

Neutral Killer

Passive: Mage Armor: You are immune to death, occupation, poison, and bleeding. If you would die visiting someone, you instead do nothing. (Bear, Defend, etc.)

Passive: Rivalry: When the Inquisitor is dead, you lose your cool down on Magic Missile.

Day Ability: Power Shot: Ignore the cool down for Magic Missile tonight, and make it bypass immunity and healing tonight. 2 uses.

Day Ability: Magic Cloak: Make yourself appear to be a class of your choice tonight. 3 uses.

Night Ability: Magic Missile: Kill someone. 1 day cool down.

Night Ability: Circle of Death: Kill all visitors to your target. 2 uses.

Defeat the Blue Dragon, Cult or Unseen, and the Inquisitor

Inquisitor

Neutral Killer

Passive: Nobody expects the Spanish Inquisition!: You are immune to death, poison, and bleeding. Your visits don’t count as visits.

Passive: Rivalry: When the Sorcerer is dead, you lose your cool down on Ruthless Effiency.

Day Ability: Holy Hand Grenade: Your will attack your target tonight. Bypasses immunity. 2 use.

Night Ability: Ruthless Efficiency: Kill someone. 1 day cool down, does not trigger if you kill a magic user.

Night Ability: Find Magic: Detect if someone is a magic user.

Defeat the Blue Dragon, Cult or Unseen, and the Sorcerer

Lower Scale

Sorcerer

Neutral Offensive

Passive: Mage Armor: You are immune to death, poison, and bleeding.

Day Ability: Magic Dust: Make everyone appear to be a magic user. 3 uses.

Night Ability: Curse: Occupy your target, and prevent healing and guarding on them. Unlimited uses.

Night Ability: Circle of Death: Kill all visitors to your target. 1 use.

Live to see the Blue Dragon lose

Inquisitor

Neutral Investigative

Passive: Nobody expects the Spanish Inquisition!: You are immune to death once.

Passive: Failure: If the Sorcerer is killed by someone else, or the Sorcerer is alive and you have no charges of Ruthless Efficiency, you will commit suicide.

Night Ability: Find Magic: Detect if your target is a magic user. Unlimited uses.

Night Ability: Ruthless Efficiency: Kill your target. Bypasses Sorcerer’s immunity. 2 uses.

Kill the Sorcerer with Ruthless Efficiency.

Head to Head

Sorcerer

Neutral Investigative

Passive: Mage Armor: You are immune to death.

Passive: Rivalry: You commit suicide if the Inquisitor is killed by any means besides Magic Missile.

Day Ability: Magic Dust: Make everyone appear to be Sorcerer to the Inquisitor tonight. 3 uses.

Night Ability: Perception: Detect if a player is the Inquisitor. Unlimited uses.

Night Ability: Magic Missile: Kill a player. Bypasses the Inquisitor’s immunity. 2 uses.

Kill the Inquisitor with Magic Missile

Inquisitor

Neutral Investigative

Passive: Experienced: You are immune to death.

Passive: Rivalry: You commit suicide if the Sorcerer is killed by any means besides Ruthless Effiency.

Day Ability: Nobody Expects the Spanish Inquisition!: Make everyone appear to be Inquisitor to the Sorcerer tonight. 3 uses.

Night Ability: Find Magic: Detect if a player is the Sorcerer. Unlimited uses.

Night Ability: Ruthless Effiency: Kill a player. Bypasses the Sorcerer’s immunity. 2 uses.

Kill the Sorcerer with Ruthless Effiency
