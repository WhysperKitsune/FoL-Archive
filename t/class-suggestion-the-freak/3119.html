The Freak - Neutral Offensive

Passive: Decaying Body - Your body is frail and weak but with dark magic, your body will be reformed. You are immune to death at night.

Passive: Rotten Flesh - You cannot step up to be King as the Royals at Court will notice your rotting flesh.

Night Ability 1: Bad Blood - Select two targets. If one of them has Royal Blood, you will swap it with the other. (3 uses - no self target)

Night Ability 2: Graverob - Select a dead target that has Royal Blood and a living target. You will transfer their Royal Blood to the living target. (Infinite uses - can’t use on the same dead target twice)

Day Ability: Demise - If you are attacked tonight, you will bring them down with you. (2 uses)

Goal: See the Blue Dragon’s demise.
