Hi peeps,

with the upcoming tournament i got reminded that a spectator mode would be pretty awesome.

But of course restrictions would be needed:

  • Spectators can see everyone’s class & faction in the player list as well as their logs.

  • Spectators can only present during a custom lobby, not in public games.

  • Limited to 2 Spectators a game, Spectators can’t join randomly, they have to be invited to be a spectator by the Lobby-Host.

This would make streaming & commentating as well as watching a tournament alot more fun in the future.
