[Class Suggestion] Master Vampire

Class/Lore/Fanfic
Q3Visor January 12, 2018, 10:49am #1

Neutral Killer

Win condition - Get voted up as new King. Make sure BD does not win the game.

Passive 1 - Noble - He will be first in line among Prince and Princess etc. to become the new King
Passive 2 - True Vision - Can see if the current King is Neutral, Evil or Good.

Night ability 1 - Drink Blood - Will visit a player to drink their blood. The Master Vampire will gain one charge of Transform. The visited player will be occupied for the night by this, and will be marked as “Vampire Servant” for the Master Vampire. Vampire Servant is a hidden passive that only the Master Vampire can see, so people that get bitten will not know they have it. Does not work on players guarded by a Knight or Cult/Unseen Members, or players that already have been bitten. (Same as Sorceress Kill)

Night ability 2 - Transform - Useable after the Vampire has 3 charges from Drink Blood. Transform into a giant Bat to kill the current King. Bypasses any protections on the king like Knight Guarding or Phys Healing. The King dies 100% from this.

When voted for new King, the Vampire will always be an Evil King.

Vampire Servant Passive
People will not know when they are vampire servant. The Vampire Servant will automatically vote for the Master Vampire when electing a new King. They will also be unable to talk for the day.
