Parallel Universes

Probably the Worst SFoL Idea that someone has had the displeasure to read

The Basics

Players will get two classes (of the same wincon)
Each day, the game will swap universes. Giving them their other class and possibly a different gamemode

King, Prince, Mastermind, Assassin, Cult Leader, Spiritualist/Revenant and Non-Killing Neutrals will stay the exact same on swap.

Neutral Killers turn into a different Neutral Killer

If a player was converted, they will change into their other class’s convert on universe swap

(yes Cultists have to make 2 cult alt sets)

Players will only flip their first class on death which is why a new investigative will come into play
The Archaeologist which converts into the Gravedigger

This mode is also able to mix gamemodes because why not. (It will only work on gamemodes that I include in this list with some minor changes)
