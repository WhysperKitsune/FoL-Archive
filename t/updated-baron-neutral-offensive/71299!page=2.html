Baron

Neutral Investigative
Passive 1: Royal Blood
Passive 2: Hired guards - immune to death once

Day Ability 1: Political Pressure - Force one player to vote another
Day Ability 2: Eavesdrop - Hear whispers to and from target player until you chose another target. You may switch targets as much as you want throughout the day. You may use this Ability at the same time as political pressure. (Unlimited)

Night Ability 1: Intersept - Target player gets incorrect results from their ability’s. You will get the unaltered results. (Unlimited)
Night Ability 2: Royal Decree - if target player is royal remove the passive. Otherwise give it to them. You will know which one you do. (3 uses)
