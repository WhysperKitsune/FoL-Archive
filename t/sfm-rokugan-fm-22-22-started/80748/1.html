Special Game Mechanics

  • There are 9 clans, based on flavor. Each one of them has 2 to 6 families. Alongside that, there are 4 paths: Courtier, Shugenja, Bushi and Monk.
  • Your clan is the general grouping of your family. Each clan is more likely to have families of with a specific set of skills. For an example, if you roll a Phoenix, you have more chances to roll something that support your allies.
  • Your family is your class or role, however you like to call it. It is your set of skills. Of course, Kuni Yoriko and Kuni Tohsaka are two different characters in flavor. But as both are from the Kuni family, they both have the same mechanicals.
  • Be careful with japanese naming conventions! Your family is, in flavor, the surname (first name) of your flavor. Kitsu Shiro is from the Kitsu family.
  • Your path is your class type, defined by flavor. Courtiers dwell in the court, shugenjas are holy spellcasters, bushi are steel-bound warriors and monks are reclusive monastics.
  • Flavor is faction, path, clan and family-indicative. You can ask me what would be the faction, clan, family and path of a certain character.
  • Your clan and your family will decide your flavor. Your flavor will decide your path. (if you are of the Crab Clan and Kuni Family and I roll a shugenja flavor, you will be a shugenja).
  • Each family lists a path. It does not mean that all characters from a family will be from said path, it is just high likely they will be (out of the 50 members of the Daigotsu family, 45 of them are Bushi).
  • For clarifiance, all ninjas and berserkers in the game are considered to be Bushi here. All maho-tsukai are Shugenja. All engineers and alike are Courtier if they aren‚Äôt Bushi. Related to flavor.
  • If I ever lack flavor for the scum (Shadowlands) members, I‚Äôll take flavor from a normal town (Empire) character and add ‚ÄúCorrupted‚Äù to their name.
  • I‚Äôll only use people that were at some point alive at the 12nd century in the timeline for flavor. Dead people can‚Äôt mafia.
  • One person spawns as Daigotsu. Daigotsu is known to be the leader of the Shadowlands faction.
