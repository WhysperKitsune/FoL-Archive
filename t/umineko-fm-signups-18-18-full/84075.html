Rules:

  • Follow all Global Rules and Official Forum Mafia and Forum of Lies Rules
  • Make at least 20 game-related posts each day (unless day ends early). If you fail to meet this requirement, you might get replaced.
  • If you nightpost 5 minutes or later after day ended, I might immediately modkill you.
  • Alt accounts are allowed (however, tell me who you are privately when you sign up). Don’t forget to contact DatBird as well if you plan to play on an alt.

General information:

  • Closed setup.
  • Days are 48 hours, nights are 24 hours.
  • Executions are majority + plurality.
  • The game may contain bastard elements. However, OP will never lie, your rolecard will never lie, and no flips will ever be false.
  • Town is uninformed majority and Mafia is informed minority.
  • Town win condition is: Eliminate Mafia and all other possible threats to Town.
  • Mafia win condition is: Achieve parity with Town and eliminate all other possible threats to Mafia.
  • Other win conditions may or may not exist.

Game mechanics:

  • There are no conversions.
  • There may or may not be lost wolves.
  • Flavor/role claims are allowed at your own risk.
  • Mafia factional kill is assigned. Performing it doesn’t prevent using other night actions.
  • You can’t perform actions on N0 unless specified otherwise.
  • You can’t use day abilities during the last hour of the day.
  • If you have several different night (day) abilities, then you can use all of them during a single night (day), unless specified otherwise.
