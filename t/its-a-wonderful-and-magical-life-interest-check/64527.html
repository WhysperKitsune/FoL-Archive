Damafaud May 26, 2018, 1:39pm #1

Welcome to Primrose Academy, where you will start the happiest years in your life: Junior high school! For three years, you will study, join extracullicular activity, and lead a rose coloured junior high school life!

You are just a normal school girl with an average school life. You have many friends that likes to hang out with you, and maybe a best friend or two, and perhaps… a senpai that you have a crush on?

The cool headed student council president, maybe? Or is it the hot headed captain of the basketball team? Ah! You will work with them tomorrow for the festival! You must do your best, okay?

Well, life would go on nicely like that if you are simply a normal school girl. But you aren’t, right? You have a big, big secret you can never share to anyone, even your parents. Only those with the same secret can know! That’s right. You are secretly…

A magical girl that protect the peace on Earth!

Magical girl! Sailor Moon, Card Captor Sakura, Magical Girl Lyrical Nanoha, are few anime that features magical girls!

This roleplay will resolve around the typical live of a magical girl: Living her daily average school day, while also secretly fighting evil monster that threaten the Earth!

Roleplayers will play a school girl, who by chance find a magic artifact that turn them into A MAGICAL GIRL!

This is intended to be light hearted (push Madoka Magica into a corner). Evil monster may consist of Gold Eye that try to take all gold in the world, or Pastry Queen that want to flood the world with cheap, cold pastry. Secondary characters like teachers, adults, magical boys, will be allowed after several time, but the main cast will still be magical girls.

Let’s have an adventure with magical girls everyone!
