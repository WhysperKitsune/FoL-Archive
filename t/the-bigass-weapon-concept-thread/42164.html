Possible format to suggest a weapon:

Weapon Name:

Weapon Appearance:

Execution Animation:

Examples:

Weapon Name:
Spear

Weapon Appearance:
Long wooden spear, both ends sharpened and steel-tipped.

Execution Animation:
Executioner tries to jab the accused with the spear.
Accused backs up, barely avoiding it.
Executioner lifts spear over-hand and throws it through accused’s chest.

Weapon Name:
War Hammer

Weapon Appearance:
Like Thor’s hammer, Mjolnir.

Execution Animation:
Executioner hits accused with an under-hand hammer swing.
Accused flies up into the air from being hit.
Accused never comes down.

Weapon Name:
Poison Vial

Weapon Appearance:
A green glass vial of poison, over-sized so as to be easily seen.

Execution Animation:
Executioner trows the vial at the accused, shattering it on their face.
Accused falls down holding face as green mist surrounds them.

Weapon Name:
Hidden Daggers

Weapon Appearance:
Sleeve-mounded retractable hidden daggers.

Execution Animation:
Executioner casually walks up to accused.
Executioner holds out arms (picture Wolverine) and reveals hidden wrist blades.
Executioner makes crossing slashes in an X-shape across accused’s chest.

Weapon Name:
Toilet Plunger

Weapon Appearance:
A standard toilet plunger.

Execution Animation:
Executioner sticks the plunger to the accused’s face.
Executioner gives it a couple plunges.
Accused falls over comically.

So… post your own. Let’s get a mega-thread going!
