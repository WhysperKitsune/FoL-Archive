Now that leafia’s answered,

fluffposting is scumAI for a couple reasons:

  1. It’s very easy to do. Town can solve honestly rather easily but it can be quite difficult for scum to appear that they are honestly solving and contributing to the game. By not trying, they protect themselves from townies who can spot the difference.

  2. It doesn’t help town. Everybody in town who’s trying to solve contributes to finding the wolves. Even when fakesolving, wolves have to find a balance between being reasonable and concealing their partners. As long as they aren’t being hardsheeped by many people, I think scum doing reasonable solving does help the gamestate.

  3. It clogs the thread. When the thread has a ton of posts, it makes it harder for town to find the important scummy/towny posts that they need to make reads. Fluffposting makes actual reading by town more difficult.

  4. It’s inconsistent with the town win-con. If literally nothing were to happen all game, wolves would win every time. As town, it’s our job to hunt the wolves, as well as hunting for villagers we can trust. When someone is fluffposting, it indicates that they are happy with not trying to find wolves or villagers, which is only consistent with the wolf win-con.
