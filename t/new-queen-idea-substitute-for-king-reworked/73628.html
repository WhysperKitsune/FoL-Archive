QUEEN

This is a new take on King. Instead of changes kings abilities I think we have to mix things up. This queen version has a 50% of spawning instead of King. She also has a 35% of being evil just like current King. This will add management and more interaction with Queen.

New mechanic: important to read
No matter if day or night any player in the game can donate to the Queen. There will be a donate button next to the current Queen. When clicked the player then selects one of their limited abilities to donate to the Queen and she will gain 2 bridles from it. The Queen will know who donated to her. The player will lose one of their uses

ALL OF HER ABILITIES WILL COST BRIDLES

Now all of her abilities come in packages similar to Alch. i know this seems a bit over powered but hopefully the Bridle system balances it out

Passive 1: Hard worker
You are immune to most occu and redirection. You can use more than 1 ability every day and night,at the cost of bridles .(can use different day abilities in the same day but cant use the same ability twice during the same day)

Passive 2: Kingdom’s taxes
You start with 4 bridles at the beginning of the game. Each day you gain 1 bridle. If someone is executed then you gain 2 more bridles

Day ability 1: Queens power kit

Royal finger (1 Bridles) - your next vote will count twice
Decide fate (3 Bridle)- (same as before)
Private messages (3 Bridles) - all messages from you and to you are hidden

Day ability 2; Queens guards
Public execution (20 Bridles) - ,select a player, they will be killed on the spot,{WHEN YOU USE THIS ABILITY, YOU WILL NOT BE ABLE TO USE ANY OF THE QUEENS GUARDS ABILITY AGAIN)
signed papers (3 Bridles) - select a player, that player can not be voted or vote for today
Queens pet (3 Bridles) - select a player, that player can not be effected by any day abilities except for Prince

Night ability 1; Queens night shift guard
Allies (1 Bridles) - (Good Queen) protect a player other than yourself, giving them death immunity and discover if they are BD / (Evil Queen) protect a player other than yourself, giving them death immunity and discover if they are (Unseen/Cult)
Self Guard (2 Bridles) - have a guard protect you giving you death immunity
Order execution (10 Bridles) - Kill a player, bypassing death immunity(Will be announced to court)
safe guard (7 Bridles) - make a player occu immune

Night ability 2; Queens personal agenda
Royal guards (4 Bridles)
** throne** (5 Bridles) -
Overtime (6 Bridles) - select a player, that player can now choose two targets tonight, BUT the following night they will be prevented from using that ability again(Cant be used for conversion) (cant be used for personal abilities like butler’s party ability)

This hopefully allows the leader of the group take charge more with a variety of abilities. If you have any questions please ask
