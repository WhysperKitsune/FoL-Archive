@NuclearBurrito I’ve done some thinking and have come up with a way to make the Psychic more differentiated from the Prince, without changing its premise entirely:

Psychic (Unique Blue Dragon Social)

Social Reworks

Psychic: Night Communication
Noble: Vote Manipulation
Priest: Dead Communication
Scribe: Whisper and Message Manipulation
Apostle: A jack of all Social

The Psychic

Unique Blue Dragon Social

(Converts to current Illusionist)

Passives

Astral Medium: Anyone connected to the Astral Network may talk to each other at night anonymously. If they are in jail, they cannot hear or speak. Passive remains even if class changes.

Abilities

Focus Link: (D) (2)
If Alter Link is on a cooldown, you may reset the cooldown and use Alter Link tonight.

Alter Link: (N) (oo) (1C)
Choose a player and connect them to the Astral Network. If they were already in it, remove them from the Astral Network. The Network has a maximum of 4 players at once. There is a 1 day cooldown.

The Illusionist

Unique Unseen Social

Passives

Astral Medium: Anyone connected to the Astral Network may talk to each other at night anonymously. If they are in jail, they cannot hear or speak. Passive remains even if class changes.

Skilled Medium: You can use both night abilities in the same night.

Abilities

Focus Link: (D) (2)
If Alter Link is on a cooldown, you may reset the cooldown and use Alter Link tonight.

Alter Link: (N) (oo) (1C)
Choose a player and connect them to the Astral Network. If they were already in it, remove them from the Astral Network. The Network has a maximum of 4 players at once. There is a 1 day cooldown.

Mind Warp: (N) (oo)
Choose a player. They will receive incorrect feedback from their abilities tonight.

Blue Dragon Social

(Converts to Forger)

Passives

Abilities
