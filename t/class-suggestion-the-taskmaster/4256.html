[Class Suggestion] The Taskmaster

Class/Lore/Fanfic
Finnland_Seal October 15, 2017, 8:06pm #1

The Taskmaster (Unseen Killer) - |Converted Prince|

Passive: Royal Blood
[Only usable upon the death of a king] As a Royal, you can put your name forward for nominations to become the next King.

Day Ability: Constrain
Imprison a player in one of the underground prison that looks like Prince’s prison at night, Occupying the target (Bypasses Occupy Immunity).

Day Ability: Bewilderment
Shock a player that causes he can’t speak and accusing anyone during the day.

Night Ability: Haze
If you will imprison same person next day you have 2 options to do:

  1. You bully a player that causes him to leave a court for 2 days. He will come back to the court after these 2 days [Of course If game won’t end earlier ). In this time person sees dead chat but he can’t say anything. If physcian do “Exhume” it will shows that taskmaster did it.
  2. You bully a player that causes him to convert himself into unseen. If he’s role can’t be converted [That means only every neutral role]/ or there’s too many unseen he changes automatically his role to your Mercenary.

Lore: Once upon a time there was a king which had 2 childs. When he died a battle of throne had started. One of sons wanted so much to gain a title of king that he secretly killed his brother. Court found out what happened and didn’t want to have king like him. So he was banished from the castle. Few months later he came back with fake personal data and had started live under the castle…

Comments: I think it would be really interesting role that could give a lot of fun
