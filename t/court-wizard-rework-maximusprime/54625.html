Class/Lore/Fanfic
MaximusPrime April 3, 2018, 1:18am #1

Court Wizard (BD Support)

Passive 1: Arcane Armor - If you would die(at night), you will instead start bleeding and die the next night unless you are healed. Can only be activated once
(Note: I can also change this to make it so he dies one night later, so it’s simply unhealable)

Day Ability 1: Poof! - Tonight anyone trying to target the person you selected will be prevented. Cannot target king (2)
(Note: basically ice ward lol, dunno why I changed the name)

Night Ability 1: Charm - Your target will be unable to use abilities on the person you select tonight. Can select self as second target. (attempts will fail, it will show as prevented to the Charmed tarhrt). (Infinite)
(Note: I can just make this Magic Barrier, but lots of people think it’s not fun to use, so I tried giving a different ability)

Night Ability 2: One Way Portal - Redirect anyone visiting your first target to the second. Cannot select the king. Bypasses occ immunity (3)
(Note: Different than Tornado, gives reliable defense to him or an important BD class. Can reduce uses if it’s too OP)

The Sage (Unseen Support)

Passive 1: Forbidden Magic - If the assassin was attacked(at night), you will use up your life force and heal him. You will die the next night but you get a charge on all abilities and cannot be occupied. Cannot be healed.

Day ability 1: Transfigure - Make your target appear as the class of your choice if they are executed the next day. Cannot target king. (Infinite)
(Note: basically a day defile and helps unseen with fake claiming)

Night Ability 1: Paradox - Your target will be unable to use abilities on the Unseen tonight. All attempts will be shown as prevented. (3)

Night Ability 2: Dark Dimension: Redirect anyone visiting your first target to the second. Does not affect the Unseen and cannot select the king. Bypasses occ immunity. (2)

BTW I got the “One Way Portal” idea from RawkHawk
