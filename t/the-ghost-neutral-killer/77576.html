The Ghost

Neutral Killer

Intangible (Passive) - You may perform night actions while imprisoned, and may visit imprisoned players.

Ghosting (Passive) - You are secretly death immune, a Knight’s Defend cannot kill you, and the first time the prince uses Execute on you, it will fail.

Grave Ritual (Night) - Select two players instead of one to kill tonight, bypassing death immunity.
