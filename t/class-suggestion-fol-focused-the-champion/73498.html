[Class Suggestion; FoL focused] The Champion!

Class/Lore/Fanfic
Geyde October 31, 2018, 11:37pm #1

This class with its hammer mechanic is more geared toward FoL

Once, I was bored. And tired.

The idea came from nowhere; a Blue Dragon JOAT that is not only unconfirmable but utterly ridiculous. Mashing together Alch, Miller, and plenty of stupid ideas, I came up with this.

It basically only works in FoL, but in there it would be really interesting. I’d imagine this class being claimed quite often, as an asset to its unconfirmability.

To confirm it, an observer is required.

Feedback is appreciated; Keep flavor in mind when thinking of changes.

Champion
Blue Dragon Unique Special

Crowd Favorite (Passive) - Whenever you hammer a member of the Unseen or Cult, gain a use of all of your abilities after the trial ends in execution. If you hammer BD, disable this passive after the execution.
Blatant Cheat (Passive) - You will appear suspicious to all investigative abilities.

Signature Move (Day) - You will be able any number of your abilities tonight. Once used, disable this ability. (0 Uses)

Scheme (Night) - Choose your night action! (Infinite Uses)

  • Background Check - Determine if a player is a member of the Unseen or Cult. (2 Uses)
  • Heroic Strike - Attack a player. (1 Use)
  • Fan Support - Make a player immune to death tonight. (1 Use)
  • Brazen Taunt - Make yourself immune to conversion tonight. You will also appear as not suspicious to investigative abilities. (1 Use)

Your objective is to defeat the Unseen or Cult and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.

Criminal
Unseen Special

Crowd Favorite (Passive) - When you hammer a member of the Blue Dragon, all Unseen classes will gain a use of their abilities. This resolves after the trial ends in execution.

Cheap Shot (Day) - Remove a BD player’s passives for tonight. (1 Use)

Scheme (Night) - Choose your night action!

  • Blackmail - Target player will be unable to vote tommorrow. Can only target the King if the Mastermind is dead. (2 Uses)
  • Backstab - Attack a player. Your visit will be hidden. (0 Uses)
  • Mob Mentality - Occupy a player. (1 Use)
  • Forge Evidence - Frame a player. (1 Use)

Your objective is to defeat the Blue Dragon and any neutrals that would dare stand in your way.

When converted to the Cult, the class you would become depends upon the last night ability used. (otherwise, it is chosen randomly)

  • Background Check : Seeker
  • Heroic Strike : Invoker
  • Fan Support : Ritualist
  • Brazen Taunt : Apostle

For FoL

Cult Alt

Hellish Favor (Passive) - When you hammer a member of the Blue Dragon, all cult members gain one use of any limited ability of their choice. This resolves after the trial ends. (Eradicate excluded).
