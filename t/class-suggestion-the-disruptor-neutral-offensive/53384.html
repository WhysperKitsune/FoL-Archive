This is a test take, so it’s a WIP

The Disruptor

Neutral Offensive
Win Condition: Win with the Neutral Killer. Cannot spawn with the Inquisitor and Sorcerer.
Traitorous (Passive): Immune to target changing and death at night.
Deranged (Passive): You will know if the Neutral Killer visited you. If the Neutral Killer dies, you will end your life.
Silence (Day): Silence a player for 30 seconds. They will not be able to hear anyone or vote in a trial while silenced. (2 uses)
Disrupt (Night): Disrupt a player, forcing them to target you. You will appear to be a Drunk to investigators that you force to target. (Infinite Uses)
Disorder (Night): Force 2 players to target each other. If one of the players is the Neutral Killer, their ability will bypass everything. (3 Uses.)

Conversion Immune

Tell me what you think! This hopefully addresses the current problem of NK being too hard to win as, as NKs require more effort to win as than any other class in the game.
