Let me explain myself.

There is a lot of good stuff on the Patch (Like the Psycho King buff). But a lot of things are just… Not correct.

First of all i want to say: I love the game, and i really liked the patches, like the Psycho King patch. But this one is, in my opinion, a “no fun allowed” patch.

King:
Removed Allies from starting King.
Added Night Ability Order to starting King - Prevent a player from voting tomorrow. 4 uses.
Abilities no longer count as visiting.

As long as I really liked Allies, I can understand a nerf or the remove of the ability. But “Order” is a joke.
Now neutral kings will always lose, Starting kings will start to get executed Day 4, and there is Nights when the kings has literally nothing to do but Guard Himself. What a shame.

Herbalist:
Replaced Defile with Abscission - Make an Unseen member appear as Blue Dragon tonight. Unlimited uses.

Now the Mastermind or the Assassin can appear as Blue Dragon Day 6,7,8,9,10… this is a really bad change, Sheriffs will be more Useless if Unseen gets and Herbalist.

Noble & Aristocrat:
Snoop limited to 1 use. Improved Snoop, so that the journals will now transfer over when converted.

When you re-added Noble and Aristocrat, i was really happy, Noble is actually not the best Blue Dragon class, but he could check 2 players logs, making him a good “Evil-Catcher” member of the court.

Now if he gets an empthy log, he become useless. He cant get another log to investigate more.

Physician:
Renamed to Medic and recieved new night abilities.
Night Ability First Aid - Heal a player back to life if they were attacked.
Night Ability Quarantine - Heal a player’s poison and bleeding, and prevent all visitors. Can only be used on a player once.

This is, by a long shot, the worst change in the patch.
Now the “Medic” (Terrible name for this class by the way) can only heal poison once… What if the Alchemist did not spawn in the game? what if the other physician is dead? What if Alchemist decides to side with evil?

Now the Cult leader/Assassin can just wait until 1 healer is dead and start poisoning/bleeding people, and they will not get healed twice.

Princess:
Flirt now show Killer/Support and Offensive/Social.
Will-o-Wisp changed - Discover a player’s faction. 3 uses.

This one is actually, the second worst change in the patch.

Killer/Support? this gives you no info at all, Mercenarys will get executed, Alchemist will need to prove themselves even more, and people will start to execute Healers.

Just ask yourself this: What will people do if they get a Neutral being Killer/Support and NK is alive? Buy the Alchemist claim?

Also, Princess now has a 3 uses “Allies!” ability. If you wanted to remove it from the king because it was too powerful, Why do you add it to a class who can spawn twice on a game?

Sorcerer:
Magic Dust uses reduced to 2.
Circle of Death uses reduced to 2.
New Day Ability Magic Cloak - Hides your visits at night. 2 uses.

No words. Sorcerer was actually the most weak Neutral killer in the game, Sorc can die Night 1, Circle of Death was not that useful, and you are nerfing it more.

Now inquisitor has more chances to not get fooled by magic dust since Sorc can hide visits now…

Duchess:
Dark Wisp changed - Discover a player’s class. 2 uses.

This ability is almost the same as other classes has… whats the point on Duchess getting this change?

I just… dont like the patch at all. I know you are amazing devs and you will hear us. But in my opinion, this is a gamechanger patch. And a boring one.

Good night.

EDIT: I want to say something more about Princess and Duchess:

Yea, its not the same getting accused by King “Allies!” than this new Wisp, but, there is logs, and when Princess dies, the logs will say: WOW (Number) UNSEEN/CULT/NEUTRAL, so its the same afterwards.

And Duchess, yea, they added it because it was removed from other classes, but i think it was a mistake since she needed another ability, this maybe will make Duchess more efective but its not what she needs. Thats all.
