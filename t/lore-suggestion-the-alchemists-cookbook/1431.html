Lore:
You will need:
3 medium sized cooking Apples
1 clove of garlic
Blood (any animal will suffice)
1 fillet of salmon
Magical Essence
Salt
Red Wine
Cooking Oil

  1. Remove the cores from the apples and cut them into wedges, place the wedges in a bowl of water and boil for 30 minutes
  2. Mash the garlic and add to the salmon, then cut the salmon into fine slices and fry in cooking oil
  3. Remove the apple wedges and place them in the blood, add a pinch of salt and let them marinade for 45 minutes
  4. Pour the red wine over the salmon to taste, and add the magical essence
  5. Add the apples and salmon into a cauldron full of water and let them boil for 2 hours
  6. Mix the oil, blood, wine and magical essence together
  7. Take the water from the cauldron and add to the mixture
  8. Remove and eat the salmon and apples
  9. You now have a cauldron full of lovely Crimson Potion, serve in a medicinal flask with a sip of Ale
