So let’s talk about possibilities for the Ozzkozz N1 check.

  1. Ozzkozz is a legitimate Sheriff, who legitimately found Datbird N1. I was definitely townreading Datbird yesterday, but based on the presentation of information and a pattern of N1 deaths consistent with an Unseen game, I do think this is the most likely options.

  2. Ozzkozz is a legitimate Sheriff, who unluckily hit a Scorned frame. I think this is very unlikely, as I think that a real Scorned would’ve marked SirDerp, since they were a very likely exe today, and if they’d marked someone else it would’ve been someone in the PoE.

  3. Ozzkozz is a Scorned making a Scorned play. I again think this is unlikely, since based on discussion yesterday they wouldn’t know that they needed to disguise someone as Assassin, and because, again, there was the easy mark on SirDerp sitting right there.

  4. Ozzkozz is starting Unseen (or Cult, if it turns out to be a Cult game) and making a suicide play. I’m elevating this substantially in likelihood because Ozzkozz is a ToL veteran, and the concept of suicide plays has come up a lot on the ToL Discord lately. This would be an awfully bold move, but if Ozzkozz was expecting to be the D2 lynch it might be worth it to take a BD down first. If it turns out to be this option, it’s worth looking into the ToL-veteran-FoL-newbie contingent as likely scumbuddies.

4a. Ozzkozz is N1 convert, making a suicide play. Suicide plays in ToL are generally regarded as a better idea for converts than for starting evils, particularly converts that in ToL as less useful (Handmaiden, Herbalist, etc). I think this is very unlikely because I don’t think Ozzkozz was a likely N1 convert.

  1. Ozzkozz is NK, making a ridiculously bold play. I think this one is basically impossible.

  2. Ozzkozz is a Fool, hoping to save scum from being exe’d by making sure a BD dies instead (and, presumably, that they get executed tomorrow, wasting another day for us) and chose a consensus townread to minimize the odds of hitting scum.

Of these possibilities, I think only 1, 4, and 6 deserve serious consideration, and 1 is by far the most likely, based on the presentation of the information.
