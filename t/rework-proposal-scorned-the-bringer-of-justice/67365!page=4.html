ShadowKnight June 17, 2018, 6:24pm #72

The Scorned’s wincon is very fragile and can be hit or miss. You might have a gullible group that just accepts your accusation at face value and your target mounts a poor defence and you straight up win d2 (it’s happened to me) or you can instantly lose one night because both your targets die at night out of nowhere.

And the core of the matter is that Scorned just isn’t fun to play (and neither is Fool, for that matter, but that’s a different topic). Your abilities are simple and straightforward and have little interaction with others, you have very limited decision-making and strategy and you often find that winning or losing is out of your hands.

Every other Neutral class other than Fool and Scorned is interesting to play. They both serve an important purpose and that’s fine, but you can give them something else to make them more fun to play as, and to me, fixing the Scorned involves either an alternate wincon or a full rework. So this is my attempt to fix Scorned with minimal changes to its core identity. If you disagree fundamentally with this, that’s fine, we’ll just agree to disagree.
