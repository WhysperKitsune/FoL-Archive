The Mailman

Unseen Special

Passive (Special Delivery) - The First time you get Investigated , you will appear as not suspicious.(This applies to every Investigative class’ first check)
Passive (Letter Stack) - Immune to Death at Night. You are Immune to Most Occupation and Redirection. You will appear as not occupation/redirection Immune.

Night Ability (Promotion!) - Promote one of your Unseen to become The Assassin.(Unlimited Uses - Can target yourself if you are the last Unseen member alive)
^ Optional: This will only be available after N3.

Night Ability (Convert) - Convert a player to The Unseen.(Infinite Uses - [1 Day Cooldown] - There may only be 4 living members at a time)
Night Ability (Blackmail) - Select a player. That player will be unable to speak and vote tomorrow.(Infinite Uses)
^ Optional: You will hear the target if they try to whisper/talk. If you up someone , they will up them too. Their votes won’t count if they up someone by themselves. Possible 1 day cooldown?
Goal: The Unseen’s Goal.

If The Mailman is the leader of The Unseen , there will be no Assassin unless Promotion is used. The oldest Unseen member will become The Mailman(Until N4). If The Mailman has died , The oldest Unseen member will become The Assassin with 3 uses of Distract and 2 uses of Nightshade (Unless someone is already The Assassin).

This class was made to help The Unseen gather as much information as possible. Feedback is welcome!
