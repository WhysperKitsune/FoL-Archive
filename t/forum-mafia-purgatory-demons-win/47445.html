Welcome to Purgatory!

Hosted by Marluxion!

You and 14 others are trapped in purgatory! Can you send the demons to hell or will you doom everything by allowing them into heaven?!

The game is played in two phases. Heaven Phase and Hell Phase.

During the Hell Phase, you must choose a player to send to hell. To vote for someone, simply type /Vote (Playername) or /Send (Playername). After someone is sent to hell, their alignment will be revealed and they will be unable to talk in the thread. After one player has been sent to hell, the heaven phase will begin.

During the Heaven Phase, you must choose a player to send to Heaven. Voting is done the same way, their alignment will flip and they’ll be unable to speak. After the heaven phase, a new hell phase starts and the cycle repeats.

There is no night time in purgatory! Immediately after a flip, the next phase will begin!

Votes will be compulsive and plurality based. Majority is in effect. If there is a tie, it will be broken by RNG.

Phases have a maximum length of 72 hours. At the end of the phase, if no one has been sent the player with the most votes will be sent.

The humans will win if they manage to send the majority (3 of the 5) Demons to hell!

Demons will win if the majority of them are sent to heaven!

The Demons will have a private chat where ALL Demons (including those sent to hell/heaven) can communicate at any time.

When 50% of the Living players are demons, a Judgement Day phase begins.

The Living players lose their ability to vote (but may still speak in the thread). All of the Lost Souls sent to heaven (does not include demons currently in heaven) must privately (NOT IN THE THREAD.) vote to send a player to hell.

If the player sent is a lost soul, the Demons win!
If the player sent is a demon, the game proceeds to the next hell phase.
