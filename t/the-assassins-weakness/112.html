Noitcelfer January 12, 2017, 9:15pm #16

Simple answer: add in tactical kills, and find a different night use for Assassin. I believe a thread similar to this reply already exists. Tactical kills allow any Rose member can kill someone, sacrificing their night action to do so. Of course, only one Rose can kill per night. This adds depth and interesting strategies to the game. Is it more important for X to perform his action, or should Y?

It also doesn’t make Occupies practically useless, like in the GF/Maf setup in Town of Salem. Escorts are trash in Ranked mode, because Godfather counters pretty much any hope of blocking Mafia kills. With tactical kills, they can still block a kill from happening if they guess the Rose killer for the night. However, it doesn’t hard-counter it either, as the Roses can then switch the kill to someone they doubt will be Occupied.

Just an idea, but I think it would be a cool addition.
