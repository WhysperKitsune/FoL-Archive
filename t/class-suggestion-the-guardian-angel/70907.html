The Guardian Angel

Neutral Support

Day Ability (Purge) - Selected player can not be the target of negative effects today and tonight.(2 Uses - Negative Effects : Mindwarp , Frame , Reap , Nightshade , Bleeding ~ Does NOT Protect attacks - Can NOT Target The King)

Night Ability (Angelic Wings) - Prevents abilities targeting a player.(2 Uses - Can NOT Target The King)
Night Ability (Last Breath) - If your target gets attacked tonight , you will die instead of them.(1 Use - Not Usable until N4)
Goal: The Unseen/The Cult loses.

Hidden Passive (Second Chance)

If The Guardian Angel dies , The Guardian Angel will become The Lost Spirit , possessing the last player that they have targeted. If all The Unseen/The Cult are dead , you will not come back as The Lost Spirit.

The Lost Spirit

Neutral Investigative

Passive (Thirsty for Revenge) - Immune to Occupation and Redirection.
Passive (Afterlife) - Immune to Death at Night. Attackers will learn your identity.

Day Ability (Haunt) - Get a player’s exact class.(Infinite Uses)
Day Ability (Check The Past) - See if the target has killed anyone.(4 Uses)

Night Ability (Revenge) - Attack a player.(Infinite Uses - Only Works on Haunt’s and Check the Past’s targets ~ Bypasses Healing and Night Immunity)
Goal: Your killer dies.

Overall the class is an extremely broken version of the Mercenary.

This is a pro-BD neutral with instant confirmation and too many immunities.

NAOtheRitualist:

Passive (Little Helper) - Immune to Nightshade and Bleeding . Immune to Reap .

There is no reason why a supportive neutral that doesn’t even need to survive should have these immunity bonuses. Get rid of it.

NAOtheRitualist:

Passive (Truthful) - Immune to Trollbox . Your vote can not be tempered with.

NAOtheRitualist:

Day Ability (Denounce) - Your target can not vote or be voted today.(1 Use - Can NOT Target The King)

This essentially automatically confirms the user. Reminder that a neutral class must only check out at most two of the following three:

  1. Be of use to BD. - The fact that it has a ton of protective abilities and must defeat the Unseen/Cult makes this a hard yes.
  2. Must be immune to conversions. - Binary option, your class is immune to it.
  3. Can be easily confirmed. - The day ability essentially gives automatic confirmation regarding it.

Additionally, there is no reason why a BD-aligned player should have denounce. Remove it as it’s an instant-confirming negative utility ability.

NAOtheRitualist:

Night Ability (Angelic Wings) - Prevents abilities targeting a player.(5 Uses - Can NOT Target The King)

Too strong as there are few counters to it. The Mercenary’s primary ability has the drawback of not being able to be used on the same target for two nights in a row, which is what this ability needs.

NAOtheRitualist:

Night Ability (Last Breath) - If your target gets attacked tonight , you will die instead of them.(1 Use - Not Usable until N4)

He has 5 nights of preventions. Why does he even need this?

NAOtheRitualist:

Passive (Afterlife) - Immune to Death at Night. Attackers will learn your identity.

BD-aligned classes should not have unlimited night immunity.

NAOtheRitualist:

Day Ability (Haunt) - Get a player’s exact class.(Infinite Uses)

This is stupidly broken. BD-aligned investigative should not be able to have access to an ability with perfectly accurate results as there’s no counter-play to this.

NAOtheRitualist:

Night Ability (Revenge) - Attack a player.(Infinite Uses - Only Works on Haunt ’s and Check the Past ’s targets ~ Bypasses Healing and Night Immunity)

This ability is just downright useless for the LS’s wincon. The entire point of it is to get your accuser executed, not killed at night.
