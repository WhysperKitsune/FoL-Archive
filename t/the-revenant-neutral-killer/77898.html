Revenant
Neutral Killer

Passive:
Seeking Vengence: If you target a Killer class your attack will bypass all forms of protection. You will also be immune to occupation and redirection.
Rejuvenation: You are death immune. If you are attacked, you will occupy yourself.

Day:
Stalk the Guilty (inf use): Target a player, you will know their action and results at the end of night.
Glare (2 use): Target a player, they will be occupied tonight. They will also be considered as a Killer class if you attack them tonight.

Night:
Bludgeon (inf use): Attack a player.
Multistrike (0 use): Attack 2 players. Gains 1 use for each Killer class you killed with Bludgeon.

Win con: Eliminate both major factions.
