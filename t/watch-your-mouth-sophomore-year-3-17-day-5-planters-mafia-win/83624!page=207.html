Okay, Vulgard and then Appel later asked similar questions about why I didn’t do much a couple days ago. I’m not sure if they are both rhetorical questions or not, but I’ll go ahead and answer.

The main reason I didn’t post as much 2 days ago was because my day ended up being busier than expected. The secondary reason was that I felt the situation had pretty much already been decided for the next step. Derps was going to be the lynch but we were going to wait until the next day. The likely wolf team was Derps/Kat-Esq, though we’d have the next day cycle to continue the discussion.

As a side to better explain myself in general, I’m the type of person who doesn’t really feel comfortable with needless chatter, so I tend to limit what I say to what’s necessary or helpful. Yes, I know that’s not so good with these social deduction games. That’s actually part of the reason I’m pushing myself to play them. I know it would be wise to learn such skills as reading people, making coherent arguments, socializing, and such. So even though it is often stressful for me playing, I’ve decided I want to keep going. And so while often these criticisms of my play hurt, they aren’t all unwelcome since I need to hear them in order to eventually improve.

Yesterday I actually happened upon an old thread here about emotional stress in FoL. That was actually comforting to read through and see that others were having similar feelings as me. This game can indeed be highly stressful for some people.
