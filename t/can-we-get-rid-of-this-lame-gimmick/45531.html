I like the DK as it is. Interactions with the dead are necessary to keep them engaged, which is desirable overall - if people leave the moment they die, engagement with the game overall diminishes. The game either needs to have a fair number of dead interactions, and powerful ones, or none at all, and I’m absolutely not in favor of going for none at all.

Beyond that, I feel that evils should always be punished for being too obvious, regardless of what stage of the game it’s at. The point where evils can afford to be completely obvious should be after the game has already ended. DKs help contribute to that, and reduce late-game situations where the game becomes boring because absolutely everything is known 100%.
