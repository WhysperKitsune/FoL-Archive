[Class Suggestion] The Butcher (Rework)

Class/Lore/Fanfic
ThePlex June 27, 2017, 10:14am #1

The Butcher - (Neutral Killer)

Passive: Seeing Red
Nothing will stop your Blood obsession, you are immune to death at night.

Passive: Bloody Mess
You will spill Blood on anybody that visits you as long as you are covered in Blood. Blood will appear in all Investigative results.

Night Ability 1: Slaughter - Infinite uses
You will slaughter your victim tonight, covering yourself and anybody visiting in Blood.

Night Ability 2: Butcher - 2 uses
You will butcher your target tonight, destroying any Journal they have created. Only you will get covered in Blood.

Day Ability 1: Clean - 2 uses
You will wash the Blood off of yourself today.

Day Ability 2: Wipe - 2 uses
You will wipe some of the Blood onto your target, covering them with Blood.

Goal: Go on a murderous rampage and kill anyone that would oppose you, Blue Dragon, Unseen and even the Cult who’re using Blood in the wrong way!

Lore: “If I don’t kill somebody soon, I’m going forget how! My knives are dry like bone and need to be dripping with Blood!”

Once thought to be just an average local butcher, this individual became obsessed with Blood. After watching carcass after carcass be dragged through his butchery, he wanted to see more. He offered locals to see his merchandise and lead them to their demise. He often bathed in their Blood, sometimes even painted portraits of his victims using their own Blood. He was considered almost demonic.

Even The Cult considered The Butcher to be out of his mind. It was said that even Mithras denied any form of contact with The Butcher’s mind. His obsession with Blood was beyond any sort of mystical power. It made a lot of people sick. But little did they know, the Blood obsessed Butcher found his way into Castle Adiart. His wrath will soon strike again…

Notes:

  • Currently, The Butcher is going to be used in SFoL6: The Brotherhood, but this one I’ve reworked with a similar style that would be better fitted into Unseen/Cult games.
  • In terms of Investigative - Princess would be somewhat of a counter to The Butcher’s blood. If she sees “Killer - Covered in Blood” - then she may have The Butcher. But if she sees “Not a Killer - Covered in Blood” then it’s not The Butcher.
    Sheriff, Observer, Paladin and other Investigatives however, will see “Covered in Blood” after their results.
  • I did think about adding something extra, further the blood. Different stages to the blood. An example:

Stage 1 Blood: Invest result - Your target has blood on them
Stage 2 Blood: Your target has too much blood on them to detect anything

That being said, it could be too powerful for The Butcher.
