The Rebel

Neutral Special

Passive (Hidden) - Immune to Occupation and Redirection.
Passive (Minion Horde) - The Newest Minion will guard you every night. If you get attacked , The Newest Minion and The Attacker die.

Day Ability (Stealth Slap) - Make a player unable to use their day abilities.(2 Uses - Will not notify The Target.)

Night Ability (Recruit) - Recruit a player. They will be known as The Minion.(Infinite Uses - 1 Day Cooldown - Can NOT target The King)
Night Ability (Scouting The Castle) - Learn a player’s faction.(3 Uses)
Goal: Survive.

The Minion

Neutral Special

Passive (Leadership) - You will keep your current class. You will know who is The Rebel and who are the other Minions .
Passive (In The Shadows) - If The Rebel dies , the oldest Minion will become The Rebel.(Available until N3)
Goal: The Rebel wins.
