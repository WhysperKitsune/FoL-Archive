Callinety Falls

A realm dominated by concrete jungle, Callinety Falls technology is similar to that of the modern world. Humans with extraordinary power, dubbed Metahumans, have long been a part of society. The appearance of Metahumans date back as far as the 14th Century.

Callinety Falls is connected to Nexus Hub through an underground passage, similar to an underground passage for cars. This passage is located at the end of a what was a dead end in a residential area. The exit in Nexus Hub is that of a gigantic tree.
