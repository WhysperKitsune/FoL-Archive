#The Forgotten

[Neutral] [Killer]

{Illusive}(Passive) - [Immune to Occupation and target changing. Will not appear Occupy Immune to other
players. Your visits will also be hidden from Other players at night.]

{Odd Familiarity}(Passive) - [For the first 3 Night’s appears to be Blue Dragon and Social]

{Lapse Memory}(Day) - [Change a Marked Players vote] - [3] Uses

{Exert Presence}(Day) - [Your visit tonight will be able to be seen by other players.] - [3] Uses

{Mark Player}(Night) - [Marks a target player. Within two nights the player will die. You will hear whispers to and from that player.] - [Infinite.] Uses

{Mind Collapse}(Night) - [Clear a Marked players Log and Chat. Gain a copy of that player’s logs.] - [2] Uses

Your objective is to [see all members of the main faction killed.]
