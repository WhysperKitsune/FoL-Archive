I would like to point out that I do not blame BBB for this, but it was absolutely the wrong thing to do.

After this I had a private conversation with XBlade and we made our opinions on the matter clear to one another.

On January 29th, 2019, I left the Discord server of my own free will.

Cut to today, January 31st, 2019.

A member of the server, CCC, invites someone, DDD, to join Site B, to chill out and relax and all that cool stuff.

At roughly 3:45 PM UTC, DDD posts a wall of text directed at me that was, in my opinion, an attack, no screenshots exist of this message because DDD was banned from Site B soon after and the messages were deleted.

At 3:53, another member of the server, EEE, mentions that XBlade attempted to discredit Site B in a private chat.

At 4:36, EEE is banned from the ToL discord server, and it becomes evident that there is another person, FFF, in Site B passing information to XBlade.

At 4:46, I rejoin the official server, less than 8 minutes later I am banned for “Supreme shit talking”.

In case this is my last post here, goodbye everyone, I’ll never forget this community. I wouldn’t say I love you all but there’s a fair few of you I quite like : P
