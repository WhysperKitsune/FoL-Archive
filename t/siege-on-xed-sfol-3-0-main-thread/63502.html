What is this Siege on the Xed thingy?

You are not in Castle Adiart and everything is completely rethemed.
All aboard. Ship Xed, which houses the Green Kraken. A pirate faction that is friendly towards the Blue Dragon. With similar classes to BD (and some unique ones)

Well, the Unseen or Cult have decided that Castle Adiart is out of their reach to conquer, so what is the second best option to take over? None other than a pirate ship. Pirate ships are cool.

There are entirely new threats on this pirate ship. Venomous snakes that poison you, the idea of giving evils their special abilities.

Gameplay and Name Changes:

  • The King is now named The Captain.
  • Royal Blood is no longer a concept here and has been replaced with a different concept.
  • The game is now set on a Pirate Ship instead of the usual Castle.
  • There is no Blue Dragon, instead having a new main faction called the Green Kraken.
  • All new classes.
  • Plunder Abilities which are 1 time use special abilities which you gain from being given the ability from somebody else. You must use plunder abilities at night.
  • You may use abilities on trial.
  • Day occupation and day redirection are both concepts here.
  • Starting Cowardly King/Captain has been removed in favor for 65/35.
  • Spiritualist/Revenant and Fool have both been disabled.

What is a Plunder?

Plunder Abilities are a new feature to this gamemode. The Captain or Plunder Organiser (even when converted) may give a targeted player the ability to use their plunder ability. Which is a one time use ability that is more powerful than their regular abilities. They will only be able to gain and use it once in a game unless stated otherwise.

Plunder abilities are always free regarding Cult Points.
