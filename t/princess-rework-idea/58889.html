Princess Rework Idea

Class/Lore/Fanfic

The Princess

Blue Dragon Investigative
Royal Blood (Passive) - As a Royal, you can put your name forward for nominations to become the next King.
Will O’ Wisp (Day) - Tells you if the target attacks anyone tonight. Only detects attacks that count as visiting. - 2 uses
Flirt (Night) - Check to see what Class Type the selected player is.You will receive two results, one of which will be their real type, and one won’t be. - Infinite uses
Woman Intuition (Night) - Check what player is capable of. - 2 uses
Defeat the Unseen or Cult, and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.

The Duchess

Unseen Investigative
Royal Blood (Passive) - As a Royal, you can put your name forward for nominations to become the next King.
Bat Eyelashes (Night) - Determine the target’s class type and faction. - Infinite uses
Dark Wisp (Night) - Uncage a dark wisp, making Unseen appear as not suspicious to investigative roles. - 2 uses
Defeat the Blue Dragon and any neutrals that seek to do you harm.

Since The Princess is overall the weakest investigative class in the game and there already came some ideas for reworks i thought i made a thread about it as well.

The most noticeable change is moving Will O’ Wisp into day abillities and adding new night abillity that is i believe fit to Princess. It can determite which types of abillities (Except passives) player have. Their names have from class types.
