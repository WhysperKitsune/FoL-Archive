Noble was prevented from having any visitors

Bug Reports

What happened?

During the later stages in a 16-player game there were 6 players left. 2 alchemists, MM (me) and assassin, a neutral king and a noble. King was guarding himself and alchemists kept healing the king giving the assassin and me multiple nights to wrap things up. Then the following happened:
nX: try kill king and convert noble: king was healed and mm was “prevented from visiting noble” (e.g. the message displayed when you target an ice warded player)
dX+1: execute alch 1
nX+1: assassin kill alch 2 and convert noble and mm was “prevented from visiting noble”
dX+2: no executes
nX+2: assassin attack king and convert noble. This way king couldn’t guard both and either king would die or noble converted and unseen would win. mm was “prevented from visiting noble” and king guarded himself
dX+3: execute assassin
nX+3: convert noble and mm was “prevented from visiting noble”
dX+4: execute mm and BD and king win

What was SUPPOSED to happen?

noble should have been converted at some point and unseen would have won immediately (he was only bd left with alchs and neutral king)

Steps to reproduce:

What possible triggers the “prevented from visiting” message has and which of those flags does not get cleared properly. But he did cast wall of fire the night after when the assassin tried to kill the noble and that kill failed as well. So maybe it is wall of fire related
