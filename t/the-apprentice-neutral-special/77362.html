The Apprentice [Neutral Special]

Passives

Contracted - Instead of your contractor visiting and doing their night action, you will do it instead.
Apprenticeship - You gain all of the passives that your contractor has.

Day Abilities

None

Night Abilities

Make a contract - Choose someone to become their apprentice. When they die, if more than 3 days have passed, you will become their class with all uses of limited abilities that they had. They will win if you finish their objective. Inf uses, you will be occupy immune while doing this. If it succeeds, you will lose use of this ability

Workshop accident - Instead of doing their night action for your contractor, you will attack them. 1 use

Goal: Make a contract with another player and furfill their win condition

You can make a contract with any class

This class is unique

If it contracts someone who is later converted (or possessed), The Apprentice will be alerted.

The contractor will be alerted that they have an apprentice.
