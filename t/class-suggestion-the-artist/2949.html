[Class suggestion] The Artist

Class/Lore/Fanfic
Luxx September 10, 2017, 6:55am #1

I know this class will never be added but it’s fun to try

The Artist - Neutral wildcard

Icon drawn by me

Passive - Artistic
Despite not being a killer, you have access to the death note tab.

Night ability 1 - Plagiarism (4 uses?)
Copy someone’s Death Note. If your target has no death note for you to steal, you’ll receive a message telling you so.

Night ability 2 - Self advertisement (unlimited use)
If your target is killed tonight, you will replace the killers death note with your own. Works on any death that occurs at night, excluding suicide.

Day ability 1 - Bribe (2 uses)
Make the selected person target someone of your choosing tonight. Won’t work if selected person uses an ability that targets no-one. Also won’t work if either target dies that day.

Goal
Successfully replace 2 death notes. Live to the end.

The idea of this class is that it’s more or less someone who can draw all game…
But I tried making it useful to both BD and Unseen/Cult/Neutral Killers by adding the Plagiarism ability. Your information could benefit any side as you’ll find killing classes.

With the alternative win goal you should lean more towards siding with killers in general, sharing info in whispers, potentially making noble more useful.

It’ll also be a good claim for scum, as the artist can never truly confirm his class. But since he’s neutral you might not know if you wanna risk losing a potential ally.

I’m tired and writing using Mobile, sorry if anything is spelt wrong
