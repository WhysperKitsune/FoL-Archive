1.0.7 Patch Notes

Official Announcements
Ellie October 30, 2017, 1:49pm #1

General:

  • Journal has been renamed to Logbook.
  • There can now only be a max of 2 Neutral Socials that spawn in a game.
  • Report summary min. characters increased from 1 to 15. Please include as many details as possible when reporting.
  • Added the ability to purchase gold with money from your Steam Wallet.

There are 4 Tiers:

  • $2.49 for 1,500
  • $4.99 for 3,500
  • $9.99 for 8,000
  • $19,99 for 18,000

Priest:

  • Removed day ability ‘Bind Soul’.
  • The Priest now gives up their own life when they revive a player.
