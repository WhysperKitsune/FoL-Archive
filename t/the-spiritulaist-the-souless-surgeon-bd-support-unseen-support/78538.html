The Spiritualist [Blue Dragon Support]

Passives

Spiritual Equilibrium - If you are bled, you cannot be healed by others.

Day Abilities

Deep Cleanse - You will not bleed yourself if the attack against your target is unhealable tonight. (1 use)

Night Abilities

Cleanse - Heal a player. If the attack is unhealable, you will bleed yourself to prevent the attack*. (Inf use)
Spiritual Ritual - Heal yourself from any status effects. If the attack is unhealable, you will bleed yourself, preventing the attack*. (1 use)

Goal: Defeat the Cult, Unseen, and any Neutrals that seek to do you harm.

The Souless Surgeon [Unseen Support]

Passives

Souless Slave - If you are attacked, nothing can prevent the attack apart from yourself.

Day Abilities

None!

Night Abilities

Spirit Balancing - Heal target Unseen Member. If the attack was unhealable, you will bleed yourself to prevent it*. (2 uses)
Soul Trading - If you die tonight, your target will die instead. (1 use)

Goal: Defeat the Blue Dragon and any Neutrals that seek to do you harm.

*Does not work on Prince’s Execute

DeathReaperRH October 5, 2019, 4:28am #2

Can I just ask how Unholy cleansing would ever be useful? The MM is already immune to bleed and the only class that can bleed Unseen is Hunter which doesn’t even spawn every game. Also, it occupies the assassin, which means Unseen loses a kill. Basically it has no utility as even if an assassin is bleeding you would much rather take 2 potential kills over just one. So in the BEST case scenario, there is a Hunter, its D4, and he bled the assassin. The assassin also wasn’t jailed and you’re able to heal him at the expense of a kill. If the best-case scenario is that then this is one of the worst converts ever. Also, it could lead to some Soulless Surgeons to trolling their teammates by occupying them.
I actually think that the Blue Dragon version is super cool though. I think that having a class that can prevent unhealable attacks is a neat albeit niche feature. It also has some weaknesses like having to recover from bleed themselves and only being able to do it once. Deep cleanse could use a buff to 2 uses but that’s just my opinion. (The thought process is it won’t proc that much anyway so giving people another use of it probably doesn’t matter.)
I think there are scenarios where Chronomancers, Physicians, and Spiritualists are all good which is what you want.
Side note: This class seems almost like a direct counter to Reaper. I assume this was intentional in the design and if this class were to be implemented Reaper would become significantly weaker when this class spawned.
