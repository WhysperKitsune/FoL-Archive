Hosting Forum of Lies, derived from "Throne of Lies" online social deduction game on Steam @ discord.gg/tol

Gameplay

  • Days will be 4 IRL days long, or shorter if someone dies
  • Nights will be 2 IRL days long
  • Actions are done by messaging me
  • House will talk through a special Discord server
  • Cards at bottom of page
  • When you receive a card you will have 24 hours to reply, confirming you have received it

Executions

  • To accuse someone say “/accuse [player]” or “/vote [player]”
  • When someone is on Trial they will be able to post a 1000 character defense through me, during this time no-one should post, everyone will then vote Guilty or innocent by messaging me
  • Executions end the Day
  • Trials pause the counter
  • Players may place bets on a player and if the player that was executed was evil, they get double their money.

Special Info

  • House has a limit of 4 memebers at a time
  • After 2 days of debt, you die
  • FAQ will be allowed and will be posted in this area
