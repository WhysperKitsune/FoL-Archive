orangeandblack5 January 27, 2018, 1:05pm #1

So the next update looks to bring back the old, unconvertible Sheriff. We know that this can work, because it has in the past, and without King’s Allies I can see why @Ellie is making this change. However, I think there’s a few changes that should be made before this patch goes live.

First off, I absolutely love the direction of this change. Making one of Sheriff/Pally guaranteed and unconvertible while making the other convertible is a great idea and will really help to differentiate the gameplay of these two factions.

However, I think we might have taken the wrong road here. With only 3 Unseen, one of whom is the Mastermind, they’re pretty static. The Cult, on the other hand, is much more fluid, with up to 4 members and Cult Leader being an inheritable role. I personally believe that keeping the current Sheriff and instead making the Paladin guaranteed to spawn and unconvertible will result in better gameplay for both factions, and it seems that others agree.

Hopefully this gets changed, because I really do believe that it would be the best direction for the game to go.
