Livicus March 2, 2018, 2:15am #425

Oh and can we include these classes to the thread?

The Thief

Neutral Offensive

Goal: Successfully Rob the Treasury

Passives

Keen Sense:
At the start of each morning, the number of remaining Royals that need to be robbed will show up to the Thief. When the number hits 0, the Thief will have “robbed” the Palace Treasury and wins. He will then be convert immune.

If:

  • Thief uses Robbery on a Royal
  • Royal who has not been robbed dies

Keen Sense will go down by 1.

If:

  • Baron or Duke gives Royal Blood to a player who can earn it via Kinship

Keen Sense will go up by 1.

Royal Hatred:
You cannot become a candidate for a New King. You can vote for a New King still.

Abilities

Drugged Dagger: (D) (3)
Choose a player. They will not see names before all messages, accusals, and votes for the rest of the day.

Spying Eyes: (N) (oo)
Check two players at night to see if at least one is a Royal. If at least one is a Royal, gain one charge of Robbery.

Robbery: (N) (0) (Maximum of 3)
Choose a player. If they’re a Royal, remove their immunities and occupy them. They will then count as a robbed target toward your goal and be deducted from the Keen Sense passive.

The Rogue

Unseen Offensive

Goal: Same as Unseen

Passives

Shadowform:
The Rogue will not count towards the Maximum Unseen limit. He cannot become an Assassin even if there is no other Unseen candidate.

Royal Hatred:
You cannot become a candidate for a New King. You can vote for a New King still.

Abilities

Deprive: (N) (oo)
During the Night, choose a person to occupy. If successfully occupied, deprive one use from all limited night abilities of target.

Alcoholic Dagger: (D) (2)
Your Deprive tonight will also remove a target’s immunities.

Dirty Work: (D) (oo)
If there is no other Unseen left besides the Mastermind, become the Assassin that night. All your passives and abilities will be changed to an Assassin.
