Gameplay (the tl;dr version)

Official Rulebook

The Crime Phase

When you receive a card you will have ~24 hours to reply, confirming you have received it in your classcard.
The murderer will also gain the identity of the accomplice and vice versa.
Everyone except the Forensic Scientist will also gain 4 clue cards and 4 means cards upon this.
The Forensics Scientist gains the identity of everyone.
Upon this. The murderer must choose his means of murder and key evidence.

The Investigation Phase

Forensic Scientist
The Forensic Scientist must place a bullet on each scene tile that is entered into play.
Even if some of these scene tiles don’t have clues relevant to the case. The Scientist MUST place a bullet to one of these clues anyway.
If an Investigator presents their findings for the case, this is when the Forensic Scientist may reply with “Yes” or “No” response as to if the ENTIRE CASE is correct.
The Forensic Scientist and the host are the only ones who are allowed to use big text.

Investigators
During each round, each Investigator looks at the clues the Forensic Scientist is providing and this may lead to them discussing who the murderer is.
Via each phase before and after the forensic scientist place their clues There is a 24 hour time period for the players to talk about their opinions on the case.

Additionally if a player feels strongly about their findings. They may use their /badge token to present their case to the Forensic Scientist.

Using Your Badge Token

Once per game, every player (this includes the murderer, witness and accomplice) and present their theory of how the murder occurred in the thread. The accusation must get all three variables correct in order to solve the case.

  1. The Murderer
  2. The Key evidence card
  3. The means of the Murder card

If any part of the theory is incorrect, the Forensic Scientist will state that the accusation was incorrect and the player who guessed it may no longer officially guess to the forensic scientist.

End of Game

The game ends if one of the following conditions occur:

  • An investigator uses their Badge Token and correctly accuses.
  • All players have used their badge tokens with incorrect acussations/
  • The third round completes without a correct accusation.

Who Wins?

Forensic Scientist/Investigators/Witness wincon:
If the murderer, key evidence and means of murder is correctly stated before the end of the game. They must also make sure their key witness doesn’t die if this is the case.

Murderer/Accomplice wincons:
If nobody is able to guess correctly or if their witness kill is correctly placed.

Rolecards

The Forensic Scientist

You are the forensic scientist. You know exactly what everyone’s role is and you may not directly speak to the Investigators. You also know exactly what the cause of murder and and the key evidence is.
Your objective is to get the Investigators and Witness to correctly guess who the murderer is and make sure they protect the witness.

The Witness

You are the witness, you were there to see exactly who the Murderer and Accomplice are.
The two players that are associated with the crime are [x] and [y].
Your objective is to correctly solve the case and make sure you don’t die after the case is solved.

The Investigator

You are the Investigator, you were tasked to solve the murder case. The Forensic Scientist is here to help you though.
Your objective is to correctly solve the case and make sure The Witness doesn’t die after the case is solved.

The Murderer

You are the Murderer, which is very self explanatory. Upon your cards. You must choose which of your owned cards is the Key Evidence and the Means of Murder respectively in this DM in 12 hours upon recieving this card or it will be randomised. Once this is done, the game may officially start.
Your accomplice who helped you with your crime is [x]
Your objective is to get away with your crimes or kill the Witness.

The Accomplice

You are the Accomplice, you helped organise the murder with the murderer. With this, you also know the solution to the crime.
The murderer is [x]
Your objective is to get away with your crimes or kill the Witness.
