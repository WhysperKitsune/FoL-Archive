Chronomancer Allignment Chart

On the Forum of Lies Forum Mafia Forums, there are many great touchstones of design. The Lying Darkness. Putting an Even Night Tracker and an Odd Night Watcher in every single game. But none come close to the majesty that is putting a literal Chronomancer in any game, no matter how thematically or design unrelated it is to the rest of the setup.

However, some people wish to know: what constitutes a Chronomancer? Well, look no further. As the illustrious original designer of this iconic role, I know everything there is to know about chronomantic epistomology, and have devised a chart that creates nine definitive positions as to what constitutes a Chronomancer, from which you can take your pick.

The Chronomancer Allignment Chart Effect Purist - A Chronomancer must be able to both cause and prevent death. Effect Neutral - A Chronomancer must be able to either cause or prevent death. Effect Rebel - A Chronomancer must have an effect on the game.
Ability Purist - A Chronomancer must have a night ability that causes an effect on a later night. Hardline Traditionalist - Dave Strider is a Chronomancer. Effect Neutral, Abillity Purist - A Delayed Vigilante is a Chronomancer Effect Rebel, Ability Purist - A Parity Cop is a Chronomancer.
Ability Neutral - A Chronomancer must have a night ability. Ability Neutral, Effect Purist - JOAT (2x Doctor, 2x Vigilante) is a Chronomancer. True Neutral - A Doctor is a Chronomancer. Effect Rebel, Ability Neutral - A Watcher is a Chronomancer.
Ability Rebel - A Chronomancer must have an ability. Ability Rebel, Effect Purist - A 1-shot Bulletproof Paranoid Gun Owner is a Chronomancer. Effect Neutral, Ability Rebel - A Tea Lady (Blood on the Clocktower) is a Chronomancer. Radical Chronomancer Anarchy - A Doublevoter is a Chronomancer.
