Infection refers to the Corruption/Crimson state of
someone. (Whether they have it or not)
Purity refers to whether or not a player is free of any Infection or Hallowing.

At the start of the game, This class has an equal chance to be either Infected or Hallowed.
The Infection type of a game is completely randomized. If there are other rolls of this class, there may be both Infections in the game.

If there is Multiball and there are multiple Infections, Different Informed Minorities will all be the same infection. There will always be at least one of each Infection that rolls for the Informed Minorities.
If there is not Multiball, but there are multiple infections, then at least one member of the Informed Minority will be One infection, and another will be the other. If there is only 1 starting Informed Minority member, they will contain both infections.

Infected and Hallowed Players (Other than the Civilian) will spread their state onto others that they visit/ visit them. If a hallowed player visits/is visited by an infected player, they will become Pure, regardless of how many visit them, and vice versa with Infection.
It is possible for players to be infected with both infections, in which case they will spread/lose one at random, when the circumstances occur.
It is not possible for Civilians to infect/purify each other.
