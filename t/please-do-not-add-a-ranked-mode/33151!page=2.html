TinyDeer January 9, 2018, 8:14am #25

Not surprisingly, I agree with the arguments against Ranked. The ToL community is not just good - it’s outstanding compared to other games. One of the features of ToL as it stands is that experienced players mix with new players. That’s a good thing! It helps remove the stigma of being new! I LOVE helping new players get to know the game through explaining their beginner mistakes. That opportunity is something special.

I play multiple games a day and all of them feel competitive, like people are trying their best. Sure, often one person will leave, but it doesn’t meaningfully impact the game for me - the punishment system is really stellar here. And I think it’s FUN to have a mix of serious players and people just playing for kicks (not trolls, but to have a good time) because gamethrowers and toxic players are actually punished here. It adds a layer of unpredictability, surprise, and good cheer to have someone unexpectedly have “jailed by a hawt pwince” in their logs instead of only seeing the kind of bland and concise logs that I write as an experienced player ("N1 - Jailed | N2 - heal [3], etc.). If everyone was good, the game would be far less interesting. It would feel like a competition and not a game. That being said, I find that 80% of the time, enough people are good that it feels like a balanced match.

I think it’s a good thing and a wonderful thing that the community is decent and friendly enough to help newer players get better and to just have fun playing the game. Adding a ‘serious mode’, Veterans or otherwise, would destroy that beautiful feature.
