4lan September 15, 2018, 4:50pm #1

This is a revamp from my old post called ‚ÄúTotal Madness Gamemode‚Äù

Madness Imperium is a gamemode idea made by me.

It works like this:

25 players needed to start the game.
There is no Fool or Scorned.

Both Cult And Unseen Spawns , If Cult Leader or Mastermind tries to convert the other faction members, it will fail, if both factions tries to convert the same Blue Dragon in the same night, it will also fail.

Layout:

----------------------------- The King

65% Chance to be Blue Dragon

34% Chance to be Evil (Random evil faction)

1% Chance to be Psycho
If the King is Psycho, there will be only 1 other Neutral Killer (See below)

----------------------------- Blue Dragon

1 Prince with infinite uses of execution until he uses it on a Blue Dragon member

14 Blue dragon members (1 confirmed Sheriff with a 2nd Sheriff chance and 1 confirmed Paladin with 2nd Paladin chance)

Sheriff and Paladin rework: If a Paladin finds a Unseen member, Sheriff will have this message: ‚ÄúA paladin found an Unseen member last night‚Äù, same with Sheriff and cult

Paladin will not know he found an Unseen, and Sheriff will not know he found a Cult

Blue Dragon can have 3 of the same class if not unique

----------------------------- Evil And Neutrals

1 Mastermind
1 Assassin
1 Cult Leader
1 Random cult member
2 Neutral Killers
3 Neutrals, There is 0% chance of a Social Neutral spawning in these games.

Whats the point of Total Madness gamemode?

Total Madness would be a super fun mode to play. Nobody will know whats happening the first days. Its a challenge to all veteran players and a new way to play Throne of Lies!

FAQ

Unseen and cult has to defeat each other?
Yes.

Neutral Killers can side to erradicate all 3 factions?
No, they have to fight, Only 1 neutral killer should win

Why no Fool or Scorned?
Trollboxes in a 20 player game is too OP, And Scorned would have a really easy game since 4 executions would be super normal, also frame would be just too powerful.

Can Paladin find a Unseen member and Sheriff Cult members?
Yes. In this gamemode, both investigators can get evil members, however, I explained above how it works in this gamemode.

Smite kills the Mastermind?
No, but the Paladin gets a notification saying Mastermind tried to convert. Mastermind will fail the conversion. Paladin does not know who is the Mastermind.

What happens if Mastermind converts a Paladin?

He becomes a Marshal.

Hope you like the Idea! if you like it, I would love to see your feedback. Also if you have a suggestion please tell me so!
