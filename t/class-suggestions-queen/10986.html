Class/Lore/Fanfic

Objectives of this class:

#1 Replace neutral Killers. Queen allows to speed up the game, but its not as random and chaotic as neutral killers. I would put Queen in the game and remove Reaper/Possessor.
#2 Balance the game: If king is evil, at least queen won’t be evil.

Features:

Queen is in every games, like the King.
Queen’s alligment is random, but never the same one as the King. But she is never neutral. Unlike the king, she is hidden. Nobody knows who she is. When she dies, her alligment is not revealed until the end of the game. Could consider doing the same for the King(s).

Ability:

Passive: Occupation immune. Cannot be converted.

Night ability 1: Thirst for blood

Disabled night 1,
This ability can only be used when nobody was executed during the day. The queen can choose someone that will be instantly executed at night. This target cannot be the king. This ability bypass all protections, just like an execution.

Day ability 1: Queen’s message. 2 uses

Works similar to psychic. Allows queen send one anonymous messsage in her name.
