The Saint

Neutral Special
Vow of Moonlight (Passive) - Immune to being occupied at night, conversion and target changing. Additionally, you cannot accuse others or vote to execute.
Argent Ward (Day) - You choose one target to bear an invisible ward tonight. If your target dies at night, they will be instantly resurrected but will not remember dying. - 3 uses

Heart Sight (Night) - You will visit a target to peer into their heart, learning if they are able to kill at night. If they intend to kill someone that night, you will occupy them, saving their target. Bypasses occupation immunity, but cannot be used on the same target twice in a row. - 5 uses
Sacred Lunar Seal (Night) - Protect yourself with ancient holy magic, making you immune to death at night. - 2 uses
Goal: Save three people other than you from death at night. You lose your abilities upon winning.
