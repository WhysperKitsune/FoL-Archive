Been fleshing out the Monocoins mechanic. So here two new functions for it.

The Monomono Machine

Monomono Machine

From day two onward the Monomono Machine may be used if the player who is in charge of maintaining it is alive. For a cost of 40(+20 per day after D2) Monocoins up to two players may use it and obtain random prizes.

Interaction Between the Hope List and Monocoins

The players who show up in most Hope Lists will gain extra Monocoins per night.

In short, if you are widely scumread you’ll be gladiated. If you are widely townread you’ll gain extra Monocoins. Remember to update your Hope/Despair Lists each night to accurate reflect your reads.

Also I changed the method of randing limited items in the shop. For the gun and the crystal ball the item will be handed to the player 24 hours in after the SoD. If more than one player bid on it then it will be randed between the bidders. The removal of the limit of one for both factions was done in order to prevent people from being confirmed for buying the town gun and other issues like this.
