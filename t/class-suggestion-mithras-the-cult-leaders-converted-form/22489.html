[Class Suggestion] Mithras : The Cult Leader's 'Converted' Form

Class/Lore/Fanfic
Dusked December 1, 2017, 4:17pm #1

A new ability would be added ONLY to the original Cult Leader. This is to make the original Cult Leader a much larger threat and give the Cult Leader more options later into the game.

Ability : Metamorphosis
Only usable with a full cult (4 members) OR as the last member of the cult past day 5
Turn into Mithras tonight. People will be unable to vote tomorrow.
A message will appear the next day alerting the folk of Mithras’s presence :

A deafening roar kept you from sleeping last night, you feel uneasy.

Mithras
The portrait for Mithras would be the red creature present behind the Cult Leaders portrait. (This is what inspired me in the first place)
Wins with cult

Passive 1: Unstoppable Onslaught
Kill all those visiting you at night. Immune to Occupation and Imprisonment. If accused and executed, kill the first player to accuse you. (Similar to the hunter passive)

Passive 2 : Blood lust
You are unable to communicate or understand the words of the living (Words will be jumbled and random). Your lust for blood will be sated in 2 Nights and you will die.

Night ability : Devastation
Target a player, kill them and all their visitors. Ignores Night Immunity.
.
Lore: I am the thing under the bed.

Counter-play: Keep Cult numbers limited at all time. If the beast has awoken use the day to figure out who is unable to communicate. Don’t visit anyone when Mithras is alive to avoid being dinner.
