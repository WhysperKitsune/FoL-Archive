Hydras!

This game features hydras! That is, rather than playing as individuals, each slot will be filled by two players, who must work together. Hydras sign up as a pair.

Hydras will have a private chat on the forums which they can use at any time, and may create private chats elsewhere so long as the host is added.

A note: by moderator request, all participating players will have their title changed to designate their hydra name, to minimize confusion. This will be reverted after the game.

A single member of a hydra replacing out will be handled on a case by case basis.

If there are insufficient hydra sign-ups, non-hydras may be allowed to join so that the game can fill; however, hydras will have preference.

Summary

Vanilla Townie

You are a Vanilla Townie. Your only weapons are your voice and your vote.

Defeat the Mafia.

Town Jailkeeper

You are the Town Jailkeeper. Each night, you may jailkeep a player in the game. You will simultaneously roleblock that player, and protect that player from any number of nightkills.

Defeat the Mafia.

Mafia Goon

You are a Mafia Goon. You share a 24/7 private chat with your fellow members of the Mafia. Each night, the Mafia may kill a player outside your faction.

Reach parity with the town.

Gameplay

  • The set-up consists of 1 Town Jailkeeper, 6 Vanilla Townies, and 2 Mafia Goons. Note that this differs from some other set-ups with the same name.

  • Days are 48 hours unless majority is reached early and nights are 24 hours.

  • Lynches are majority and plurality. In cases of a tie, the lynch will be determined randomly among the players receiving the most votes.

  • No-lynch is a valid vote, and may be hammered to end the day early. In cases where no-lynch and another vote are tied at the end of the day, no-lynch will have priority in the rand.

  • Self-votes are considered valid votes.

  • Votes are locked in MyLo and LyLo scenarios. That is to say, in situations where a mislynch or a no-lynch will guarantee a Mafia victory, the first vote cast by a given player is final and cannot be changed.

  • All votes must be in the form /vote [name of player] to be considered valid, and all unvotes must be in the form /unvote. This is to enable the smooth processing of votes. Votes for either member of a hydra, or for a hydraâ€™s hydra name, will count as votes for that hydra.

  • The Mafia factional kill is NOT mandatory.

  • The Mafia factional kill IS assigned. That is, a specific member of the Mafia must be chosen to perform the factional kill. If no member is selected, the last member who submitted a kill will be assumed to perform it.

  • The Mafia will communicate through a private Discord server or forum topic.
