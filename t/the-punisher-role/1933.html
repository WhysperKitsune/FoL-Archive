The Punisher

The Punisher is a Neutral Special or Neutral Killer type class.
The Punisher cannot be converted.

Passive 1: Defense Mode
In Defense Mode Punish works on players who vote for you. You start in Defense Mode.

Passive 2: Offense Mode
In Offense Mode Punish works on players who vote for your target.

Day Ability 1: Defensively Offensive
Switch from Defense Mode to Offense Mode. (unlimited uses)

Day Ability 2: Offensively Defensive
Switch from Offense Mode to Defense Mode. (unlimited uses)

Night Ability 1: Select Target
Select a target, can only be used in Offense Mode. (unlimited uses)

Night Ability 2: Punish
Punish a player, making them unable to chat, vote or use abilities for 1 day.
Punished players die the night after. “You Are Being Punished!”

The Punisher Unique.

Win Condition:
Survive or kill everybody, haven’t decided yet…

Lore:
A giant, hulking, sadistic monster of a human that enjoys the pain of others.
It gives its victims a slow, painful death.

Comments:
This adds a new mechanic, fear of voting.
For example Helen Whyte votes for Thomas Bailey.
Little does Helen know that The Punisher has targeted Thomas…
Inspired by “The Golem”.

EDIT: merc/sellsword cause “fear of voting”. But ONLY merc/sellsword.
What i’m saying is that we could use another “fear of voting” role.
