New Class: Kingsguard

Feedback/Suggestions
CarnivalMaster August 23, 2018, 5:53am #1

The Kingsguard:
Faction: Neutral, Support. Unique, Immune to Conversion
Win Requirement: Ensure that the starting king survives until the end of the game. You do not need to be alive at the end.
Passives:
Sworn Sword - Immune to being occupied at night and immune to target changing. You know how many more uses the King has of their Guards! ability.
Redemption - I currently am unable to think of a replacement for the old one, I especially want feedback on how the Kingsguard could redeem itself.
Day Ability -
Convene Meeting - At night you will be able to speak to the King. He will know your name.(2 uses)
Night Abilities -
Preside Over - You will protect the King with your life tonight. If he is attacked the attacker will instead target you that night. You and the attacker both are dealt a lethal blow. This bypasses death immunity and redirection immunity. (3 uses)
Inspiring Leader - Tonight you rouse the spirits of the King’s guards. The king gains one use of Guards! (Two uses, but has a one day cool down)

All feedback is welcome! Please write what you think about this.
